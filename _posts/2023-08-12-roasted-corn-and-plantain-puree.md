<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>8 Corn On The Cobs</li><li>Olive Oil, For Coating</li><li>Butter, For Basting</li><li>1 Tbsp Smoked Paprika</li><li>1 Tbsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li> 1/2 Tbsp Ground Cumin</li><li>3 Ripe Plantains</li><li>3 Tbsp Dark Tahini</li><li> 1/2 X 400G Tin Chickpeas, Drained</li><li> 1/2-1 Roasted Scotch Bonnet Chilli (See Tip), Seeds Removed</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Crushed</li><li>1-2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>Roasted Cashew Nuts (Optional)</li><li>Spicy Tomato Salsa</li><li>Avocado Mashed With Mayo And Pesto, Topped With Parmesan Shavings</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. Preheat oven to 200 c/400 f/gas mark 4. Lightly oil a baking tray. </li><li>2. Wrap corn cobs in foil and roast for 15 minutes until tender. Remove from oven and remove corn cobs. Cut corn cobs into 1 inch pieces and place in a bowl. </li><li>3. Melt butter in a small pan. Once melted, add paprika, cinnamon and cumin. </li><li>4. Combine plantains, tahini, chickpeas, chilli and clove in a food processor and blend until smooth. </li><li>5. Add 2 tbsp of oil and process to a smooth paste. </li><li>6. Pour puree over corn cobs and gently stir to combine. Season to taste with salt and pepper. </li><li>7. Spoon into a serving dish, sprinkle cashew nuts over, and garnish with spicy tomato salsa and avocado mayo pesto shavings. Serve immediately. </li></ul>
