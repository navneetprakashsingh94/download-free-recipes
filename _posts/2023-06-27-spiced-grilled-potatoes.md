<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>800G/1Lb 12Oz Large Charlotte Potatoes (Or Other Large, Elongated New Potatoes)</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 1/2 Tsp Paprika</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Cayenne Pepper</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Prepare an outdoor grill for medium heat about 300 450f</li><li>Clean and quarter potatoes cut wedges in quarters</li><li>Place in a zip lock bag</li><li>Pour oil, spice mixture over potatoes seal bag, gently shake</li><li>Place potatoe pieces on pre heated grill, cover. </li><li>Cook till desired tenderness is reached. </li></ul>
