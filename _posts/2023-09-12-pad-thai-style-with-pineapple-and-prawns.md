<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li> 1/2 Pineapple, Peeled, Cored, And Cut Into 2 1/2Cm/1In Chunks</li><li>3 Tbsp Grapeseed Oil</li><li>3 Spring Onions, Trimmed And Thinly Sliced</li><li>1 Tbsp Fresh Ginger, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Tbsp Finely Chopped Garlic</li><li>1 3/4 Tsp Black Peppercorns, Crushed</li><li>1 3/4 Tsp Fermented Black Beans (Available From Chinese Supermarkets), Rinsed, Squeezed Dry And Chopped</li><li>3 Tbsp Sweet Soy Sauce</li><li>1 Tbsp Soy Sauce</li><li>1 Lime, Juice Only</li><li>1 1/2 Tbsp Sugar</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Salt</li><li>12 Large Prawns, Cleaned, Peeled, Veins Removed And Halved</li><li>30G/1Oz Diced Jicama (Optional), To Garnish</li><li>10G/ 1/2Oz Baby Pea Shoots, Thinly Sliced</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cook pineapple in plenty of boiling water for 3 minutes, drain and save the water. Cut the pineapple in half and remove the peel. </li><li>Heat the oil in a wok over medium high heat. Add the spring onions, ginger, chili sauce and peppercorns and fry for a minute. Add the beans and cook for a minute. Add the prawns and pineapple cook for 3 4 minutes, turning frequently. </li><li>Add the reserved pineapple water and the lime juice, sugar, salt and the prawns cook for a further 3 4 minutes. Add the pea shoots and cook for a few minutes. Serve hot or cold. </li></ul>
