<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#chorizo sausages" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chorizo Sausages</a> <a href="/tags#wine" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Wine</a> <a href="/tags#feta cheese" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Feta Cheese</a> <a href="/tags#baby squid tubes tentacles" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Baby Squid Tubes Tentacles</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#fennel bulbs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Fennel Bulbs</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp quality virgin oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Quality Virgin Oil</a> <a href="/tags#salt ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Ground Pepper</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Uncooked Chorizo Sausages</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz White Wine</li><li>200G/7Oz Feta Or Feta-Style Cheese</li><li>20 Baby Squid, Cleaned, Tubes And Tentacles Only</li><li>Vegetable Oil, For Frying</li><li>2 Fennel Bulbs, Thinly Sliced</li><li>1 Tbsp Good Quality Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Plus Extra To Serve</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Mix a little olive oil with the squid tentacles</li><li>Pour a bit of the olive oil into a pan</li><li>Dip the squid tentacles in the wine</li><li>Cook in pan at a medium high heat</li><li>Place the chorizo in a bowl to marinate while you prepare the fennel</li><li>Remove the fennel from the pan and cut off top</li><li>Slice fennel lengthwise into 8 slices, sprinkle with oil and sprinkle with pepper</li><li>Fry fennel in hot olive oil until soft and golden brown</li><li>Add the fried squid into the pan and mix thoroughly. </li><li>Place chorizo on plates and place the squid mixture on top</li><li>Sprinkle feta cheese on top</li></ul>
