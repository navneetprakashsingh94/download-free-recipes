<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#potatoes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Potatoes</a> <a href="/tags#good-quality sausages" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Good-Quality Sausages</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Oil</a> <a href="/tags#Savoy cabbage" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Savoy Cabbage</a> <a href="/tags#spring onions" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Spring Onions</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Milk</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#salt pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Pepper</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1Kg/2Lb 4Oz Potatoes</li><li>8 Large Good-Quality Sausages</li><li>1 Tbsp Vegetable Oil</li><li>250G/9Oz Savoy Cabbage</li><li>4 Spring Onions</li><li>5 Tbsp Milk</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>Salt And Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>In a large pot cover the potatoes with water bring to the boil, then reduce heat, cover and simmer for 30 to 40 mins or possibly till tender drain and put into bowl mash with the butter, salt and pepper. </li><li>Meanwhile, to make the cabbage and sausages heat the oil in a frying pan, add in the cabbage and onion and fry for 1 minute. </li><li>Add in the sausages, lowfat milk and seasoning and cook for a further 2 3 mins till the cabbage is soft and the sausage is heated through. </li><li>Serve with the mash spooned over. </li></ul>
