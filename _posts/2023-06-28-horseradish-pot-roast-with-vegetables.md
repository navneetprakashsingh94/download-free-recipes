<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Horseradish Pot Roast With Vegetables using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 50 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#Maris Piper potatoes thirds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Maris Piper Potatoes Thirds</a> <a href="/tags#beef" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Beef</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp horseradish" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Horseradish</a> <a href="/tags#peppercorns" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Peppercorns</a> <a href="/tags#sea salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sea Salt</a> <a href="/tags#rib beef" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Rib Beef</a> <a href="/tags#carrots" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Carrots</a> <a href="/tags#parsnips thirds lengthways" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Parsnips Thirds Lengthways</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2Kg/4Lb 8Oz Maris Piper Potatoes, Sliced Into Thirds</li><li>600G/1Lb 5Oz Beef Dripping</li><li>3 Tbsp Grated Fresh Horseradish</li><li>2 Tbsp Black Peppercorns</li><li>2 Tbsp Sea Salt</li><li>3.5Kg/7Lb 11Oz Rib Of Beef</li><li>8 Large Carrots, Chopped Into 3</li><li>8 Large Parsnips, Sliced Into Thirds Lengthways</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 300 degrees f. In a large bowl, combine potatoes, beef fat and horseradish and stir until potatoes are coated with fat. Add peppercorns and season to taste with salt. </li><li>Place roast in an ovenproof roasting pan, rub beef with 1 tbsp oil, and season to taste with salt and pepper. Roast for 45 minutes, or until meat is medium rare. </li><li>In the meantime, in a small bowl, combine turnips, carrot, parsnip, and remaining oil. Season to taste with salt. </li><li>Remove roast from oven and place vegetables around it. Increase oven temperature to 475 degrees f. Pour pan juices into roasting pan, scraping up browned bits. </li><li>Transfer roast to roasting pan and roast for 15 minutes. Slice roast to serve. </li></ul>
