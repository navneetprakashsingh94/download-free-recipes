<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>250G/9Oz Picked Spider Crab Meat, Shell Reserved</li><li>Olive Oil</li><li>3 Garlic Cloves, Sliced</li><li>1 Banana Shallot, Very Finely Chopped</li><li>Txacoli Wine For Deglazing (Or Use A Dry, Sparkling White Wine)</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Brandy</li><li>250G/9Oz Cherry Tomatoes</li><li>1 Small Sourdough, Inside Only (Discard The Crust), Broken Into Fine Breadcrumbs</li><li>1-2 Tbsp Sriracha Hot Sauce, To Taste</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Butter</li><li>Small Handful Of Chopped Fresh Parsley</li><li>Pinch Sea Salt</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Secreto Iberico (A Pork Cut, Taken From Between The Shoulder Blade And The Loin)</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Fried, To Serve</li><li>Handful Of Beetroot Leaves, To Garnish</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to gas 5 / 190c / 425f and line a baking tray with baking paper</li><li>In a bowl, combine the crab meat, ground pepper, garlic, shallots, wine, brandy, tomato paste, breadcrumbs, sriracha sauce and a pinch of sea salt and a good grind of black pepper. </li><li>Mix well and set aside</li><li>Take each pork cutlet and make a pocket in the side with your fingers and stuff the crab mixture evenly. </li><li>Gently prick the surface of each cutlet twice, with a fork. </li><li>If you like, lightly beat the egg white and drizzle it on the top of the cutlets</li><li>Heat a frying pan with the oil and gently fry the cutlets until golden brown on each side</li><li>Garnish with parsley. </li><li>Optional lay some beetroot leaves on a plate and place the cutlets in the center. </li><li>They should come right out of the shell when cooked. </li><li>If you don t have the sriracha sauce for the jus use some sriracha powder instead. </li><li>Then add the meat, jus, parsley and eggs to the plate and enjoy</li></ul>
