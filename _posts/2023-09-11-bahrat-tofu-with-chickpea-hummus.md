<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cook the tarka, stir in the flour and a pinch of salt, stir well until combined and allow to stand for 20 mins. </li><li>Meanwhile, place the yeast with the warm water in a small bowl, and stir in the tsp of salt, stir until dissolved and set aside for a few minutes. </li><li>Once the tarka has rested, knead the dough until smooth, form it into a ball and knead it in the bowl of a stand mixer with a paddle attachment. </li><li>Place the dough into a bowl and cover it with plastic wrap. </li><li>Leave for 1 hour until doubled in size. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 220c. </li><li>Put the chilled tarka into an oven proof dish and place in the oven to warm. </li><li>Brush with oil and bake for 30 40 mins until lightly golden. </li><li>Remove from the oven and leave to cool. </li><li>To make the hummus puree the chickpeas, cloves, cumin and coriander in a food processor and with the motor running, gradually add the lemon juice, tahini and oil. </li><li>Season with salt and stir. </li><li>Transfer to a bowl, sprinkle the sesame seeds and cashew nuts over the surface, and mix well. </li><li>Gently stir in all the remaining ingredients and season with additional salt</li></ul>
