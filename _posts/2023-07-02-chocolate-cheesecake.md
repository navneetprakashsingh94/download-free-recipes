<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Chocolate Cheesecake using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 30 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#plain chocolate biscuits" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Chocolate Biscuits</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#chocolate pieces" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chocolate Pieces</a> <a href="/tags#cheese" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cheese</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#vanilla extract" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Vanilla Extract</a> <a href="/tags#eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Eggs</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>100G/4Oz Plain Chocolate Digestive Biscuits, Crushed</li><li>50G/2Oz Butter, Melted</li><li>150G/5Oz Dark Chocolate, Broken Into Pieces</li><li>700G/1 1/2Lb Full-Fat Soft Cheese</li><li>225G/8Oz Caster Sugar</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>2 Large Eggs</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Melt butter and chocolate pieces in saucepan. </li><li>Press in bottom of a 20cm loose bottomed tin. </li><li>Beat cheese, sugar, vanilla extract and eggs until smooth. </li><li>Pour over base, top with remaining biscuits. </li><li>Bake 375 for 40 45 minutes, or until cheesecake has puffed. </li><li>Let stand for 5 minutes, then chill. </li></ul>
