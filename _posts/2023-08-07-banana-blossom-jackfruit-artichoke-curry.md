<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>3 Tbsp Sunflower Oil</li><li>1 Tsp Cumin Seeds</li><li>1 Tsp Yellow Mustard Seeds</li><li>1 Red Onion, Finely Sliced</li><li>1 Red Chilli, Finely Sliced</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Fresh Ginger, Finely Grated</li><li>3 Garlic Cloves, Finely Grated</li><li>1 Tsp Garam Masala</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Turmeric</li><li>2 Tsp Tomato Puree</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Caster Sugar</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Flaked Sea Salt</li><li> 1/2 X 400Ml Tin Coconut Milk</li><li>510G/1Lb 2Oz Tinned Banana Blossom, Jackfruit Or Artichoke Hearts, Well Drained And Sliced In Half</li><li>10G/ 1/3 Oz Bunch Fresh Coriander, Leaves Chopped</li><li>20G/ 3/4Oz Toasted Flaked Almonds (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>In a small pan, heat the oil and fry the seeds until they start to pop. </li><li>Add the onion, chilli, ginger, and cloves and fry until onion is soft. </li><li>Add the garam masala and turmeric and stir well. </li><li>Add the tomato puree, sugar, salt and coconut milk and bring to the boil. </li><li>Stir in the artichoke hearts, cover and simmer for 30 mins. </li><li>Add the coriander and almonds and stir together. </li><li>Remove the lid and let it simmer for further 5 10 mins until sauce is thickened and warmed through. </li></ul>
