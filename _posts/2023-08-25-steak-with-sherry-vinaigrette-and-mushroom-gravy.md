<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Melt 3 tbsp butter in a small saucepan and sweat shallots, clove and garlic until softened. Add chervil and tarragon and cook for 30 seconds. Add cream and simmer for 4 minutes. Add salt and pepper. Cool and refrigerate until ready to use. </li><li></li><li>Heat 2 tbsp oil and 1 tbsp butter in a large skillet and sear both sides of fillets and remove to a heated serving platter. Add remaining butter to pan and cook shallots, mushrooms, and red wine over high heat until liquid is evaporated. </li><li></li><li>Deglaze pan with a good amount of beef stock and reduce by half. Remove from heat and whisk in 2 tsp wholegrain mustard, tomato and tarragon. Taste and adjust seasoning. </li><li></li><li>Pour a bit of the genoise into a small saucepan and add oil, lemon and garlic. Heat to smoking and add the asparagus tips and garlic. Cook on medium high heat for 2 3 minutes until asparagus is bright green and a deep golden brown. Pour over the beef and serve with the pan gravy. </li></ul>
