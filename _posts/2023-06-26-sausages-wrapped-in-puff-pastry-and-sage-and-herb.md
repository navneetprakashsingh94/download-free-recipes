<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#sausagemeat sausages skins" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sausagemeat Sausages Skins</a> <a href="/tags#mustard powder" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Mustard Powder</a> <a href="/tags#tsp sage" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Sage</a> <a href="/tags#tsp thyme" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Thyme</a> <a href="/tags#pudding" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pudding</a> <a href="/tags#sheet puff pastry" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sheet Puff Pastry</a> <a href="/tags#egg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg</a> <a href="/tags#dipping sauce" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dipping Sauce</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Sausagemeat Or Sausages With Skins Removed</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Mustard Powder</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Dried Sage</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Dried Thyme</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Black Pudding, Casing Removed, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Sheet (320G) Ready-Rolled Puff Pastry</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten</li><li>Your Favourite Dipping Sauce, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. Preheat oven to 190c and line two baking trays with a silicone mat. </li><li>2. Roll the sausage skins out to roughly an 8 cm square. Mix the mustard powder, sage, thyme and pudding in a bowl. </li><li>3. Toss the sausage skins with the mixture. </li><li>4. Place the sausage roll out over the pastry and brush the edges with egg. </li><li>5. Fold over the pastry and crimp the edges. Brush the outside of the pastry with egg. Cut vents in the pastry. </li><li>6. Place on the prepared trays and bake in preheated oven for 20 30 mins, until golden. Serve warm with dipping sauce. </li></ul>
