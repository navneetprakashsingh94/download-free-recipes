<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Red Onions, Finely Sliced</li><li>2 Tsp Soft Brown Sugar</li><li>1 Tbsp Red Wine Vinegar Or Balsamic Vinegar</li><li>Pinch Sea Salt</li><li>200G/7Oz Cherry Tomatoes, Halved</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Crushed</li><li> 1/2 Cucumber, Peeled, Cut Into 4 Lengthways, De-Seeded And Diced</li><li>1 Tbsp Roughly Chopped Fresh Flatleaf Parsley</li><li>Pinch Dried Oregano</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Stale Sourdough Or Ciabatta, Ripped Into Small Pieces</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 400 f</li><li>Toss onion in a small bowl with sugar, vinegar and salt, let stand 10 minutes. </li><li>Place tomato halves in a shallow pan and place in oven to bake 10 minutes. Let cool slightly then remove tomatoes from oven. Let cool slightly, then cut into wedges. </li><li>In a large bowl, combine tomatoes, cucumber, parsley and oregano. Add onion, vinegar, oil and season with salt and pepper. Toss gently to combine. Place in a lightly greased 8 cup casserole dish. Bake 25 minutes. </li><li>Meanwhile, preheat oven to 350 f. Spread ciabatta slices with oil and season with sea salt and pepper. Bake until golden and crisp, about 15 minutes. </li><li>Serve sliced tomatoes with salad and ciabatta. </li></ul>
