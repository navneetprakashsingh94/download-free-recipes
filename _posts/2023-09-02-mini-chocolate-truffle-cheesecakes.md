<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Dark Chocolate, Broken Into Pieces</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Full-Fat Cream Cheese</li><li>200Ml/ 1/3  Pint Soured Cream</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>3 Large Free-Range Eggs</li><li>Sunflower Oil, For Greasing</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Chocolate Oat Biscuits</li><li>50G/2Oz Butter, Melted</li><li>Belgian Chocolate Truffles</li><li>50G/2Oz White Chocolate, For Grating, Or Icing Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 175c. </li><li>Crush the biscuits and add the butter, and stir until well combined. </li><li>Place in a piping bag with a 1/2 inch plain round tip. </li><li>Pipe 20 20cm rounds onto a lined baking tray, leaving about 1cm between them. </li><li>Spoon the cream cheese into a bowl and whisk until smooth. </li><li>Stir in the cream and sugar, then add the eggs, a little at a time. </li><li>Pour this mixture into a measuring cup and fill the rest of the way up to two thirds of the way up. </li><li>Bake for 18 20 minutes, or until just set. </li><li>Remove from oven, carefully tip out onto a wire rack to cool. </li><li>Leave to cool to room temperature, then chill in the fridge for at least 2 hours. </li><li>Just before serving, drizzle with melted chocolate, if desired. </li><li>Dust with chocolate sugar, if using, and serve. </li></ul>
