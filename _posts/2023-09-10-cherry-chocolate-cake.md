<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>225G/8Oz Butter, Softened, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>225G/8Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>160G/5 1/2Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>65G/2 1/4Oz Cocoa Powder</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>4 Medium Eggs</li><li>340G/11 3/4Oz Jar Of Morello Cherry Jam</li><li>2 X 80G/2 3/4Oz Packs Of Sweetened Dried Sour Cherries</li><li>2-3 Tbsp Kirsch</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Cherry Brandy (Ideally Morello Cherry Brandy)</li><li>500Ml/18Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>50G/2Oz Dark Chocolate, Coarsely Grated</li><li>Fresh Cherries, To Decorate</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 375 degrees. </li><li>Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. </li><li>Add flour, cocoa and baking powder and mix until just combined. </li><li>Add eggs one at a time and mix until just combined. </li><li>Spread mixture into a 9 springform cake pan. </li><li>Bake for 10 to 15 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean when inserted into the center. </li><li>Remove cake from oven and allow it to cool completely. </li><li>Cover the bottom of the pan with the morello jam. </li><li>Place the cherries in a pot with the cherry syrup and booze. </li><li>Bring to a simmer and cook for 1 minute. </li><li>Set aside to cool. </li><li>For the cream filling, bring the cream to a simmer and add the chocolate. </li><li>Simmer for 1 minute and add the cherries. </li><li>Pour on top of the jam layer and set aside. </li></ul>
