<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>650G/1Lb 7Oz Small Maris Piper Potatoes, Scrubbed And Cut Into 3Cm/1 1/4In Chunks</li><li>2-3 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Large Garlic Clove, Sliced</li><li>6 Spring Onions, Trimmed And Cut Into 3Cm/1 1/4In Lengths</li><li>200G/7Oz Tenderstem Broccoli, Cut Into 5Cm/2In Lengths</li><li>8 Asparagus Spears, Trimmed And Cut Into 5Cm/2In Lengths</li><li>12 Cherry Tomatoes</li><li>4 X 150G/5 1/2Oz Salmon Fillets</li><li>2 Tbsp Finely Chopped Fresh Flatleaf Parsley</li><li>1 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Dill</li><li>2 Tbsp Capers, Chopped</li><li>3 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li> 1/2 Unwaxed Lemon, Zest And Juice Only</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place potato in a saucepan, add cold water to cover and cook for 10 mins. Drain and set aside to cool. Place a large non stick frying pan over medium heat. When hot, add oil and garlic and brown for 2 mins. Add vegetables, tomatoes and salmon and cook for 5 mins. Season and mix through. </li><li>In a large bowl, combine herbs and capers. Add cooled potatoes. Add oil, lemon zest and seasonings to taste. Mix together. Serve on a bed of salad leaves. </li></ul>
