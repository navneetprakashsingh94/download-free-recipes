<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Double Lamb Rump, Boned And Butterflied To Equal Thickness</li><li>Half Bunch Fresh Parsley</li><li>Half Bunch Fresh Mint</li><li>Half Bunch Fresh Oregano</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Peeled</li><li>1 Small Green Chilli, Deseeded</li><li>4 Tsp Capers</li><li>250Ml/9Fl Oz Olive Oil, Plus Extra To Drizzle</li><li>200G/7Oz Large Pitted Green Olives, Roughly Chopped</li><li>Sea Salt Flakes And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>50G/13/4Oz Butter</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Olive Oil</li><li>150G/51/2Oz Couscous</li><li>10G/1/2Oz Almonds, Toasted</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Season the beef and place in a roasting tin</li><li>Add the following ingredients to the tin 1/4 cup oil, 3 garlic cloves, 1/2 teaspoon oregano and the 1/2 cup parsley. </li><li>Mix in to the tin salt pepper, olives, capers, and 2 tablespoons of oregano. </li><li>Let it sit in the refrigerator for at least 12 hours. </li><li>To a large hot grill, add 2 tablespoons oil, 3 tablespoons butter, and oil to the tin. </li><li>Grill the beef briskly ten minutes per side, until cooked throughout. </li><li>Remove from the grill, let it rest for a couple of minutes before slicing. </li><li>Add lemon juice and seasoning and serve with rice. </li><li>To toast the almonds, put almonds in a dry frying pan and set over a low heat for 10 minutes, shaking the pan so they brown evenly. </li><li>When the almonds are crispy on the outside, they re finished. </li><li>Store in a warm place. </li></ul>
