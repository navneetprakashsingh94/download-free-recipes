<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Soft Light Brown Sugar</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Sultanas</li><li>125Ml/4 1/2Fl Oz Malt Vinegar</li><li>2 Eating Apples, Cored And Roughly Chopped</li><li>3 Tomatoes, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>8 Slices Dry-Cured Back Bacon</li><li>350G/12Oz Mature Cheddar Cheese, Grated</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Beer</li><li>1 Tsp English Mustard</li><li>3-4 Dashes Tabasco Sauce</li><li>1 Tsp Worcestershire Sauce</li><li>2 Tbsp Plain Flour</li><li>Sea Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>4 Slices Thickly Sliced White Bread</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Mix the brown sugar, sultanas vinegar and set aside. </li><li>Chop the apples, tomatoes onion. </li><li>Lay the bacon slices on a rack, sprinkle with sea salt and grill for 2 4 mins until slightly browned. Drain the bacon on paper towels to remove excess grease. </li><li>Tear the cheese into 1 pieces and place in a bowl. </li><li>Mix the beer, flour mustard, tabasco and mohlershire sauce in a small bowl. Mix well. </li><li>Remove the bacon from the grill and crumble it. </li><li>Add the flour mixture to the bacon, mix well. Add the diced apples, tomato onion mixture, and salt and pepper to taste. </li><li>Mix. </li><li>Place 1 piece of bread, buttered side down, on a cutting board. Place a heaping tablespoon of cheese mixture on 1 piece of bread. Cover with another slice of bread, buttered side up. </li><li>Place on a griddle. </li><li>Cook until the cheese is melted. </li><li>Serve on a large platter. </li><li>Enjoy. </li></ul>
