<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#pint tomato juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pint Tomato Juice</a> <a href="/tags#celery bunch celery" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Celery Bunch Celery</a> <a href="/tags#dash Tabasco sauce taste" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dash Tabasco Sauce Taste</a> <a href="/tags#dash" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dash</a> <a href="/tags#dash HP sauce taste" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dash Hp Sauce Taste</a> <a href="/tags#limes juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Limes Juice</a> <a href="/tags#ice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ice</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Litre/1 3/4 Pint Tomato Juice</li><li>1 Bunch Celery Leaves (Washed And Picked From  1/2 A Bunch Of Celery)</li><li>Dash Tabasco Sauce (To Taste)</li><li>Dash Creamed Horseradish</li><li>Dash Hp Sauce (To Taste)</li><li>2 Limes, Juice Only</li><li>Crushed Ice (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Put your tomato juice in a pitcher. </li><li>Dice your celery bunch celery into little pieces, put them in the pitcher. </li><li>Chop your hot sauce into small bits, put in the pitcher. </li><li>Squeeze your lime into the pitcher. </li><li>Add some ice to the pitcher. </li><li>Put your tomato juice in your pitcher. </li><li>Stir it all up so it s all mixed up together. </li><li>You can add more ice if you like. </li><li>Put your celery to your rim. </li><li>Enjoy. </li></ul>
