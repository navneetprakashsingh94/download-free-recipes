<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Aubergines, Thinly Sliced</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Ricotta</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Mozzarella, Chopped</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg Yolk</li><li>3 Tbsp Vegetarian Italian-Style Hard Cheese, Grated</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Piquillo Peppers, Roughly Chopped</li><li>Small Bunch Fresh Basil, Leaves Picked And Shredded</li><li>3 Tbsp Ready-Made Tomato Sauce Or Passata</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 180 c. </li><li>Peel the aubergines and cut them into 1 inch cubes. </li><li>Toss the cubes with the oil and roast for about 15 20 minutes until soft. </li><li>Mix the ricotta, mozzarella, egg yolk, and 1 cup of the grated cheese in a large bowl. </li><li>Place the ricotta mixture, roasted aubergine and piquillo peppers in a 9 by 13 inch baking dish. </li><li>Sprinkle with 2 cups of the grated cheese and the basil leaves. </li><li>Bake for 20 minutes. </li><li>Pour the tomato sauce over the top. </li><li>Add a little salt and pepper and bake for a further 30 minutes. </li></ul>
