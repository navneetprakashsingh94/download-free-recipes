<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>175Ml/6Fl Oz Liquid Instant Dashi</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Soy Sauce</li><li>50Ml/1 3/4Fl Oz Mirin</li><li>2 Tbsp Groundnut Oil</li><li>2 Tsp Sesame Oil</li><li>450G/1Lb Fillet Or Well-Trimmed Rib-Eye Beef Steak, Thinly Sliced (A Little Thicker Than Carpaccio)</li><li>1 1/2 Tbsp Sugar</li><li>12 Button Mushrooms, Sliced</li><li>4 Brown Cap Mushrooms Sliced</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Enoki Mushrooms, Split Into 4</li><li>6 Fresh Shiitake Mushrooms (Remove Stalks And Cut In  1/2)</li><li> 1/2 Chinese Cabbage (Ideally Napa Cabbage) Sliced 2.5Cm/1In Thick</li><li>1 1/2 Sticks Celery, Sliced On A Diagonal 5Mm/ 1/4In Thick</li><li>160-175G/5 3/4-6Oz Block Firm Tofu, Cubed</li><li>200G/7Oz Sliced Bamboo Shoots Sliced, Vacuumed-Packed (Alternatively Used Canned And Drained Bamboo Shoots)</li><li>6 Spring Onions, Cut Into 2.5Cm/1In Pieces On A Slant</li><li>Cooked Rice, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Combine dashi, soy sauce, mirin, oil and slant in container of an electric blender blend till smooth. </li><li>For each serving, sprinkle beef with 1/2 tsp. </li><li>Sugar. </li><li>Saute/fry in two batches in separate skillets, over medium heat, for 5 min, or possibly till browned and almost cooked through. </li><li>Transfer to a large saucepan. </li><li>Add in 1/3 c. Dashi liquid, button mushrooms, cap mushrooms, enoki mushrooms and shiitake mushrooms. </li><li>Cook, stirring often, for 5 min, or possibly till enoki mushrooms are tender. </li><li>Add in cabbage, celery, tofu, bamboo shoots, spring onions and remaining dashi. </li><li>Cook over low heat, stirring occasionally, about 10 min, or possibly till heated through. </li><li>Ladle into bowls and serve with warm cooked rice. </li></ul>
