<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Ground Almonds</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Salt</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Bicarbonate Of Soda</li><li> 3/4 Tbsp Maple Syrup</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Butter, Softened</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg</li><li>2 Eating Apples, Peeled</li><li>1 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>35G/1 1/4Oz Raisins</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>2 Tsp Maple Syrup</li><li>10G/ 1/2Oz Cold Butter, Diced</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Almond Flakes</li><li>400G/14Oz Full-Fat Creme Fraiche</li><li>2Cm/5In Piece Fresh Root Ginger, Peeled And Finely Grated</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 170c</li><li>Mix the ingredients from the almonds to the vanilla extract in a food processor. </li><li>Transfer to a large mixing bowl. </li><li>Add the apples, raisins and cinnamon and blend well. </li><li>Pour the mixture into a buttered and lined 21cm loose bottomed cake tin and bake for about 1 1/2 hours. </li><li>Meanwhile, make the glazed apple and raisin filling</li><li>Put the maple syrup and butter into a saucepan over a low heat until melted and combined. </li><li>Remove from the heat and stir in the almond flakes. </li><li>Spread half of the filling over the cake then cover with the remaining filling. </li><li>Melt the remaining half of the butter in a small frying pan and fry the ginger. </li><li>Remove from the pan and pour over the creme fraiche. </li><li>Sprinkle over the cinnamon and serve with the ginger glazed apple and raisin creme fraiche. </li><li>It s also good served with fresh sliced pears or apples. </li></ul>
