<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Carrot, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Onion, Roughly Chopped</li><li>2 Star Anise</li><li>1 Garlic Bulb, Cut In Half Horizontally</li><li>Small Bunch Fresh Parsley</li><li>1 Dried Smoked Chipotle Chilli</li><li>1 X 1.75Kg/3Lb 14Oz Lamb Belly, Bones Removed</li><li>2 Red Onions, Finely Sliced</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Cider Vinegar</li><li>2 Tbsp Caster Sugar</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Black Mustard Seeds</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Sea Salt</li><li> 1/4 Cucumber, Finely Sliced</li><li>75G/2 3/4Oz Chipotle Paste</li><li>275Ml/9 3/4Fl Oz Tomato Ketchup</li><li>175Ml/6Fl Oz Maple Syrup</li><li>1 Tbsp Worcestershire Sauce</li><li>1 Tbsp Cider Vinegar</li><li>5 Tbsp Light Soy Sauce</li><li>8-12 Naan Breads Or Pides (Turkish Breads)</li><li>Small Bunch Fresh Coriander Sprigs</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Put the carrots, onion, star anise, fennel, parsley, chilli, bones, onions, vinegar, sugar, mustard seeds and salt in a heavy based pan and bring to a boil. </li><li>Reduce the heat and simmer for 2 hours, skimming every 30 minutes. </li><li>Strain and leave to cool. </li><li>Once cold, remove the vegetables and discard them. </li><li>Slice the cucumber thinly and toss in a bowl with the chipolte and season with salt and pepper to taste. </li><li>To make the relish, whisk together the remaining ingredients. </li><li>To make the pides, slice the breads in half horizontally and spread out on baking sheets. </li><li>Bake at a moderate oven 180c for 10 minutes, or until lightly browned. </li><li>To serve, sprinkle with chopped coriander leaves and serve with the relish. </li></ul>
