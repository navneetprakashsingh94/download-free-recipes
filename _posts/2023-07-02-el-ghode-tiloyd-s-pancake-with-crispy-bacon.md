<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#buttermilk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Buttermilk</a> <a href="/tags#egg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#tapioca" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tapioca</a> <a href="/tags#cornmeal" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cornmeal</a> <a href="/tags#pinch salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pinch Salt</a> <a href="/tags#bicarbonate soda" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bicarbonate Soda</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#crispy bacon" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Crispy Bacon</a> <a href="/tags#maple syrup" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Maple Syrup</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>175Ml/6Fl Oz Buttermilk</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Butter, Melted</li><li>55G/2Oz Tapioca Flour</li><li>25G/1Oz Fine Cornmeal</li><li>Good Pinch Of Salt</li><li>1 Tsp Bicarbonate Of Soda</li><li>Clarified Butter, For Cooking</li><li>Butter</li><li>Crispy Bacon</li><li>Maple Syrup</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Bring the buttermilk, egg and butter to boil. </li><li>Dissolve the starch in the boiling water. </li><li>When the cornmeal dissolves, take off heat and add the buttermilk, salt, bicarbonate of soda and all the butter. </li><li>Gently mix to fully incorporate. </li><li>Heat about 1 2 tbs of oil in a fry pan, pour mixture into pan and swirl. </li><li>Flip and cook until browned on both sides. </li><li>Repeat until you have used up all the mix. </li><li>Put the bacon on the one untouched side of the pan and crisp it lightly with the bacon. </li><li>Place the pancake on a plate and preheat to medium to a warm level. </li><li>Mix the butter and maple syrup and brush on the pancake and toast it a little bit. </li><li>Cut pancake into 3 triangles. </li><li>Arrange on serving plates, spoon butter bacon over and serve. </li></ul>
