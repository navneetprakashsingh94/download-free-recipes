<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>150G/6Oz Chocolate Oat Biscuits, Crushed</li><li>75G/3Oz Butter</li><li>2 Tbsp Clear Honey</li><li>4 Bananas, Peeled And Cut Into Chunks</li><li>400G/14Oz Cream Cheese</li><li>4 Tbsp Icing Sugar</li><li>1 Tbsp Maple Syrup</li><li>1 X 225G/8Oz Dulce De Leche Or Canned Caramel</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>175Ml/7Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>225G/8Oz Milk Chocolate, Roughly Chopped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Combine chocolate oat biscuits, butter and honey. Press into the base of an 8 springform cake tin. Chill. </li><li>Prepare banana chunks. Spread tinned banana chunks evenly over crumb base and chill. </li><li>Beat cream cheese, sugar and syrup until smooth. Spread over banana chunks. </li><li>In a saucepan, heat caramel caramels, butter and cream together until melted. Remove from heat and stir in milk chocolate. Drizzle over tart base. Chill until firm. </li></ul>
