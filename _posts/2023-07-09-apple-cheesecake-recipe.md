<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>70G/2 1/2Oz Cold Butter, Cut Into Small Pieces</li><li>75G/2 3/4Oz Demerara Or Light Brown Sugar</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, At Room Temperature, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>2 Large Free-Range Eggs</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>1 1/2 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>4 Dessert Apples</li><li>1 Unwaxed Lemon, Finely Grated Zest And Juice</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 160c/350f/gas 4 and spray a loose bottomed 20cm/27cm 9 inch springform pan with non stick cooking spray. </li><li>Place the flour, butter and sugar in a food processor and process to a crumbly texture. </li><li>Transfer to the prepared pan, sprinkle with sugar and press firmly into the base. </li><li>Bake for 20 min, then let cold on a wire rack. </li><li>Reduce the oven temperature to 350f/180c/gas 3. </li><li>Blend the sugar and butter in a food processor or blender till smooth. </li><li>Add in the large eggs, 1 at a time, and mix well between each addition. </li><li>Mix the flour and baking powder together in a large bowl. </li><li>Add in the sugar mix and mix lightly. </li><li>Arrange half the apple slices on the base of the prepared pan, followed by half the lemon juice and cinnamon. </li><li>Spoon the streusel topping over the apples. </li><li>Repeat with the remaining apples and lemon juice and cinnamon. </li><li>Bake for 40 mins. </li><li>Allow to cold on a wire rack before removing the sides of the springform pan. </li></ul>
