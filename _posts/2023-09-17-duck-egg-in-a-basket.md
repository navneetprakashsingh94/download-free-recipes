<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#duck eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Duck Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#slices bread" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Slices Bread</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>3 Free-Range Duck Eggs</li><li>3 Slices White Bread, Toasted</li><li>Butter, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 325 f. </li><li>Lightly coat the eggs with a layer of water. </li><li>Place bread slices on a baking sheet and brush with melted butter. </li><li>Place the egg in the oven and cook for 5 10 minutes, or until the bread turns brown and crispy. </li><li>Spoon some duck egg on the bread and serve with mustard on the side. </li></ul>
