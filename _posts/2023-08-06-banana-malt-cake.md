<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>75G/2 3/4Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>275G/9 3/4Oz Plain Flour</li><li>1 Tsp Bicarbonate Of Soda</li><li>2 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>120G/4 1/4Oz Golden Caster Sugar</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Dark Or Milk Chocolate Chips (Or A Mixture)</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Fine Salt</li><li>2 Tbsp Flavourless Oil, Such As Sunflower Or Light Rapeseed, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Milk</li><li>1 Large Free-Range Egg</li><li>1 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>250G/9Oz Ripe Banana (About 3 Large Bananas), Mashed</li><li>Seeds Or Chopped Nuts, For Decorating (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Pre heat the oven to 180c/350f. </li><li>Line a 2 pound cake tin with baking paper. </li><li>Place the butter and sugar in a medium bowl and cream with the whisk of a mixer until fluffy, 2 3 minutes. </li><li>Add the egg and vanilla and mix again. </li><li>Add the milk and mix again. </li><li>Add the bananas and mix again. </li><li>Stir in the dry ingredients in three batches, alternating until combined. </li><li>Stir in the chocolate chips. </li><li>Pour the mixture into the tin and cook for 30 45 minutes or until a tester inserted in the middle comes out clean. </li><li>Leave to cool in the tin for 10 15 minutes. </li><li>Run a butter knife around the edge of the tin and turn the cake out of the tin. </li><li>Cool on a wire rack. </li><li>Serve. </li></ul>
