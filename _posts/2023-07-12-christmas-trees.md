<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>250G/9Oz Strong White Bread Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>5G Salt</li><li>7G/ 1/3 Oz Instant Yeast</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Unsalted Butter, Softened, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>105Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Milk</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Lightly Beaten</li><li>90G/3 1/4Oz Unsalted Butter, Softened</li><li>70G/2 1/2Oz Light Muscovado Sugar</li><li>120G/4 1/2Oz Ready-To-Eat Dried Apricots, Chopped And Soaked In Orange Juice</li><li>35G/1 1/4Oz Plain Flour</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Raisins</li><li>65G/2 1/4Oz Chopped Walnuts</li><li>1 Orange, Zest Only</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Apricot Jam</li><li>200G/7Oz Icing Sugar</li><li>25G/1Oz Flaked Almonds</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Bread dough combine flour, salt and yeast in a bowl. Rub together the butter and milk in a mixer and add milk and egg to the flour. Mix the dough for a few minutes. Transfer dough into an oiled bowl and cover with a cloth. Allow dough to double in size. </li><li>Filling melt butter. Add sugar and apricots, stir well. Add plain flour, raisins, walnuts and orange zest and knead into the dough. </li><li>Roll dough out on a lightly floured surface to about 12x12 inch rectangle. Brush rectangle with jam and roll up into a log. Cut on the diagonal into 3/4 inch strips. Twist each strip and seal the ends. Place on greased baking sheets, leave 1 1/2 inches of space between. Allow to rise in a warm place until doubled in size. </li><li>Bake in a preheated oven 190 degrees c 350 degrees f for 20 minutes or until golden brown. Cool on wire racks. Glaze with icing sugar. </li><li>Sprinkle with almonds before serving. </li></ul>
