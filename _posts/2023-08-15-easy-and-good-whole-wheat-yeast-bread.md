<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#bread flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bread Flour</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#fast-action yeast" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Fast-Action Yeast</a> <a href="/tags#sea salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sea Salt</a> <a href="/tags#water" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Water</a> <a href="/tags#honey" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Honey</a> <a href="/tags#beaten egg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Beaten Egg</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Strong White Bread Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Unsalted Butter, Diced (Optional)</li><li>7G Fast-Action Dried Yeast</li><li>10G Fine Sea Salt</li><li>350Ml/12Fl Oz Lukewarm Water</li><li>1 Tbsp Runny Honey (Optional)</li><li>Beaten Egg, To Glaze (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. Place flour, butter, yeast and salt in bread machine and set to dough mode. </li><li>2. Heat water and honey until lukewarm. Pour into bread machine pan. </li><li>3. When cycle is completed, remove dough and form into loaf. </li><li>4. Place into greased 9 x5 bread pan. Cover with greased plastic and let rise for 30 minutes. </li><li>5. Bake for 20 minutes at 350f degrees until golden brown. </li></ul>
