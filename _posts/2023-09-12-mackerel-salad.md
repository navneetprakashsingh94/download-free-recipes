<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>4 Fillets Fresh Mackerel, Pin-Boned</li><li>2 Tsp Dijon Mustard</li><li>3 Tbsp Cracked Black Pepper</li><li> 1/2 Head Celery With Leaves, Sliced</li><li>110G/4Oz Walnut Halves</li><li>2 Green Apples, Cored And Cut Into Wedges</li><li>2 Red Apples, Cored And Cut Into Wedges</li><li>1 Tbsp Dijon Mustard</li><li>1 Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>3 Tbsp Olive Oil</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>In a small bowl, mix 1 tablespoon mustard, 1/2 teaspoon pepper, 1/2 teaspoon salt. </li><li>Rinse and dry mackerel. </li><li>Spread each fillet with 1/2 teaspoon mustard. </li><li>Toss together 1 tablespoon mustard, 1/4 teaspoon pepper and 1/2 teaspoon pepper. </li><li>Set aside. </li><li>Combine celery leaves, walnuts and 1/4 cup apple wedges. </li></ul>
