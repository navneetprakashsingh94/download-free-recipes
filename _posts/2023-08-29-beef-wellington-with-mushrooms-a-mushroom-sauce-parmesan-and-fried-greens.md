<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Tsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 X 1Kg/2Lb 4Oz Beef Fillet (Ask Your Butcher For The Middle Section)</li><li>50-100G/2-3 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>4 Portobello Mushrooms, Cleaned And Very Finely Chopped</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Baby Spinach Leaves</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs, Plus 3 Free-Range Egg Yolks</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Plain Flour</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Milk</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Chicken Liver Pate</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Readymade Puff Pastry</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Mixed Wild Mushrooms, Cleaned And Trimmed</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Madeira</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Concentrated Beef Stock (Boil 600Ml/1 Pint Beef Stock Until The Volume Has Reduced By Half)</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>To prepare the filling, heat the oil in a pan and brown the beef on all sides. </li><li>Add the butter and cover. </li><li>Cook for a couple of minutes. </li><li>Add the mushrooms and cook for 2 3 minutes. </li><li>Add the spinach, cover and simmer for a few minutes or until wilted. </li><li>Keep warm. </li><li>Whisk together the eggs, flour, milk and half the chicken liver pate until smooth. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 180 c 400 f. </li><li>Grease 2 oven proof bowls or baking tins. </li><li>Line with a pastry sheet. </li><li>Spread a thin layer of the chicken liver pate mixture in each bowl and a third of the beef filling. </li><li>Mix together the mushrooms, madeira and stock. </li><li>Season with salt and pepper to taste and leave to marinate. </li><li>Roll out the remaining pastry to a 12 x 20cm rectangle. </li><li>Place in the bowls and cover with a damp cloth. </li><li>Spread with the mushroom mixture and fold in half. </li><li>Place seam side down in the prepared tins. </li><li>Brush the tops with a little of the mushroom sauce. </li><li>Bake for 20 minutes or until golden and bubbly. </li></ul>
