<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Flour</a> <a href="/tags#muscovado sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Muscovado Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#tsp ground cinnamon" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Ground Cinnamon</a> <a href="/tags#apples" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Apples</a> <a href="/tags#free-range eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Free-Range Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp demerara sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Demerara Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp jam" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Jam</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Butter, Softened</li><li>200G/7Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Light Muscovado Sugar</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>3 Dessert Apples, About 350G/12Oz Before Peeling</li><li>3 Free-Range Eggs, Beaten</li><li>1-2 Tbsp Demerara Sugar, To Taste</li><li>2 Tbsp Warmed, Sieved Apricot Jam</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 200c 350f and grease a 9 inch flan pan. </li><li>Place butter, flour, sugar, cinnamon, apples and a pinch of salt in a medium bowl and rub together with fingers until mixture resembles breadcrumbs. </li><li>Pour into the dish and bake for 50 55 minutes or until top is golden brown and apples are tender. </li><li>Mix together the eggs and demerara sugar and stir into the apple mixture. </li><li>Pour over the jam if using. </li><li>Cool slightly and serve warm. </li></ul>
