<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>4 Tbsp Soy Sauce</li><li>4 Tbsp Sweet Chilli Sauce</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Crushed</li><li>1 Heaped Tsp Chinese Five-Spice Powder</li><li>1 Tsp Light Muscovado Sugar</li><li>1 Large Lime, Juice Only</li><li>2 X 200G/7Oz Rump Steaks, Trimmed (See Recipe Tips)</li><li>150G/5Oz Fine Egg Noodles (About 2 Nests)</li><li>3 Tbsp Sunflower Oil</li><li>2 Red Peppers, Seeds Removed, Thinly Sliced</li><li>1 Brown Onion, Thinly Sliced</li><li> 1/2 Head Chinese Leaves, White And Green Parts Separated And Very Finely Sliced</li><li>1 Small Bunch Coriander, Chopped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Mix together all the ingredients for the sauce and leave to marinade. </li><li>Cut the meat into bite size pieces. </li><li>Add the noodles to a pan of boiling water and cook for 5 minutes until just tender. </li><li>Drain the noodles well, and return them to the pan. </li><li>Heat the oil in a wok and fry the peppers and onions for 5 minutes on a high heat. </li><li>Add the beef and the marinade and stir fry for 3 minutes. </li><li>Add the lettuce leaves and the coriander and cook for another 2 minutes until all the juices are absorbed. </li><li>Serve with noodle salad. </li></ul>
