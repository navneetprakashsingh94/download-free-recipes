<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>6 X 150G/5Oz Salmon Fillets, Skinned</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>150G/6Oz Cream Cheese</li><li>Small Clove Garlic, Crushed</li><li> 1/2 Lemon, Grated Rind Only</li><li>25G/1Oz Fresh White Breadcrumbs</li><li>25G/1Oz Fresh, Coarsely Grated Parmesan</li><li>2 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Parsley, Plus Extra To Garnish</li><li>Paprika</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 400 f. </li><li></li><li>In large skillet, heat butter and add garlic. Cook over medium heat, stirring, until garlic is soft. Remove from heat and add cheese, lemon rind, and salt and pepper to taste. Mix well. Fillets. </li><li></li><li>Combine breadcrumbs, parsley, and paprika in a shallow dish. </li><li></li><li>Place halibut in prepared baking pan. Stuff each fillet with cheese mixture and sprinkle with breadcrumb mixture. </li><li></li><li>Bake at 400 f for 15 to 20 minutes. Serve over rice. </li></ul>
