<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Fish Salad With Honey Mustard Dressing using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#potatoes ones" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Potatoes Ones</a> <a href="/tags#fillets approx" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Fillets Approx</a> <a href="/tags#beans broccoli" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Beans Broccoli</a> <a href="/tags#sugar snap peas mangetout" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sugar Snap Peas Mangetout</a> <a href="/tags#round shallots" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Round Shallots</a> <a href="/tags#wine vinegar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Wine Vinegar</a> <a href="/tags#salt ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Ground Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#runny honey" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Runny Honey</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp wholegrain mustard" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Wholegrain Mustard</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Small New Potatoes, Larger Ones Halved</li><li>4 Salmon Fillets, Approx. 150G/5 1/2Oz Each, Scaled</li><li>250G/9Oz Green Beans (Or Thin-Stemmed Broccoli), Trimmed</li><li>250G/9Oz Sugar Snap Peas (Or Mangetout)</li><li>1 Long Or 2 Round Shallots, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Tbsp White Wine Vinegar</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>3 Tbsp Runny Honey</li><li>3 Tbsp Wholegrain Mustard</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place potato in a pan of salted water. Cook until they are tender. </li><li>Meanwhile, cook the fish fillets in a frying pan with a little oil for 7 minutes per side, or until cooked through. </li><li>Add the broccoli and snap peas to the pan and continue cooking for another 3 4 minutes. </li><li>Arrange the potato, fish, vegetables and shallots on plates. </li><li>Whisk together the vinegar and season to taste with salt and pepper. </li><li>Whisk in the honey and mustard. </li><li>Season to taste. </li><li>Drizzle over the potatoes, fish and vegetables and serve immediately. </li></ul>
