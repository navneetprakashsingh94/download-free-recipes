<div>Delicious Ginger Dal With Salmon With Mint Sauce using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 30 min. Enjoy!</div>

<a href="/tags#salmon steaks" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salmon Steaks</a> <a href="/tags#root ginger slices" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Root Ginger Slices</a> <a href="/tags#cloves slices" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cloves Slices</a> <a href="/tags#tomatoes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tomatoes</a> <a href="/tags#chilli" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chilli</a> <a href="/tags#pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#cardamom pods" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cardamom Pods</a> <a href="/tags#oz milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Milk</a> <a href="/tags#sea salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sea Salt</a> <a href="/tags#limes juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Limes Juice</a> <a href="/tags#handful mint leaves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Handful Mint Leaves</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>4 Thick Salmon Steaks</li><li>2.5Cm/1In Piece Root Ginger, Peeled And Cut Into Small, Very Thin Slices</li><li>2 Large Cloves Garlic, Peeled And Cut Into Small, Very Thin Slices</li><li>350G/12Oz Tomatoes, Peeled And Halved</li><li>1 Fresh Red Chilli, De-Seeded And Sliced As Finely As Possible</li><li>1 Small Yellow Pepper, De-Seeded And Thinly Sliced</li><li>4-6 Cardamom Pods, Roughly Crushed</li><li>400Ml/14Fl Oz Tinned Coconut Milk</li><li>Sea Salt</li><li>2 Limes, Juice Only</li><li>Handful Of Fresh Mint Leaves, Chopped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Take salmon out of the fridge 20 30 minutes before cooking, and slice it into thin slices</li><li>Heat oil in a pan, add the ginger, cloves, and chillies, stir in a little salt. </li><li>Once the ginger has been pressed, add the tomatoes, and cook for another 10 minutes until the tomatoes start to give up their juice. </li><li>Add the salmon and cardamoms, stir and add 1 pint of milk and season to taste. </li><li>Mix the fish and cook on a medium heat for another 10 15 minutes until the fish is cooked. </li><li>Remove the fish and keep aside for a minute, then take it out, and keep warm. </li><li>Keep the pan juices, then place over high heat and stir for 10 seconds. </li><li>Add lime juice, mint and return to heat. </li><li>Stir, then serve with the fish and milk sauce on the side. </li></ul>
