<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1.25Kg/2Lb 12Oz Baking Potatoes (Such As Catriona Or Spunta)</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil, Plus Extra For Frying</li><li>2 Leaves Cavolo Nero, Stalks Removed, Cut Into 10Cm/4In Pieces</li><li>400G/14Oz Pumpkin (Such As Delica), Peeled, Seeds Removed, Cut Into 1Cm/ 1/2In Dice</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten</li><li>100-150G/3 1/2-5 1/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>530Ml/18Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>Small Bunch Fresh Chives, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>Salt</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven 350 degrees</li><li>Remove and discard the shells and peel potatoes. Slice them thinly</li><li>Mix all ingredients together in a bowl. </li><li>Cut potatoes into very small cubes and cook gently in a large skillet, over medium heat until golden brown. </li><li>Add cream half half and butter and heat till butter melts. </li><li>Add chives and lemon juice and season with salt. </li><li>Place pancakes on an oven dish and pour sauce over them. Bake for 20 min. </li></ul>
