<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Cloves</li><li> 1/4 Cinnamon Stick</li><li>2 Star Anise</li><li>Plain Flour, For Dusting</li><li>1 Tbsp Salt</li><li>1Kg/2Lb 4Oz Braising Steak, Cut Into Large Pieces</li><li>3-4 Tbsp Vegetable Oil</li><li>4 Carrots, Chopped</li><li>2 Onions, Cut Into Quarters</li><li>4 Celery Stalks, Trimmed, Cut In Half</li><li>3 Large Bramley Apples, Cored And Diced</li><li>500Ml/18Fl Oz Ale</li><li>1.5 Litres/2 1/2 Pints Chicken Stock</li><li>1 Bunch Rosemary, Tied Together With Cook'S String</li><li>5 Bay Leaves</li><li>115G/4Oz Fresh White Breadcrumbs</li><li>115G/4Oz Beef Suet</li><li>115G/4Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>2 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Flatleaf Parsley Leaves</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten</li><li>2 Tbsp Milk</li><li>Rapeseed Oil Or Melted Butter, For Glazing</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Season the steak with salt and pepper. In a stockpot, heat the oil, add the steak and brown on all sides. Remove from the pot. </li><li>Add the carrots, onions, celery and apples to the pot and cook, covered, for 10 minutes. Add the ale and cook over high heat, scraping up any brown bits. Bring to the boil and simmer for 1 hour. </li><li>Transfer the meat to the stockpot and add the stock to cover. Add the rosemary and bay leaves. Bring the mixture to the boil and simmer until the meat is tender, about 45 minutes. </li><li>Meanwhile, make the pastry. Place the breadcrumbs in a bowl and stir in the suet, flour, salt and parsley. Make a well in the middle and in a separate bowl, beat the egg with the milk, then brush into the well. </li><li>Turn out onto a lightly floured surface and knead lightly until smooth. Wrap in cling film and leave for 30 minutes. Roll out on a floured surface to a 12inch round. Cut out eight 2in circles from the pastry, then transfer to a baking tray. Spoon the beef mixture into the piecrust and then top with the pastry circles and press down. Trim excess pastry and arrange on top of the pie. Brush the top with oil and bake in a hot oven for 30 minutes. </li></ul>
