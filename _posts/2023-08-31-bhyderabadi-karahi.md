<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Cooked Beetroot, Grated</li><li>225G/8Oz Paneer, Crumbled</li><li>2 Tbsp Lemon Juice</li><li>4 Tbsp Greate Fresh Ginger Root</li><li>2 Tbsp Finely Chopped Chillies</li><li>2 Tsp Sea Salt Flakes</li><li>Handful Finely Chopped Fresh Coriander</li><li>2 Tbsp Gram Flour</li><li>1 Tbsp Smoked Paprika</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Fennel Seeds</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Black Mustard Seeds</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cumin</li><li>Vegetable Oil, For Frying</li><li>8 Dinner Rolls Or Burger Buns, Split And Toasted</li><li>Raita</li><li>Brinjal Chutney</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Prepare beetroot by rubbing it in a water bath for 30 40 mins. Remove the skin and chop. Process the beetroot in a food processor for 2 3 mins. Add the rest of the beetroot ingredients and blend well. Let it rest at least one hour at room temperature. </li><li></li><li>To make the bhajis, mix the gram flour with the paprika, ground fennel seeds, mustard seeds and cumin powder. Make small balls of beetroot mixture and coat each with the gram flour mixture. </li><li></li><li>Heat oil in a large, heavy bottomed pan and deep fry the bhajis for 7 8 mins or until golden brown. Serve immediately with raita and brinjal chutney. </li></ul>
