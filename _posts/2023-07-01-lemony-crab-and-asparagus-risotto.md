<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Amalfi Lemon, Quartered And Thinly Sliced</li><li>10G/1/3Oz Sugar</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Olive Oil</li><li>Salt</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>2 Shallots, Finely Chopped</li><li>700Ml/1 1/4 Pint Vegetable Stock</li><li>250G/9Oz Carnaroli Or Arborio Rice</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz White Wine</li><li>80G/3Oz Unsalted Butter, Diced</li><li>80G/3Oz Parmesan, Finely Grated</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Mascarpone</li><li>1 Amalfi Lemon, Zest Only</li><li>Salt</li><li>200G/7Oz Picked White Crab Meat</li><li>3 Tbsp Confit Lemon, From Above</li><li>3 Baby Courgettes, Thinly Sliced</li><li>2 Tsp Finely Chopped Chives</li><li>1 Amalfi Lemon, Zest Only</li><li>2 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>Baby Basil Cress (Optional)</li><li>Drizzle Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>1 Amalfi Lemon, Zest Only</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the lemon sugar oil</li><li></li><li>1. In a blender, blend together all ingredients. To a large sauce pan, add the oil, shallots and risotto rice. Heat till shallots are softened. Add wine and simmer till wine is absorbed. Add stock a cup at a time, stirring as necessary. Add more stock until rice is cooked. Add butter and parmesan and cook 1 minute. </li><li></li><li>2. Meanwhile, in a small saucepan, heat mascarpone with lemon zest until bubbling. Add remaining ingredients and season to taste. </li><li></li><li>3. Heat a small frying pan with the oil. Add the asparagus and cook for 2 3 minutes till slightly browned. Add courgettes and cook for a further 2 minutes. Add the asparagus and crab meat and cook for 2 3 minutes till heated through. </li><li></li><li>4. Serve risotto in individual bowls, spooned on top of asparagus. Spoon lemon sugar syrup in the centre. Place an amalfi lemon slice in the center. Drizzle the oil and lemon slices over top. Garnish with basil cress and serve immediately. </li></ul>
