<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Vegetable Oil</li><li>4Kg/8Lb 12Oz Braising Steak, Cut Into Thin Strips</li><li>10 Large Onions, Sliced</li><li>15 Red Peppers, Seeds Removed And Sliced</li><li>2.5Kg/5Lb 8Oz Button Mushrooms, Sliced</li><li>2 Tbsp Paprika</li><li>1 Litre/1 3/4 Pints Water</li><li>2.5 Litres/4 1/2 Pints Plain Yoghurt</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat the oil in a large deep sided pan and add the beef and brown. </li><li>Remove the meat and add the onions and peppers to the pan and saute for 8 minutes. </li><li>Return the meat to the pan along with the mushrooms and sprinkle over the paprika. </li><li>Cover with water, cover and simmer for 1 hour. </li><li>Stir in the yoghurt and serve with basmati rice. </li><li>Notes the original recipe called for 100g of baby portobello mushrooms and add 2/3 of the mushrooms during the last 15 minutes. </li></ul>
