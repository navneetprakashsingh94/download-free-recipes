<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>30G/1Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>4 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Cubed Pancetta</li><li>1 Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Celery Stick, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Carrot, Finely Chopped</li><li>400G/14Oz Lean Beef Mince</li><li>2 Tsp Tomato Puree</li><li>250Ml/9Fl Oz Red Wine</li><li>1 Bay Leaf</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Beef Or Chicken Stock</li><li>Freshly Grated Nutmeg, To Taste</li><li>400G/14Oz Penne</li><li>75G/2 3/4Oz Parmesan, Finely Grated</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>35G/1 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>30G/1Oz Plain Flour</li><li>350Ml/12Fl Oz Milk</li><li>75G/2 3/4Oz Gruyere, Grated</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Melt the butter with the oil in a large pan. Add the pancetta, onions, celery and carrot and saute for 2 3 mins, stirring. Add the beef mince and cook over a medium heat for 8 10 mins until browned. Add the tomato puree, wine, bay leaf, stock and green beans and stir together. Simmer for 1 hour. Add the pasta and parmesan and toss well. Season with salt and pepper. </li><li>For the white sauce, melt the butter in a saucepan. Add the flour and cook for 5 mins, stirring constantly. Whisk in the milk and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to low, add the cheese and stir until the cheese is melted. </li><li>For the fried green beans, heat 1 tbsp oil in a frying pan. Cook the green beans, in batches, for 2 3 mins, until golden brown. Drain on paper towels. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 350 f. To serve, spoon the drained pasta into the bottom of a casserole. Top with the beef and bolognese sauce. Bake for 20 mins. </li></ul>
