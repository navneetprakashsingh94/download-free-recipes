<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1.5Kg/3Lb5Oz Belly Of Pork</li><li>3 Tbsp Sea Salt</li><li>2 Star Anise, Ground</li><li>1 Large Orange, Zest Only</li><li>2 Fresh Bay Leaves</li><li>8 Garlic Cloves</li><li>1 Tbsp Sichuan Peppercorns</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Chinese Five-Spice Powder</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Olive Oil</li><li>2 Tbsp Sesame Oil</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Mash the salt into the pork in a large bowl. </li><li>Add the rest of the ingredients and knead until everything is evenly incorporated. </li><li>Leave the pork to sit at room temperature for about 15 minutes. </li><li>Heat some oil in a large skillet and add the pork on all sides. </li><li>Cook until brown, stirring occasionally. </li><li>Reduce heat to medium low. </li><li>Add sesame oil to taste and serve. </li></ul>
