<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Blueberry Tart using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#icing sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Icing Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#puff pastry" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Puff Pastry</a> <a href="/tags#punnet blueberries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Punnet Blueberries</a> <a href="/tags#oz whipping cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Whipping Cream</a> <a href="/tags#icing sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Icing Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#vanilla pod drops vanilla extract" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Vanilla Pod Drops Vanilla Extract</a> <a href="/tags#lemon zest squeeze juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lemon Zest Squeeze Juice</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>115G/4Oz Icing Sugar, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>250G/9Oz Shop-Bought Puff Pastry</li><li>200G/7Oz (Or 1 Punnet) Blueberries</li><li>165Ml/5 1/2Fl Oz Whipping Cream</li><li>25G/1Oz Icing Sugar</li><li>1 Vanilla Pod, Seeds Only (Or Alternatively 2 Drops Of Vanilla Extract)</li><li>1 Lemon, Finely Grated Zest And A Squeeze Of The Juice</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cut pastry into 24 squares and place on a greased and lined baking tray. </li><li>Prick pastry all over with a fork and bake for 8 minutes until golden brown. </li><li>Remove from the oven and allow to cool completely. </li><li>Divide blueberries and place each pastry square atop a piece of pastry. </li><li>Whip cream until soft peaks form. </li><li>Add sugar, vanilla and lemon rind and continue to beat until stiff peaks form. </li><li>Spread the cream mixture over the blueberries and bake in a pre heated 190 degree c oven for 35 minutes. </li><li>Cool for 10 minutes before cutting and serving. </li></ul>
