<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Small-Medium Cauliflower, Cut The Cauliflower Into Florets, Approx. 4-6Cm/1 1/2-2 1/2In, Save The Stalk And Leaves For Another Recipe</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>1 Tsp Paprika</li><li>Cooking Oil Spray</li><li>2-3 Tbsp Buffalo Hot Sauce (Check That It'S Vegan)</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>175G/6Oz Unsweetened Oat-Based Yoghurt</li><li>1 Small Garlic Clove, Crushed Or Finely Grated</li><li>1 Lemon, Zest Only, Plus Juice Of  1/2 Lemon</li><li>3 Tbsp Finely Chopped Fresh Herbs, Such As Coriander, Chives, Dill, Mint - Or A Mix</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Bring 1 cup water to the boil in a large pan. </li><li>Add the cauliflower and cook for 10 15 min. </li><li>To check if it s cooked, drain and let cool. </li><li>Mix the dry ingredients and add half the amount of flour. </li><li>Press into the base and preheat oven at 220 c/400 f/gas mark 4. </li><li>Spray with oil and bake for 15 20 minutes or until golden brown. </li><li>Remove from oven, sprinkle with paprika, buffalo sauce and season to taste. </li><li>For the cheesecake, mix together all the ingredients and mix well. </li><li>Chill for 30 minutes or until needed. </li><li>Spoon over the cheesecake and serve at once. </li></ul>
