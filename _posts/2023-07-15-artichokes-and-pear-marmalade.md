<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>8 Small Globe Artichokes, Outer Leaves Peeled, Stems Trimmed</li><li>2 Lemons, Juice Only</li><li>1 Tsp Fennel Seeds</li><li>1 Tsp Coriander Seeds</li><li>4 Carrots, Peeled, Chopped</li><li>1 Onion, Peeled, Finely Sliced</li><li>4 Garlic Cloves, Peeled, Finely Sliced</li><li>2 Celery Sticks, Trimmed, Stringy Parts Peeled Away, Sliced</li><li>3 Bay Leaves</li><li>6 Black Peppercorns, Left Whole</li><li>300Ml/10 1/2Fl Oz White Wine</li><li>1 Tsp Caster Sugar</li><li>Sea Salt Flakes</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Water</li><li>Dash Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>Handful Chopped Fresh Parsley Leaves</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Mix all ingredients in a deep heavy bottomed pan. </li><li>Simmer, covered, over a medium heat for 1 hour. </li><li>Check after half an hour if needed, add water to hydrate. </li><li>When tender, pour out, cover and set aside. </li><li>In a pan heat oil, add the artichokes, pour sauce on top, cover and simmer for 15 minutes, remove artichokes and cover again for a minute. </li><li>Transfer artichokes to a plate and keep warm. </li><li>Boil the sauce until reduced by half and add pears. </li><li>Pour sauce over artichokes and garnish with parsley leaves. </li></ul>
