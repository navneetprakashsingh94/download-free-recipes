<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>250G/9Oz Plain Flour (280G/10Oz Plain Flour, If Making The Chocolate Biscotti)</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>250G/9Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>3 Free-Range Eggs, Beaten</li><li>220G/8Oz Shelled And Chopped Pistachios</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Dried Cranberries, Chopped</li><li>1 Lemon, Zest Only</li><li>200G/7Oz Shelled And Chopped Hazelnuts</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Dates, Chopped</li><li>1 Lemon, Zest Only</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Melted Dark Chocolate, Cooled To Tepid Temperature</li><li>25G/1Oz Chocolate Chips</li><li>35G/1 1/4Oz Chopped Almonds</li><li>1 Orange, Zest Only</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Vanilla Essence</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Good-Quality Dark Chocolate</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Espresso Coffee</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Small Marshmallows</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Honey</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Whisk together the flour, baking powder and sugar in a bowl. Add the eggs, pistachios, cranberries and lemon zest and beat for 4 5 minutes or until the mixture is thick and fluffy. Stir in the hazelnuts and dates and pour the mixture in a lined loaf tin. Chill for 2 3 hours or overnight. </li><li></li><li>For the chocolate temperature</li><li></li><li>Heat the chocolate chips in a double boiler until melted. Remove from heat and add the almonds, orange zest and vanilla essence. When the chocolate has melted, stir in the cream and espresso to make a smooth chocolate sauce. Put the chocolate sauce in the fridge until needed. </li><li></li><li>On a lightly floured surface, roll the chilled biscotti out to about 3/4 inch thick. Place the biscotti onto a baking sheet lined with a silicone baking mat and spread out with a spatula. Chill the biscotti for 15 minutes. </li><li></li><li>Heat the oven to 350 f. Using a sharp knife, cut the biscotti into rounds and place on a lined baking sheet. Bake for 12 15 minutes or until lightly browned on top. </li><li></li><li>Sprinkle with hazelnuts and cranberries. Brush the honey vanilla over the top of the biscotti and place under the grill for 3 4 minutes or until lightly toasted. </li><li></li><li>Remove the biscotti from the oven, turn over onto a wire rack and let rest for 10 minutes. </li><li></li><li>Pour the honey vanilla over the biscotti and then sprinkle the marshmallows and hazelnuts over the top. </li></ul>
