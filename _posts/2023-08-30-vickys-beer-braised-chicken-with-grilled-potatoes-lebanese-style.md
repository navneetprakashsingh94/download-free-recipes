<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 1/2 Tbsp Sweet Smoked Paprika</li><li> 1/2 Tbsp Hot Smoked Paprika</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Chilli Flakes</li><li>2 Tbsp Dijon Mustard</li><li>1 Tbsp Red Wine Vinegar</li><li>3 Limes, Juice Only, Plus Extra Lime Wedges For Serving</li><li>3 Lemons, Juice Only, Plus Extra Lemon Wedges For Serving</li><li>5 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>2Kg/4Lb 8Oz Chicken</li><li>4 Baking Potatoes, Scrubbed</li><li>2 Garlic Bulbs</li><li>440Ml Can Of Beer, Half Full</li><li>3 Tbsp Finely Chopped Chives</li><li>250G/9Oz Butter, Softened</li><li>Sea Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>1 Head Lettuce, Root Removed, Leaves Separated And Washed</li><li>4 Ripe Tomatoes, Cut Into Chunks</li><li> 1/4 Cucumber, Cut In Half Lengthways, Then Into Half-Moon Slices</li><li>2 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Rub the chicken with the seasoning, cover with plastic and put in the fridge. </li><li>To make the vinaigrette, whisk the mustard, vinegar, lime juice and rinds into a thick paste, add some olive oil if the mixture is too thick. </li><li>Season and set aside. </li><li>Heat some olive oil in a large pan, cook the potatoes for 5 minutes until browned. </li><li>Remove from the pan and keep warm. </li><li>Add the garlic and beer to the pan, bring to the boil and add the chives. </li><li>Gently simmer for 3 4 minutes until reduced slightly. </li><li>Return the chicken to the pan, lower the heat and simmer for 20 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through. </li><li>Remove the chicken from the pan, leave to rest for 10 minutes on a clean kitchen towel, then slice into 5 6 pieces. </li><li>Preheat a grill to high. </li><li>Brush the chicken with a little of the vinaigrette and grill for 2 3 minutes on each side or until cooked through. </li><li>Season to taste. </li><li>To prepare the salad, arrange lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, potatoes and chicken on serving plates. </li><li>Drizzle with extra virgin oil and serve with the remaining vinaigrette. </li></ul>
