<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>160G/5 3/4Oz Porridge Oats</li><li>160G/5 3/4Oz Roasted Whole Hazelnuts</li><li>60Ml/2Fl Oz Coconut Oil, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>185G/6 1/2Oz Golden Syrup</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>7 Frozen Bananas, About 580G/1Lb 4 1/2Oz, Chopped Into Pieces</li><li>2 Tbsp Golden Syrup</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>1 Tbsp Cocoa Powder</li><li>250G/9Oz Fresh Or Frozen Blueberries</li><li> 1/2 Lemon, Zest And Juice</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the crumb crust in a medium sized mixing bowl, combine the porridge oats, hazelnuts, coconut oil, agave syrup and salt. Mix to combine. </li><li>Press the mixture into a greased tin or a square pan. Cover the top and allow the crust to sit in a cool place for at least 4 hours. </li><li>For the filling in a frying pan, mix the bananas, agave syrup and cinnamon with an electric whisk. Heat for about 5 minutes and set aside. </li><li>For the topping mix the cocoa powder, blueberries, lemon zest and caster sugar in a food processor or blender until combined. Pour this mixture over the crust. Bake in the preheated oven at 200 degrees centigrade for 12 14 minutes. Allow to cool before cutting and serving. </li></ul>
