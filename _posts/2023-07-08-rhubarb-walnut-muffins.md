<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>6 Tbsp Sunflower Oil</li><li>200G/7Oz Soft Brown Sugar</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten</li><li>250Ml/9Fl Oz Buttermilk</li><li>1 1/2 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>85G/3Oz Rhubarb, Cut Into 1Cm/ 1/2In Pieces</li><li>85G/3Oz Apple, Peeled, Cored And Diced</li><li>55G/2Oz Walnuts, Chopped</li><li>1 Tbsp Baking Powder</li><li>250G/9Oz Plain Flour, Sifted</li><li>55G/1Oz Oat Bran</li><li>1 Tbsp Demerara Sugar</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Ground Nutmeg</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 425 f</li><li>Combine oil, brown sugar, egg, buttermilk, and vanilla in a large bowl. </li><li>Stir in rhubarb, apple, walnuts and baking powder. </li><li>Stir in flour until blended. </li><li>Lightly spoon oat bran or whole wheat flour into six muffin pan cups, dividing evenly. </li><li>Spoon batter into cups, filling 2/3 full. </li><li>Mix remaining ingredients together and sprinkle over muffins. </li><li>Bake for 15 to 20 minutes until golden brown. </li><li>Cool and serve. </li></ul>
