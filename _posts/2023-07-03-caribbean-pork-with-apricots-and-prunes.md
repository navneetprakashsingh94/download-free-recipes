<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Medium To Large Pork Fillet</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Prunes, Chopped</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Soft Dried Apricots, Chopped</li><li>10G/ 1/2Oz Parsley, Chopped</li><li>2 Tbsp Paprika</li><li>1 Tsp Dried Oregano</li><li>1 Tsp Dried Thyme</li><li>1 Clove Garlic, Crushed</li><li>1 Tbsp Coarse Salt</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>1 Lemon, Cut Into Wedges, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cut pork into thin strips. </li><li>Combine prunes, apricots, parsley, paprika, oregano, thyme, garlic and coarse salt in blender and process until paste forms. </li><li>Place strips of pork in shallow glass dish or roasting pan and coat thoroughly with seasoned oil, salt, pepper and herbs. </li><li>Bake in moderate oven, 375, for about 30 minutes, basting with pan juices every 15 minutes. Serve with lemon wedges. </li><li>If meat seems too dry during cooking, add 1/2 cup water during baking. </li></ul>
