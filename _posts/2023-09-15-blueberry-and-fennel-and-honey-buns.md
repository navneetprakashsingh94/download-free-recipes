<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Strong White Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>1 1/2 Tsp Salt</li><li>1 Tbsp Runny Honey</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil (Not Extra Virgin), Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>1 X 7G Sachet Instant Yeast</li><li>240Ml/9Fl Oz Tepid Water</li><li>1 Medium Free-Range Egg, Beaten</li><li>75G/2 3/4Oz Dried Blueberries</li><li>Oil, For Greasing</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Lightly Beaten, For Glazing</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz White Chocolate, Finely Grated</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place 2 cups of the flour in a large bowl and add the salt, fennel fronds and honey. Mix and rub the seeds from the fennel into the flour. </li><li>Put the fennel into a blender and puree. Place the puree, 4 fennel bulbs, the remaining 2 fennel bulbs and 1 1/2 cups water into a pan and cook over medium heat for 5 minutes. Take off the heat and allow to cool. </li><li>Dissolve the yeast in the warm water and leave to stand for 5 minutes. Mix the egg, the oil and the yeast mixture into the flour, kneading until well combined and smooth. </li><li>Return the mixture to a clean mixing bowl and leave to rise in a warm place for 45 minutes, or until doubled in volume. </li><li>Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured work surface and knead for 2 minutes. </li><li>Cut the dough into 12 pieces. Roll each piece into a circle about 3/4 to 1cm thick. Place a small amount of blueberries in the middle and fold the dough over. Moisten the edges and seal by pressing with your fingers. Place on a baking tray and cover with plastic wrap. Leave to proof in a warm place for about 1 hour. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 400 f. Line a large baking tray with baking paper. </li><li>Heat the oil in a pan and deep fry the buns for 3 4 minutes, or until golden brown. Leave to drain on kitchen paper.
