<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Vegetable Oil</li><li>8 Maris Piper Potatoes, Par-Boiled</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Semolina</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>3 Garlic Cloves, Minced</li><li>1 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Thyme</li><li>1 Bay Leaf</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>1 Beef Top Rump Or Picanha Cut</li><li>2 Heads Confit Garlic</li><li>2 Confit Banana Shallots</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>200G/7Oz French Beans, Trimmed</li><li>2 Shallots, Peeled And Diced</li><li>2 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Tarragon</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>Moscatel Vinegar, To Taste</li><li>20G/ 3/4Oz Horseradish Root, Peeled And Grated</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Make the lintas by melting 2 ounces butter and the butter. Add the whole cloves, thyme, bay leaf and salt. Stir to combine and let stand for 30 minutes to 1 hour. Remove and strain. </li><li>Meanwhile, saute the beef in two ounces of oil on a medium heat for about 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. </li><li>Add the escarole to the beef and saute for 2 minutes. </li><li>Add lima beans, shallots, tarragon, olive oil and vinegar and simmer, stirring frequently, for 3 minutes. </li><li>Sprinkle a little salt on the potato halves. Place a potato half on each of the potatoes and serve with some of the grilled picanha. </li></ul>
