<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>225G/8Oz Unsalted Butter, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>225G/8Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>225G/8Oz Plain Flour</li><li>1 1/2 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>3 Tbsp Cocoa Powder</li><li>4 Large Eggs</li><li>2 Tsp Arrowroot</li><li>450G/1Lb Canned Morello Cherries</li><li>275Ml/10Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>1 Tbsp Caster Sugar</li><li>2-3 Tbsp Rum</li><li>50G/2Oz Dark Chocolate</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 180c. </li><li>Grease and line an 8 inch tin. </li><li>Beat together the butter and sugar until light and creamy. </li><li>In another bowl, combine the flour, baking powder and cocoa, stir into the butter mixture. </li><li>Beat in the eggs, one at a time. </li><li>Press into the tin and bake for 20 minutes. </li><li>Mix together the cherries with the arrowroot and 2/3 of the cream. </li><li>Beat the remaining cream, sugar and rum until stiff, fold in the cherries. </li><li>Turn the cake out on a wire rack, spread the cherry cream over it. </li><li>Scatter the chocolate evenly over the cherry cream. </li><li>Leave to cool. </li><li>Cut into bars. </li></ul>
