<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Chocolate Caramel Fudge Bites using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 13 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#hazelnut chocolate spread" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Hazelnut Chocolate Spread</a> <a href="/tags#plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Milk</a> <a href="/tags#chocolate type" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chocolate Type</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Hazelnut Chocolate Spread</li><li>75G/2 3/4Oz Plain Flour</li><li>3 Tbsp Full-Fat Milk</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Chocolate (Any Type), Melted, To Decorate</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 190 degrees celsius. </li><li>Blend the hazelnut spread, flour, and milk until smooth. </li><li>Add the caramels. </li><li>Drop teaspoon sized balls of the mixture on a greased baking sheet. </li><li>Bake for 15 minutes. </li></ul>
