<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>450G/1 Lb Fillet Of Beef, Cut Into 5Cm/2In Thin Strips</li><li>1 Tbsp Light Soy Sauce</li><li>2 Tsp Sesame Oil</li><li>1 Tbsp Shaoxing Rice Wine Or Dry Sherry</li><li>2 Tsp Cornflour</li><li>3 Tbsp Groundnut Or Vegetable Oil</li><li>3 Tbsp Oyster Sauce</li><li>1 1/2 Tbsp Finely Chopped Spring Onions</li><li>2 Tsp Dijon Mustard</li><li>2 Tsp Madras Curry Powder</li><li>2 Tbsp Light Soy Sauce</li><li>2 Tsp Salt</li><li>1 Tsp Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>4 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Broccoli Florets, Cut Into Small Florets</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz French Beans, Trimmed</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Cauliflower Florets, Cut Into Small Florets</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Peas</li><li>225G/8Oz Fresh Tomato, Peeled, Seeds Removed, Chopped</li><li>50G/2 Oz Fresh Water Chestnuts, Peeled, Sliced</li><li>3 Tbsp Finely Chopped Shallots, Squeezed Dry Through A Linen Cloth</li><li>3 Tbsp Finely Chopped Fresh Chives</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cut beef into 1 to 1 1/2 inch strips. </li><li>Combine beef with the next 5 ingredients, marinate at least 1 hour. </li><li>Mix groundnut oil and next 6 ingredients together to make a dressing. </li><li>In a large saucepan heat the oil. </li><li>Add beef, and fry over high heat, turning often, for 2 to 3 minutes. </li><li>Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels. </li><li>While the beef is still hot, add vegetables to pan. </li><li>Cook for 2 to 3 minutes. </li><li>Reduce heat to low, and add dressing to pan, making sure all are coated. </li><li>Place pan on the burner and add the tomatoes and water chestnuts. </li><li>Let steam for 30 seconds, stir, and serve immediately. </li><li>Place in a glass bowl, and sprinkle with chives. </li></ul>
