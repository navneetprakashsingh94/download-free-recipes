<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>3 Skinless Chicken Breasts, Sliced Into Strips</li><li>1 Red Pepper, Seeds Removed, Sliced</li><li>1 Yellow Pepper, Seeds Removed, Sliced</li><li>1 Red Chilli, Seeds Removed, Sliced</li><li>1 Tbsp Vegetable Oil</li><li>1 Tsp Garlic Granules</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cumin</li><li>1 Tbsp Paprika</li><li>1 Tsp Cajun Seasoning</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Coriander</li><li>Salt</li><li>4 Tortilla Wraps, Warmed</li><li>200G/7Oz Guacamole</li><li>8 Tsp Soured Cream</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. Coat chicken breasts with 2 tbsp of tamari pepper seeds, 3 tbsp of chilli seeds and 1 tsp of oil. Set aside for 30 min. </li><li>2. Mix all spices together in a large bowl. </li><li>3. Heat tortilla wraps under grill. </li><li>4. Warm the guacamole and cream, stir in spices and whisk together. </li><li>5. Heat the tortilla wraps, dip, spread with guacamole and cream. </li><li>6. Place the chicken and rice mixture into the wrap and fold and serve. </li></ul>
