<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>675G/1 1/2 Lb Beef Fillet, Preferably Cut From The Tail End</li><li>65G/2 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>1 1/2 Tbsp Paprika (Hot Hungarian, If You Like A Little Subtle Heat)</li><li>1 Large Onion, Very Thinly Sliced</li><li>350G/12Oz Button Mushrooms, Thinly Sliced</li><li>3 Tbsp Sunflower Oil</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Soured Cream</li><li>2 Tsp Lemon Juice</li><li>Small Handful Of Parsley Leaves, Finely Chopped</li><li>Salt</li><li>Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>450G/1Lb Floury Potatoes, Such As Maris Piper, Peeled</li><li>Sunflower Oil, For Deep-Frying</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cut the steak into about 2mm thick strips. Set aside. </li><li>In a saucepan melt the butter and heat the heat till the butter has completely melted. </li><li>Add the onion and mushrooms, cook for a couple of minutes until the onions have caramelized. </li><li>Add the butter and olive oil, bring to the boil. </li><li>Add the beef strips and stir. </li><li>Cook for 5 minutes till the beef is lightly browned. </li><li>Pour in the cream, stir well and cook for 10 minutes till the beef and sauce are just heated through. </li><li>Season with salt and pepper to taste. </li><li>Add the lemon juice and the chopped parsley. </li><li>Cook for 5 minutes on a low to medium heat. </li><li>Boil the potatoes. </li><li>Mash. </li><li>Serve the meat over the mashed potatoes. </li><li>Garnish with parsley. </li></ul>
