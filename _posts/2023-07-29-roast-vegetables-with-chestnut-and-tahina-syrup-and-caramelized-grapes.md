<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Salted Butter</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li>2.5Cm/1In Fresh Horseradish, Finely Grated</li><li>Pinch Fine Sea Salt</li><li>Pinch Unrefined Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Carrot, Cleaned And Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Parsnip, Roughly Chopped</li><li> 1/4 Celeriac, Peeled And Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Red Onion, Roughly Chopped</li><li>2 Extra-Large Jerusalem Artichokes, Washed And Roughly Chopped</li><li>2 Baby Beetroots, Washed, Topped And Tailed And Roughly Chopped</li><li> 1/4 Red Kuri Squash, Seeds Removed, Roughly Chopped</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Spigarello, Roughly Chopped</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Kale, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Tbsp Extra Virgin Rapeseed Oil</li><li>Pinch Fine Sea Salt</li><li>Pinch Unrefined Caster Sugar</li><li>180G/6Oz Cooked Chestnuts (Either Tinned Or Vacuum-Packed)</li><li>400G/14Oz Full-Fat Milk</li><li>25G/1Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>10G/ 1/3 Oz Muscovado Sugar</li><li>10G/ 1/3 Oz Molasses</li><li>5G/ 1/8 Oz Fine Sea Salt</li><li>24 Red Grapes, Halved</li><li>1 Tbsp White Balsamic Vinegar</li><li>4 Tbsp Smoked Rapeseed Oil</li><li>8 Sprigs Lemon Thyme, Leaves Picked</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Pre heat the oven to 160 c/325 f. Mix the ingredients and wring out, then lay in a baking tray. </li><li>Roast for 30 minutes. </li><li>Saute the vegetables, in batches, in a frying pan until browned all over. </li><li>Drain on kitchen paper and sprinkle the seeds over the vegetables. </li><li>Combine the spigarello, kale, oil, salt and sugar and season. </li><li>Line a baking tray with parchment paper and place a roasting tin on top. </li><li>Spread half of the roasted vegetables in the base of the tray. </li><li>Heat the chestnuts, milk and butter in a pan, until the butter has melted. </li><li>Add the sugar and molasses, then stir to combine. </li><li>Season with salt. </li><li>Cook on the stovetop until the liquid is reduced by half. </li><li>To make the syrup, heat the balsamic vinegar in a frying pan until it caramelizes. </li><li>Add the grapes. </li><li>Cook for 5 minutes. </li><li>Add the oil and thyme leaves. </li><li></li></ul>
