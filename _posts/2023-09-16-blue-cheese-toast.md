<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#egg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg</a> <a href="/tags#blue cheese" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Blue Cheese</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Cream</a> <a href="/tags#Dijon mustard" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dijon Mustard</a> <a href="/tags#dash Worcestershire sauce" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dash Worcestershire Sauce</a> <a href="/tags#salt ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Ground Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#slices bread" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Slices Bread</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Yolk Only</li><li>85G/3Oz Danish Blue Cheese, Crumbled</li><li>3 Tbsp Double Cream</li><li>1 Tsp Dijon Mustard</li><li>Dash Worcestershire Sauce</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>2 Slices Crusty White Bread, Griddled</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat a griddle over med heat. </li><li>Whisk together egg, cheese, cream, mustard and salt pepper. </li><li>Pour into the griddle. </li><li>Turn when egg is set and edges are golden brown. </li><li>Cut bread into pieces about 1 inch square. </li><li>Spread mustard mixture on bread. </li><li>Place 1 bread square on top of dip and toast on side. </li></ul>
