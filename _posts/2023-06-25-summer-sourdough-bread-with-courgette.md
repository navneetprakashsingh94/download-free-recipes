<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Brown Sugar</li><li>3 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>30G/1Oz Plain Flour</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>200G/7Oz Ground Almonds</li><li>200G/7Oz Courgettes, Grated</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Sultanas</li><li>70G/2 1/2Oz Pine Nuts</li><li>1 Lemon, Grated Zest And Juice</li><li>Icing Sugar, For Dusting</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Pre heat the oven to gas mark 4 475 f</li><li>Grease and flour a loaf tin. </li><li>Cream the butter and sugar together. </li><li>When the mix is a fluffy and white, add the eggs, one by one, beating continuously. </li><li>Stir in the flour, baking powder, and salt. </li><li>Then mix in the ground almonds, grated courgettes, sultanas, pine nuts, and lemon zest. </li><li>Mix well. </li><li>Pour the mixture into the loaf tin and bake for about 1 hour or until the loaf tests done. </li><li>Take it out and leave to rest in the tin for a few minutes before turning out onto a cooling rack. </li><li>Sprinkle with the sugar to taste. </li></ul>
