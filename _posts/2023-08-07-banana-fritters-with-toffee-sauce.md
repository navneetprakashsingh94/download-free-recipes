<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#toffee sauce" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Toffee Sauce</a> <a href="/tags#banana" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Banana</a> <a href="/tags#flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Flour</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#tsp spice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Spice</a> <a href="/tags#egg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg</a> <a href="/tags#custard" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Custard</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>Vegetable Oil, For Greasing</li><li>2 Tbsp Ready-Made Toffee Sauce</li><li>1 Ripe Banana, Peeled, Sliced</li><li>70G/3Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>70G/3Oz Butter, Softened</li><li>70G/3Oz Caster Sugar</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Mixed Spice</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten</li><li>Custard, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the toffee sauce</li><li>Blend the toffee sauce and banana until smooth. Allow to cool. </li><li>For the fritters</li><li>Combine the flour, butter, sugar, spice and the xanthan gum. Mix using a fork until well combined. </li><li>Crack the egg into a small bowl, beat well and add. Mix until combined. </li><li>Heat a deep fryer to 350f, and a spoonful of oil. </li><li>Drop the fritters into the bowl of wet ingredients and mix until lightly coloured. </li><li>Use a spoon to remove fritters from the bowl and set them on a paper towel. </li><li>Heat a thin layer of toffee sauce in a frying pan and cook fritters for 1 minute on each side until golden brown. </li><li>Serve with banana fritters. </li></ul>
