<a href="/tags#beetroot" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Beetroot</a> <a href="/tags#rashers bacon lardons" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Rashers Bacon Lardons</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#English mustard" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">English Mustard</a> <a href="/tags#wine vinegar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Wine Vinegar</a> <a href="/tags#rapeseed oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Rapeseed Oil</a> <a href="/tags#salt ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Ground Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#watercress" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Watercress</a> <a href="/tags#Gem lettuce" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Gem Lettuce</a> <a href="/tags#slices goats cheese rind" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Slices Goats Cheese Rind</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Large Beetroot, Cooked, Peeled And Thinly Sliced</li><li>6 Rashers Smoked Streaky Bacon, Cut Into Lardons</li><li>2 Tsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Tsp English Mustard</li><li>1 Tbsp White Wine Vinegar</li><li>3 Tbsp Rapeseed Oil</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>Caster Sugar, To Taste</li><li>75G/3Oz Watercress, Trimmed</li><li>2 Little Gem Lettuce, Leaves Separated</li><li>4 X 1Cm/ 1/2In Thick Slices Goats' Cheese, Rind On</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat a large nonstick skillet. Add 2 tsp of the veg oil and fry the beetroot and bacon for 5 minutes or until golden and crispy. Set aside to cool. </li><li>Add remaining 2 tsp of veg oil to the skillet and fry the mustard for 1 minute or until fragrant and golden. Stir in the vinegar and season to taste with salt and black pepper. Reduce the heat and allow to simmer for 2 minutes. </li><li>Whisk the rapeseed oil, sugar and the remaining veg in a bowl until the mixture is emulsified. Season the dressing, to taste, and place in a jug. </li><li>Add the watercress and baby gem lettuce and gently toss until evenly coated. Divide the greens among four plates and top with the beetroot mixture and bacon lardons. Sprinkle with the cheese rind and serve immediately. </li></ul>
