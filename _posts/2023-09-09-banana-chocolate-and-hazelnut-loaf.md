<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Banana Chocolate And Hazelnut Loaf using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 50 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#sugar light muscovado half caster" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sugar Light Muscovado Half Caster</a> <a href="/tags#hazelnuts" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Hazelnuts</a> <a href="/tags#free-range eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Free-Range Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Flour</a> <a href="/tags#bananas weight" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bananas Weight</a> <a href="/tags#drop vanilla extract" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Drop Vanilla Extract</a> <a href="/tags#dark milk chocolate chips" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dark Milk Chocolate Chips</a> <a href="/tags#demerara sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Demerara Sugar</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>175G/6Oz Unsalted Butter, Softened</li><li>175G/6Oz Sugar (Half Light Muscovado, Half Golden Caster)</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Hazelnuts</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>175G/6Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>2 Very Ripe Bananas (About 250G/9Oz Total Weight)</li><li>Drop Vanilla Extract</li><li>175G/6Oz Good-Quality Dark Or Milk Chocolate Chips</li><li>A Little Demerara Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 160c/350f/gas mark 4. Line a 9in loaf tin with baking parchment. </li><li>Beat butter and sugar together until light and creamy. Beat in egg yolks one at a time. Stir in flour and mashed banana. Mix in the vanilla and chocolate chips. </li><li>In another clean, dry bowl whisk egg whites to soft peaks. Gradually add demerara sugar, 1 tbsp at a time, whisking constantly, until stiff peaks form. Fold into the banana mixture, but not too vigorously. Spoon into the prepared tin. </li><li>Bake for 1 hour or until a skewer inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool in the tin for 20 minutes before turning out onto a wire rack. </li></ul>
