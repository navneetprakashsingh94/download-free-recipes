<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1Kg/2 1/4Lb Beef Bones</li><li>2 Carrots, Peeled And Roughly Chopped</li><li>2 Onions, Peeled And Quartered</li><li>2 Sticks Celery, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Tbsp Vegetable Oil</li><li>8 Peppercorns</li><li>2 Dried Bay Leaves</li><li>3-4 Fresh Parsley Stalks</li><li>1 Sprig Fresh Thyme</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place beef bones, carrots, onions and celery in pot and cover with water. </li><li>Bring to a boil and boil for 2 hours, then skim off fat. </li><li>Add oil, peppercorns, bay leaves, parsley and thyme. </li><li>Cook about 1 hour longer. </li><li>Strain broth. </li></ul>
