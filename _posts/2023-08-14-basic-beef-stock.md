<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2-2.5Kg/4Lb 6Oz-5Lb 8Oz Of Beef Bones (Rib, Leg Marrowbones - You Can Buy These From Your Butcher)</li><li>3 Onions, Unpeeled</li><li>1 Whole Head Garlic</li><li>Large Bouquet Garni Of Parsley, Sage And Thyme</li><li>2 Leeks, Cut In Half</li><li>2 Carrots, Cut In Half</li><li>1 Stalk Celery, Including The Leaves, Roughly Chopped</li><li>2 Large Tomatoes, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Tsp Sea Salt</li><li>1 Tbsp Peppercorns</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cut up the meat, remove any excess fat, and wash thoroughly. </li><li>Bring 6 qts water to boil, add salt, beef bones, and the onions, and cook for 1 1/2 to 2 hours. </li><li>This is a medium low temperature but can be made in a crock pot, so be sure to keep an eye on it. </li><li>Take the meat off the bones and put it in a crock pot or dutch oven. </li><li>Add the remaining ingredients, the crock pot will be hot, add the meat. </li><li>Stir it in with the meat to loosen any stuck bones. </li><li>Cover, and cook in a slow oven 325f to 350f until the meat is very tender, about 5 hours. </li><li>Strain the stock, put it in a clean pot, and skim off as much fat as possible from the top. </li><li>This is your stock. </li><li>Use in your favourite recipes, recipes, or even in soups and stews. </li><li>The stock is for people who dont like beef and it does not always look that way. </li><li>This stock is for oamc, and can also be frozen. </li><li>To reheat, place the stock in the microwave for one minute, warm in the microwave, and then let it cool or reheat. </li><li>When ready to serve, strain the stock into a clean bowl. </li><li>You can also put it in a plastic bag, and seal it with the lid, and freeze. </li></ul>
