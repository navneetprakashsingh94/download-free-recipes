<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tsp Vegetable Oil</li><li>2 Red Onions, Peeled, Sliced</li><li>1 Red Chilli, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Sliced</li><li>200G/7Oz Good-Quality Herb Sausages, Grilled Until Completely Cooked Through, Cooled, Thickly Sliced</li><li>350G/12Oz Penne, Cooked According To Packet Instructions, Drained</li><li>200G/7Oz Ricotta</li><li>2 Ripe Tomatoes, Finely Chopped</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Parmesan Shavings, Plus Extra To Serve</li><li>1 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Parsley</li><li>1 Ready-Made Garlic Baguette, Sliced</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. Heat the oil in a large frying pan and add the onion. Saute for about 3 minutes on a low medium heat. </li><li>2. Add the sausage and simmer for around 10 minutes. </li><li>3. Add the penne, the ricotta and tomatoes. </li><li>4. Season with salt and pepper and toss. </li><li>5. Serve with the parmesan shavings and some croutons. </li></ul>
