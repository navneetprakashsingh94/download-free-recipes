<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>420G/15Oz Strong White Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>4 Tbsp Caster Sugar</li><li>2 Tsp Dried Fast-Action Yeast</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Milk</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Butter, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>Oil, For Greasing</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Blueberries, Halved</li><li>1 Orange, Zest Only</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>Grating Of Nutmeg</li><li>4 Tbsp Brown Sugar</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten For Glazing</li><li>70G/2 1/2Oz Icing Sugar</li><li>1-2 Tbsp Lemon Juice Or Water</li><li>Candied Walnuts, To Decorate</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Mix flour, sugar and yeast. Whisk milk, melted butter and 1 tbsp oil in a bowl. Add liquid ingredients to dry ingredients. Mix well. </li><li>Knead dough until soft and elastic, adding additional flour if required. Let dough sit in a warm place for 15 minutes. </li><li>Preheat oven to 200c. Place blueberries, orange juice, cinnamon, nutmeg, brown sugar and egg into a small bowl. Mix well. </li><li>Roll dough onto a floured surface into a 16 x12 rectangle. </li><li>Spread blueberry mixture over dough. Roll up like a jelly roll. Cut into 12 slices. </li><li>Place slices into greased 8 inch baking pan. Cover let rise in a warm place for 30 minutes. </li><li>Bake for 25 minutes, or until lightly golden. Turn onto wire rack. </li><li>Mix icing sugar and lemon juice and drizzle over cooled rolls. Sprinkle with walnuts. </li></ul>
