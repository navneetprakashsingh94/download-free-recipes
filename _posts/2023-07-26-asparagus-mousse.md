<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#egg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg</a> <a href="/tags#slice salmon slice ham" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Slice Salmon Slice Ham</a> <a href="/tags#handful mushrooms" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Handful Mushrooms</a> <a href="/tags#sauteed" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sauteed</a> <a href="/tags#parsley chives top" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Parsley Chives Top</a> <a href="/tags#salt ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Ground Pepper</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Egg</li><li>1 Slice Smoked Salmon, Chopped Or, 1 Slice Ham Chopped</li><li>Or, Handful Of Mushrooms,</li><li>Sauteed</li><li>Chopped Parsley And Chives, To Scatter On Top</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat up the broiler with a rack above the top of your oven. </li><li>Crack two eggs into your mixing bowl and whisk them together. </li><li>Add some oil, salt, and pepper. </li><li>If the egg is runny and clumps are hard enough then break it up slightly. </li><li>Roughly chop the mushrooms and salmon slice. </li><li>Drain the asparagus and add it to a bowl with the asparagus. </li><li>Drain the oil from the asparagus and use it to grease your ramekins. </li><li>Divide the egg, asparagus and salmon into ramekins and top with parsley and chives. </li><li>Roast your ramekins in a preheated oven to 180c for 25 minutes. </li></ul>
