<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>4 Small-Medium Beetroot, Washed, Trimmed And Cut Into Quarters</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Or Rapeseed Oil</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Butter</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Pancetta, Diced</li><li>200G/7Oz Curly Kale, Tough Stalks Removed, Roughly Chopped</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Star Anise</li><li>1 Cinnamon Stick</li><li>1 Orange, Zest And Juice Only</li><li>5 Tbsp Irish Whiskey (Optional)</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Readymade Shortcrust Pastry</li><li>250G/9Oz Pheasant Breast, Cut Into Thin Strips</li><li>200G/7Oz Readymade Confit Duck Meat, Shredded</li><li>2 Rhubarb Stems, Thinly Sliced Into Batons</li><li>Handful Wild Garlic (Or 4 Chard Leaves With Stems Removed)</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten To Glaze</li><li>Sea Salt And Cracked Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 180c / fan 220c</li><li>Place beetroot in a baking tray, drizzle with oil, salt and pepper and bake for 45 mins or possibly till tender. </li><li>Heat butter in a large pan, add in pancetta and fry for 5 mins over a medium heat. </li><li>Remove with slotted spoon and set aside. </li><li>Add in beetroot, kale, orange rind, star anise, cinnamon and spice mix to pan and cook for 1 min. </li><li>Remove from heat, add in lemon juice and whiskey then season with salt and pepper. </li><li>Set aside to cool and refrigeratefor 20 mins. </li><li>Roll out pastry and line a deep 22cm loose bottomed pie dish. </li><li>Spread the pastry with the kale mix then distribute the fish, duck meat and rhubarb on top. </li><li>Fold pastry edge over the filling and pinch to seal. </li><li>Brush with egg. </li><li>Bake for 35 40 mins or possibly till golden brown. </li><li>Cool, slice and serve. </li></ul>
