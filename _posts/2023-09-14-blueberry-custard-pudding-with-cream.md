<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#blueberries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Blueberries</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Flour</a> <a href="/tags#free-range eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Free-Range Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#oz cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Cream</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>25G/1Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Blueberries</li><li>115G/4Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>115G/4Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>115G/4Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>3 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>30Ml/1Fl Oz Double Cream, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Pour melted butter into an 8 cup x 2 cup souffle dish. Press to cover the bottom. Sprinkle the blueberries over the butter. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 400 f. Grease a shallow ovenproof serving dish and set aside. </li><li>Cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add the flour, eggs and cream and beat until combined. Pour the mixture over the berries in the souffle dish and bake for 30 40 mins until top is browned and the pudding is cooked through. Serve warm or cold with cream. </li></ul>
