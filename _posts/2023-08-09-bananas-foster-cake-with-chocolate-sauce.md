<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Shelled Hazelnuts, Toasted, Skins Rubbed Off</li><li>25G/1Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Tbsp Butter</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Dark Chocolate, Broken Into Pieces</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Whipping Cream</li><li>1 Tbsp Golden Syrup</li><li>Small Knob Of Butter</li><li>1 Tbsp Rum</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Butter</li><li>4 Large Bananas, Split Lengthways And Halved</li><li>75G/2 3/4Oz Light Soft Brown Sugar</li><li>Pinch Of Salt</li><li>Ice Cream</li><li>Whipped Cream</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the cake preheat the oven to 375 degrees f. To make the cake sprinkle the hazelnuts and sugar over a baking sheet and bake for 8 10 minutes until the nuts are golden and fragrant. Set aside to cool. </li><li>Add the butter and mix well to create the hazelnut crumbs. Transfer to a large mixing bowl, add the chocolate and cream and stir until smooth. Set aside. </li><li>For the bananas heat the rum in a small saucepan and add the butter. Once melted add the bananas to the pan and stir together to coat. Remove from the heat and cover with foil. </li><li>Spoon the rum sauce into a large, square cake tin, sprinkle the bottom with a layer of bananas. Spoon on the chocolate sauce, then top with another layer of bananas. Repeat this process with a final layer of chocolate sauce and bananas. Place in the freezer for about 30 minutes to set the chocolate sauce. </li><li>Once chilled transfer to a serving bowl and allow to set. </li><li>To serve slice the cake in half horizontally and place one half side down onto individual serving plates. Place the cake on top of the bananas and drizzle over the remaining syrup. Top with whipped cream and a pinch of salt. </li></ul>
