<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>175G/6Oz Rice Flour</li><li>50G/2Oz Tapioca Flour</li><li>1 Tsp Bicarbonate Of Soda</li><li>2 Tsp Gluten-Free Baking Powder</li><li>1 Rounded Tsp Xanthan Gum</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Salt</li><li>150G/5Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>60G/2 1/2Oz Butter, Melted And Cooled</li><li>1 Egg, Preferably Free-Range, Beaten</li><li>60G/2 1/2Oz Buttermilk</li><li>2 Tbsp Milk</li><li>150G/5Oz Fresh Blueberries</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Mix all dry ingredients except sugar and blueberries in a bowl. </li><li>Mix in wet ingredients, but beat eggs with a whisk and add to flour mix. </li><li>Fold in the berries. </li><li>Let rest for 15 minutes. </li><li>Heat a frying pan on high. Pour about 1/8 of the mixture onto the pan. </li><li>Cook on one side until the top has bubbled and is crispy. </li><li>Flip and cook for another minute. </li><li>Remove from pan and serve hot. </li></ul>
