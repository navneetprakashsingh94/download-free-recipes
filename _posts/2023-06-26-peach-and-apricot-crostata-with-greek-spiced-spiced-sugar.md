<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>4 Ripe Peaches, Stones Removed, Roughly Chopped</li><li>4 Dates, Stones Removed, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Sprig Fresh Rosemary, Leaves Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Tbsp Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Orange, Grated Zest And Juice</li><li>Plain Flour, For Dusting</li><li>500G Block Readymade Puff Pastry</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten</li><li>4 Tbsp Icing Sugar</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Finely Chopped Fresh Rosemary Leaves</li><li> 1/2 Orange, Juice Only</li><li>2 Tbsp Toasted Almonds</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 400f. </li><li>Using a sharp knife, peel peaches, pit and slice thinly, placing in a mixing bowl. </li><li>Add dates, rosemary, caster sugar, orange zest juice, apricots and apricots and mix gently, but thoroughly. </li><li>Roll pastry and fit into a 23cm fluted tart tin. </li><li>Carefully pour fruit mixture into pastry, smoothing over top. </li><li>Pierce pastry with a fork and brush with beaten egg. </li><li>Bake for 25 30 minutes until golden brown. </li><li>Remove tart from oven and allow to cool. </li><li>For the icing, mix together icing sugar, mint sp blend and orange juice, until you get a drizzling consistency. </li><li>To serve, place tart in centre of serving plate. </li><li>Drizzle with orange icing then decorate with almonds. </li></ul>
