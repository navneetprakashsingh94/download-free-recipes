<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>350G-400G/12-14Oz Bavette Or Skirt Steak</li><li>1 Tbsp Groundnut Or Sunflower Oil</li><li>1 Tbsp Capers, Drained</li><li>50G/2Oz Pine Nuts, Toasted</li><li>1 Tbsp English Mustard</li><li>3 Tbsp Mayonnaise</li><li>1 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Plus Extra For Drizzling</li><li>2 Tbsp Lemon Juice</li><li>2 Tbsp Finely Chopped Fresh Flatleaf Parsley</li><li>A Few Shavings Parmesan (Use A Vegetable Peeler To Make These)</li><li>Sea Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Prepare the marinade by combining the capers, pine nuts, mustard and mayonnaise. Mix until smooth. Whisk in the oil gradually until well combined. Cover and refrigerate. </li><li></li><li>Lay a sheet of foil out and drizzle with the groundnut oil. Season and set aside. </li><li></li><li>Preheat grill to high. Brush the skirt steak lightly with oil and place on the grill. Cook for about 5 10 minutes, then turn the steak over and repeat on the other side until golden brown and the meat is rare about 5 minutes. Serve steak on a platter with the caper pine nut sauce and top with parsley shavings. Serve with the salad. </li><li></li><li>I serve mine with brown rice. </li></ul>
