<div>Delicious Berry Meringue Cream using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 10 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#berries strawberries raspberries blackberries blueberries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Berries Strawberries Raspberries Blackberries Blueberries</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#gin" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Gin</a> <a href="/tags#oz cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Cream</a> <a href="/tags#meringue nests homemade shop-bought" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Meringue Nests Homemade Shop-Bought</a> <a href="/tags#mint leaves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Mint Leaves</a> <a href="/tags#sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sugar</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Mixed Berries, Such As Strawberries, Raspberries, Blackberries Or Blueberries, Hulled And Chopped</li><li>2 Tsp Caster Sugar</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Gin</li><li>500Ml/18Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>4 Meringue Nests (Homemade Or Shop-Bought)</li><li>Fresh Mint Leaves, To Decorate</li><li>Icing Sugar, To Decorate</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Toss the berries with the sugar and gin. Marinate in the fridge for 20 minutes. </li><li>Once the cream is cool put half in the fridge for a couple of hours. </li><li>To make the mousse, whip the cream with the mint. </li><li>Add the berries, 1 tablespoon of sugar and 1 tablespoon of gin to the whipped cream. </li><li>To serve, spoon the cream into individual cups, add the creme anglaise on top and serve with a dollop of meringue nests. </li></ul>
