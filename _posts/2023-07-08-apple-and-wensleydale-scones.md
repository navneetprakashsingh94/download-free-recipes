<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Apple And Wensleydale Scones using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 30 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#butter cubes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter Cubes</a> <a href="/tags#cooking apples" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cooking Apples</a> <a href="/tags#eating apples" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Eating Apples</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#Wensleydale cheese" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Wensleydale Cheese</a> <a href="/tags#milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Milk</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Caster Sugar</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>350G/12Oz Plain Flour</li><li>175G/6Oz Cold Unsalted Butter, Cut Into Roughly 1Cm Cubes, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Cooking Apples</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Eating Apples</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Wensleydale Cheese, Crumbled</li><li>A Little Milk</li><li>About 1 Tbsp Caster Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Sift flour into a bowl and rub in butter until mixture resembles breadcrumbs. </li><li>Stir in 1/2 the apple, 1 1/2 tbsp caster sugar and the grated cheese and season to taste. </li><li>Make a well in the centre and add 1/2 the milk and enough additional milk to make a soft dough. </li><li>Knead on a lightly floured surface until smooth. </li><li>Wrap in foil and chill for at least 20 minutes. </li><li>To make scones, gently roll out dough into an 8 round and cut out 8 rounds. </li><li>Sprinkle rounds with remaining sugar and place on a greased baking tray, brush with remaining milk. </li><li>Bake at 220c for 20 minutes or until golden. </li><li>Serve warm with a generous portion of apple and wensleydale scones, if desired. </li></ul>
