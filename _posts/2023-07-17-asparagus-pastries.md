<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#puff pastry" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Puff Pastry</a> <a href="/tags#flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Flour</a> <a href="/tags#cream cheese" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cream Cheese</a> <a href="/tags#spears" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Spears</a> <a href="/tags#egg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg</a> <a href="/tags#parmesan cheese" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Parmesan Cheese</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>150G/5Oz Ready-Made Puff Pastry</li><li>Flour, For Dusting</li><li>25G/1Oz Cream Cheese</li><li>10 Asparagus Spears</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten</li><li>2 Tbsp Freshly Grated Parmesan (Or A Similar Vegetarian Hard Cheese)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 200 degrees celcius. </li><li>Roll out the puff pastry to a large rectangle and cut into 2cm squares. </li><li>Place 1 tablespoon of the cream cheese and 1 2cm of the asparagus on the center of each square and bring the sides of the square up over the filling and press to seal. </li><li>Brush each pastry square with the beaten egg and sprinkle with the parmesan cheese. </li><li>Place the pastries on an oven tray and bake for 15 minutes or until golden. </li></ul>
