<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#pasta" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pasta</a> <a href="/tags#feta cheese" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Feta Cheese</a> <a href="/tags#dill" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dill</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp lemon juice bottle fresh" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Lemon Juice Bottle Fresh</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#beetroot" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Beetroot</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt</a> <a href="/tags#cloves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cloves</a> <a href="/tags#chilli seeds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chilli Seeds</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Pasta</li><li>200G/7Oz Feta Cheese</li><li>20G/ 3/4Oz Fresh Dill, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Tbsp Lemon Juice (Out Of A Bottle Or Fresh)</li><li>Extra Olive Oil, For Drizzling</li><li>600G/1Lb 5Oz Cooked Beetroot, Drained</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Olive Oil</li><li>1 Tsp Fine Salt</li><li>4 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Large Red Chilli (With Or Without Seeds)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Wash the beetroot and cut it into small squares. Roast it in the oven at 180c / gas mark 4. </li><li>When you are ready, place it in a bowl with 3 tbsp oil, salt, cloves and chilli seeds to taste and mix well. </li><li>Boil water for the pasta and then pour into a colander. Take the pasta and rinse it with hot water. Put it into a serving bowl and set aside. </li><li>Once the beetroot is roasted, peel and cut into cubes. </li><li>Once the pasta is done, add it to the bowl of beetroot mixture. </li><li>Add the feta, dill and lemon juice and mix the mixture well. </li></ul>
