<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Plain Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>125G/4Oz Cold Unsalted Butter, Cut Into Cubes</li><li>30G/1Oz Sugar</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>2 Tbsp Milk (Optional)</li><li>225G/8Oz Unsalted Butter, Softened</li><li>225G/8Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>225G/8Oz Ground Almonds</li><li>3 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>1 Lemon, Finely Grated Zest Only</li><li>50G/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>1 Jar Raspberry Jam</li><li>Flaked Almonds, For Sprinkling</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the biscuit base, mix together the flour, butter, sugar and 2 eggs in a large bowl. </li><li>Press into the base of a 12 cup loaf pan and bake in a moderate oven 180 degrees c for 30 minutes or until golden brown. </li><li>Remove from the oven and allow to cool before filling. </li><li>For the filling, heat the milk until it simmers. </li><li>Melt the butter and sugar in a large saucepan over a medium heat, stirring until the sugar is completely dissolved and the mixture is golden in colour. </li><li>Remove from heat. </li><li>Combine the ground almonds and remaining eggs in a bowl, then whisk in the milk and lemon rind. </li><li>Add the flour and mix well to combine. </li><li>Spoon the jam into the prepared clafoutis. </li><li>Pour the almond cream over the jam and sprinkle with the toasted almonds. </li><li>Bake for 45 to 50 minutes or until golden brown and risen. </li><li>Allow to cool completely before serving. </li></ul>
