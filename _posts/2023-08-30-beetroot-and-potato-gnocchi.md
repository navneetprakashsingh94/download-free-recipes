<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Kg/2Lb 4Oz Floury Potatoes, Such As King Edward, Peeled And Roughly Chopped</li><li>400G/14Oz Cooked Beetroot, Peeled And Roughly Chopped</li><li>300G/11Oz Plain Flour</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Lightly Beaten</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>110G/4Oz Butter</li><li>6 Sage Leaves</li><li>1 Orange, Juice Only, Plus Zest Of 2 Oranges, To Serve</li><li>Salt, To Taste</li><li>4 Tbsp Freshly Grated Parmesan Cheese</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the beetroot and potato gnocchi, grate the beetroot and add the potatoes, flour, egg, and pinch of salt to a bowl. Stir everything together and then turn the dough out onto a lightly floured work surface. Knead until smooth, about 5 7 minutes. </li><li>For the orange sage sauce, melt the butter in a saucepan and add the sage leaves and cook for 2 3 minutes. Add the orange juice and season with salt and pepper. Simmer for 5 minutes. </li><li>Gently roll the dough into golf ball sized balls, and drop them into the boiling liquid. Simmer for 8 10 minutes until the gnocchi rise to the surface. </li><li>Place a little orange sage sauce on the plate and top with the gnocchi and sauce. Garnish with grated cheese. </li></ul>
