<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>175G/6Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>1 Rounded Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>3 Large Eggs At Room Temperature</li><li>175G/6Oz Very Soft Butter</li><li>175G/6Oz Golden Caster Sugar</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>A Little Sifted Icing Sugar, For Dusting</li><li>6 Passion Fruit</li><li>250G/9Oz Mascarpone</li><li>1 Level Dessertspoon Golden Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Fromage Frais</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 350 f. Grease and line an 8 inch square pan. Line the base and long sides with parchment paper, extending paper 2 inches above the edge. </li><li>In a medium bowl, sift the flour and baking powder together. Whisk together the eggs, butter, caster sugar, vanilla extract and 1 tbsp water. Fold in the dry ingredients just until blended and pour into prepared pan. </li><li>Bake for 50 60 mins or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Cool in pan for 5 mins. Remove from pan cool completely on a wire rack. </li><li>For the passion fruit filling, place the passion fruit pulp in a small saucepan. Stir on medium heat until boiling then reduce heat and simmer for 3 mins. Remove from heat. Cool slightly. </li><li>Beat the mascarpone, 3 tbsp caster sugar and vanilla extract until fluffy. Fold in the fromage frais. Spread 1/4 of the filling over the cake. Top with the remaining filling. </li></ul>
