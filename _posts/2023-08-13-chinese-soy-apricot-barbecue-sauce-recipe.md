<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#tbsp molasses" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Molasses</a> <a href="/tags#dark brown sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dark Brown Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#oz hoisin sauce" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Hoisin Sauce</a> <a href="/tags#oyster sauce" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oyster Sauce</a> <a href="/tags#soy sauce" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Soy Sauce</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp dry sherry" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Dry Sherry</a> <a href="/tags#knob ginger" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Knob Ginger</a> <a href="/tags#cloves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cloves</a> <a href="/tags#powder" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Powder</a> <a href="/tags#ancho chillies" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ancho Chillies</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Tbsp Molasses</li><li>30G/1Oz Soft Dark Brown Sugar</li><li>40Ml/1 1/2Fl Oz Hoisin Sauce</li><li>20Ml/ 3/4Fl Oz Oyster Sauce</li><li>3 Tbsp Soy Sauce</li><li>2-3 Tbsp Dry Sherry</li><li>1 Knob Of Ginger, Peeled And Grated</li><li>3 Garlic Cloves, Grated</li><li>2 Tsp Chinese Five-Spice Powder</li><li>2 Ancho Or Chipotle Chillies, Rehydrated</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan, over medium heat. </li><li>Bring the mix to a boil and cook, stirring constantly, till the sauce reduces by about one third, approximately 10 min. </li><li>Remove from heat, cool. </li><li>Store, covered, in the refrigerator for up to two weeks. </li></ul>
