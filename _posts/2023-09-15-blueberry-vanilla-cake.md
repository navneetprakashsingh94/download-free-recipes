<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>4 Medium Free-Range Eggs</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, Melted And Cooled, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Plain Flour, Sifted</li><li>600Ml/20Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>170G/6Oz Condensed Milk</li><li>1 Tbsp Vanilla Bean Paste</li><li>200G/7Oz Blueberries</li><li>1 Orange, Zest Only</li><li>3 Tbsp Icing Sugar, Sifted (Optional)</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Chocolate Digestives</li><li>2 Tsp Whole Fennel Seeds</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 300 f. Grease 8 x 8 inch cake pan line base and sides with parchment paper. </li><li>Whisk together eggs and sugar until light and fluffy. Add butter, mix until combined then fold in flour, cream, milk and vanilla and pour into pan. Bake for 1 hour, or until the cake has springy to the touch. </li><li>Allow to cool in pan, then turn out onto a plate. Allow to cool for 30 mins then invert onto a plate. </li><li>Meanwhile, mix together blueberries, orange zest, juice and icing sugar. Let sit until fruit is soft and juice is absorbed. </li><li>To assemble, cut cake in half horizontally to form 2 layers. Spread a layer of blueberry mixture on half the cake layer then cover with another layer, reserving some blueberry mixture for garnish. Cover top layer with remaining blueberries and then spread remaining blueberry mixture over top of cake. Sprinkle with crushed biscuits and fennel seeds. </li></ul>
