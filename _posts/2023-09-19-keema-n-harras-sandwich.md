<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Bombay Mix, Plus Extra For Serving</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Spicy Coated Chilli Nuts</li><li>300Ml/10 1/2Fl Oz Boiling Water</li><li>1 1/2 Tbsp Chaat Masala</li><li>1 Medium Free-Range Egg, Beaten</li><li>Oil, For Greasing The Tray</li><li>4 Large Red Onion Rings, About 5Mm/ 1/4In Thick</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>4 Spicy Cheese Slices</li><li>4 Brioche Burger Buns Or Mini Naans</li><li>4 Tbsp Mayonnaise</li><li>Lime Pickle</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Boil water</li><li>Add chaat masala and make a well in the centre and slowly pour in egg</li><li>Mix the cooled naan dough i steamed one at a time and shape into a round ball</li><li>Heat oil on high</li><li>Fry the flattened onion rings, flipping them with tongs until lightly browned on all sides, season with salt and remove to drain</li><li>On the bottom piece of naan, place a slice of cheese</li><li>Place the naan over the grilled naan</li><li>Cut in half and serve</li></ul>
