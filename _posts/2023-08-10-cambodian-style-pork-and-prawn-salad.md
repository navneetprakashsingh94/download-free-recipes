<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Caster Sugar</li><li>4 Tbsp Fish Sauce</li><li>1 Lime, Juice Only</li><li>3 Garlic Cloves</li><li>1 Bird'S-Eye Chilli</li><li>1 Carrot, Julienned</li><li>220G/8Oz Rice Flour</li><li>2 Tbsp Cornflour</li><li>1 Tsp Turmeric</li><li>1 X 400Ml Tin Coconut Milk</li><li>2 Tbsp Peanut Oil</li><li>4 Banana Shallots, Halved And Cut Into Thin Wedges</li><li>4 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Pork Loin, Thinly Sliced</li><li>12 King Prawns, Peeled, Halved</li><li>130G/4 1/2Oz Beansprouts</li><li>1 Cos Lettuce, Washed And Leaves Separated</li><li>1 Cucumber, Cut Into Thin Batons</li><li>Bunch Fresh Mint</li><li>Bunch Fresh Coriander</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the dressing, combine sugar, fish sauce, lime juice, cloves and chilli in a small saucepan and bring to the boil. </li><li>Reduce heat to low and simmer for 3 4 minutes or until the sugar has dissolved and the sauce has thickened slightly. </li><li>Cover with plastic wrap and keep warm. </li><li>For the salad, place carrot, flour, salt, pepper and turmeric in a medium bowl and mix to combine. </li><li>Stir in coconut milk until just combined. </li><li>Heat a wok over medium high heat and add oil. </li><li>Add the shallots and a pinch of salt and cook for 1 minute. </li><li>Add pork and prawns and cook for 1 minute. </li><li>Remove from heat and drain off any fat. </li><li>Add the bean sprouts and lettuce, toss until wilted, then pour in the dressing. </li><li>Divide between two serving plates. </li><li>Top with cucumber, mint and coriander, and season with sea salt and pepper. </li><li>Serve with lime wedges. </li></ul>
