<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Blueberry Buttermilk Ice Cream using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 10 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#oz cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Cream</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#vanilla extract" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Vanilla Extract</a> <a href="/tags#sheets bowl water alternative" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sheets Bowl Water Alternative</a> <a href="/tags#buttermilk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Buttermilk</a> <a href="/tags#blueberries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Blueberries</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#star anise" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Star Anise</a> <a href="/tags#blueberries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Blueberries</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>70G/2 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>2 1/2 Sheets Gelatine, Soaked In A Bowl Of Cold Water (Or Use A Vegetarian Alternative)</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Buttermilk</li><li>90G/3 1/4Oz Blueberries</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Star Anise</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Blueberries</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Whisk the cream until stiff. </li><li>Mix in sugar and vanilla. </li><li>Lay a sheet of plastic wrap on a flat surface and set up a bowl for freezing the ice cream mixture. </li><li>Spoon in the buttermilk and gently mix. </li><li>Fill the bowl with ice and freeze. </li><li>For the blueberry sauce place all ingredients in a saucepan over a medium heat. </li><li>Cook until the sugar dissolves, then bring to the boil. </li><li>Reduce the heat and simmer for 20 minutes until sauce thickens. </li><li>Let cool. </li><li>Spoon the sauce onto the freezer to harden. </li><li>Divide the ice cream mixture between 4 small serving glasses or a large resealable container. </li><li>Top with blueberries and serve immediately. </li><li>Enjoy</li></ul>
