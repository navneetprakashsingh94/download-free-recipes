<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>4 Small Bananas, 3 Mashed (340G/12Oz Prepared Weight), 1 Sliced Lengthways</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Salted Butter, Melted, Plus Extra For Greasing The Tin</li><li>175G/6Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>1 Large Sprig Fresh Thyme, Leaves Picked</li><li>120Ml/4Fl Oz Olive Oil</li><li>75Ml/2 1/2Fl Oz Full-Fat Milk, At Room Temperature</li><li>300G/10Oz Self-Raising Flour, Sifted</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>45G/1 1/2Oz Salted Butter</li><li>60Ml Double Cream</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Salt Flakes</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat oil in a deep frying pan or wok. </li><li>Peel and cut bananas into thick chunks. Place them in a large bowl. </li><li>Pour over the milk and toss until all the banana pieces are coated. </li><li>Place bananas in the butter and stir until well coated with sugar and add salt and thyme sprigs. </li><li>Preheat oven to 200c. </li><li>Line a baking tray with baking paper. </li><li>Scoop the banana mixture into round flattened biscuits and bake in preheated oven for about 30 minutes, or until golden brown. </li><li>Let cool on the tray for 10 minutes before serving. </li></ul>
