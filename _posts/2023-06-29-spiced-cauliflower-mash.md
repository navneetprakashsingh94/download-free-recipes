<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li> 1/2 Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Turmeric</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Paprika</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Cayenne Pepper</li><li>4 Baby Cauliflowers, Quartered</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>2 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Coriander</li><li>1 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Coriander</li><li>50G/2Oz Toasted Flaked Almonds</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat oil over medium heat in large saucepan. Add onion cook, stirring frequently, until slightly softened. Add turmeric, cumin, cayenne and cauliflowers cook, stirring occasionally, until cauliflowers are softened about 10 minutes. Add cream simmer 1 minute. Stir in fresh coriander. </li><li>Serve sprinkled with chopped coriander and slivered almonds. </li></ul>
