<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Runny Honey</li><li>4 Tbsp Soy Sauce</li><li>1 Lime, Zest And Juice</li><li>2 Tbsp Sesame Oil</li><li>Pinch Chilli Flakes</li><li>20G/ 2/3 Oz Fresh Root Ginger, Peeled And Grated</li><li>8 Chicken Thighs, Skin On, Bone In</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Salted Butter, Diced</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>2 Spring Onions, Finely Sliced</li><li>2 Tsp Sesame Seeds</li><li>10G/ 1/3 Oz Fresh Coriander, Stalks Finely Chopped, Leaves Roughly Chopped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 200c / 475 f. </li><li>Rinse chicken and pat dry. Mix together oil and tumeric and brush over wings. Place in a baking dish, with seam down and bake until golden brown approx 20 mins. </li><li>Blend honey, soy sauce, vinegar, oil and sesame seeds. </li><li>When wings are fully cooked, remove from oven and transfer to a dish with remaining oil mixture. Sprinkle with onions. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. </li><li>Toss gently and serve. </li></ul>
