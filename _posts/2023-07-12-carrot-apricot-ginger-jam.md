<div>Delicious Carrot Apricot Ginger Jam using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 30 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#carrot" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Carrot</a> <a href="/tags#apricots" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Apricots</a> <a href="/tags#piece ginger" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Piece Ginger</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#lemon juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lemon Juice</a> <a href="/tags#almonds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Almonds</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Carrot, Grated, Peeled</li><li>600G/1Lb 5Oz Fresh Apricots</li><li>5Cm/2In Piece Fresh Ginger, Peeled, Grated</li><li>400G/14Oz Granulated Or Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Small Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>50G/2Oz Chopped Almonds</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Bring carrots, apricots, ginger, water, sugar and lemon juice to a boil. </li><li>Simmer for about 15 minutes or until carrots and apricots are very tender. </li><li>Remove ginger and carrots and drain, reserving the cooking liquid. </li><li>Add almonds and cook gently for a further 3 minutes. </li><li>Pour into hot, sterilized jars and seal. </li><li>This can be used in place of baking spices. </li><li>To make your own apricot jello, crush apricots with hand and put into a saucepan. </li><li>Cover with boiling water and boil for about 5 minutes or until jello is clear. </li><li>Remove from heat and allow to cool. </li><li>Using a blender or fork, blend until a smooth paste is formed. </li><li>Return to the pan and bring to boil again. </li><li>Stir in the almonds and remove from the heat. </li><li>Pour into hot sterilised jars, filling to within 1/2 inch of the top. </li><li>Seal immediately and store in a cool, dark place. </li></ul>
