<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Chocolate Oaty Biscuits</li><li>115G/4Oz Butter</li><li>115G/4Oz Soft Dark Brown Sugar</li><li>397G/14Oz Can Sweetened Condensed Milk</li><li>450Ml/16Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>5 Ripe But Firm Bananas</li><li>1 Tsp Fresh Lemon Juice</li><li>25G/1Oz Coarsely Grated Plain Chocolate Or Chocolate Curls</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Combine the biscuit crumbs, butter and brown sugar. Pat evenly into a greased 9 inch pie plate and refrigerate for 20 minutes. </li><li>Beat the milk and cream of coconut in a large bowl until creamy. Peel and slice the bananas, sprinkle with lemon juice and mash lightly. Fold the bananas into the cream. Pour into prepared crust. Refrigerate for 2 hours or until set. Garnish with chocolate curls. </li></ul>
