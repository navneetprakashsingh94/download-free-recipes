<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2-3 Long Red Chillies, Seeds Removed And Finely Chopped</li><li>10-12 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Large Handfuls Finely Chopped Fresh Curly-Leaved Parsley, Leaves Only</li><li>4 Heaped Tsp Dried Oregano</li><li>2 Tsp Sea Salt Flakes</li><li>5 Tbsp Red Wine Vinegar</li><li>4-5 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>2Kg/4 1/2Lb Well-Hung Boneless Beef Sirloin, Not Rolled (Weight After Bone Removed)</li><li>Sea Salt Flakes And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. In food processor, blend all ingredients except beef together, till powdered. Pour the marinade over beef in a sealable bag. Chill overnight or at least 4 hrs to make the meat. </li><li></li><li>2. Remove beef from marinade, pat dry, sprinkle with the salt and pepper, roll up into a jelly roll. Place the beef into a baking dish, wrap, and refrigerate for a minimum of 1 hour. </li><li></li><li>3. Preheat oven to 150 degrees celsius 400 degrees fahrenheit. Grill the beef for 15 minutes on all sides, until golden brown and the meat is cooked to desired doneness. Serve on top of rice. </li></ul>
