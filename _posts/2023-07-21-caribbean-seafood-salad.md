<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Cherry Tomatoes, Halved</li><li>2 Sprigs Fresh Thyme, Leaves Picked</li><li>Salt And Pepper</li><li>1 Ripe Unbruised Avocado</li><li> 1/2 Lime, Juice Only</li><li>1 Cooked Cock Crab, White And Brown Meat Separated</li><li>1 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Coriander</li><li>1 Tbsp Mayonnaise</li><li>Sea Salt And White Pepper</li><li>2 Pink Grapefruit, Juice Only</li><li>1 Tbsp Sugar Syrup</li><li>1 Tsp Of Xanthan Gum</li><li>Pinch Smoked Paprika</li><li>1 Tbsp Cream</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>2 Large Diver-Caught King Scallops, Shell And Corals Removed</li><li>Selection Of Sea Aster, Sea Kale & Purslane, Blanched And Kept Warm</li><li>1 Tbsp Coriander Cress</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Add enough water into pan to bring to the boil then add the tomatoes and thyme. Boil for 1 minute then drain. </li><li>Cut the avocado in half and remove the pit. With a teaspoon scoop out the flesh and place in a bowl. Add the lime juice, crab meat, coriander, mayonnaise, and salt pepper and mash with a fork. </li><li>Mix all ingredients well and refrigerate until chilled. The avocado may be added after the tomatoes. </li><li></li><li>Place the grapefruit juice, sugar syrup, xanthan gum and paprika in the bowl of a food processor. Mix and then add the cream. Season with salt and pepper. </li><li></li><li>Warm a large skillet with oil. Place the scallops in the pan and fry for 2 3 minutes on each side until a golden color. </li><li></li><li>Pour 1 1 1/2 cup of the salsa over the avocado. Top with the crab salad and the purslane. Drizzle the remaining salsa over the salad. Sprinkle with the coriander. Serve. </li></ul>
