<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>Spray Oil, For Frying</li><li>600G/1Lb 5Oz Lean Braising Steak, All Visible Fat Removed</li><li>1 Litre/1 3/4 Pints Beef Stock, Made From 2 Low-Sodium Cubes</li><li>1 Large Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Crushed</li><li>2 Celery Sticks, Finely Chopped</li><li>3 Carrots, 1 Finely Chopped, Rest Cut Into Chunks</li><li>1 Tbsp Tomato Puree</li><li>1 Tbsp Balsamic Vinegar</li><li>1 Parsnip, Cut Into Chunks</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Butternut Squash (About Half A Squash)</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>Pinch Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>2 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Parsley Or Thyme (Depending On Preference)</li><li>1 Egg Yolk, Lightly Beaten</li><li>1 Tsp Olive Oil</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Brown the beef and drain fat, heat oil, add beef and brown. </li><li>Add stock and seasonings. </li><li>Cover and simmer 30 minutes. </li><li>Add celery and carrots and cook for 15 minutes. </li><li>Add tomato puree, vinegar and 2 cups water. </li><li>Cover and simmer for 5 minutes. </li><li>Remove from heat. </li><li>Sprinkle parsnips and squash cubes with salt. </li><li>Coat well with flour. </li><li>Cut into cubes and deep fry until golden. </li><li>Remove from oil and drain on paper towel. </li><li>Season with salt, pepper and thyme. </li><li>Toss in egg yolk and serve. </li></ul>
