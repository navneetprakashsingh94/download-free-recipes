<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>175G/6Oz Digestive Biscuits</li><li>75G/3Oz Butter</li><li>200G/7Oz Bramley Apples</li><li>75G/3Oz Butter, Softened</li><li>75G/3Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs, Beaten</li><li>75G/3Oz Ground Almonds</li><li>1 Tsp Almond Extract</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Flaked Almonds</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 170 c. </li><li>Line a cake tin with baking paper. </li><li>Slice each biscuit in half horizontally. </li><li>Place butter into a hot frying pan, turn heat on, and melt, then cook biscuits until golden brown. </li><li>Place in a bowl and toss with butter. </li><li>Peel apples and chop finely. </li><li>Beat together butter and sugar until pale and fluffy. </li><li>Beat in eggs one at a time. </li><li>Mix in apple and almonds and almond extract. </li><li>Place half the biscuits into the prepared tin, top with half the apple mixture, followed by remaining biscuits. </li><li>Smooth the top, and sprinkle with sliced almonds, if desired. </li><li>Bake for about 40 45 minutes, or until the cake is set and the edges start to brown. </li><li>If desired, pour sugar syrup over the top to serve. </li></ul>
