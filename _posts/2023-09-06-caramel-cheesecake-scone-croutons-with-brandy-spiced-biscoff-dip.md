<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>4 Tsp Brandy (Optional)</li><li>200G/7Oz Speculoos Biscuits, Plus A Few Extra For Decoration</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>340G/12Oz Cream Cheese</li><li>200G/7Oz Smooth Biscuit Spread, Such As Biscoff</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>2 Tbsp Brandy (Optional)</li><li>4 Tbsp Ready-Made Caramel Sauce</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>2 Tbsp Icing Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 350. Mix butter and brandy in a medium bowl. Add biscuits and 2 teaspoons cinnamon toss well. Spread into a 12 x 7 inch baking pan. Bake until lightly browned. Let cool. </li><li></li><li>Beat cream cheese and biscuit spread with an electric mixer on medium speed until well combined. Add cream cheese mixture and brandy beat on low speed until well blended. </li><li></li><li>Pour over biscuits, spreading evenly. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or until chilled. </li><li></li><li>Caramel sauce</li><li>Beat cream cheese in a medium bowl on medium speed until frothy. Gradually add icing sugar, beating until fluffy. Store in refrigerator. Serve with scone dip. </li></ul>
