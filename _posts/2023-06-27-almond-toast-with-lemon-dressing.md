<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Whole Blanched Almonds</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz White Bread, Crusts Removed</li><li>600Ml/20Fl Oz Iced Water</li><li>2 Fat Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li> 1/2 Cucumber, Peeled, Deseeded And Roughly Chopped</li><li>75Ml/2 1/2Fl Oz Olive Oil</li><li>1-2 Tbsp Sherry Vinegar Or White Wine Vinegar</li><li>Salt And White Pepper</li><li>1 Tbsp Flaked Almonds</li><li>24 Seedless Green Grapes, Peeled And Halved</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1 make almond toast</li><li>1 soak bread in water for 2hrs</li><li>2 in blender, grind drained almond and bread until combined</li><li>3 add water and blend another 2 min. </li><li>4 strain the water, add olive oil and sherry vinegar, blend again</li><li>5 mix remaining ingredients to almond toast, stir in lemon juice and serve, garnish with avocado and grapes</li></ul>
