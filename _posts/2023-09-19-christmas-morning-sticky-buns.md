<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Make the bread batter in a saucepan, heat the milk until bubbles form around the edges. Add the flour, salt, sugar and vanilla paste, and stir until combined. Add the melted butter and let it sit for 5 minutes to let it relax. Meanwhile, make the bread dough in a mixer fitted with the paddle attachment, whip the eggs, milk and vanilla paste together until light and fluffy. Add the flour mixture to the mixing bowl in 3 batches, scraping down the sides of the bowl when necessary. </li><li>Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured work surface and knead until smooth. Place the dough in a large lightly greased bowl and turn once to coat. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and set aside to rise in a warm place until doubled in volume, about 1 hour. </li><li>Roll the dough out to a 22cm round. Cut the round into four and line the bottom and sides of a 23cm x 30cm baking tin. Leave an overhang and wrap the dough in a sheet of baking paper. Place the dough over the baking paper and turn the dough over to completely cover the bottom. Leave to rise again for about 30 mins until doubled in volume again. </li><li>Make the caramel in a medium bowl, add the egg white, milk, sugar, cream and vanilla paste and whisk until pale. Add the cream tartar and whisk again until it forms soft peaks. Fold in the melted butter. </li><li>Make the cinnamon cinnamon roll in a mixing bowl, beat together the egg white and milk</li></ul>
