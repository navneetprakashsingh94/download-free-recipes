<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Undyed Smoked Haddock</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Hot Chicken Stock</li><li>1 Clove Garlic</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Peas</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>50G/2Oz Butter</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li> 1/2 Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>1 Tsp Dijon Mustard</li><li>1 Tbsp Chives, Chopped</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>Chervil, To Garnish</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For sauce</li><li>In a small pan over med. Heat, combine butter, cream cheese, lemon juice and mustard. </li><li>Heat, stirring constantly until cheese is melted and sauce is warm. </li><li>Season with salt and pepper. </li><li>Place haddock fillets in a hot, non stick pan, and fry until the fish has risen and is opaque in the center. </li><li>Remove fish from pan, cover with foil and set aside. </li><li>In the same pan, add the chicken stock and apricots with skins, garlic. </li><li>Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. </li><li>Lower heat, add peas and cook until tender, about 5 mins. </li><li>Add cream cheese and cook until sauce has thickened. </li><li>Stir in fish back in, then add chives and season. </li></ul>
