<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>225G/8Oz Plain Flour</li><li>50G/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Butter, Slightly Softened</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Plus 1 Egg Yolk</li><li>1 Vanilla Pod, Seeds Scraped Out</li><li>3-4 Bananas, Peeled, Thickly Sliced</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Plus 2 Egg Yolks</li><li>2 Tsp Dark Rum</li><li>50G/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>400Ml/14Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>75G/3Oz Butter</li><li>2 Bananas, Peeled, Sliced Diagonally</li><li>2 Tbsp Rum</li><li>50G/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Allspice</li><li>250G/9Oz Clotted Cream</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>To make the pastry mix the flour and sugar in a food processor and cut in the butter with a pastry blender until the mixture is crumbly. </li><li>Add the egg yolk and vanilla seeds and mix a bit more with a fork. </li><li>Wrap in plastic wrap and chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes. </li><li>Roll the pastry out onto a lightly floured surface, and line a 22cm flan tin. </li><li>Remove the baking paper, and roll out the pastry between two sheets of baking paper. </li><li>Place it on the pastry and trim the edges. </li><li>Prick the base of the pastry with a fork. </li><li>Bake in a preheated oven at 220c for 12 15 minutes or until golden. </li><li>Meanwhile peel the bananas and slice them thickly. </li><li>Beat the egg yolks with the dark rum and sugar until pale in colour and light. </li><li>Add the cream and continue beating until thickened. </li><li>Add the bananas, stir through, and spread the banana mixture over the pastry. </li><li>Bake in an oven for 15 20 minutes until just set. </li><li>To make the sauce combine the butter, bananas, rum and sugar in a saucepan and heat gently until the mixture just comes to the boil. </li><li>Remove from the heat and add the cinnamon and allspice. </li><li>Cool slightly and pour over the bananas. </li><li>To serve top the tart with a dollop of double cream and cut into wedges. </li><li>Serve warm or chilled. </li></ul>
