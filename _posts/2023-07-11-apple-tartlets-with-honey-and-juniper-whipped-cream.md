<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>450G/1Lb All-Butter Puff Pastry</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg Yolk, Beaten</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Marzipan</li><li>4 Dessert Apples, Peeled, Cored And Finely Sliced</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Butter</li><li>4 Tbsp Clear Honey</li><li>Vanilla Ice Cream, To Serve</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>2 Star Anise</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Cloves</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Freshly Grated Nutmeg</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Juniper Berries</li><li>175Ml/6Fl Oz Double Cream</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 425 f. On a lightly floured surface, roll out pastry and cut out 12 rounds. Lightly brush with egg yolk and sprinkle with marzipan. Place on a baking tray lined with parchment paper and refrigerate for 20 minutes. Bake for 20 minutes, remove from oven and sprinkle with remaining marzipan. </li><li>Meanwhile, melt butter in a skillet on medium low heat. Add apple slices and saute for 4 minutes, add honey and saute for 4 minutes longer. </li><li>Remove apples from pan and spread ice cream onto the tartlets, mounding it slightly on top of the apples. Bake for 4 minutes or until golden brown. Meanwhile, mix caster sugar, star anise, cloves, nutmeg and juniper berries together in a small saucepan. Add cream and stir until sugar has dissolved and mixture is hot. Serve tartlets warm with whipped cream. </li></ul>
