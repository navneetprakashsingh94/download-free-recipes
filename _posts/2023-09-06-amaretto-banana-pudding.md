<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#biscuits" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Biscuits</a> <a href="/tags#biscuits" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Biscuits</a> <a href="/tags#bananas" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bananas</a> <a href="/tags#almond liqueur amaretto" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Almond Liqueur Amaretto</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#light brown sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Light Brown Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#almonds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Almonds</a> <a href="/tags#creme fraiche" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Creme Fraiche</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>6 Amaretti Biscuits, Crumbled</li><li>6 Shortbread Biscuits, Crumbled</li><li>3-4 Bananas, Sliced</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Almond Liqueur, Such As Amaretto</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Salted Butter</li><li>2 Tbsp Soft Light Brown Sugar</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Whole Almonds, Toasted And Roughly Chopped</li><li>250G/9Oz Creme Fraiche</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 350f degrees. </li><li>Grease a 5 cup souffle dish. </li><li>Prepare biscuits and add them to dish, mixing them into the sauce. </li><li>In a bowl, mash the bananas with the almond liqueur. </li><li>Melt the butter in a saucepan, over low heat, stirring with a wooden spoon. </li><li>Add the brown sugar, stirring constantly, and simmer for 3 to 4 minutes, until the mixture comes to a boil. </li><li>Pour the sauce over the biscuits and bake for 40 to 50 minutes, until the top is golden and bubbly. </li><li>Remove from oven, let cool and serve with creme fraiche or vanilla ice cream. </li></ul>
