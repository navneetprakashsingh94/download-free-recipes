<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#water" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Water</a> <a href="/tags#ice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ice</a> <a href="/tags#vodka" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Vodka</a> <a href="/tags#apple liqueur" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Apple Liqueur</a> <a href="/tags#apple juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Apple Juice</a> <a href="/tags#lemon juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lemon Juice</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Water</li><li>2 Handfuls Ice</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Vodka</li><li>25Ml/1Fl Oz Green Apple Liqueur</li><li>25Ml/1Fl Oz Fresh Apple Juice</li><li>25Ml/1Fll Oz Freshly Squeezed Lemon Juice</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place sugar and water in a small saucepan and cook over a gentle heat, stirring, until sugar has dissolved. </li><li>Set aside to cool. </li><li>In blender or food processor, puree the cooled syrup, ice, vodka, champagne, apple juice, and lemon juice until well combined. </li><li>Pour into chilled glasses, sprinkle with sugar if desired and serve. </li></ul>
