<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Gluten Free Breakfast Tart using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 10 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#plain yoghurt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Yoghurt</a> <a href="/tags#honey" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Honey</a> <a href="/tags#porridge oats" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Porridge Oats</a> <a href="/tags#berries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Berries</a> <a href="/tags#tsp ground cinnamon" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Ground Cinnamon</a> <a href="/tags#tsp ground ginger" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Ground Ginger</a> <a href="/tags#apples" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Apples</a> <a href="/tags#pears" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pears</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>250G/9Oz Plain Yoghurt, Plus Extra To Decorate (Optional)</li><li>2 Tbsp Runny Honey (Optional)</li><li>200G/7Oz Porridge Oats</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Frozen Mixed Berries</li><li>1 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon, Plus Extra To Decorate (Optional)</li><li>2 Tsp Ground Ginger</li><li>2 Apples (About 300G/10 1/2Oz), Coarsely Grated</li><li>2 Pears (About 200G/7Oz), Coarsely Grated</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. Place the yoghurt, honey, porridge oats, wheat berries, cinnamon and ginger in a large bowl and mix well. </li><li></li><li>2. Place the apples and pears into a 2 litre ovenproof dish. Using a fork, mix well, but make sure everything is well mixed. </li><li></li><li>3. Pour the yoghurt mixture over the fruit and cover the dish with a lid. </li><li></li><li>4. Place the dish in the microwave oven, uncovered, for 35 40 minutes. Toss the tart mixture after 15 minutes if it seems too dry, add a little water to help the mix spread through the apples and pears. Check for doneness by piercing the apples, if they are not soft then keep heating for an additional 5 8 minutes. Serve warm or room temperature. </li></ul>
