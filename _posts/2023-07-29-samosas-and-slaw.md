<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>4 X 400G/14Oz Tins Baked Beans</li><li>1 Large Free-Range Egg</li><li>6 Garlic Cloves, Crushed</li><li>1 Large Onion, Chopped</li><li>1 Tsp Salt</li><li>1 Tsp Chilli Powder</li><li>1 Tbsp Ground Cumin</li><li>1 Tbsp Ground Coriander</li><li>120G/4 1/2Oz Chickpea Flour</li><li>A Large Handful Of Fresh Parsley, Finely Chopped</li><li>Cooking Oil Spray</li><li>Soft Bread Baps, To Serve</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Grated</li><li>1 Tsp Salt</li><li>4 Tbsp Mayonnaise</li><li>Squeeze Lemon Juice</li><li>1 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Parsley</li><li>2 Tbsp Sriracha Sauce</li><li>3 Carrots, Grated</li><li> 1/2 White Cabbage, Finely Shredded</li><li> 1/2 Red Cabbage, Finely Shredded</li><li>1 Red Onion, Thinly Sliced</li><li>Reserved Baked Bean Juice</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>First make the slaw by removing the core and chop all the vegetables and toss in a blender. </li><li>Pour the bean juice over all, add the rest of the ingredients and blend at high speed until smooth, seasoning and let stand in the fridge for 2 hours. </li><li>Heat some oil in a shallow baking dish, and cook the samosas on both sides until lightly golden. </li><li>Make the slaw by combining the mayonnaise, lemon juice, parsley and sriracha sauce in a jug. </li><li>Mix the salad ingredients together and season well. </li><li>Serve samosas with slaw</li></ul>
