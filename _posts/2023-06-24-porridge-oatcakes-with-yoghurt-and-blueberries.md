<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Porridge Oatcakes With Yoghurt And Blueberries using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#free-range eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Free-Range Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Milk</a> <a href="/tags#honey maple syrup" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Honey Maple Syrup</a> <a href="/tags#porridge oats" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Porridge Oats</a> <a href="/tags#tsp baking powder" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Baking Powder</a> <a href="/tags#pinch salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pinch Salt</a> <a href="/tags#blueberries berries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Blueberries Berries</a> <a href="/tags#plain yoghurt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Yoghurt</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>400Ml/14Fl Oz Milk</li><li>4 Tbsp Runny Honey Or Maple Syrup</li><li>200G/7Oz Porridge Oats</li><li>2 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>Large Pinch Salt</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Fresh Blueberries Or Any Frozen Berries</li><li>Plain Yoghurt, To Serve (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 170 c. Lightly grease a large shallow, ovenproof dish. </li><li>Whisk together eggs, milk and honey. </li><li>Whisk together oats, baking powder and salt. </li><li>Add the egg mixture, then fold in the blueberries. </li><li>Spoon the batter into the prepared dish. </li><li>Bake for 20 25 minutes, until golden and a skewer inserted comes out clean. </li><li>Serve with yoghurt and blueberries if desired. </li></ul>
