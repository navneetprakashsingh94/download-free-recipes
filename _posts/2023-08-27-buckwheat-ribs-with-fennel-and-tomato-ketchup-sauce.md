<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>To make the ribs, place the ribs in a large pot, add water to cover by 2 inches, and bring to the boil. Skim off any foam with a spoon, reduce the heat, and simmer, skimming occasionally, for 1 hour, or until tender. Let cool slightly, then drain, reserving the cooking liquid. </li><li>In a spice grinder or in a mortar and pestle, grind the fennel seeds and star anise. Preheat the oven to 400f 200c. </li><li>In a large roasting pan, toss the potatoes with oil, season and bake for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until golden. Remove and let cool. </li><li>Meanwhile, to make the fennel ketchup, melt the butter in a large saucepan. Add the onion, clove, dried chillies, fennel seeds, star anise and muscovado sugar. Cook, stirring, for 2 minutes, or until the onion is softened. Add the tomato ketchup, chilli ketchup and dark soy sauce. Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes, or until the mixture thickens. Season. </li><li>Using a sharp knife, cut a small slice off the top of each rib</li></ul>
