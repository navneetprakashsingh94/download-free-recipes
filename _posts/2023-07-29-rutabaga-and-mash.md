<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. Cut the rutabagas into very fine slices and fry in a little oil until golden brown. </li><li>2. Add the thyme and turn the heat down until the liquid has simmered away. </li><li>3. Once the potato slices are soft, put them in a bowl and mash with the cream, butter, pancetta and thyme. </li><li>4. Cut the cabbage into strands and then boil them in salted water for 5 minutes. Add the parsley, oregano and mustard and mix well. </li><li>5. In a separate pan, add a little oil and put the rutabaga and mash together well. </li><li>6. Fry the cabbage until just soft and then add the rutabaga and cook again until the rutabaga is all fried. </li><li>7. Season with pepper and serve with the potato wedges and mushroom gravy. </li></ul>
