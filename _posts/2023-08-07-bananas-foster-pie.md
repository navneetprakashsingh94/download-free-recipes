<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>6 Bananas</li><li>250G/9Oz Unsalted Butter, Softened</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Dark Brown Sugar</li><li>100G/4Oz Golden Syrup</li><li>4 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Milk</li><li>2 Tsp Bicarbonate Of Soda</li><li>550G/1Lb 4Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>4 Overripe Bananas, Mashed</li><li>100G/4Oz Muscovado Sugar</li><li>80G/3Oz Rum</li><li>25G/1Oz Miso Paste</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Milk</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz G Double Cream</li><li>100G/4Oz Unsalted Butter, Cut Into Cubes</li><li>200G/8Oz Sunflower Seeds, Heavily Toasted</li><li>100G/4Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>25G/1Oz Miso Paste</li><li>50G/2Oz Muscovado Sugar</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Milk</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>100G/4Oz Creme Fraiche</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 180 c and grease a 20cm pie tin. </li><li></li><li>Cream butter and sugar until pale. Add eggs, bananas, syrup, milk and bicarb. Fold in the flour, fold in 2tbsp of flour and pour into the pie tin. </li><li></li><li>Bake for 30 35 minutes. </li><li></li><li>Melt butter and sugar together and spread over base, sprinkle with sunflower seeds and bake in oven for another 10 minutes, remove and cool. </li><li></li><li>Melt butter and milk together, mix in miso and muscovado, then stir in milk, cream and creme fraiche, return to oven and cook for 20 minutes. </li><li></li><li>Cut bananas into small pieces, whack with a rolling pin and pour over the base of the pie, then sprinkle with sunflower seeds. </li></ul>
