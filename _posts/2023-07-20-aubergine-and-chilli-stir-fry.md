<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>3 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Brown Onion, Chopped</li><li>250G/9Oz Lamb Mince</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cumin</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Coriander</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Long Red Chilli, Seeds Removed And Finely Chopped</li><li>2.5Cm/1In Piece Fresh Root Ginger, Finely Chopped</li><li>55G/2Oz Baby Spinach, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 X 400G Tin Chopped Tomatoes</li><li>2 Large Aubergines</li><li>2 Fresh, Ripe Tomatoes, Roughly Chopped</li><li>2 Tbsp Freshly Chopped Basil</li><li>25G/1Oz Butter</li><li>25G/1Oz Plain Flour</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Milk</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Parmesan, Grated</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat the oil in a large frying pan over a medium heat. Cook the onion for 3 mins, until softened. Add the ground beef and cook, stirring, for 5 mins. Add the spices and ginger and cook, stirring, for 1 min. Add the spinach and stir well. </li><li>Add the tin tomatoes and stir well. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Remove from the heat and let cool. </li><li>Heat the oil in a frying pan and add the aubergine slices. Stir fry for 3 4 mins, until golden brown. Transfer to a heat proof plate and top each with 2/3 of the tomato mixture. Sprinkle with basil. </li><li>Meanwhile, make the bechamel melt the butter in a saucepan. Add the flour and stir until smooth. Slowly add the milk, whisking constantly. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly, and simmer for 2 3 mins until thickened. </li><li>Pour the bechamel on top of the aubergines and sprinkle with parmesan and some pepper. Serve immediately. </li></ul>
