<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Large Aubergine, Cut Lengthways Into 6 Slices About 5Mm/ 1/4 Inch Thick</li><li>2 Tbsp Plain Flour</li><li>2 Eggs, Lightly Beaten With Some Salt And Pepper</li><li>Olive Oil For Frying</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Parmesan Cheese (Or A Similar Vegetarian Hard Cheese), Freshly Grated</li><li>18 Large Fresh Basil Leaves</li><li>300G/11Oz Mozzarella Cheese, Roughly Sliced</li><li>Salt</li><li>Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>4 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Small Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li> 1/2 Celery Stalk, Finely Chopped</li><li>400G/14Oz Tin Of Plum Tomatoes</li><li>Leafy Green Salad</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 200 degrees c</li><li>Cut aubergine slices into 4cm squares, season with salt pepper. Lightly dredge in flour, shaking off excess, dip in egg, and press into a frying pan. Lightly fry in hot oil until golden brown. </li><li>In a large mixing bowl mix together, basil, mozzarella, salt, pepper and oil, season to taste. </li><li>Place aubergine squares in greased x 11cm round tin. Pour tomato mix over aubergine, then cover with cheese mixture. </li><li>Bake in oven for 25 min until cheese is melted. </li><li>Serve with hot green salad. </li></ul>
