<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>30G/1Oz Butter</li><li>2 Bay Leaves</li><li>Small Bunch Thyme, Leaves Picked</li><li>2 Onions, Diced</li><li>1 Leek, Sliced</li><li>2 Potatoes, Peeled And Diced</li><li>1 Litre/1 3/4 Pint Vegetable Stock</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Pearl Barley, Soaked</li><li>2 Tbsp Nutritional Yeast Flakes</li><li>2 Carrots, Peeled And Diced</li><li>1 Small Swede, Peeled And Diced</li><li>Splash Worcestershire Sauce</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Salted Butter</li><li>1-2 Tbsp Chopped Horseradish, From A Jar</li><li>1 Tsp Chopped Fresh Thyme</li><li>2 Tbsp Snipped Fresh Chives</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>2 Onions, Sliced</li><li>1 Small Red Cabbage, Sliced</li><li>2 Bramley Apples, Peeled And Sliced</li><li>1 Bay Leaf</li><li>75G/2 2/3 Oz Light Brown Sugar</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Red Wine Vinegar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Melt the butter over medium heat in a large, heavy based pan. Add the bay leaves, thyme, onion, leek, potatoes, stock, barley, yeast flakes and seasoning. Add the carrots and swede. Bring to the boil then simmer gently for 1 hour. </li><li></li><li>Preheat the oven to 180c. </li><li></li><li>Mix the flour with the butter, seasonings, and 1 tbsp flor, and stir into the soup to form a smooth dough. Transfer to a flat baking tray and cover with a piece of grease proof paper and some flour. Bake for 20 minutes. </li><li></li><li>In the meantime, saute the onions in some oil in a skillet, for 5 minutes, then add the cabbage, apples, bay leaf, sugar, and vinegar. Cook for a further 5 minutes, and then season with salt, pepper, and thyme. </li><li></li><li>Pour the pie filling into the center of the roasting tin, then crumble the topping over the pie. Bake for another 25 minutes. The pie is done when the topping is crisp and bubbling. Serve with flor de laurentiis</li></ul>
