<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>225G/8Oz Unsalted Butter, Softened, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>225G/8Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>4 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>1 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>225G/8Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>225G/8Oz Icing Sugar</li><li>110G/4Oz Unsalted Butter, Softened</li><li>1 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>1 Tbsp Milk</li><li>Assorted Biscuits, Meringues, Sweets, Sprinkles And Chocolates</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. </li><li>Add eggs and vanilla extract and beat well. </li><li>Sift in flour and beat until incorporated. </li><li>Spread the mixture into a greased 23cm x 27cm ovenproof dish and bake in a moderate oven 190 degrees c for 15 minutes or until risen and golden. </li><li>Leave to cool slightly. </li><li>Blend the icing sugar, butter, vanilla, and milk until smooth. </li><li>Spread over the top and sides of the cooled cake and decorate with meringues sweets. </li><li>Serve. </li></ul>
