<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Kg/2Lb 2Oz Potatoes, Such As King Edward Or Maris Piper, Peeled, Cut Into Equal-Sized Pieces</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Peeled</li><li>2 Bay Leaves</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>75-110Ml/3-4Fl Oz Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>4 X 200G/7Oz Slices Fillet Steak, Sliced Into 1Cm/ 1/2In Wide Strips</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>2 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Plus Extra For Drizzling</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Peeled, Finely Diced</li><li>1 Fresh Rosemary Sprig</li><li>1 X 225G/8Oz Jar Piquillo Peppers, Drained, Sliced</li><li>6 Tbsp Dry Oloroso Sherry</li><li>4 Fresh Flatleaf Parsley Sprigs, Leaves Only, Chopped</li><li>4 Fresh Mint Sprigs, Chopped</li><li>2 Heads Crisp Lettuce, Such As Romaine Lettuce, Leaves Separated, Sliced</li><li>25G/1Oz Flaked Toasted Almonds</li><li>1 Pinch Hot Smoked Paprika</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Boil potato, cloves bay leaves in salted water with some pepper. </li><li>Drain potatoes, remove peel and slice into 1/2 inch pieces. </li><li>In a bowl mix the olive oil,add potatoes and toss well. </li><li>Spread potatoes on baking sheet cover with plastic wrap,seal, and bake in oven at 180 degrees c for 1 hour. </li><li>Cut into squares,spread oil on pan,cook steak, sprinkle with salt pepper, let rest for 10 minutes. </li><li>Heat oil in small pan. </li><li>Add cloves, dill and chilies, sprig of parsley, mint. </li><li>When peppers are softened add sherry, and cook on low heat until melted. </li><li>Meanwhile, arrange lettuce lettuce leaves, tomato and steak on serving plates. </li><li>Arrange piquillo peppers on lettuce. </li><li>Serve immediately. </li></ul>
