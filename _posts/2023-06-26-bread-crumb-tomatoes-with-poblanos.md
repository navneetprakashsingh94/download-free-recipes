<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Slices White Bread (Approx. 80G/2 3/4Oz), Cut Into 1Cm/ 1/2In Cubes</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Tsp Finely Chopped Fresh Thyme</li><li>1 Tbsp Finely Grated Parmesan</li><li>4 Red Peppers, Seeds Removed And Chopped Into 2Cm/ 3/4In Chunks</li><li>2 Medium Red Onions, Finely Chopped</li><li>4 Tsp Finely Chopped Fresh Thyme</li><li>2 Medium Tomatoes (Approx. 200G/7Oz), Sliced In Half</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>600Ml/20Fl Oz Chicken Or Vegetable Stock</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>4 Tbsp Creme Fraiche</li><li>4 Tsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Tsp Finely Chopped Fresh Thyme</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>To make bread crumb</li><li></li><li>To make the bread crumbs</li><li></li><li>In a medium bowl, place bread cubes. Drizzle with oil, toss until evenly coated, add thyme leaves and parmesan and mix well until evenly distributed throughout the crumbs. </li><li></li><li>Heat a small non stick pan, add poblanos, onions and thyme leaves. Cook for about five minutes until peppers begin to soften and onions begin to soften. </li><li></li><li>Add tomatoes to the pan and fry for another 5 minutes. Add oil to pan, stir well and add chicken stock. Bring to a boil, add salt pepper and cover. </li><li></li><li>Continue to simmer uncovered for about 10 minutes until tomato are tender. </li><li></li><li>To make thyme creme fraiche</li><li></li><li>Heat oil in a small pan, add mushrooms and saute for about 10 minutes. Stir occasionally and season with salt pepper. </li><li></li><li>To serve</li><li></li><li>Lay one slice of bread in a small oval dish, spread with a generous layer of tomato sauce, place mushroom and onion mixture on top. Garnish with a sprig of thyme. </li><li></li><li>Ect. </li><li></li><li>Per serving 00</li><li>Calories kcal 260</li><li>Fat kcal 210</li><li>Protein g 4g</li><li>Carbohydrates g 6</li><li>Sodium mg 340</li><li>Fiber g 2</li></ul>
