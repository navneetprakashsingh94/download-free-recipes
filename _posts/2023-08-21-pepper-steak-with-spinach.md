<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>3 Tbsp Black Peppercorns, Coarsely Ground, Sieved, Dust Discarded</li><li>4 X 175G/6Oz Fillet Steaks, Cut In Half</li><li>4 Tsp Dijon Mustard</li><li>Freshly Ground Sea Salt And Freshly Ground Pepper</li><li>2 Tbsp Sunflower Oil</li><li>50G/2Oz Butter</li><li>200G/7Oz Fresh Cep (Porcini) Mushrooms, Thickly Sliced</li><li>450G/1Lb New Potatoes, Scrubbed, Boiled Until Tender</li><li>50G/2Oz Butter</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground White Pepper</li><li>3 Tbsp Chopped Flatleaf Parsley</li><li>25G/1Oz Butter</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Baby Leaf Spinach</li><li>3 Tbsp Water</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground White Pepper</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Whisky</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Beef Stock</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Double Cream</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Remove mushroom stems and slice mushrooms and soak in warm water about 2 hours. Drain and chop finely. </li><li>In a roasting pan, place mushroom stems and roast at 450f for about 20 minutes, or until lightly browned. </li><li>Meanwhile, chop dry mushroom stems and sprinkle over potatoes. </li><li>Add 1/4 lb of butter and mash with salt, pepper, and chopped parsley. </li><li>Melt 1/4 lb of butter in a pan and saute mushrooms. </li><li>Add spinach and cook 3 minutes. </li><li>Add water, pepper, whisky and beef stock, stirring, until liquid is reduced by half. </li><li>Remove from heat and add cream. </li><li>Add salt and pepper. </li><li>To serve, slice steak, put potato mixture on plate and arrange steak on top. </li><li>Pour sauce over, sprinkling with cracked pepper. </li></ul>
