<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Plain Flour</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>1 Kg/2Lb 4Oz Blade Of Beef, Cut Into Large Cubes</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Pancetta, Cut Into Small Chunks</li><li>2 Shallots, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Crushed</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Brandy</li><li>500Ml/18Fl Oz Burgundy Red Wine, Or Other Red Wine</li><li>250Ml/9Fl Oz Beef Stock</li><li>1 Bouquet Garni Made Up Of 2 Bay Leaves, 2 Sprigs Thyme, 2 Sprigs Flatleaf Parsley</li><li>25G/1Oz Butter</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Peeled Baby Shallots (Or Frozen Button Onions)</li><li>200G/7Oz Chestnut Mushrooms</li><li>1Kg/2Lb 4Oz Floury Potatoes, Peeled And Cut Into Chunks</li><li>110G/4Oz Butter</li><li>110Ml/4Fl Oz Full-Fat Milk</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>25G/1Oz Butter</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Small Pearl Onions, Peeled (Or Frozen Button Onions)</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Pancetta, Cut Into Lardons</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Button Mushrooms, Stalks Removed Left Whole</li><li> 1/2 Bunch Flatleaf Parsley, Finely Chopped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>To prepare beef, mix flour, salt and pepper. Dredge beef cubes in the mixture and set aside. </li><li>Heat oil in a large dutch oven over medium heat, add pancetta and cook until crisp. Remove and drain on paper towels. </li><li>Add shallots and clove and saute until soft. Add brandy, reduce by half and add wine. </li><li>Bring to the boil and stir to deglaze the pan. Add stock and bring to the boil. Add beef cubes, bouquet garni and reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer 2 hours, or until beef is tender. Remove beef cubes and rest for 5 minutes. </li><li>For the mushrooms, melt butter in a large saucepan, add baby shallots and saute until golden brown. </li><li>Meanwhile, boil or steam the potatoes until tender. Drain and return to the pot. Add butter and milk and mix until smooth. Season. </li><li>Meanwhile, heat oil in a large frying pan, add butter and pearl onions and fry over low heat until golden brown. Stir in pancetta and saute for 5 minutes. </li><li>Add button mushrooms and saute until cooked to your liking. Season. </li><li>Reheat the sauce, season to taste and serve with meat and mushrooms. Garnish with parsley. </li></ul>
