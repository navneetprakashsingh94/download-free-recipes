<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>125G/4Oz Unsalted Butter, Softened, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>200G/7Oz Golden Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Lightly Beaten</li><li>150G/5Oz Plain Flour</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Salt</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Grated Nutmeg</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Pecans, Roughly Chopped</li><li>3 Medium Apples (Mix Of Granny Smith And Golden Delicious), Peeled, Cored, Chopped Into Small Pieces</li><li>5 Plums, Stone Removed, Chopped Into Small Pieces</li><li>2 Tbsp Finely Chopped Stem Ginger</li><li>200G/7Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>250Ml/8Floz Whipping Cream</li><li>1-2 Tbsp Dark Rum</li><li>1 Tsp Vanilla Essence</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. Beat in the egg and cook over medium heat until the mixture thickens. Set aside and leave to cool. </li><li>Sift the flour and spices together into a mixing bowl. Stir in the pecans. Stir in the baking powder mixture and combine well. </li><li>On a greased oven tray, pour the batter and smooth out the top. Bake in preheated oven at 180c for approximately 1 hour or until a toothpick comes out clean. Remove from oven and allow to cool completely before removing from the pan. </li><li>To prepare the glaze, place the sugar and cream in a saucepan and bring to the boil. Stir in the rum, vanilla essence, and ginger. Reduce heat and simmer for about 5 minutes until the mixture has thickened slightly. </li><li>When the cake is completely cool, cut it in half and place the bottom half on a serving plate. Spoon the apples and plums on the bottom half and pour the glaze over the top. </li><li>To serve, turn over the cake carefully, letting some of the glaze and fruit drip out of the bottom. Slice and eat. </li></ul>
