<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Thyme</li><li>1 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Rosemary</li><li>4 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Mint</li><li>1 Tsp Sea Salt</li><li>Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>4 X 375G/13Oz Barnsley Chops</li><li>2 Tbsp Sunflower Oil</li><li>1 Orange, Zest Cut Into Matchsticks, Juiced</li><li>1 Piece Stem Ginger, Drained And Cut Into Very Fine Strips</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Ruby Port</li><li>150G/5Fl Oz Redcurrant Jelly</li><li> 1/2 Lemon, Juice Only</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cut rib bones in half crosswise, and remove excess fat. </li><li>Mix herbs and salt in small bowl. Rub herb mixture onto chops, and let stand at room temperature for about 30 minutes. </li><li>Heat 1 tbsp oil in 12 inch heavy skillet over medium high heat until just smoking. </li><li>Add half the ribs, and cook until well browned, 5 to 7 minutes. Transfer to plate. Repeat with remaining 1 tbsp oil and rib bones, adding more oil as necessary. Transfer ribs to plate. </li><li>Add orange slices and ginger to skillet, and saute over medium heat until just softened, about 2 minutes. </li><li>Transfer to plate. Add port, jelly, and lemon juice to skillet, and boil over medium high heat, stirring, until glaze thickens slightly, about 2 minutes. </li><li>Return ribs to skillet, turning to coat with glaze. Transfer to serving platter. </li><li>Sprinkle ribs with salt and pepper, and serve with warm redcurrant glaze. </li></ul>
