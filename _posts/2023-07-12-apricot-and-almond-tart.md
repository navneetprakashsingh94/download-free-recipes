<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>225G/8Oz Plain Flour</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Butter, Cubed</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg</li><li>175G/6Oz Butter</li><li>175G/6Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>4 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>175G/6Oz Ground Almonds</li><li>1 Tsp Almond Extract</li><li>200G/7Oz Apricot Jam</li><li>8 Ripe Apricots, Halved And Stoned</li><li>4 Tbsp Apricot Jam</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Flaked Almonds, Toasted</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 180c/gas 4. </li><li>Line a 25cm springform tin with foil and grease with cooking spray. </li><li>To make the pastry, rub the butter into the flour until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs. </li><li>Stir in the sugar, then the egg. </li><li>Roll out to a rough 12cm circle and place in the tin, pressing down with your fingers. </li><li>Chill in the freezer for 30 minutes. </li><li>To make the filling, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. </li><li>Beat in the eggs one by one then stir in the almonds and almond extract, beating for another 30 seconds between each addition. </li><li>Spoon the filling into the pastry case and bake in the oven for 30 minutes. </li><li>Remove from the oven and cover loosely with a sheet of foil. </li><li>Put back in the oven for a further 30 minutes or until firm. </li><li>To make the topping, cut the apricots in half and place them in a medium bowl and cover with cold water. </li><li>Soak for 15 minutes, drain and squeeze excess water out. </li><li>Add the jam and stir until blended. </li><li>Cover with cling film and leave to cool for 30 minutes. </li><li>Remove the tart shell from the tin and spoon the topping over the top. </li><li>Sprinkle with the almonds and place in the freezer for 30 minutes. </li><li>To serve, remove the foil and slice into wedges. </li></ul>
