<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Asparagus Endive Salad With Blue Cheese Dressing using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 10 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#cheese aside leaves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cheese Aside Leaves</a> <a href="/tags#wine vinegar cider vinegar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Wine Vinegar Cider Vinegar</a> <a href="/tags#walnut hazelnut oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Walnut Hazelnut Oil</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Oil</a> <a href="/tags#dash juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dash Juice</a> <a href="/tags#salt ground pepper season" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Ground Pepper Season</a> <a href="/tags#endives" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Endives</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Blue Cheese, Crumbled (Keep Some Aside To Scatter Over The Endive Leaves)</li><li>2 Tbsp White Wine Vinegar Or Cider Vinegar</li><li>3 Tbsp Walnut Or Hazelnut Oil</li><li>3 Tbsp Sunflower Oil</li><li>Dash Of Lemon Juice</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper, To Season</li><li>2 Endives, Leaves Only</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Drain the asparagus and place on a serving dish. </li><li>Put the blue cheese, vinegars, nut oil and olive oil and lemon juice on a blender and mix until well blended. </li><li>Add salt, pepper and a dash of olive oil. </li><li>Pour the dressing over the asparagus and sprinkle the blue cheese on top. </li><li>Mix gently. </li><li>Toss and serve. </li></ul>
