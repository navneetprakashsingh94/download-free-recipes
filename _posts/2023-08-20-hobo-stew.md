<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>25G/1Oz Butter</li><li>750G/1Lb 10Oz Beef Braising Steak, Chopped Into Bite-Sized Pieces</li><li>2 Tbsp Plain Flour</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Crushed</li><li>175G/6Oz Baby Onions, Peeled</li><li>150G/5Oz Celery, Cut Into Large Chunks</li><li>150G/5Oz Carrots, Cut Into Large Chunks</li><li>2 Leeks, Roughly Chopped</li><li>200G/7Oz Swede, Cut Into Large Chunks</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Red Wine</li><li>500Ml/18Fl Oz Beef Stock</li><li>2 Fresh Bay Leaves</li><li>3 Tbsp Fresh Thyme Leaves</li><li>3 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Flatleaf Parsley</li><li>Worcestershire Sauce, To Taste</li><li>1 Tbsp Balsamic Vinegar, Or To Taste</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Plain Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>60G/2 1/2Oz Suet</li><li>Water, To Make A Dough</li><li>Mashed Potato</li><li>1 Tbsp Chopped Flatleaf Parsley</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the stew, heat the oil and butter in a pan and brown the meat on all sides. </li><li>Add the flour and stir over heat until all the flour has dissolved. </li><li>Add all the vegetables and the wine, scraping any browned bits off the bottom of the pan. </li><li>Add the stock, herbs, spices, sauce and vinegar. </li><li>Cover and simmer for 1 hour until meat is tender. </li><li>For the biscuits, sift flour, baking powder and salt into a bowl. </li><li>Add suet, rub together to form a crumbly dough. </li><li>Add just enough water to form a soft dough. </li><li>Roll out on a lightly floured surface and cut into wedges. </li><li>Divide the beef mixture among 6 shallow bowls. </li><li>Spoon the biscuits over the beef mixture. </li><li>Sprinkle with the parsley. </li><li>Serve with a bowl of cornichons. </li></ul>
