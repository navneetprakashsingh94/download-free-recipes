<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Large Potato (About 285G/10Oz), Peeled And Cut Into Large Chunks</li><li>400G Tin Red Kidney Beans, Drained And Rinsed</li><li>1 Small Red Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Tsp Salt</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Dried Chilli Flakes</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cumin</li><li>1 Slice Bread (About 65G/2 1/4Oz), Blitzed To Crumbs (See Recipe Tip)</li><li>3-4 Tbsp Vegetable Oil</li><li>4 Burger Buns, Cut In Half</li><li>3-4 Ripe Tomatoes, Sliced</li><li>Shredded Iceberg Lettuce</li><li>Sauces Of Your Choice, Such As Mayonnaise, Mustard Or Ketchup (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place potato in saucepan and cover with boiling water bring to boil, then cook for about 5 mins. </li><li>Drain and leave to cool. </li><li>Drain kidney beans. </li><li>Toss potatoes in the kidney beans, add onion, salt, chilli and cumin. </li><li>Mix well and form into 4 burgers. </li><li>Place a slice bread tip on top of each burger patty and press down gently to seal completely. </li><li>Heat oil in a non stick frying pan and saute burgers over a medium heat for 3 4 mins, turning occasionally. </li><li>Arrange on buns with lettuce, tomato slices and sauces. </li></ul>
