<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7 1/4Oz Butter</li><li>1Kg/2Lb 4Oz Piece Rump Steak, Cut Into Steaks</li><li>6 Onions, Thinly Sliced</li><li>2 Bay Leaves</li><li>1 Tbsp Thyme Leaves, Finely Chopped</li><li>6 Garlic Cloves, Peeled</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>250G/9Oz Plain Flour</li><li>125G/4Oz Butter</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>3 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Tbsp Red Wine Vinegar</li><li>6 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>6 Anchovy Fillets</li><li>2 Dried Chillies</li><li>1 Large Bunch Flatleaf Parsley, Finely Chopped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. </li><li>Trim off any silverskin and fatty cartilage from the steak. </li><li>Melt the butter in a large flameproof casserole or possibly shallow ovenproof dish and brown the meat, add in the onions, bay leaves, thyme, cloves and salt and pepper to taste. </li><li>Cook gently till the onions are translucent/soft. </li><li>2. </li><li>Remove the meat to a large cutting board and leave to cold, discarding the bay leaves and spices. </li><li>3. </li><li>Make a smooth paste with the flour, butter and egg, season with salt and pepper and stir in the vinegar. </li><li>Roll into about twelve 1 inch balls, cover with cling film and chill in the fridge for at least 1 hour. </li><li>4. </li><li>Heat the oil in a frying pan. </li><li>In batches, cook the meat, a few at a time, till browned and cooked through, turning. </li><li>Drain on paper towels and season with salt. </li><li>5. </li><li>Serve with prawn fillets, sprinkled with minced chillies and parsley. </li></ul>
