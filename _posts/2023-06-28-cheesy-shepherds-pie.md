<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1Kg/2Lb 4Oz Floury Potatoes, Such As Maris Piper Or Desiree, Peeled And Roughly Chopped Into Equal-Sized Pieces</li><li>3 Garlic Cloves, Crushed</li><li>250Ml/9Fl Oz Full-Fat Milk</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>250G/9Oz Lancashire Cheese, Grated</li><li>250G/9Oz Gruyere, Gated</li><li>Salt, To Taste</li><li>Dash Oil</li><li>6 Beef Sausages</li><li>6 Round Shallots, Quartered</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Red Wine</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Beef Stock</li><li>1 Bay Leaf</li><li>6 Garlic Cloves, Peeled And Left Whole</li><li>Small Bunch Thyme</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 180 c. Wash and pricke potatoes with a clove and bake in the oven for 45 minutes. Stir once half way through. Transfer to a mixing bowl and mash with milk and butter. </li><li>For the filling, boil water in a saucepan and cook beans until they are soft. Drain, add potatoes, cream, cheese and half the cheese, cook until thickened. Season with salt and pepper. </li><li>Heat oil in a saucepan and saute sausages until browned. Add shallots and cook for 2 minutes. Add wine, beef stock, bay leaf, cloves and thyme. Add water if it seems too dry. Season. </li><li>Pour the filling into a baking dish and top with the potatoes. Sprinkle the remaining cheese and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Bake for 30 minutes until bubbly. </li></ul>
