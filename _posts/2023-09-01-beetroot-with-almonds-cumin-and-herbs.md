<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Medium Beetroot</li><li>Dash Olive Oil, For Drizzling</li><li>20G/1Oz Blanched Almonds</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Cumin Seeds</li><li>1 Red Eating Apple, Cut Into 2Cm/1In Cubes</li><li>1 Green Eating Apple, Cut Into 2Cm/1In Cubes</li><li> 1/2 Small Red Onion, Sliced</li><li>Handful Fresh Herbs</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Plain Yoghurt</li><li>1 Lime, Zest And Juice</li><li>2 Tsp Honey</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat the oven to 400 degrees. </li><li>Heat the oil in a saucepan. Add the cumin and almonds, and fry for a minute. </li><li>Add the beetroot, apple, onion and herbs and saute for 2 3 minutes. </li><li>Season to taste with salt and pepper and spoon into a ovenproof dish or individual baking dishes. </li><li>Place in the oven for 20 minutes. </li><li>To make the lime yoghurt, mash the yoghurt with the honey and lime zest. Mix well and taste. You may want to add extra honey to make it a bit sweeter. </li><li>Serve with grilled fennel and sliced mushrooms. </li></ul>
