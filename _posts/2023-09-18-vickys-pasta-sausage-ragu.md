<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>400G/14Oz 00 Pasta Flour</li><li>4 Free-Range Eggs, Lightly Beaten</li><li>2 Tsp Salt</li><li>400G/14Oz Good Quality Coarse Pork Sausage Meat</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Small Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Sticks Celery, Chopped</li><li> 3/4 Tsp Fennel Seeds, Roughly Ground In A Pestle And Mortar</li><li>1 Sprig Of Fresh Rosemary, Leaves Finely Chopped</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Chilli Flakes</li><li>1 Large Garlic Clove, Grated</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Dry White Wine</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Chicken Stock</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Parmesan Cheese, Freshly Grated</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Sift the flour into a large bowl and make a well in the centre</li><li>Put in the eggs and salt and knead the dough gently and gently until smooth</li><li>The dough should just come together into a ball and hold together. </li><li>It can be difficult to work with, but do not overwork it</li><li>Take a handful of the dough and roll it in your hands to form a round disk it should be just about a centimeter thick</li><li>Repeat for all of the dough</li><li>Remove the dough from the bowl and roll it out as thin as possible it shouldn t be too thick it should be about 7mm</li><li>Cut the dough into 5mm strips</li><li>Sprinkle with a little salt</li><li>If you don t want to freeze the strips, you can wrap them individually in clingfilm, then toss them in a bowl and leave for a couple of hours then cook according to directions on the box. </li><li>Preheat oven to gas mark 4, 200 c</li><li>Prepare the sausage sausage meat in a saute pan with 2 tbsp oil and 1 tbsp of butter</li><li>Then fry the meats over a medium heat until cooked through</li><li>Add the onion and celery and fry for 3 minutes until soft and golden brown</li><li>Put the meats and all the remaining meats and any other vegetables in a pan and fry for 5 minutes over a medium heat</li><li>Then add the pestle, herbs, chilli and cloves</li></ul>
