<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Dark Soy Sauce Or Tamari</li><li>2 Tsp Runny Honey</li><li>2 Tsp Cornflour</li><li>2 Tbsp Tomato Ketchup</li><li>2 Tsp Runny Honey</li><li>1 Tsp Vinegar</li><li>3 Tbsp Peanut Butter</li><li>2 Tbsp Sweet Chilli Dipping Sauce</li><li>1 Tbsp Dark Soy Sauce Or Tamari</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cut beef into large pieces and marinade for 1 hour</li><li>Meanwhile, mix the ingredients for the sauce in a bowl, then mix to a smooth consistency</li><li>Heat a griddle pan or frying pan until hot</li><li>Add the beef pieces and cook until they brown on all sides and are cooked through</li><li>Serve in warmed bowls topped with the peanut sauce</li></ul>
