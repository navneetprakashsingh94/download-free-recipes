<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Smooth Speculoos Spread, Such As Biscoff</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Speculoos Biscuits</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>4 Overripe Bananas, Peeled And Sliced</li><li>1 Vanilla Pod, Seeds Removed</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Dark Rum</li><li>2 Bananas, Peeled And Sliced</li><li>20G/1Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Double Cream</li><li>20G/1Oz Icing Sugar</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Creme Fraiche</li><li>Chocolate Curls Or Shavings</li><li>Cocoa Powder</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the biscoff spread, finely crush the speculoos biscuits in a food processor or blender. Melt the butter in a frying pan and add the crushed speculoos biscuits. Stir and cook on a low heat for about 2 minutes until the speculoos are softened. </li><li>For the bananas, put the vanilla seeds, sugar, rum and a few tablespoons of water in a pan and stir until the sugar dissolves. </li><li>Slice the bananas and add to the pan, stirring until they are coated in the caramel. </li><li>Spoon into a shallow tin and refrigerate. </li><li>For the cream, beat the cream with the sugar until it starts to hold its shape. Whisk in the creme fraiche until smooth. </li><li>Remove the banana mixture from the fridge, spoon it on top of the biscuits and decorate with chocolate curls. </li><li>Dust the top with the cocoa powder and serve</li></ul>
