<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Butter, Softened</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Tbsp Plain Flour</li><li>70G/2 1/2Oz Flaked Almonds</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Panko Breadcrumbs</li><li>5 Large Royal Gala Apples, Coarsely Grated</li><li>5 Cardamom Pods, Seeds Only</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Whipping Cream</li><li>1 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>2 Free-Range Egg Yolks</li><li>1 1/2 Tbsp Caster Sugar</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Cornflour</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 180c/350f/gas mark 6. Grease a 23cm/9 inch fluted tart tin with removable base. </li><li></li><li>Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Stir in flour, almonds, cinnamon and panko. Pack the mixture into the pan and bake for 10 minutes, until golden brown. Cool on a wire rack. </li><li></li><li>Slice the apples into rounds, peel and core them, then place them in a saucepan along with the cardamom. Cover and cook over low heat until tender. Drain the juice and reserve. </li><li></li><li>Beat together cream and vanilla extract and bring just to boil. Beat egg yolks until light and thick. Gradually add sugar, beating constantly. Reduce heat and cook over low heat, stirring constantly until the mixture thickens slightly and coats the back of a spoon. Stir in the apples and cook, stirring, for about 5 minutes. Remove from heat, then stir in the cinnamon. Pour into the tart shell and set aside for 5 minutes until cool. </li></ul>
