<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>3 Tbsp Rapeseed Oil (Or Vegetable Oil)</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Turmeric</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Cinnamon</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Coriander</li><li>2 Large Aubergines, Cut Into Bite-Sized Chunks</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>1 Tbsp Coconut Or Rapeseed Oil</li><li>1 Large Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Tsp Mustard Seeds</li><li>1 Tsp Cumin Seeds</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Nigella Seeds</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Fennel Seeds</li><li>3 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Chilli Flakes</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Turmeric</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Cinnamon</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Sweet Paprika</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Fresh Tomatoes, Chopped (Or A 400G Tin Chopped Tomatoes)</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>Fresh Mint Leaves</li><li>Fresh Green Chilli, Sliced (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Pour oil into baking dish sprinkle with turmeric, cinnamon and coriander. Add aubergines and season. Cover. Cook at 200 degrees celsius for about 30 mins or until tender and lightly browned. </li><li></li><li>For sauce heat oil and saute onion. Add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, nigella seeds, fennel seeds, cloves and chilli flakes. Once fennel seeds start popping, add remaining spices and cook 2 mins. </li><li></li><li>Drain aubergine, add to sauce, reduce heat and simmer 25 minutes. Stir in tomatoes, salt and pepper. Cook for 10 15 mins. Garnish with mint and sliced chilli. </li></ul>
