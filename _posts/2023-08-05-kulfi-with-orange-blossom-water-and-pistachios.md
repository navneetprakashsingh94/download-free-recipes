<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#sheets pastry tea-towel" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sheets Pastry Tea-Towel</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#pistachios walnuts" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pistachios Walnuts</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#tsp ground cardamom" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Ground Cardamom</a> <a href="/tags#oz sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#water" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Water</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp lemon juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Lemon Juice</a> <a href="/tags#orange blossom water" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Orange Blossom Water</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>18 Sheets Ready-Made Filo Pastry (Unwrap And Keep Under A Damp Tea-Towel Until You Are Ready To Use)</li><li>150G/5Oz Unsalted Butter, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>200G/7Oz Mixed Pistachios And Walnuts, Roughly Chopped</li><li>2 Tbsp Granulated Sugar</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cardamom</li><li>300G/10 1/2Fl Oz Granulated Sugar</li><li>250Ml/9Fl Oz Water</li><li>1 Tbsp Lemon Juice</li><li>1 Tbsp Orange Blossom Water</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the filling, mix pistachios, sugar, and cardamom, set aside. </li><li>To make the filling, place sugar and water in a large heavy bottomed pan. </li><li>Stir together on medium heat until the sugar is dissolved. </li><li>Decrease the heat to low and simmer for 15 mins. </li><li>Remove from heat. </li><li>Add the lemon juice and orange blossom water to the pan. </li><li>Leave the mixture to cool for 20 mins. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 350f. </li><li>On a floured surface, roll the pastry out to a rectangle that is about 1/4 inch thick. </li><li>Spread the butter on the pastry and arrange the pistachios over the top of it all. </li><li>Roll the pastry up from one long side and place it on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. </li><li>Prick the pastry on both sides and bake for 15 mins. </li><li>Reduce the heat to 350f and bake for 10 more mins. </li><li>Remove from the oven and cool. </li><li>Serve with a glass of rose water. </li></ul>
