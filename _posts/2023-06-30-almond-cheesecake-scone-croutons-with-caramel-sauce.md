<div>Delicious Almond Cheesecake Scone Croutons With Caramel Sauce using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 40 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#egg yolk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg Yolk</a> <a href="/tags#almonds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Almonds</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Milk</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Plain Flour, Sifted, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>75G/2 3/4Oz Butter, Chilled</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg Yolk</li><li>225G/8Oz Flaked Almonds</li><li>120G/4 1/4Oz Butter</li><li>120G/4 1/4Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>4 Tbsp Milk</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 180 c. Grease two baking trays. </li><li></li><li>Put the flour into a bowl and rub in the butter and caster sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the egg yolk. Mix everything together to form a soft dough. </li><li></li><li>Divide the dough into three and press each one into a rectangle on a floured surface. </li><li></li><li>To form scone base, divide the dough into two and flatten out each half into a round disk. </li><li></li><li>Flatten each round with a rolling pin, then cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 hour. </li><li></li><li>To make the caramel, heat the sugar in a medium sized pan, over a low heat, stirring, until golden and caramel coloured. Stir in the milk. Boil for 1 minute, until thickened. </li><li></li><li>Meanwhile, preheat oven to 200 c. </li><li></li><li>Place one of the pastry rounds in the centre of an oven proof dish. Pour over the caramel. Sprinkle the almonds evenly over the pastry. Cover with the remaining pastry round and press down so that it is completely encased. Brush with a little of the caramel. </li><li></li><li>Bake for 10 15 mins, until the pastry is golden and a skewer inserted into the center comes out clean. </li><li></li><li>Transfer to a wire rack and cool. Cut into cubes. Serve drizzled with the remaining caramel. </li></ul>
