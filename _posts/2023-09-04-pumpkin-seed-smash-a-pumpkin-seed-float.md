<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Medium-Sized Pumpkin, Flesh Scooped Out And Chopped, Fibres Removed; Seeds Removed, Toasted And Reserved For Garnish</li><li>1 Litre/1 3/4 Pints Freshly Squeezed Orange Juice</li><li>2 Tsp Ground Allspice</li><li>2 Tbsp Clear Honey</li><li>35Ml/1 1/4Fl Oz Bourbon Whiskey</li><li>1 Tbsp Sour Cherry Liqueur</li><li>2 Tsp Herb Liqueur</li><li>3 Tbsp Spiced Pumpkin Puree (See Above)</li><li>Ice</li><li>Pumpkin Seeds, Toasted</li><li>Pinch Freshly Grated Nutmeg</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>To make the orange orange granita. Mix the orange juice with the ginger and allspice and let sit for at least 30 minutes. </li><li>Process the orange juice with the honey to form a slush. Pour it into a bowl and add the remaining ingredients and stir to combine. </li><li>Divide among 6 highball glasses, garnish with a sprinkling of toasted pumpkin seeds, and a pinch of nutmeg. </li></ul>
