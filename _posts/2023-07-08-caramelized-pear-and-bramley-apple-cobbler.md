<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 180 c. </li><li>Place the sliced pear in a pie dish and pour the hot oil over. If you have a cake baking tray, you can also add the pear pieces in at this point. Stir in the cream tartar, karo syrup and treacle. Cover and place in the preheated oven for 30 mins, or until the pear is golden brown. Let cool. </li><li>Beat the butter with the sugar until smooth then add the egg yolk. Gradually mix in the flour. Stir until just incorporated. </li><li>Cover the bottom of a 23 cm x 20 cm baking dish with the sticky bottom coating. Press in the dough and prick all over with a fork. Bake for 10 mins, then reduce the oven temperature to 180 c and bake for another 10 mins until golden brown. </li><li>Meanwhile, for the apple mixture, melt the butter in a saucepan with the sugar and orange zest. Add the apples and cinnamon and cook for 8 10 mins until tender. Cover and leave to cool. </li><li>Remove the bottom coating from the apple mixture, sprinkle with the crumble topping and transfer the apple mix to the pie dish. Spoon the cox royal galette over the top and cook for 20 25 mins, or until the custard has set. Serve with creme fraiche. </li></ul>
