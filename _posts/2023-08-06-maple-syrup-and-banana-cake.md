<a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#bananas" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bananas</a> <a href="/tags#maple syrup" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Maple Syrup</a> <a href="/tags#oz milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Milk</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#free-range eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Free-Range Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#tsp baking powder" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Baking Powder</a> <a href="/tags#nuts" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Nuts</a> <a href="/tags#oz cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Cream</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Butter, Softened, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>400G/14Oz Plain Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>4 Ripe Bananas, Peeled</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Maple Syrup</li><li>25-50Ml/1-2Fl Oz Milk (Optional)</li><li>150G/5 1/4Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>4 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>2 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Pecan Nuts, Roughly Chopped</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Double Cream</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place butter and 1 cup flour in a large mixing bowl and mix well until incorporated and then gradually add milk, stirring briskly until the batter is smooth. </li><li>Place the bananas, syrup, sugar, eggs and baking powder in the mixing bowl, mix on a low setting for 4 minutes then mix until combined. </li><li>Pour the mixture into a x 15cm 8 inch spring form cake tin and bake for 15 minutes at 160c. </li><li>When the cake has cooled, remove from the tin and cut into 6 wedges. </li><li>When cool, use the back of a spoon to make indentations in the cake, and drizzle with the cream if desired. </li></ul>
