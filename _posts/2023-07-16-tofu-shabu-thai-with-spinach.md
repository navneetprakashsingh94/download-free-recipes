<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li> 1/2 Lime, Zest And Juice</li><li>Large Pinch Chilli Flakes</li><li>2 Tbsp Soy Sauce</li><li>2 Tbsp Honey Or Agave Syrup</li><li>2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Tofu, Cut Into Pieces</li><li>2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil</li><li> 1/4 Onion, Finely Sliced</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Crushed</li><li> 1/4 Red Pepper, Chopped</li><li>Handful Spinach Leaves</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Basmati Rice, Cooked According To Packet Instructions And Drained</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Mash the tofu in a bowl, add the soy sauce honey syrup. Mix well. Mix the oil with the marinade and place tofu in a container and put in refrigerator to marinate for at least 1/2 hour. </li><li>Meanwhile, heat the oil in a large frying pan, add the onion and cook on a high heat for 3 4 minutes, then add the garlic pepper, cover for 2 minutes. Then add the spinach, cover again and continue to cook and stir until the spinach is wilted, it should be crunchy and crisp not mushy. </li><li>Drain the tofu mash it with a fork and add to the onion mixture. Heat through for another minute. </li><li>Serve with basmati rice. </li></ul>
