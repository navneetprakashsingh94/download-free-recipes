<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>250G/9Oz Plain Flour</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>1 Tsp Salt</li><li>1 Tbsp Caster Sugar</li><li>3 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>425Ml/15Fl Oz Whole Milk</li><li>115G/4Oz Unsalted Butter, Melted</li><li>8 Slices Cooked American-Style Bacon (Or Rindless Smoked Streaky Bacon), Chopped</li><li>1 Shallot, Finely Diced</li><li>3 Peppercorns</li><li>1 Bay Leaf</li><li>2 Tbsp White Wine Vinegar</li><li>2 Large Free-Range Egg Yolks</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Butter, Melted</li><li>Sea Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>2 Tbsp White Wine Vinegar</li><li>4 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>12 Slices Streaky Bacon</li><li>2-3 Tbsp Finely Chopped Chives</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Sift the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar into a bowl. In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk. Pour the liquid ingredients over the flour, mixing only until just combined. Stir in the butter. Cover and refrigerate for 30 mins. </li><li>Meanwhile, make the hollandaise sauce. In a saucepan, combine the bacon bacon, shallot, peppercorns, bay leaf, vinegar and 1/2 cup water. Bring to a simmer then reduce the heat and simmer for 10 mins. Transfer to a blender and blend to a paste. Pour through a sieve into a heatproof measuring cup. Discard the peppercorns and bay leaf. </li><li>Whisk the egg yolks in a bowl until pale. Melt the butter in a small saucepan over low heat. Whisk in half the vinegar and season with salt and pepper. Slowly whisk the eggs into the milk mixture. Continue to whisk until the mixture has thickened, about 3 mins. Pour the butter over the egg mixture in a thin stream. Add the fried bacon slices and chives, fold through with a rubber spatula and season. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 400 f. </li><li>Brush a 10 cup muffin pan with the remaining vinegar and dust with flour. Fill the pan with the batter and bake for 10 mins. Remove the pan from the oven. Spread the hollandaise over the top and bake for 10 mins. Let the pancake cool in the pan for 10 mins before turning out onto a plate. </li><li>Serve with hollandaise</li></ul>
