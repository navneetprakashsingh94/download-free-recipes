<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>85G/3Oz Pork Tenderloin</li><li>2 Sprigs Fresh Rosemary</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>8 Anya Potatoes, Halved</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>2 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Chives</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>1 Tsp Dijon Mustard</li><li>1 Tbsp Lemon Juice</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 375</li><li>1. Coat tenderloin in evoo, season with salt pepper</li><li>2. Place on cookie sheet and roast for 30 35 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 160. Let meat rest for 5 minutes. Slice and serve with lemon mustard cream sauce and fresh thyme sprigs. </li><li></li><li>3. Prepare potatoes as usual until cooked, drain and return to the pot. Add all other ingredients and mash well to desired consistency, adjust seasoning with salt pepper. </li></ul>
