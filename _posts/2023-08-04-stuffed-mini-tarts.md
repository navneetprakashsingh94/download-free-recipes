<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>130G/4 3/4Oz Ground Almonds</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Butter, Softened</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten, Plus 1 Free-Range Egg Yolk</li><li>Few Drops Vanilla Extract</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Ready-Made Shortcrust Pastry (Use All Butter If Possible)</li><li>Plain Flour, For Dusting</li><li>5 Tbsp Raspberry Jam</li><li>2 Tbsp Milk</li><li>1-2 Tbsp Flaked Or Whole Almonds</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. Place the ground almonds, butter, sugar, egg and vanilla in the bowl of an electric mixer fitted with a paddle attachment. Beat on medium speed until light and fluffy, about 1 minute. Set aside. </li><li>2. Meanwhile, place the pastry butter in a heatproof bowl. Microwave for 20 seconds, stirring once or twice, until soft and melted. Stir in the flour and 1/4 cup hot water. With a rubber spatula, fold the pastry mixture into the butter, until a stiff dough forms. Form into a ball. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 hour. </li><li>3. Preheat the oven to 325 f. Roll the dough out between 2 sheets of parchment paper to the same thickness as the baking pans. Cut out 12 large squares. </li><li>4. Pierce the cookies with a fork and top each with a teaspoon of jam. Bake on the centre shelf of the oven for 13 minutes. Turn off the oven. Let the cookies rest in the oven for a further 5 minutes. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with milk and toasted flaked almonds. Let cool completely before storing in a glass jar. </li></ul>
