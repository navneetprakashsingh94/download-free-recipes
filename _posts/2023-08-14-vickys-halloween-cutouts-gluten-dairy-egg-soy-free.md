<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>140G/5Oz Butter, Softened</li><li>280G/10Oz Icing Sugar</li><li>1-2 Tbsp Milk</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>Few Drops Food Colouring</li><li>1 Large Orange, Zest And 2 Tbsp Juice</li><li>25G/1Oz Cocoa Powder</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Milk Or Dark Chocolate, Melted</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Beat the butter for 5 minutes until smooth, gradually add the icing sugar, a little at a time, mixing well between each addition</li><li>Add the milk, vanilla, food colouring and orange zest and mix well</li><li>Add the cocoa and mix until well combined</li><li>Tip into a pastry bag fitted with a round round tip. </li><li>Pipe a 6cm cross in the center of each square of baking paper</li><li>Bake for 11 13 minutes until the base of the sugar cookies are just slightly golden and the middles feel firm and springy to the touch</li><li>Transfer to a wire rack to cool completely</li><li>Melt the chocolate in the microwave for 30 seconds or until smooth and spreadable</li><li>Pipe onto the centre of each cooled chocolate cookie</li><li>Using a piping bag fitted with a round or star tip, pipe chocolate a little bit at a time along one end of each piece and allow to set up</li><li>Enjoy</li></ul>
