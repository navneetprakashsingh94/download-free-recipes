<a href="/tags#chocolate cent cocoa solids" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chocolate Cent Cocoa Solids</a> <a href="/tags#eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#jar cherries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Jar Cherries</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp cherry brandy" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Cherry Brandy</a> <a href="/tags#oz cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Cream</a> <a href="/tags#eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#cocoa powder" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cocoa Powder</a> <a href="/tags#chocolate % cocoa solids" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chocolate % Cocoa Solids</a> <a href="/tags#level tbsp morello cherry jam" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Level Tbsp Morello Cherry Jam</a> <a href="/tags#cocoa powder" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cocoa Powder</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>225G/8Oz Dark Chocolate (75 Per Cent Cocoa Solids)</li><li>2 Large Eggs, Separated</li><li>1 Jar Pitted Morello Cherries</li><li>2 Tbsp Cherry Brandy</li><li>225Ml/8Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>6 Large Eggs, Separated</li><li>150G/5Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>50G/2Oz Cocoa Powder, Sifted</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Dark Chocolate (75 % Cocoa Solids)</li><li>1 Level Tbsp Morello Cherry Jam</li><li>A Little Cocoa Powder</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 180c/375f/gas 4. </li><li>Line the bottom of a deep springform tin with foil and grease with cooking spray. </li><li>Place the cocoa and eggs in a small bowl and whisk until all the lumps are dissolved. </li><li>Pour the cocoa and egg mixture into the prepared tin and bake for 45 minutes. </li><li>For the cherry sauce cook the cherries in the cherry brandy in a small pot until they soften and release their liquid. </li><li>Beat the cream and eggs in a medium bowl with a hand held mixer until light and fluffy. </li><li>Gradually stir in the sugar. </li><li>Fold in the cocoa. </li><li>Place the cream in a large saucepan and place over a low heat. </li><li>Beat continuously until a custard forms and coats the back of the spoon. </li><li>Cool completely. </li><li>Beat in the morello jam. </li><li>Chill until needed. </li><li>To serve line the pastry case with a piping bag fitted with a 1 cm plain tube. </li><li>Pipe a spoonful of the cream in the centre of the paper. </li><li>Arrange the cherries over the cream, then brush the remaining cream over the surface. </li><li>Dust with cocoa powder. </li><li>Serve. </li></ul>
