<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>3 Chicken Thigh Fillets, Cut Into Pieces</li><li>1 Aubergine, Cut Into 2Cm/ 3/4In Cubes</li><li>4 Spring Onions, Sliced Into 2Cm/ 3/4In Pieces</li><li> 1/2 Courgette, Cut Into 1Cm/ 1/2In Slices</li><li>2 Banana Shallots, Cut Into 8 Segments Lengthwise</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Fresh Root Ginger, Peeled And Finely Grated</li><li>4 Garlic Cloves, Finely Sliced</li><li>1 Fat Red Chilli, Finely Sliced</li><li>2 Tsp Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>4 Tbsp Reduced-Sodium Soy Sauce</li><li>1 Tbsp Dark Brown Sugar</li><li>1 Tsp Cornflour</li><li>Handful Basil Leaves, Roughly Chopped</li><li>Freshly Squeezed Lime Juice, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 200c/fan 130c/gas 4. </li><li>Heat the oil in a large ovenproof frying pan. </li><li>Add the lamb fillets and cook for 1 2 minutes on each side, until well browned. </li><li>Transfer the lamb to a plate and set aside. </li><li>Add the aubergine and spring onions to the pan and cook, stirring, for 2 minutes, until they just start to soften. </li><li>Stir in the courgette and cook, stirring, for 3 5 minutes, until they just start to soften. </li><li>Return the lamb to the pan. </li><li>Add the shallots, ginger, cloves, chilli and pepper. </li><li>Stir to combine. </li><li>Add the soy sauce, brown sugar and garlic and stir to combine. </li><li>Transfer the mixture to the oven and roast for 20 25 minutes, until the lamb is cooked through and the vegetables are tender. </li><li>Stir in the basil and lime juice. </li><li>Remove from the oven. </li><li>Serve hot. </li></ul>
