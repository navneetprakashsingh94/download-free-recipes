<div>Delicious Cajun Style Prawns using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#cloves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cloves</a> <a href="/tags#virgin oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Virgin Oil</a> <a href="/tags#celery salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Celery Salt</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp vodka" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Vodka</a> <a href="/tags#tin cherry tomatoes juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tin Cherry Tomatoes Juice</a> <a href="/tags#Worcestershire sauce" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Worcestershire Sauce</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#dashes Tabasco sauce" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dashes Tabasco Sauce</a> <a href="/tags#king prawns" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">King Prawns</a> <a href="/tags#handful flatleaf parsley" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Handful Flatleaf Parsley</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>3 Large Garlic Cloves, Thinly Sliced</li><li>1 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Celery Salt</li><li>1 Tbsp Vodka</li><li>400G Tin Cherry Tomatoes In Juice</li><li>1 Tbsp Worcestershire Sauce</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Caster Sugar</li><li>7-8 Dashes Tabasco Sauce</li><li>10-12 Large Raw King Prawns, Shelled And Deveined</li><li>Small Handful Flatleaf Parsley, Roughly Chopped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 200c/400f/gas mark 5. </li><li>Put cloves in a tea towel and tie with kitchen string. </li><li>Heat oil in a large flameproof frying pan over medium high heat. </li><li>Add a pinch of celery salt and cook 2 minutes, or until toasted. </li><li>Remove from heat and add vodka. </li><li>Return to heat, cover and simmer 2 minutes. </li><li>Add tomatoes and cook until soft, about 10 minutes. </li><li>Remove lid, stir in remaining ingredients and bring to boil. </li><li>Place prawns on rack set in a roasting tin. </li><li>Brush prawns with marinade and cook under low heat 5 minutes, or until prawns have turned pink. </li><li>Serve with parsley leaves sprinkled over. </li></ul>
