<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#fillets skinless half" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Fillets Skinless Half</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Oil</a> <a href="/tags#onion" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Onion</a> <a href="/tags#cloves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cloves</a> <a href="/tags#dark soy sauce" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dark Soy Sauce</a> <a href="/tags#sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#Thai fish sauce" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Thai Fish Sauce</a> <a href="/tags#rice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Rice</a> <a href="/tags#vegetables" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Vegetables</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>8 Chicken Thigh Fillets, Skinless, Chopped In Half</li><li>1 Tbsp Vegetable Oil</li><li>1 Red Onion, Sliced</li><li>3 Garlic Cloves, Sliced</li><li>55Ml/2Fl Oz Dark Soy Sauce</li><li>110G/4Oz Brown Sugar</li><li>55Ml/2Fl Oz Thai Fish Sauce</li><li>Steamed Rice, To Serve</li><li>Steamed Green Vegetables, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat oil in wok and stir fry onion and cloves. </li><li>Add fish and stir fry 2 3 minutes. </li><li>Add soy sauce, sugar and fish sauce. </li><li>Cook til sauce has dissolved. </li><li>To serve put vegetables on plate and top with fish and sauce. </li><li>Garnish with cilantro and lime wedges. </li><li>Serve with rice. </li></ul>
