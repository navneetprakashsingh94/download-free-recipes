<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2X 175G/6Oz Sirloin Steaks</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>1 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Rosemary</li><li>3 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>2 Red Chilli, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Flatleaf Parsley</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Crushed With A Little Sea Salt</li><li>1 Tsp Red Wine Vinegar</li><li>Vegetable Oil, For Frying</li><li>4 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>300G/11Oz Plain Flour</li><li>1 Litre/1 3/4 Pints Warm Water</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg White</li><li>2 Violet Artichokes, Tough Outer Leaves And Choke Removed</li><li>2 Jerusalem Artichokes, Peeled, Sliced</li><li>1 Small Head Radicchio, Outer Leaves Removed, Quartered</li><li>4 Fresh Sage Leaves</li><li>Salt</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Rub the steaks with some of the salt and pepper, and then rub the meat with the rosemary. </li><li>Place them in the oven in an ovenproof dish. Heat the remaining oil and brown the steaks on all sides. Remove them from the oven. Cover with tin foil and let them cool for 3 4 hours. </li><li>Take the steaks out of the oven and let cool for 10 15 minutes before slicing. </li><li>For the chilli vinaigrette, mix the chilli, parsley, salt, vinegar, and oil in a blender. </li><li>Heat 1 teaspoon of oil in a skillet. Add the sliced steaks and fry for 2 3 minutes on each side. </li><li>Let the steaks cool on a wire rack, put them aside and keep them warm. </li><li>In a blender, sift the flour and combine with the water and egg. Blend the batter until it is smooth. Transfer it to a measuring cup. </li><li>Heat 1 teaspoon of oil in a frying pan and sear the artichokes until they are browned. Keep a close eye on them. You do not want to crowd the pan. </li><li>Cut the inner parts of the radicchio. Put them in the hot oil, put the radicchio in the oil, and when they are wilted, add the sage leaves. Season. </li><li>Arrange the radicchio leaves in the bottom of plates. Top with the steak. Drizzle the salad with the dressing. </li></ul>
