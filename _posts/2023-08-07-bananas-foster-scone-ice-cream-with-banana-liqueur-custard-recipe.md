<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Golden Syrup</li><li>2 Large Bananas (1 Cut Into Slices On The Diagonal, 1 Mashed)</li><li>175G/6Oz Softened Butter, Plus Extra To Line The Pudding Basin</li><li>175G/6Oz Soft Light Brown Sugar</li><li>3 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>175G/6Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>Squeeze Lime Juice</li><li>250Ml/9Fl Oz Full-Fat Milk</li><li>250Ml/9Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>1 Vanilla Pod, Split Down The Middle, Lengthways Or 1 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>6 Free-Range Egg Yolks</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Tbsp Banana Liqueur (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 400 degrees f. Butter the basin and line with baking paper. </li><li>Peel the bananas, halve, and quarter. </li><li>Pour 1cm of water in the bottom of the basin and then place a slice of banana in each basin. </li><li>Sprinkle with the sugar, then mix the large eggs and flour, then add in the lime juice and lowfat milk. </li><li>Place the basin in a roasting tin and pour in the water. </li><li>Bake the banana clafoutis for 20 30 min. </li><li>Remove from the oven and cold. </li><li>Meanwhile, combine the lowfat milk, cream and vanilla beans in a saucepan and bring to the boil. </li><li>Remove from the heat and pour over the egg yolks. </li><li>Return the mix to the pan and cook gently for 6 8 min or possibly till thickened to the consistency of pudding. </li><li>Stir in the caster sugar and banana liqueur. </li><li>Pour into a clean, jar. </li><li>Chill. </li><li>To serve, remove from the fridge. </li><li>Cut the clafoutis in half and fill with the banana clafouti. </li></ul>
