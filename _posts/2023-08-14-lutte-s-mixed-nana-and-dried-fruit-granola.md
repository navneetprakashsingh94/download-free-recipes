<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Lutte S Mixed Nana And Dried Fruit Granola using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#porridge oats" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Porridge Oats</a> <a href="/tags#bran wheat germ" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bran Wheat Germ</a> <a href="/tags#barley rye flakes oats" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Barley Rye Flakes Oats</a> <a href="/tags#hazelnuts" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Hazelnuts</a> <a href="/tags#almonds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Almonds</a> <a href="/tags#sultanas" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sultanas</a> <a href="/tags#apricots" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Apricots</a> <a href="/tags#figs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Figs</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Jumbo Porridge Oats</li><li>25G/1Oz Flaked Bran Or Wheat Germ</li><li>75G/3Oz Barley Or Rye Flakes (Optional, You Can Always Add More Oats Instead)</li><li>50G/2Oz Hazelnuts, Lightly Crushed</li><li>50G/2Oz Flaked Almonds</li><li>50G/2Oz Sultanas</li><li>50G/2Oz Dried, Ready-To-Eat Apricots, Roughly Chopped</li><li>50G/2Oz Dried, Ready-To-Eat Figs, Roughly Chopped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Pre heat the oven to 180c. </li><li>Meanwhile, mix together the porridge, bran wheat germ, barley rye flakes, hazelnuts and almonds in a large bowl. </li><li>Pour over the sultanas, apricots and dried figs. </li><li>Mix together and spread out onto a baking tray. </li><li>Bake for 30 40 minutes, stirring about halfway through. </li><li>When the granola is crispy and golden, turn it out onto a rack to cool. </li><li>Keep in an airtight container. </li><li>Enjoy this tasty treat</li></ul>
