<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Tsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>1 Large Garlic Bulb, With The Top Sliced Off</li><li>200G/7Oz Low-Fat Cream Cheese</li><li>3 Tbsp Low-Fat Greek Yoghurt</li><li> 1/2 Bunch Chives, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Large Red Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li>400G/14Oz Can Chopped Tomatoes</li><li>2 Sprigs Thyme, Leaves Finely Chopped (About 1 Tsp Chopped Leaves)</li><li>Pinch Caster Sugar (Optional)</li><li>Flaked Sea Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>Large Handful Fresh Basil Leaves, Roughly Torn</li><li>3 Medium Free-Range Eggs</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Wholemeal Or Plain Flour</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Dried Natural Breadcrumbs</li><li>2 Packed Tbsp Finely Grated Parmesan Cheese (Or A Similar Vegetarian Hard Cheese)</li><li>2 Tsp Chopped Rosemary Leaves</li><li>2 Medium Courgettes (As Straight As Possible)</li><li>Spray Oil</li><li>1 Small Lemon, Cut Into Wedges</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 180c/gas mark 5. </li><li>In a medium bowl, place philly, yoghurt, fennel bulbs, chives and 3 tbsp oil. Season with salt and pepper. Mix until smooth. Chill until ready to use. </li><li>Heat 3 tbsp oil in a heavy based pan, add onion, cloves and cook until softened. </li><li>Add tomatoes and cook for 15 minutes until softened and then add thyme, spices and herbs and simmer for 10 minutes. Remove from the heat and set aside to cool to room temperature. </li><li>Whisk the eggs and half the philly with a fork, then fold in half the yogurt, breadcrumbs and half the grated cheese. </li><li>Slice the courgettes in half lengthways, then half the slice into 4cm pieces. </li><li>Coat each piece with a little of the philly mixture and lightly press in the breadcrumbs. Press into the zucchini slices and place on the baking tray. </li><li>Spray lightly with oil and bake for 25 minutes until golden and cooked. </li><li>Serve warm with a lemon wedge. </li></ul>
