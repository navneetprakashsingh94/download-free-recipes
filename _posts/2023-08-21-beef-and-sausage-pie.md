<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2-3 Tbsp Light Olive Oil</li><li>1Kg/2Lb 4Oz Braising Beef, Cut Into Cubes</li><li>250G/9Oz Shallots Or Silverskin Pickling Onions, Halved</li><li>2 Carrots, Thinly Sliced</li><li>200G/7Oz Button Mushrooms, Left Whole</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Plain Flour</li><li>500Ml/18Fl Oz Ale</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Beef Stock</li><li>2-3 Tbsp Onion Marmalade</li><li>1-2 Tbsp Worcestershire Sauce</li><li>3 Bay Leaves</li><li>Gravy Browning (Optional)</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>175G/6Oz Self-Raising Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>75G/1 3/4Oz Suet, Shredded</li><li>3-4 Tbsp Hot Horseradish Sauce, From A Jar</li><li>2 Tbsp Chopped Parsley</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat oil in a pan and brown the beef. Remove from pan. </li><li>Saute the onions, carrots, and mushrooms until soft. Stir in the flour until well combined. Add the ale and beef stock. Stir. </li><li>Return beef and onion mixture to the pan and add the onions, carrots and mushrooms, stir. Add the onion marmalade, and the sauce. Add the bay leaves. Season. Bring to the boil and cover with cling film or wrap. Cook in the oven at 180c for about 2 1/2 hours. Remove and leave to cool. </li><li>For the pastry, mix together the flour and suet. Press the mixture evenly onto the bottom and sides of a 9 inch greased deep dish pie dish. </li><li>Slice the sausages into thin slices and place into the pie dish. Pour over the sauce. Top with the slices of sausage. Bake at 180c for about 30 minutes until well risen and golden. </li></ul>
