<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>4 Tbsp Sea Salt</li><li>1 Tbsp Garlic Powder</li><li>1 Tbsp Celery Salt</li><li>1 Tbsp Cracked Black Pepper</li><li>1 Tbsp Ground Ginger</li><li>1 Tbsp Crushed Coriander Seeds</li><li>2 Tsp Chilli Powder</li><li>1 Tbsp Dried Herbes De Provence</li><li>1 Tbsp Smoked Paprika</li><li>1 X 3.5Kg/7Lb 10Oz Short Rib Of Beef On The Bone</li><li>200G/7Oz Dates, Roughly Chopped</li><li>250G/9Oz Drained Pickled Onions, Roughly Chopped</li><li>570Ml/1 Pint Stout</li><li>3 Tbsp English Mustard</li><li>2 Tbsp Worcestershire Sauce</li><li>300Ml/ 1/2 Pint Apple Juice</li><li>3 Tbsp Clear Honey</li><li>3 Tbsp Black Treacle</li><li>3 Tbsp Golden Syrup</li><li>1 Tbsp Tabasco</li><li>1 Tbsp Tomato Puree</li><li>3 Tbsp Soft Dark Brown Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Combine the first nine ingredients and rub it over the beef bone, then bake it in a very hot oven until it is very brown and crisp and nearly falling apart. </li><li>Drain it of all the juice. </li><li>This is your bbq sauce. </li><li>Combine all the ingredients and add them to the cooked bone. </li><li>Simmer on low for 2 hours or until the meat pulls away easily from the bone. </li><li>You want to keep some juice, but be cautious. </li><li>Just before serving, turn the bone over and baste the top, but don t flip the bone into the sauce. </li><li>Add a little of the meat juice to balance out, and to taste. </li></ul>
