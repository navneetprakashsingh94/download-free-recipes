<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#tin milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tin Milk</a> <a href="/tags#oz cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Cream</a> <a href="/tags#cardamom pods seeds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cardamom Pods Seeds</a> <a href="/tags#block puff pastry" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Block Puff Pastry</a> <a href="/tags#brown sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Brown Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#hazelnuts" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Hazelnuts</a> <a href="/tags#bananas coins" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bananas Coins</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>397G/14Oz Tin Condensed Milk</li><li>500Ml/18Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>5 Cardamom Pods, Seeds Removed And Crushed</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Block Puff Pastry</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Brown Sugar</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>30-50G/1-1 3/4Oz Chopped Hazelnuts</li><li>4-5 Bananas, Sliced Into 2Cm/ 3/4In Coins</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Soak the milk in a bowl for 24 hours. </li><li>Strain the milk through a sieve. Discard the liquid. </li><li>Transfer the milk to a saucepan with the cream and cardamom. </li><li>Over medium heat, bring to the boil, and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring constantly. Remove from the heat. </li><li>Place the puff pastry squares in a shallow greased baking dish. </li><li>Pour the milk into the pastry, and bake in the preheated oven for approximately 25 minutes, or until golden and puffy. </li><li>For the caramel sauce, mix together the brown sugar and butter until combined. </li><li>Stir in the nuts. </li><li>To serve, place a small spoonful of praline in the centre of a pastry square and arrange a banana slice on top. </li></ul>
