<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Apple Cheese Tart using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#jars apple sauce" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Jars Apple Sauce</a> <a href="/tags#tsp ground spice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Ground Spice</a> <a href="/tags#nuts" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Nuts</a> <a href="/tags#raisins" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Raisins</a> <a href="/tags#oil spray" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil Spray</a> <a href="/tags#pack filo pastry sheets" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pack Filo Pastry Sheets</a> <a href="/tags#demerara sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Demerara Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#ice cream custard" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ice Cream Custard</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 X 285G/10Oz Jars Chunky Apple Sauce</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Mixed Spice</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Mixed Nuts, Roughly Chopped</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Raisins</li><li>Cooking Oil Spray</li><li>270G/9 3/4Oz Pack Filo Pastry, Or 6 Sheets</li><li>Demerara Sugar, For Sprinkling</li><li>Ice Cream Or Custard, To Serve (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 400 f. Line 2 baking trays with baking paper. </li><li>Heat apple sauce and spice in a small saucepan over low heat. Remove from heat and set aside to cool. </li><li>Place the nuts and raisins in a small bowl and sprinkle with the spiced apple sauce. </li><li>Spray a large baking tray with oil spray. Sprinkle with some of the pastry sheets and sprinkle with half of the demerara sugar. </li><li>Place a heaped teaspoon of nut and raisins in the centre of each pastry sheet and fold each pastry sheet up from the base, forming a well. Sprinkle with a little more demerara sugar. </li><li>Bake for 25 30 minutes or until pastry is puffed and golden. </li><li>Serve topped with ice cream custard. </li></ul>
