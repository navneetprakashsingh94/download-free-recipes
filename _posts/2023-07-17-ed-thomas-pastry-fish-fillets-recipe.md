<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#fillets" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Fillets</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#butter puff pastry" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter Puff Pastry</a> <a href="/tags#tips" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tips</a> <a href="/tags#egg tbsp milk egg-wash" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg Tbsp Milk Egg-Wash</a> <a href="/tags#sesame seeds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sesame Seeds</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>6 Tinned Anchovy Fillets</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>200G/7Oz All Butter Puff Pastry</li><li>12 Asparagus Tips, About 12Cm/5In Long</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Mixed With 1 Tbsp Milk, For Egg-Wash</li><li>1 Tbsp Sesame Seeds</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 400. </li><li>Season fillets with salt and pepper. </li><li>Heat oil in a large frying pan. </li><li>Cook fish for 2 mins on each side, or possibly till golden. </li><li>Remove from the oil and set aside. </li><li>Roll pastry to 1/8 inch thickness. </li><li>Cut into 4 inch squares. </li><li>Place 1 fillet in the middle of each square, and sprinkle with the onion tips. </li><li>Brush edges of each square with egg wash and fold in half to seal. </li><li>Brush edges with egg wash. </li><li>Bake for 10 mins or possibly till golden. </li><li>Serve with a side of couscous. </li></ul>
