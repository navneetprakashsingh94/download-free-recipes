<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Dried Or Fresh Egg Noodles, Cooked According To The Packet Instructions And Drained</li><li>1 1/2 Tbsp Oil</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Tbsp Fresh Root Ginger, Finely Chopped</li><li>3 Tbsp Spring Onions, Chopped, Plus Extra For Garnish</li><li>450G/1 Lb Minced Pork</li><li>3 Tbsp Yellow Bean Sauce Or Black Bean Sauce</li><li>1 1/2 Tbsp Shaoxing Rice Wine Or Dry Sherry (Optional)</li><li>2 Tbsp Soy Sauce</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>2 Tsp Chilli Oil (Optional)</li><li>2 Tsp Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place noodles in a bowl and cover with boiling water. Let soak. </li><li></li><li>Heat a wok and add 1tbsp oil. Add cloves, ginger and spring onions. Stir fry until fragrant. Add pork and stir fry for 2 minutes or until pork is tender. Add bean sauce, sherry, soy sauce, ground pepper, chilli and sugar. Stir fry for 1 minute. </li><li></li><li>Drain noodles and divide between two plates. Top with the pork mixture and enjoy. </li></ul>
