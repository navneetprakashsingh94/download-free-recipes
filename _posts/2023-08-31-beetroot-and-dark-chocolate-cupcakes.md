<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Butter, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>250G/9Oz Cooked And Peeled Beetroot</li><li>200G/7Oz Dark Chocolate (70% Cocoa Solids)</li><li>4 Tbsp Hot Espresso</li><li>135G/5Oz Plain Flour</li><li>1 Heaped Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>3 Tbsp Cocoa Powder</li><li>5 Free-Range Eggs, Separated</li><li>190G/6 1/2Oz Golden Caster Sugar</li><li>Creme Fraiche Or Double Cream, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Melt butter in a pan and add to the cooked beetroot. </li><li>Take off heat and stir in cocoa solids and espresso. </li><li>Cool completely. </li><li>Sift flour, baking powder and cocoa powder and sift into a bowl. </li><li>Beat eggs until thick and pale. </li><li>Slowly add caster sugar and whip until mixture has a ribbon like texture and the sugar dissolves. </li><li>Fold in melted chocolate and stir through beetroot mixture. </li><li>Scoop into cupcake cases and bake in an oven preheated to 190 c for 25 minutes. </li><li>Serve warm or cold with creme fraiche cream, as desired. </li><li>I recommend serving with vanilla ice cream. </li></ul>
