<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>225G/8Oz Baking Spread, Straight From The Fridge, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>225G/8Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>225G/8Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>4 Large Free-Range Eggs, Beaten</li><li>2 Eating Apples, Peeled, Cored And Grated (See Recipe Tips)</li><li>Icing Sugar, For Dusting</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>3 Tbsp Lemon Curd</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Line a 375ml tin with paper liners. </li><li>Beat together the butter and sugar for 5 8 minutes or until pale and creamy. </li><li>Add the flour and baking powder and mix well to make a smooth batter. </li><li>Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the apples. </li><li>Spoon the mixture into the paper cases. </li><li>Bake at 150c/gas 4 for 20 25 minutes. </li><li>When baked, transfer to a wire rack to cool. </li><li>For the icing, beat the cream and lemon curd together, then slowly beat in the sugar until thick. </li><li>Serve the muffins with the lemon icing. </li></ul>
