<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>225G/8Oz Unsalted Butter, In Pieces</li><li>430G/15Oz Plain Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>1 Lemon, Zest Only</li><li>Pinch Ground Cinnamon</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>4 Tbsp Cornflour</li><li>3 Braeburn Apples, Peeled, Cored And Cut Into 1Cm/ 1/2In Thick Slices</li><li>3 Cox Apples, Peeled, Cored And Cut Into 1Cm/ 1/2In Thick Slices</li><li>200G/7Oz Marzipan, Cut Into Small Cubes</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Dried Sour Cherries</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Lightly Beaten With A Pinch Of Salt</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>In a bowl mix the flour and caster sugar. Rub the butter in with your fingertips until you have a crumble like mixture. Then add the salt, lemon zest, cinnamon and enough cold water to make a dough. Turn the dough out on a floured surface and knead until smooth. Cover and set aside to rest at room temperature for 1 hour. </li><li>Preheat oven to 190c. Mix the sugar and rum in a bowl and set aside. </li><li>Peel, core and slice the apples. </li><li>Combine the apples, marzipan and cherries and sprinkle with caster sugar mixture. </li><li>Dust with flour. Lightly grease a 26 cm cake tin and line the bottom with baking parchment. </li><li>Transfer the dough to the pan. Smooth out the surface and press into the base. Sprinkle the cinnamon sugar evenly over the dough. Bake for 40 45 minutes until golden brown. </li><li>Transfer to a wire rack and allow to cool for 15 minutes before turning out. </li><li>Serve with cream and vanilla ice cream. </li></ul>
