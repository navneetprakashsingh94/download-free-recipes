<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Arbroath Smokies</li><li>2 Lemons, Juice Only</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>25G/1Oz Fresh Chives, Finely Chopped</li><li>Sea Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Sherry Vinegar</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Red Wine Vinegar</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>Mixed Leaves (Baby Red Chard, Lamb'S Tongue, Watercress, Little Gem)</li><li>2 Quails' Eggs, Soft-Boiled And Peeled</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Cooked Beetroot, Sliced</li><li>Fresh Chervil Leaves, To Garnish</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Marinate the smokies in the lemon juice, pepper, cream, sherry vinegar, vinegar vinegar for 15 minutes. </li><li>Remove from the marinade and put in the fridge for 2 to 3 hours. </li><li>Boil the eggs in salted water for 5 6 minutes, when cooked, remove from the pot, peel, chop, and add to the bowl with the smokies. </li><li>Season with sea salt and pepper. </li><li>Add some mashed beetroot, mix in and garnish with chervil. </li></ul>
