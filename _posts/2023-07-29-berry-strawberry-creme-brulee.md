<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#pint cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pint Cream</a> <a href="/tags#pint milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pint Milk</a> <a href="/tags#vanilla pod" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Vanilla Pod</a> <a href="/tags#egg yolks" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg Yolks</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#egg whites" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg Whites</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp cherry jam" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Cherry Jam</a> <a href="/tags#circle sponge cake thick" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Circle Sponge Cake Thick</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>300Ml/ 1/2 Pint Double Cream</li><li>300Ml/ 1/2 Pint Milk</li><li>1 Vanilla Pod (Soft And Fresh)</li><li>3 Free-Range Egg Yolks</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>3 Free-Range Egg Whites</li><li>175G/6Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>2-3 Tbsp Cherry Jam</li><li>20Cm/8In Circle Ready-Made Sponge Cake About 2.5Cm/1In Thick</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat the cream and milk to simmering point. </li><li>Cut the vanilla pod in half and scrape the seeds into the hot mixture. </li><li>Take off the heat and allow to stand for 10 minutes. </li><li>Beat the egg yolks and caster sugar until light in colour and creamy. </li><li>Add the hot cream and milk into the egg mixture, continuing to stir to incorporate. </li><li>Strain through a sieve into a bowl, discarding the vanilla pod. </li><li>Whip egg whites to stiff peaks in a clean glass bowl and gradually sprinkle in the extra caster sugar. </li><li>Mix in the cherry jam. </li><li>Using a spoon, spread the batter into the base of four 2 cup ramekins and bake in a medium hot water bath at 160c for 15 20 minutes. </li><li>Remove ramekins from water bath and let cool. </li><li>Chill for 2 4 hours or overnight. </li><li>Slice each cake into 3 slices. </li><li>Fill the ramekins with cream and freeze until solid. </li><li>Serve topped with whipped cream and raspberry sauce. </li></ul>
