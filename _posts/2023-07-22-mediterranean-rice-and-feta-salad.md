<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#rice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Rice</a> <a href="/tags#spring onion" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Spring Onion</a> <a href="/tags#tomato" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tomato</a> <a href="/tags#avocado" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Avocado</a> <a href="/tags#feta" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Feta</a> <a href="/tags#sprigs coriander" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sprigs Coriander</a> <a href="/tags#virgin oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Virgin Oil</a> <a href="/tags#salt ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Ground Pepper</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Brown Rice</li><li>1 Spring Onion, Sliced</li><li>1 Tomato, Sliced</li><li> 1/2 Avocado, Sliced</li><li>30G/1Oz Feta, Crumbled</li><li>2 Sprigs Coriander, Leaves Picked</li><li>1 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat oil in a wok until very hot. </li><li>Add fresh coriander and fry for about one minute. </li><li>Combine rice, onions, tomato, avocado, salt, and pepper. </li><li>Place salad into a bowl, mix gently. </li><li>Scatter with feta cheese and a sprig of coriander. </li></ul>
