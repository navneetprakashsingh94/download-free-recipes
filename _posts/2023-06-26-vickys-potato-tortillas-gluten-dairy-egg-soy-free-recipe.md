<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#floury potatoes Recipe Tip" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Floury Potatoes Recipe Tip</a> <a href="/tags#salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Large Floury Potatoes (Approximately 450G/1Lb) (See Recipe Tip)</li><li>Salt, To Taste</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Peel the potatoes and roughly grate them using a grater</li><li>Heat some oil in a pan over a medium heat and coat the bottom of the pan with the oil</li><li>Season the potatoes with salt and pepper</li><li>Add half the potatoes and fry until lightly golden and crispy</li><li>Remove to a plate and repeat with the remaining potatoes</li><li>Serve warm with butter and a green salad</li></ul>
