<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>350G/12Oz Penne Pasta</li><li>2 X 80G Packs Parma Ham, Snipped Into Small Pieces</li><li>250G/9Oz Small Brown Chestnut Mushrooms, Halved Or Quartered</li><li>200G/7Oz Full-Fat Creme Fraiche</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Parmesan, Grated</li><li>2 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Parsley</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper, To Taste</li><li>Green Salad</li><li>Crunchy Bread</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cook pasta in a large pan of boiling salted water for 8 10 mins, until tender. Drain and return to pan. </li><li>Meanwhile, cook sausages and mushrooms in a frying pan on medium heat for 4 5 mins, until golden brown and cooked through. Remove and set aside. </li><li>Return pan to heat. Add ham and mushrooms and cook for 3 mins, until heated through. Season to taste. Add creme fraiche and parmesan and stir. </li><li>Serve pasta topped with sausage mixture, parsley, salt and pepper. Serve with salad and crunchy bread. </li></ul>
