<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>115G/4Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>3 Unwaxed Lemons, Zest And Juice</li><li>350Ml/12Fl Oz Eau-De-Vie (Colourless Fruit Liqueur), 40-80% Proof, Or Vodka</li><li>250G/9Oz Plain Flour</li><li>250G/9Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 1/2 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs, Lightly Beaten</li><li>90G/3 1/4Oz Sultanas</li><li>90G/3 1/4Oz Raisins</li><li>90G/3 1/4Oz Dried Sour Cherries</li><li>115G/4Oz Dried Apricots, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Lemon, Zest Only</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Pre heat oven to 150 degrees c</li><li>Butter three loaf tins, line the bottoms with baking paper and set aside. </li><li>Combine all the dry ingredients in a large bowl. </li><li>Stir in the juice and liqueur and mix well, then stir in the eggs. </li><li>Blend the fruit in a separate bowl, drain, and chop. </li><li>Add to the dry ingredients and mix well. </li><li>Place half the fruit in each loaf tin, and add half the liqueur. </li><li>Top each loaf with jam, spreading evenly. </li><li>Carefully pour the remaining fruit in each loaf tin. </li><li>Bake the cakes at 160c for 1 3/4 hours, then reduce to 250f and bake for 3 more hours, or until done. </li><li>Allow the cakes to cool in their tins for 10 min, then turn out on to a wire rack to cool completely. </li></ul>
