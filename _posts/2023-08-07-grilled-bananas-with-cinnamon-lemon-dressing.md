<div>Delicious Grilled Bananas With Cinnamon Lemon Dressing using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 50 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#bananas thick diagonal" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bananas Thick Diagonal</a> <a href="/tags#rapeseed vegetable oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Rapeseed Vegetable Oil</a> <a href="/tags#lemon juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lemon Juice</a> <a href="/tags#ground cinnamon" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ground Cinnamon</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2-3 Large, Almost Ripe Bananas, Peeled And Thinly Sliced (3Mm/No More Than  1/8 In Thick) On A Sharp Diagonal</li><li>Light Rapeseed, Vegetable Or Sunflower Oil</li><li>1 Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>Ground Cinnamon (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Grill bananas on a hot grill until browned on both sides. </li><li>Preheat an outdoor grill for medium high heat. </li><li>In a small bowl, combine the oil, lemon juice, and cinnamon. </li><li>Place the bananas on the grill and brush with the sauce. Grill the bananas for about 4 minutes, turning once, until the skins are browned and blistered and the bananas have softened. </li><li>Serve warm or at room temperature. </li></ul>
