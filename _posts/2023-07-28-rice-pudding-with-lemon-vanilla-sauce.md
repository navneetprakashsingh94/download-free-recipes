<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>110G/4Oz Thai Jasmine Rice</li><li>450Ml/16Fl Oz Whole Milk</li><li>450Ml/16Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>75G/3Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Vanilla Pod, Split</li><li>Freshly Grated Nutmeg</li><li>25G/1Oz Butter, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>6 Tbsp Icing Sugar</li><li>250G/9Oz Raspberries</li><li>2 Tbsp Icing Sugar</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Water</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Make raspberry sauce first by placing the icing sugar and water and simmering over a low heat until dissolved. </li><li>When the sauce is warm add the raspberries and stir, simmer for 5 minutes, take off the heat and set aside. </li><li>Boil the milk, cream, castor sugar, sugar and vanilla seeds and nutmeg together until the sauce reduces by about half. </li><li>Bring the sauce back to a boil, season with salt. </li><li>Add rice and cook, stirring for 5 minutes. </li><li>Add raspberries, butter and simmer for a further 2 minutes, then turn into a large, buttered, ovenproof dish. </li><li>Bake for 20 minutes until the rice pudding is bubbling. </li><li>When the pudding has finished baking, let it cool for a few minutes before serving. </li></ul>
