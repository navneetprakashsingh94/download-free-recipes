<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Skinless Chicken Breasts</li><li>70G/2 1/2Oz Cornflakes, Crushed</li><li>1 Tsp Cajun Seasoning</li><li>1 Tsp Paprika</li><li>1 Tsp Dried Oregano</li><li>1 Tsp Lemon Salt (Optional, See Recipe Tip)</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>2 Slices Bacon, Or More, To Taste</li><li>2 Brioche Burger Buns</li><li>Light Mayonnaise</li><li>Iceberg Lettuce</li><li>Sliced Tomato</li><li>2 Tsp Pesto (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Mix crushed cornflakes and spices in a shallow pan and set aside</li><li></li><li>Divide chicken into 8 portions, add one portion of chicken into each bun, roll up and secure with a toothpick. </li><li></li><li>Dip each stuffed bun in beaten egg, then into the cornflakes mixture. </li><li></li><li>Heat a large pan to medium and lay sliced bacon on one side of pan. Turn pan when bacon has cooked, then cook burgers for approximately 7 minutes or until cooked through</li><li></li><li>Meanwhile, brush buns with mayonnaise and toast until golden brown</li><li></li><li>Spread buns with pesto</li><li></li><li>Top with lettuce, burgers, bacon, tomato and buns and serve. </li></ul>
