<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Ready-Made Mashed Potato</li><li>2 Spring Onions, Finely Sliced</li><li>Handful Fresh Coriander, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Tsp Chilli Powder</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Turmeric</li><li>1 Tsp Coarsely Ground, Toasted Cumin Seeds</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Garam Masala</li><li>Vegetable Oil, For Shallow Frying</li><li>3 Tbsp Plain Flour</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten</li><li>Salt</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Raw Cashew Nuts, Soaked In Water For 30 Minutes And Drained</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Golden Sultanas, Soaked In Water For 30 Minutes And Drained</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Fresh Coriander</li><li>1 Small Green Chilli, Roughly Chopped</li><li>75Ml/2 1/2Fl Oz Lemon Juice</li><li>4 Soft White Bread Or Brioche Rolls, Split</li><li>4 Tbsp Tamarind Chutney</li><li>1 Tbsp Mayonnaise</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Combine veg in a bowl with 2 tbsp of water, blending until combined. </li><li>Stir in half the chilli powder, the turmeric, cumin and garam masala and mix well. </li><li>Sift in flour and mix in again to combine. </li><li>Using a tablespoon measure, make into 4 patties. </li><li>Coat patties in flour then egg and fry in frying pan until golden brown. </li><li>Drain on kitchen paper and arrange on a serving plate. </li><li>Combine cashew, sultanas and salt in a bowl. </li><li>For the topping, whisk together the coriander, chilli and lemon juice, then spread the mixture over the rolls. </li><li>Combine the tamarind chutney and mayonnaise in a small bowl, then spread over the cut sides of each roll. </li><li>Serve burgers with chutney and sultana garnish. </li></ul>
