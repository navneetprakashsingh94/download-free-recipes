<div>Delicious Aubergine Dip using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#aubergines" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Aubergines</a> <a href="/tags#cloves taste" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cloves Taste</a> <a href="/tags#lemon juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lemon Juice</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp tahini" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Tahini</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp leaf parsley" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Leaf Parsley</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>3 Aubergines</li><li>1-3 Garlic Cloves, Crushed (To Taste)</li><li>1 Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>2-4 Tbsp Tahini</li><li>3 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>Black Pepper, To Taste</li><li>1 Tbsp Chopped Flat Leaf Parsley</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 190 deg celsius</li><li>Mash aubergines with fork or hand blender</li><li>Pour into a large bowl</li><li>Add cloves, lemon juice, tahini, salt and pepper</li><li>Mix. </li><li>Add oil and mix. </li><li>Transfer to serving dish. </li><li>Sprinkle with parsley</li><li></li></ul>
