<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Cheese And Fruit Brioche Toasts using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 30 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#egg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#tub cheese" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tub Cheese</a> <a href="/tags#brioche slices thick brioche loaf" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Brioche Slices Thick Brioche Loaf</a> <a href="/tags#cans halves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cans Halves</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp demerara sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Demerara Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#creme fraiche" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Creme Fraiche</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Egg</li><li>2 Tbsp Caster Sugar</li><li>250G/9Oz Tub Mascarpone Cheese</li><li>225G/8Oz Brioche Slices, 1Cm/ 1/2In Thick, Taken From A Shop-Bought Brioche Loaf</li><li>2 400G/14Oz Cans Apricot Halves, Drained</li><li>4 Tbsp Demerara Sugar</li><li>Creme Fraiche To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Whisk the egg yolk and caster sugar in a small bowl. Gently fold the egg white into the yolk mixture, then fold the cheese into the egg mixture until just combined. </li><li>Place the brioche slices on a lightly greased sprayed baking tray. Top each slice with a brioche slice. Brush with the egg white mixture and bake in a hot oven 200c / 400f for about 10 12 minutes or until the cheese has melted and the bread is crisp and golden. </li><li>Meanwhile, toss together the peach halves and demerara sugar. Cook in a medium non stick frying pan over a medium high heat, stirring, until the sugar has melted. </li><li>Place the brioche on 4 serving plates. Spread the peach mixture over the slices. Serve with creme fraiche. </li></ul>
