<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Baking Potato</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Medium Leek, Sliced</li><li>300Ml/ 1/2 Pint Milk</li><li>55G/2Oz Butter</li><li>3 Tbsp Plain Flour</li><li>110G/4 1/2Oz Cheddar Cheese, Grated</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Mustard</li><li>Pinch Ground Nutmeg</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place the potatoes in a large pot cover with cold water. Bring to a boil over medium high heat and cook until the potatoes are just tender, about 10 min. Drain and set aside. </li><li></li><li>Meanwhile, heat the oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add the leek and cook, stirring frequently, until tender, about 4 min. </li><li></li><li>Meanwhile, bring the milk to a boil in a small saucepan over medium heat. Reduce the heat to low and keep warm over low heat. </li><li></li><li>Melt the butter in a small saucepan over medium low heat. Add the flour and cook, stirring, for 1 min. Add the warm milk to the butter and whisk until the sauce is smooth. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, until thick, 5 to 6 min. Stir in the cheese, mustard and nutmeg, season with salt and pepper, and keep warm. </li><li></li><li>Coat the bottom of a 2 1/2 qt baking dish with the oil. Arrange half the potato cubes in the dish. Cover with half the leek and cheese sauce. Repeat layers, ending with cheese. </li><li></li><li>Place in the preheated oven on 375 f for 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and let stand 5 mins. Remove from the dish and serve hot. </li></ul>
