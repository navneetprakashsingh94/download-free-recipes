<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Butter, Melted, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Digestive Biscuits, Crushed</li><li>1 Vanilla Pod, Seeds Scraped Out</li><li>1 Lemon, Zest And Juice</li><li>200G/7Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Cornflour</li><li>850G/1Lb 4Oz Full-Fat Cream Cheese</li><li>375Ml/13Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>4 Large Free-Range Eggs</li><li>250G/9Oz Blueberries</li><li>250G/7Oz Blueberries</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 180 degrees celcius. </li><li>Make the base put biscuit crumbs into food processor, and process until coarsely chopped. Add butter and pulse just until combined. Press mixture into base of a lightly greased 22cm springform pan. </li><li>Mix vanilla seeds and lemon juice and brush over surface of base and sides of cake tin. </li><li>In a bowl mix sugar and cornflour, then whisk in cream cheese and cream until smooth. Whisk in eggs, and then pour mixture over biscuit base. Bake for 50 60 minutes or until set and slightly puffed in the center. Transfer to a wire rack to cool. Chill overnight. </li><li>To serve, sprinkle top with blueberries. Serve with a slice of bread and a slice of lemon to garnish. </li></ul>
