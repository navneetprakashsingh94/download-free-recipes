<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>250G/9Oz Plain Flour</li><li>Pinch Sea Salt</li><li>125G/4Oz Unsalted Butter, Diced, At Room Temperature</li><li>1 Tsp Cold Water</li><li>1 Medium Free-Range Egg</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li> 1/2 Tbsp Lemon Juice</li><li>65G/2 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li> 1/2 Tbsp Calvados</li><li>3-4 Cox'S Orange Pippin, Worcester, Russet Or Braeburn Apples, Peeled, Cores Removed, Each Cut Into 10 Segments</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>1 Medium Free-Range Egg</li><li>1-2 Tbsp Icing Sugar, For Dusting</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>To make dough sift together flour and salt in a bowl. Add butter and rub together with fingertips until coarse crumbs form. Add ice water and egg and mix until dough forms a ball. Put in a bowl and refrigerate for at least 15 minutes. </li><li>To make filling whip the butter, lemon juice, sugar and brandy in a mixing bowl until combined. Add apples and mix well to combine. </li><li>Preheat oven to 400 f. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface. Place on a nonstick baking sheet and place the apple mixture on the dough. Using a rolling pin, roll out the dough until it is 10 cm thick. Cut into 2cm squares and place squares on the pie tin. Brush the edges of the squares with the egg wash then bake for 25 30 minutes. Remove from oven, spread the cream on top and place in oven for 15 minutes. </li><li>Cool and serve sprinkled with icing sugar. </li></ul>
