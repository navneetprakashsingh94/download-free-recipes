<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>175G/6Oz Salmon Fillet</li><li>Sea Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>1 Tsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Tbsp Balsamic Vinegar</li><li>1 Tsp Honey</li><li>1-2 Sesame Seeds</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>Large Handful Of Spinach</li><li> 1/2 400G/14Oz Can Cherry Tomatoes</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Dried Chilli Flakes</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>To make dressing mix oil, vinegar, honey, sesame seeds, garlic powder, fresh ground pepper, 1/2 cup of the olive oil. </li><li></li><li>To assemble salad. Wash spinach and dry. </li><li></li><li>Slice cherry tomatoes</li><li></li><li>Heat grill pan to med heat</li><li></li><li>Spray with olive oil</li><li></li><li>Add spinach and stir fry until wilted</li><li></li><li>Plate lettuce</li><li></li><li>Arrange browned fish fillet over top</li><li></li><li>Drizzle with dressing</li></ul>
