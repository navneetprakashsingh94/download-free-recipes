<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#globe artichokes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Globe Artichokes</a> <a href="/tags#lemons" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lemons</a> <a href="/tags#pinch salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pinch Salt</a> <a href="/tags#plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#rashers bacon" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Rashers Bacon</a> <a href="/tags#egg yolks" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg Yolks</a> <a href="/tags#Dijon mustard" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dijon Mustard</a> <a href="/tags#rapeseed oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Rapeseed Oil</a> <a href="/tags#salt ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Ground Pepper</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>4 Globe Artichokes</li><li>2 Lemons, Halved</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>8 Rashers Smoked Streaky Bacon, Roughly Chopped</li><li>2 Free-Range Egg Yolks</li><li>1 Tsp Dijon Mustard</li><li>400Ml/14Fl Oz Rapeseed Oil</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Bring a large pot of water to the boil, add the lemon juice and about 2 tablespoons of salt, place the artichokes in the boiling water for a couple of minutes, remove the artichokes and plunge into a bowl of cold water. </li><li>Drain the artichokes and rinse with cold water. </li><li>Then place artichokes in a large pot. </li><li>Cook until tender, drain and add salt to taste. </li><li>In another large pot, cook the bacon until crispy. </li><li>Drain, reserving drippings in the pot. </li><li>Crumble bacon and set aside. </li><li>Put the egg yolks in a blender or food processor and add the dijon mustard and the reserved bacon drippings. </li><li>Blend well. </li><li>Add the rapeseed oil 1 tablespoon at a time, to form a mayonnaise like sauce. </li><li>Season to taste. </li><li>Stir in the salt and freshly ground pepper, taste and adjust the seasoning. </li><li>When ready to serve, place the artichokes on plates. </li><li>Top each with some mayonnaise and top with a fried artichoke half. </li></ul>
