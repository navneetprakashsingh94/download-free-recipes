<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Plain Flour</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, Cut Into Cubes</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>2 Tsp Caster Sugar</li><li>3-4 Tbsp Cold Water</li><li>250G/9Oz Raspberries</li><li>200G/7Oz Jam Sugar With Added Pectin</li><li> 1/2 Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>4 Free-Range Eggs, Plus 2 Yolks</li><li>115G/4Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Butter, Melted And Cooled</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Ground Almonds</li><li>1 Tsp Almond Extract</li><li>3 Tbsp Flaked Almonds</li><li>Icing Sugar, For Dusting</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the pastry mix the flour, butter, salt, sugar, and water together and shape into a dough. Leave in a fridge for 10 15 mins. </li><li>Roll out the dough on a floured surface until you have a 16 centimeter square. Place into a 20cm tart tin. Trim the edges and fold over the overhanging pastry and crimp the edges. Poke holes all over with a fork. Refrigerate for 30 mins. </li><li>For the raspberry filling place the raspberries, jam, lemon juice, and 1 tbsp water in a saucepan and bring to the boil. Simmer for 5 mins. Drain the raspberries. Return to the pan, bring back to the boil, then simmer until the raspberries have softened and the mixture thickens, 5 mins. Remove from the heat and allow to cool completely. </li><li>For the almond filling mix the yolks and 1 cup sugar in a bowl until light and thick. Beat in the butter, ground almonds and almond extract. </li><li>Fill the tart crust with the almond filling. Sprinkle with the grated almonds and superfine sugar, and bake in a pre heated 350f oven for 30 35 mins. </li></ul>
