<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1Kg/2Lb 4Oz Lean Beef Mince</li><li>2 Carrots, Chopped</li><li>1 Leek, Trimmed, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Sprig Fresh Thyme, Leaves Only</li><li>2 Free-Range Egg Whites</li><li>2 Litres/3 1/2 Pints Fresh Beef Stock</li><li>250G/9Oz Type '00' Pasta Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>4 Free-Range Egg Yolks</li><li>1 Whole Free-Range Egg Mixed With 2 Tsp Cold Water, Plus 1 Free-Range Egg Beaten</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Ready-Made Coarse Farmhouse Pate</li><li>1 Carrot, Peeled But Left Whole</li><li>1 Courgette</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>In a large bowl, mix the ground beef, carrots, leek, thyme and egg whites. Roll into 8 12 inch rolls. Heat 1/4 of the stock until boiling and cook the sausages for 12 15 minutes or until browned. Drain. </li><li>Meanwhile, combine the flour, egg yolks and 1/4 of the water in a large bowl and whisk to combine. Gradually whisk in the remaining stock and the egg yolk water. </li><li>Divide the mixture into 8 portions and roll each piece into a flattened circle. Cook the sausages on a heated oiled griddle for 3 4 minutes per side, until browned and cooked through. Remove from the griddle and keep warm. </li><li>To serve, place a sausage around each flattened sausage and top with a piece of pate. Garnish with julienned vegetables. </li></ul>
