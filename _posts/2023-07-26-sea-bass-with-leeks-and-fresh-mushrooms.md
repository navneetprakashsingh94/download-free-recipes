<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li> 1/2 Sea Bass Fillet, Skin Scored</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li> 1/2 Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>25G/1Oz Butter</li><li>1 Leek, Sliced Into Rounds</li><li>75Ml/2 1/2Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Finely Chopped</li><li>85G/3Oz Wild Mushrooms, Such As Oyster, Chanterelles, Porcini Etc, Cleaned And Chopped</li><li>1 Vine Tomato, Chopped</li><li> 1/2 Lemon, Juice Only</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Make the sauce first. </li><li>Heat oil in a saucepan and saute the leeks, garlic, and mushrooms and add the lemon juice, lemon juice, and salt pepper to taste, and keep on a low flame for 5 minutes, stirring regularly. </li><li>Pour in the cream and cook another 2 3 minutes over low flame. </li><li>Season the sea bass skin side down, add the sauce, and put in a preheated oven for 5 8 minutes. </li><li>Remove the fish from the oven, remove the skin, and garnish with the leeks. </li></ul>
