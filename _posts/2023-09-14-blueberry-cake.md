<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Butter, Softened</li><li>225G/8Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>250G/9Oz Soured Cream</li><li>1 Unwaxed Lemon, Zest And Juice</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Blueberries</li><li>50G/2Oz Butter, Softened</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Icing Sugar, Sieved, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>200G/7Oz Cream Cheese</li><li>1-2 Tbsp Lemon Juice</li><li>50G/2Oz Blueberries</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Beat butter and sugar together until fluffy and light. </li><li>Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each. </li><li>Beat in cream and lemon zest. </li><li>Combine flour, baking powder and blueberries in a separate bowl. </li><li>Gradually add dry ingredients to the egg mixture, mixing thoroughly. </li><li>Spoon into a greased 8 spring form cake pan and bake at 325 degrees for about 50 minutes. </li><li>When cake is done, immediately turn out of pan and turn onto a rack to cool. </li><li>Combine all the ingredients and frost the cake. </li><li>Arrange the blueberries on top as a decoration. </li></ul>
