<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>4 Tsp Sugar</li><li>2 Tsp Dried Yeast</li><li>450Ml/16Fl Oz Brown Ale, At Room Temperature</li><li>520G/1Lb 2 1/2Oz Strong White Bread Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>320G/11Oz Wholemeal Flour</li><li>200G/7Oz Cheddar, Grated</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Parmesan (Or Similar Vegetarian Hard Cheese), Grated</li><li>50G/2Oz Powdered Milk</li><li>1 1/2Tsp Salt</li><li>1 1/2Tsp Mustard Powder</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs, Beaten</li><li>2 Tsp Fennel Seeds</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, White Only, For Glazing</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>The night before, dissolve the yeast in warm beer and set aside. </li><li>Combine the flours, cheeses, lowfat milk, salt, and mustard. </li><li>Add in the yeast mix, the eggs and fennel seeds and knead for 10 min. </li><li>Shape into a round ball and place in a greased loaf pan. </li><li>Cover with plastic wrap and leave to rise for 1 1/2 hrs. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 425f. </li><li>Brush the top with the egg mix and bake for 35 min, till the loaves are deep golden and a skewer inserted in the center comes out clean. </li></ul>
