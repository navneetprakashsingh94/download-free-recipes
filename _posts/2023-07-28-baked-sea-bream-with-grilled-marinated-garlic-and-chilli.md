<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 X 450G/1Lb Sea Bream, Scaled And Gutted (Or 2 X 160G/5 1/2Oz Sea Bream Fillets)</li><li>6 Cloves Of Roasted Garlic, Skin Left On (Simply Roast Garlic Cloves, Wrapped In Foil, In A Hot Oven With A Drizzle Of Olive Oil And Sea Salt For About Ten Minutes)</li><li>1 Small Dried Chilli (Or To Taste)</li><li>A Few Sprigs Fresh Rosemary</li><li>Pinch Of Sea Salt</li><li>2 Tbsp Dry White Wine</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Good-Quality, Fruity, Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>Chopped Parsley</li><li>1 Lemon, Quartered</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 200c/375 f/gas mark 4. Wrap garlic skin and the sea bream in a packet of foil and season with salt. </li><li>Put the garlic in the oven and roast for 10 12 mins, or until the flesh of the fish is completely opaque. </li><li>Cool the garlic in the fridge and serve as an accompaniment to grilled steaks, as an hors d oeuvre. </li><li>In a saucepan, heat the wine until the wine is almost evaporated. Add the marinated garlic with a splash of olive oil. Season, to taste, with salt. </li><li>Arrange the sea bream on a large platter and drizzle the marinade over the fish. </li><li>Decorate with a small sprig of sprigs of parsley and serve. </li></ul>
