<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>3 Ancho Chillies, Stem And Seeds Removed</li><li>2 Guajillo Chillies, Stem And Seeds Removed</li><li>2 Plum Tomatoes, Roughly Chopped</li><li> 1/2 Onion, Chopped</li><li>4 Garlic Cloves, Peeled</li><li>1 Tbsp Sea Salt Flakes</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cumin</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Dried Oregano</li><li>5 Black Peppercorns</li><li>2 Cloves</li><li> 1/2 Leg Of Lamb On The Bone, Approx. 1.2Kg/2Lb 10Oz, At Room Temperature</li><li>500Ml/18Fl Oz Lager</li><li>15 Corn Tortillas, Warmed</li><li>Small Bunch Fresh Coriander, Chopped</li><li>1 Red Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Limes, Cut Into Wedges</li><li>Spicy Salsa Of Your Choice</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Combine drained tomatoes, onion, 3 cloves, 1tsp salt, tsp cumin, cinnamon, oregano, peppercorns, and cloves in a large pot on medium heat. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 45 min, or until tender. Remove from heat and let cool completely. </li><li>Using your fingers, loosen lamb skin from lamb. Trim off any visible fat. Place lamb skin side down and using a sharp knife, slice into chunks. Place into slow cooker. Add beer and cover. Cook on low until lamb is tender, 2 1/2 to 3 hours. </li><li>Heat tortillas in a dry pan on high heat. When warm remove lamb from slow cooker and place 1 tortilla on each plate. Layer with coriander, onion and lamb. Garnish with limes and salsa. </li></ul>
