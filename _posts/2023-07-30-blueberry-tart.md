<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Blueberry Tart using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 50 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#biscuits" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Biscuits</a> <a href="/tags#blueberries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Blueberries</a> <a href="/tags#cream cheese" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cream Cheese</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#vanilla extract" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Vanilla Extract</a> <a href="/tags#free-range eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Free-Range Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sugar</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Butter, Melted, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>175G/6Oz Digestive Biscuits</li><li>175G/6Oz Blueberries, Plus Extra For Decorating</li><li>450G/1Lb Cream Cheese</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>4 Free-Range Eggs, Lightly Beaten</li><li>Icing Sugar, To Dust</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 200 c. </li><li>Melt the butter or margarine and brush the base of a 20cm square dish with it. </li><li>Cut the biscuits into large pieces and place them in the dish. </li><li>Brush them with the butter or margarine and press them into the base of the dish. </li><li>Top them with the blueberries. </li><li>Set aside. </li><li>In a mixer or a food processor, beat the cream cheese with the sugar and vanilla extract until well blended. </li><li>Add the eggs and continue beating until the mixture is smooth and homogeneous. </li><li>Spread the mixture over the blueberries and bake for 20 minutes. </li><li>Sprinkle with extra sugar, then return to the oven for a further 5 minutes. </li><li>Serve warm or cold with some extra blueberries, or drizzled with a thick sauce. </li></ul>
