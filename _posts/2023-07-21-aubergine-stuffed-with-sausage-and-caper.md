<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Aubergines</li><li>Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li> 1/2 Onion, Diced</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Diced</li><li>1-2 Celery Sticks, Diced</li><li> 1/2 Fennel Bulb, Diced</li><li>225G/8Oz Spicy Luganega Pork Sausage, Skinned And Crumbled</li><li>30G/1Oz White Breadcrumbs</li><li>3 Tbsp Pine Nuts</li><li>20G/ 3/4Oz Capers</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg</li><li>1 Tbsp Dried Oregano</li><li>3 Tbsp Grated Pecorino</li><li>3 Tbsp Currants</li><li>2 Tbsp Chopped Sun-Dried Tomatoes</li><li>2 Tbsp Chopped Oregano</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 400 degrees f. Cut each aubergine in half lengthwise and scoop out the flesh. </li><li>Reserve the flesh. </li><li>Heat the 1 tbsp olive oil in a medium skillet over medium heat. </li><li>Add the onion, garlic, celery, fennel and stuffed eggplant. </li><li>Cook, stirring often, until the eggplant is soft, about 12 minutes. </li><li>Drain the aubergine, pressing out the excess liquid. </li><li>Finely chop the eggplant flesh and add to the sausage, breadcrumbs, pine nuts, capers, egg, oregano, pecorino, currants, tomatoes and oregano. </li><li>Season with salt and pepper and mix well. </li><li>Form the mixture into 12 balls and set them on a lightly oiled baking sheet. </li><li>Cover the aubergine balls with plastic wrap and freeze until firm, at least 4 hours. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 350 degrees f. When ready to cook, remove the aubergine shells from the freezer and brush with extra virgin oil. </li><li>Bake until the filling is puffed and the crust is golden, about 30 minutes. </li><li>Place the aubergine shells on a plate, drizzle with extra virgin oil and grate additional pecorino over them. </li><li>Serve immediately. </li></ul>
