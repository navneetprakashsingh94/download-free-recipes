<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Extra-Large Hand Dived Scallops, Prepared By Your Fishmonger</li><li>1 Clean Scallop Shell, Opened</li><li>20G/ 3/4Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>20G/ 3/4Oz Apple Juice</li><li>Flaked Sea Salt</li><li>200G/7Oz Crown Prince Squash, Peeled And Deseeded, Half Julienned, And Half Cut Into Star Shapes</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Elderflower Vinegar</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Roasted And Dried Yeast</li><li>10G/1/3Oz Smoked Scallop Roe (Plus More For Grating)</li><li>1 Tsp Dried Kelp</li><li>1 Tsp Dried Trompete</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Roasted Chicory Root</li><li>1 Tbsp Dried Tomatoes</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Dried Lemon Verbena</li><li>1 Tsp Dried Chive Flowers</li><li>2 Tsp Dried Ceps</li><li>250Ml/9Fl Oz Boiling Water</li><li>30G/1Oz Fermented Green Strawberries</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place oxtail, butter and apple juice in a small pan and place over a medium high heat. </li><li>When butter is melted, add salt and squash. </li><li>Cook for a minute or two, then add vinegar and season with salt. </li><li>Remove from heat. </li><li>Add a few spoonfuls of water to pan and return to heat. </li><li>Add roe, herbs and yeast, stirring constantly for a few minutes. </li><li>Stir in boiling water to cover. </li><li>Simmer until squash is tender when pierced with a knife, 25 to 30 minutes. </li><li>Drain, peel and remove seeds. </li><li>Place in the center of a deep serving dish, add a few pieces of prince squash and spoon over the sauce. </li><li>Garnish with fresh strawberries. </li><li>This is best served with steamed new potatoes and a green salad. </li></ul>
