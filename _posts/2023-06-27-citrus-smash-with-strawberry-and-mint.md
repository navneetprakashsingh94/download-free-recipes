<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Parts Gin</li><li>2 Parts Sweet Red Vermouth</li><li>2 Parts Orange Liqueur</li><li>1 Part Campari</li><li>2 Parts Pink Grapefruit Juice</li><li>10 Parts Cloudy Lemonade</li><li>Strawberries, Quartered</li><li>Mint Sprigs</li><li>1 Grapefruit, Peel Only</li><li>Ice</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>In a 2 cup container or pitcher, mix gin, vermouth, orange liqueur, campari, grapefruit juice, milk and strawberries with an ice cube/ spoon. </li><li>The mixture will be very cold. </li><li>. </li><li>Place in the refrigerator to cool. </li><li>To serve, squeeze a strawberry and a mint leaf over a glass and drop it into a tall glass. </li><li>Add 3 grapefruit slices and top with ice cubes. </li></ul>
