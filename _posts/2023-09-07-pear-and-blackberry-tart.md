<a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#demerara sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Demerara Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#pears" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pears</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#blackberries blackberries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Blackberries Blackberries</a> <a href="/tags#egg yolks" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg Yolks</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#oz milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Milk</a> <a href="/tags#oz cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Cream</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>110G/4Oz Butter, Cubed, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>180G/6 1/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>110G/4Oz Demerara Sugar</li><li>5 Pears, Peeled And Left Whole</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Blackberries (Or Frozen Blackberries)</li><li>4 Free-Range Egg Yolks</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>150Ml/5 1/2Fl Oz Milk</li><li>150Ml/5 1/2Fl Oz Double Cream</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Pre heat oven to 180 degrees celcius</li><li>Cut butter into flour and demerara sugar, mix well</li><li>Press into tart dish</li><li>Bake for 20mins. </li><li>Cook the pears, cut side down, in a hot pan for 5 7 mins until lightly browned</li><li>Add blackberries, cook for 2 more mins. </li><li>To make the custard combine the egg yolks, sugar and milk in a bowl</li><li>Add the cream, stir well and spoon over the pears, cook for 4 5 more mins until just set</li><li>Remove from oven, put the pears on top and finish cooking in the oven for 15 mins</li><li>Cool and serve chilled or at room temperature with blackberries as a sauce. </li></ul>
