<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Large Beetroot</li><li>1 Garlic Bulb</li><li>2 Sprigs Fresh Thyme</li><li>150G/5Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>300G/11Oz Shop-Bought Puff Pastry</li><li>50G/2Oz Butter</li><li>1 Red Onion, Finely Sliced</li><li>50M/2Fl Oz Port</li><li>25Ml/1Fl Oz Red Wine Vinegar</li><li>25Ml/1Fl Oz Sherry Vinegar</li><li>Large Pinch Salt</li><li>50G/2Oz Pine Nuts, Toasted In A Dry Frying Pan</li><li>150G/5Oz Fresh Goats' Curd</li><li>25Ml/1Fl Oz Sherry Vinegar</li><li>110Ml/4Fl Oz Olive Oil</li><li>1 Bunch Fresh Tarragon, Leaves Only</li><li>1 Bunch Fresh Basil, Leaves Only</li><li>1 Bunch Fresh Coriander, Leaves Only</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cook the beetroot on a baking sheet with the thyme sprigs for about 60 minutes. </li><li>Peel the beetroot and fennel and slice thinly. </li><li>Heat the butter and saute the onion. </li><li>Add the wine vinegar and sherry vinegar and season with salt and pepper. </li><li>Cook for 15 minutes over a low heat and then set aside to cool. </li><li>Prepare the tarragon, thyme and coriander. </li><li>Mix the herb infused oil, vinegar and oil together. </li><li>Arrange the beetroot in a baking dish. </li><li>Pour over the vinegar mixture, cover with the fennel and cover with the pine nuts. </li><li>Spread over the cream, sprinkle the tarragon, basil and coriander over the cream and bake in a moderate oven for 35 minutes or until crisp. </li></ul>
