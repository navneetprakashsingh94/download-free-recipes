<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Vegetable Or Sunflower Oil</li><li>1 Tsp Cumin Seeds</li><li>1 Onion, Thinly Sliced</li><li>2 X 400G Tins Mixed Beans In Water, Drained And Rinsed</li><li> 1/2-1 Tsp Dried Chilli Flakes</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cumin</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Salt</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Long-Grain Rice</li><li>900Ml/1 1/2Pints Boiling Water</li><li>Handful Fresh Soft Herbs (Such As Coriander, Parsley Or Mint), Leaves Finely Chopped And A Few Sprigs Saved For Garnish</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. </li><li>Preheat oven to 170 degc/160 deg c fan/gas 6. </li><li>2. </li><li>Heat oil in heavy based pan, add in the cumin seeds, saute/fry for a few seconds till the seeds begin to pop. </li><li>Add in the onion, beans, salt, chilli flakes and ground cumin. </li><li>Simmer for 5 min stirring constantly. </li><li>3. </li><li>Place the rice in a heatproof bowl, pour in boiling water and stir to submerge the rice. </li><li>Cook, covered, till the rice is tender but still hard, about 20 min. </li><li>Drain off the water and return the rice to the pot. </li><li>Stir in the herbs. </li><li>4. </li><li>Reheat the beans and serve on individual plates with the rice. </li><li>The bean rice recipe follows afghan adobo recipe a thai dish and a vegetable stuffed eggplant. </li><li>Aaron kright</li></ul>
