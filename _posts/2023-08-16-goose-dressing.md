<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>50G/2Oz Butter</li><li>1 Red Onion, Sliced</li><li>6 Juniper Berries</li><li>200G/7Oz Leftover Goose, Torn Into Pieces</li><li>200G/7Oz Leftover Ham, Torn Into Pieces</li><li>200G/7Oz Leftover Roast Pumpkin, Cut Into Chunks</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Leftover Stuffing</li><li>8 Leftover Roast Potatoes, Cut Into Chunks</li><li>5 Rashers Leftover Bacon, Chopped Into Large Pieces</li><li>6-8 Raw Brussels Sprouts, Halved</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Put butter in frying pan, over medium heat. </li><li>Saute the onion and juniper berries until lightly golden. </li><li>Add all the remaining ingredients to the pan. </li><li>Pour in 2 quarts of water. </li><li>Bring to a boil, then turn heat to low and simmer for approximately 25 minutes, or until cooked through. </li><li>Adjust seasoning, if needed, and keep warm until ready to serve. </li></ul>
