<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>750G/1Lb 10Oz Bramley Apples, Peeled, Cored, Quartered And Sliced</li><li>1 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li> 1/2 Unwaxed Lemon, Zest Only</li><li>2 Tsp Lemon Juice</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Golden Caster Sugar</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Raisins</li><li>95G/3 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz White Breadcrumbs</li><li>6 Large Sheets Filo Pastry</li><li>1 Tbsp Icing Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 180c and lightly grease a cookie sheet. </li><li>Combine the apples, cinnamon, lemon zest and juice, sugar, raisins, butter and breadcrumbs. </li><li>Place a sheet of pastry on a floured surface and roll out to a 1 thick round. </li><li>Cut the pastry into two equal rectangular pieces and place a heaped heaped spoonful of the apple filling in the centre. </li><li>Fold the pastry edges over the filling, then roll it out again to make a long roll. </li><li>Repeat until the pastry is used up. </li><li>Place on the prepared sheet and using a fork, make decorative cut shapes all over the top. </li><li>Bake for 25 30 minutes until lightly browned and cooked through. </li><li>Cool for a few minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely. </li><li>Sprinkle with icing sugar. </li></ul>
