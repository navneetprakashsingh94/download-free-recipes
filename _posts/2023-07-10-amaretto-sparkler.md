<div>Delicious Amaretto Sparkler using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 10 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#lemon juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lemon Juice</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp cordial" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Cordial</a> <a href="/tags#oz apple juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Apple Juice</a> <a href="/tags#water" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Water</a> <a href="/tags#mint sprig" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Mint Sprig</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li> 1/2 Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>1 Tbsp Elderflower Cordial</li><li>125Ml/4Fl Oz Apple Juice</li><li>Sparkling Water, To Taste (Optional)</li><li>Mint Sprig, To Garnish</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Combine lemon juice, cordial, apple juice, and water in small pitcher. </li><li>Refrigerate 30 minutes. </li><li>Strain mixture into ice filled rocks glass. </li><li>Garnish with mint sprig. </li></ul>
