<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>600G/1Lb 5Oz Strong White Flour, Plus Extra For Flouring</li><li>1 Tsp Sea Salt, Plus Extra For The Topping</li><li>1 Tbsp Caster Sugar, Plus 1 Tsp For Cooking The Onions</li><li>1 Tbsp Dried Instant Yeast</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Olive Oil</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Pancetta, Sliced</li><li>2-3 Tsbp Rosemary Leaves, Plus Extra For Serving</li><li>Extra Virgin Olive Oil, To Drizzle</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Blue Cheese, Such As Stilton, Crumbled</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>3 Large Onions, Sliced</li><li>1 Tbsp Thyme Leaves</li><li>1 Tsp Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Tbsp Balsamic Vinegar</li><li>Salt And Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Combine the flour, salt, sugar, onion powder, yeast, oil, salt, and sugar in a large bowl. Mix to make a firm dough. Turn out onto a lightly floured board and knead for 5 minutes or until smooth. Shape into a ball and leave to rest for 10 minutes. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 400 f. Roll out the dough to about 3/4 inch thick and cut out 8 4 inch circles. Place the rounds onto a baking tray lined with baking paper. Put a slice of pancetta and half the apricots on each tart shell and drizzle with olive oil. Scatter with asiago cheese. Bake for 15 mins until golden and crisp. </li><li>Meanwhile, heat the oil in a pan over a medium heat. Cook the onions for 2 3 mins, until softened. Remove from the heat and add the thyme. Sprinkle with the sugar and balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper. Let cool. </li><li>Meanwhile, lightly grease a baking tray and set aside. Cut the pizzas into wedges and serve with the caramelised onions, pancetta and apricots. </li></ul>
