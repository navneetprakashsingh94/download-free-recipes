<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>240G/8 1/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Bicarbonate Of Soda</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Butter, Melted</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Natural Skyr, Greek Yoghurt Or Any Other Natural Yoghurt</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs, Lightly Beaten</li><li>2 Tbsp Milk</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Honey</li><li>1 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Blueberries</li><li>2 Tbsp Granulated Or Demerara Sugar, For Sprinkling</li><li>Salt</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 180c/gas 4. </li><li>Sieve the flour, baking powder and soda into a large mixing bowl. </li><li>In a separate bowl, combine the caster sugar, butter, skyr yoghurt, eggs, milk, honey and vanilla extract. </li><li>Mix this together until light and fluffy. </li><li>With a spoon, stir in the dry ingredients and mix until it is just combined. </li><li>Gently fold in the blueberries. </li><li>Spoon into a baking tray lined with clingfilm and top with the demerara sugar. </li><li>Bake for 45 50 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. </li><li>Remove from the oven and leave to cool slightly before removing from the tray. </li></ul>
