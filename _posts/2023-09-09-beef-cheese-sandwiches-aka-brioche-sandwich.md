<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Deboned Rib Steak, Cut Into Chunks</li><li>200G/7Oz Deboned Short Rib Steak, Cut Into Chunks</li><li>2 Tsp Vegetable Oil, For Frying</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>4 Tbsp Mayonnaise</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Garlic Powder</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Onion Powder</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Smoked Paprika</li><li>4 Slices Smoked Steaky Bacon</li><li>1 Tsp Soft Brown Sugar</li><li>2 Tsp Maple Syrup</li><li>1 Tsp Treacle</li><li>2 Slices Mild Cheddar</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Blue Cheese, Grated</li><li>2 Seeded Brioche Buns, Split And Toasted</li><li>2 Dill Pickles, Sliced Lengthways</li><li> 1/2 Small Red Onion, Sliced</li><li>French Fries, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat grill to medium high and season the steak chunks with oil and seasonings</li><li></li><li>Mix the mayo, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, brown sugar, maple syrup and treacle in a large mixing bowl</li><li>Add the meat chunks and toss to coat. Cover and place the meat on the grill and cook for about 8 minutes for rare or 5 minutes for medium</li><li></li><li>Take the meat out of the grill and set aside. </li><li></li><li>Place the bacon in a saute pan and lightly brown, turning to brown all sides. </li><li></li><li>Pour off excess grease and transfer the bacon onto paper towels to drain. </li><li></li><li>Add the brioche buns on the grill until toasted</li><li></li><li>For the cheeseburger sandwich</li><li>Remove the beef from the grill and slice across the grain into thin strips. Set aside. </li><li></li><li>Place a large saute pan with 3 tablespoons of oil over medium heat. Top the beef strips with the maple syrup, treacle and the cheese. Cook until the cheese is melted. </li><li></li><li>Place the beef strips on the buns with the sauce and then the bacon, pickles, onions and fries. Serve. </li></ul>
