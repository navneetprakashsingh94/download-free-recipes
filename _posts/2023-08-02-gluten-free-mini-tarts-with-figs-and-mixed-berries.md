<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Unsalted Butter, Melted</li><li>200G/7Oz Gingernut Biscuits (About 20 Biscuits)</li><li>450G/1Lb Full-Fat Cream Cheese</li><li>250G/9Oz Ricotta</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Double Cream</li><li>3 Medium Free-Range Eggs</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>2 1/2 Tbsp Cornflour</li><li>1 Vanilla Pod, Seeds Scraped Out</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Fresh Blueberries</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Fresh Blackberries</li><li>2 Figs, Each Cut Into 6 Evenly-Sized Wedges</li><li>2 Tsp Icing Sugar, Sifted</li><li>Handful Fresh Mint Leaves (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 180c. </li><li>Combine biscuit crumbs and butter and press into the bottom of a muffin tin. </li><li>Chill for 20 minutes. </li><li>In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese, ricotta and cream until light. </li><li>Beat in the eggs and sugar until very smooth. </li><li>Stir in the vanilla seeds. </li><li>Divide mixture among the prepared tin. </li><li>Bake for 20 25 minutes. </li><li>Turn out onto a wire rack and leave to cool completely. </li><li>In a medium bowl, combine the berries and figs. </li><li>Sprinkle with icing sugar and toss to coat. </li><li>Spoon the berries onto the cheesecakes and decorate with mint leaves. </li></ul>
