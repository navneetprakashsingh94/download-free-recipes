<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#eating apples" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Eating Apples</a> <a href="/tags#orange juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Orange Juice</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank"></a> <a href="/tags#sultanas" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sultanas</a> <a href="/tags#sheets filo pastry" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sheets Filo Pastry</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp breadcrumbs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Breadcrumbs</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>680G/1 1/2Lbs Eating Apples, Peeled, Cored And Chopped</li><li> 1/2 Orange, Juice And Zest Only</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>Freshly Grated Nutmeg, To Taste</li><li>55G/2Oz Sultanas</li><li>6-8 Sheets Ready-Made Filo Pastry</li><li>55G/2Oz Butter, Melted And Cooled, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>2 Tbsp Dried Breadcrumbs</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Pre heat the oven to 180c. </li><li>Meanwhile, place the apples and orange juice in a large saucepan and heat until the juice has reduced slightly. Add the sugar and continue cooking for 2 3 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add the sultanas and remove from the heat. </li><li>To assemble the clafoutis, brush a large baking tray with melted butter and top with half of the filo pastry. </li><li>Fill with apple mixture and sprinkle with breadcrumbs. </li><li>Repeat this process until all the apple and sultana mixture is used. </li><li>Bake for 35 minutes until golden brown. </li></ul>
