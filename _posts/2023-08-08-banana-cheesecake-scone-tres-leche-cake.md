<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>180Ml/6Fl Oz Vegetable Oil, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>250G/9Oz Dark Brown Sugar</li><li>1 1/2 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>3 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>425G/15Oz Very Ripe Bananas, Peeled (About 3 Large Or 4 Small Bananas)</li><li>120Ml/4Fl Oz Natural Yoghurt</li><li>1 1/4 Tsp Bicarbonate Of Soda</li><li>1 1/4 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li> 3/4 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>1 1/2 Tsp Salt</li><li>280G/10Oz White Spelt Flour</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Raisins</li><li>1 Tbsp Caster Sugar</li><li>200G/7Oz Full-Fat Cream Cheese</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Icing Sugar, Sifted</li><li>1 Tsp Cornflour</li><li>75Ml/2 1/2Fl Oz (5 Tbsp) Double Cream</li><li>1 Banana, Sliced In Half</li><li>2 Tbsp Light Brown Sugar</li><li>Dark Chocolate, For Grating</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 170c degrees</li><li>Grease and line a 23cm round tin with baking paper. </li><li>Beat the oil, brown sugar, vanilla and eggs together until pale and creamy, then fold in the mashed bananas, then fold in the yoghurt. </li><li>Sift in the bicarbonate soda, baking powder, cinnamon and salt. </li><li>Fold in the flour and raisins, until just combined. </li><li>Pour the mixture into the prepared tin, place into the oven and bake for 40 45 minutes or until a skewer comes out clean. </li><li>Leave to cool slightly in the tin, then transfer to a wire rack and leave to cool completely. </li><li>To make the cream cheese topping, beat together the cream cheese and caster sugar until pale and creamy. </li><li>Fold in the vanilla. </li><li>To make the scone topping, whip the double cream until almost stiff. </li><li>Fold in the whipped cream and banana half. </li><li>Stack the scone slices on top of each other and decorate with the topping. </li><li>Transfer to a serving plate and set aside. </li><li>Melt the dark chocolate, drizzle over the scone and serve. </li></ul>
