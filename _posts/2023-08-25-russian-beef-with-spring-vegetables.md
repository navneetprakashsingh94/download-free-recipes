<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1Kg/2Lb 4Oz Beef Shin, On The Bone</li><li>3 Carrots, Roughly Chopped</li><li>3 Leeks, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Onion, Roughly Chopped</li><li>2 Tbsp Parsley Stalks</li><li>600Ml/20Fl Oz Vegetable Stock</li><li>Dash Oil</li><li>2 X 200G/7Oz Beef Fillets</li><li>1 Small Potato, Finely Chopped</li><li> 1/4 Swede, Peeled And Finely Chopped</li><li>3 Baby Leeks, Thinly Sliced</li><li>3 Baby Turnips, Finely Chopped</li><li>3 Baby Carrots, Trimmed</li><li>2 Small Round Shallots, Quartered</li><li>1 Small Spring Cabbage Heart, Shredded</li><li>1 Celery Heart And Leaves, Heart Chopped</li><li>Few Parsley Leaves</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Mature Hard Caerphilly Cheese, Broken Into Shards</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper, To Taste</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Put the beef bone, carrots, leeks, onion and parsley in a pan with the stock and bring to the boil. </li><li>Remove from the heat and let the beef bones cool in the stock. </li><li>In the meantime, heat the oil and saute the beef fillets. </li><li>When the beef is tender and cooked through, transfer it to a large plate and cut it into small cubes. </li><li>Add the potato, swede, baby leeks, baby turnips, baby carrots, and shallots to the stock. </li><li>Add the cabbage, heart leaves and parsley and simmer for 20 minutes. </li><li>Season to taste with salt and pepper. </li><li>Meanwhile, steam the cauliflower in a boiling water for 5 minutes. </li><li>Drain and cool under cold running water. </li><li>Drain the cauliflower and mash well. </li><li>Mound the beef cubes and vegetables onto serving plates and add the cheese shards. </li><li>Serve. </li></ul>
