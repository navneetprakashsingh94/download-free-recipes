<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#cooking apples" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cooking Apples</a> <a href="/tags#lemon" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lemon</a> <a href="/tags#water" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Water</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>225G/8Oz Cooking Apples, Peeled, Cored And Chopped</li><li> 1/2 Lemon, Zest Only</li><li>2 Tbsp Water</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>1 Tsp Caster Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Prepare the apples by cutting them in half, and scooping out the inner flesh and discard them. </li><li>Place the pips in a bowl and mash them with a fork. </li><li>Add the lemon juice, water, butter, sugar, and salt to taste and combine well. </li><li>Add additional sugar to the cores, if you like. </li><li>Put the apples in a baking dish, pour over the liquid, and bake in a moderate oven 450f. </li><li>For 1 hour. </li><li>If you prefer softer apples, bake uncovered for further 15 minutes. </li><li>If not, cook for further 5 minutes and serve warm. </li></ul>
