<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Chocolate Banana Muffins With No Chocolate using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 30 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#banana weight" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Banana Weight</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Milk</a> <a href="/tags#butter spread" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter Spread</a> <a href="/tags#plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#bicarbonate soda" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bicarbonate Soda</a> <a href="/tags#tsp baking powder" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Baking Powder</a> <a href="/tags#egg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg</a> <a href="/tags#chocolate chips" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chocolate Chips</a> <a href="/tags#chocolate" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chocolate</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Ripe Banana (Peeled Weight 100G/4Oz)</li><li>1 Tbsp Milk</li><li>50G/2Oz Soft Butter Or Baking Spread</li><li>75G/3Oz Plain Flour</li><li>75G/3Oz Caster Sugar</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Bicarbonate Of Soda</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg</li><li>50G/2Oz Dark Chocolate Chips</li><li>50G/2Oz Dark Chocolate</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 180c. </li><li>Combine banana, milk, and butter in a bowl. </li><li>In another bowl, combine flour, sugar, bicarbonate soda, and baking powder. </li><li>Stir this mixture into banana mixture. </li><li>Add egg, chocolate chips, and grated chocolate. </li><li>Mix all until just combined. </li><li>Spoon into muffin tin. </li><li>Bake for 15 minutes. </li><li>Let cool. </li></ul>
