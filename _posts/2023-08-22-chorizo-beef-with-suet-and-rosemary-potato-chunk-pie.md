<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>3 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1Kg/2Lb 4Oz Shin Of Beef, Cut Into 4Cm/1 1/2In Pieces</li><li>225G/8Oz Chorizo, Diced</li><li>1 Large Onion, Diced</li><li>2 Large Carrots, Cut To Same Size As Beef</li><li>2 Large Parsnips, Cut To Same Size As Beef</li><li>2 Tbsp Plain Flour</li><li>300Ml/ 1/2 Pint Red Wine</li><li>600Ml/1 Pint Beef Stock</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>225G/8Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>100G/4Oz Shredded Suet</li><li>1-2 Tbsp Fresh Rosemary, Finely Chopped</li><li>900G/2Lb Potatoes, Peeled And Cut Into Chunks</li><li>25G/1Oz Fresh Grated Horseradish, Or To Taste</li><li>25G/1Oz Butter</li><li>About 100Ml/4Floz Single Cream</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 425 f. Grease and line an 11 inch pie pan with dough. </li><li>Heat oil in a large pan, add beef and cook, turning until browned all over. Remove from the pan and set aside. Add chorizo to pan and cook, stirring, for 3 mins. Return the beef to the pan, add onion, carrots and parsnips. Sprinkle flour over vegetables and cook, stirring, until it browns. </li><li>Pour wine into the pan and simmer for 5 mins until reduced. Add beef stock and stir until combined. Season with salt and pepper. </li><li>Roll out the pastry on a floured surface and line the pie pan. Bake for 25 mins. </li><li>Meanwhile, combine flour and salt in a bowl. Add suet and rosemary and stir together. Mix potatoes and sugar in a bowl and season with salt and pepper. </li><li>Cut the butter into the flour mixture with a fork and add cream. Mix until just combined. </li><li>Remove beef from pan and arrange on pastry. Pour meat mixture over potatoes. Cover the pastry with foil and bake for 40 50 mins, until pastry is golden brown and meat filling is bubbling. </li></ul>
