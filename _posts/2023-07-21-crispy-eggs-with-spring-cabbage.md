<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>105G/3 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Onion, Thinly Sliced</li><li>1 Small Potato, Diced</li><li>4 Spring Cabbage Leaves, Taken From The Soft Inner Leaves</li><li>1 Tsp Roughly Chopped Flat-Leaf Parsley</li><li>Vegetable Oil, For Frying</li><li>3 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>30G/1Oz Bleu D'Auvergne Cheese, Sliced</li><li>30G/1Oz Salat Traditional Or Salers French Cheese</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. Melt the butter in a frying pan. Add the oil and cook the onion until softened. Peel the potatoes and slice into wedges. Mix the onion, potato and cabbage together. Season with salt and pepper and cover with a lid. Simmer for 20 minutes until soft. </li><li>2. Heat the oil in a frying pan and fry the eggs until scrambled. Mix the spinach and parsley into the egg. Divide the egg mixture between 4 plates and top each with 4 leaves of cabbage and 3 pieces of cheese. Sprinkle with parsley. </li></ul>
