<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>450G/1Lb Strong White Flour</li><li>7G Salt</li><li>20G/ 3/4Oz Fresh Yeast</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Butter, Plus Extra For Greasing And To Serve</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Brown Sugar</li><li>2 Tsp Mixed Spice</li><li>350G/12Oz Mixed Dried Fruit, Such As Raisins, Currants, Sultanas And Cranberries</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg</li><li>225Ml/8Fl Oz Strong Warm Tea</li><li>Oil, For Greasing</li><li>Butter, To Serve (Optional)</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Caster Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Dissolve the yeast in the tea. Add the salt and brown sugar and mix well. Add the melted butter, cranberries and raisins to the yeast. Add the egg and mix well. Add the oz tea mix to the flour and knead for a few minutes to form a soft dough. Place the dough in a bowl. Cover the bowl with a kitchen towel. </li><li>In the meantime, heat the oven to gas mark 1, 475 f. Grease a 20 cm x 18 cm loaf pan and sprinkle with caster sugar. Take a piece of the dough from the bowl and roll out into a 13 x 18 inch rectangle. Brush with the soft butter. Cut into a 3 x 3 cm rectangle. Place the rectangle on the top of the butter and then fold the dough in half. Press down the seam to seal. Cover with a kitchen towel and leave to rise in a warm place for about 20 minutes. </li><li>Brush with beaten egg and bake for about 35 45 minutes until golden. Transfer to a wire rack to cool. </li></ul>
