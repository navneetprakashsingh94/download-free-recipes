<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Barrel-Aged Feta</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>2Cm/1In Piece Fresh Ginger, Peeled And Grated</li><li>1 Tbsp Dried Oregano</li><li>2 Tsp Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>30G/1Oz Kale, Stalks Removed And Chopped</li><li>30G/1Oz Baby Spinach, Chopped</li><li>4 Spring Onions, Trimmed And Chopped</li><li>Bunch Basil, Shredded</li><li>4 Tomatoes, Seeds Removed And Chopped</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Couscous, Cooked</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>2 Lemons, Juice Only</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. For the dressing grate the ginger. Cut the kale into large pieces. Chop the basil and the spring onions, then set aside. </li><li>2. For the dressing process the tomatoes, olive oil, juice, salt and pepper until smooth. </li><li>3. Heat the oil in a pan on medium high. Add the ginger and cook for a few minutes. Add the oregano and the greens, and turn the heat to medium. Mix. Cook for 4 5 minutes, then add the lemon juice and cook for another minute. Set aside and let cool. </li><li>4. Toss the salad with the dressing and crumbled feta. Serve at room temperature. </li></ul>
