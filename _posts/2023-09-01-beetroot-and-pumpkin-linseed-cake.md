<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>Butter Or Oil, For Greasing</li><li>225G/8Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>Half A Teaspoon Bicarbonate Of Soda</li><li>A Level Teaspoon Baking Powder</li><li>Half A Teaspoon Ground Cinnamon</li><li>180Ml/6 1/4Fl Oz Sunflower Oil</li><li>225G/8Oz Light Muscovado Sugar</li><li>3 Free-Range Eggs, Separated</li><li>150G/5Oz Raw Beetroot, Peeled</li><li>Juice Of Half A Lemon</li><li>75G/3Oz Sultanas Or Raisins</li><li>75G/3Oz Mixed Seeds (Such As Sunflower, Pumpkin And Linseed)</li><li>8 Tablespoons Icing Sugar</li><li>A Little Lemon Juice Or Orange Blossom Water</li><li>Poppy Seeds, To Garnish</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. Preheat oven to 180 c. Grease the base of a 10 inch cake tin with butter. </li><li>2. Sift flour, bicarbonate soda, powder and cinnamon together into a bowl. </li><li>3. In a large bowl beat butter, sugar and egg yolks until well blended. Mix in beetroot, lemon juice and sultanas. Add flour mixture and beat well to combine. Fold in egg whites. </li><li>4. Transfer batter into prepared cake tin. Bake for 1 hour. Remove from oven and sprinkle a spoonful of sunflower seed linseed over each cake. Cool in tin for 10 minutes. Turn out onto a wire rack. Decorate with sunflower seeds. </li><li>5. Combine sugar and orange blossom water in a small saucepan over a medium heat and stir constantly until sugar dissolves and mixture bubbles. Cook for 2 minutes. Spread warm glaze over the cake. </li></ul>
