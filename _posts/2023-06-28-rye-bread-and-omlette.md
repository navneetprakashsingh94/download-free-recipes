<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>375Ml/13Fl Oz Full-Fat Milk</li><li>75G/2 3/4Oz Unsalted Butter, Cut Into Cubes</li><li>35G/1 1/4Oz Runny Honey</li><li>13G/ 1/2Oz Fresh Yeast</li><li>250G/9Oz Rye Flour</li><li>250G/9Oz Plain Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>5 Allspice Berries</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, At Room Temperature</li><li>2 Tsp Sherry Vinegar</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>2 X 100G/3 1/2Oz Beef Tenderloin, Fat Trimmed And Cut Into Cubes</li><li>1 Tsp Chopped Fresh Parsley</li><li>1 Tsp Chopped Shallots</li><li>1 Tbsp Chopped Pickled Onion</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Prepare the otlett in a large heavy pot heat the milk until hot 225 f. Combine the butter and honey and cool until it is lukewarm. Add the yeast and stir until the yeast reemining. Add to the milk mixture. Add the flours one cup at a time. Add in enough water to make the dough thick and elastic. Cover and let rise until doubled in volume, about 1 hour. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 375 f. </li><li>Remove the dough from the bowl, dust with flour, place it on a lightly floured surface and knead for about 10 minutes or until smooth. Grease a large bowl, add the dough, and turn to coat well. Cover the bowl with a towel, and let rest for 10 minutes. </li><li>Heat a skillet until hot 375 f over medium heat. Remove the dough from the bowl and roll out to a 20 x 20 inch rectangle. Spread with the butter and sprinkle with the sherry vinegar, allspice berries and salt. Using your hands, press the seasonings into the dough until they are even. Roll up the dough like a jelly roll. Pinch the seam and place the dough on a baking sheet. With a sharp knife cut through the dough to create small slits in the top. Brush with the extra egg white and bake for about 30 minutes or until golden. </li><li>In the meantime, melt the fat cubes in a pan and lightly fry with the chopped parsley and shallots for about 1 minute. Add the onion and saute for 5 minutes. </li><li>Slice the bread in thin slices. Arrange the slices on a plate and pour the onions, shallots and garlic around the bread. </li><li>Omleta can be found</li></ul>
