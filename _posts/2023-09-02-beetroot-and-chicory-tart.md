<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>Rock Salt</li><li>3 Large Beetroots</li><li>70G/2 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, Softened</li><li>70G/2 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>Plain Flour, For Dusting</li><li>200G/7Oz Puff Pastry</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Lightly Beaten</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Soft Goats' Cheese, Crumbled</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Creme Fraiche</li><li>1 Head Red Chicory, Leaves Separated</li><li>Handful Dandelion Leaves (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>In a large saucepan, combine the salt, beetroots, butter, sugar and lemon peel. Cover and bring to a boil over moderate heat. Reduce the heat to low and simmer, partially covered, for 15 mins or until tender. Let cool completely. Preheat the oven to 425 f. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. </li><li>Roll the pastry on a floured surface to a 12 inch round. Using a 3 inch biscuit cutter or round cookie cutter, cut out 6 circles. Place on the prepared baking sheet. </li><li>Spread half the pastry around the edge of the pan. Place a beetroot on the pastry and top with the remaining pastry. Press gently to seal. Trim the edges. Brush the top with the egg and bake for 15 mins or until pastry is golden brown. </li><li>Meanwhile, for the topping, place the cream, cheese and creme fraiche in a bowl. Stir until smooth. Place on a serving platter. Arrange the chicory, dandelion and beetroot on top. Serve. </li></ul>
