<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>275G/9 3/4Oz Plain Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Bicarbonate Of Soda</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Salt</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>30G/1Oz Unsalted Butter, Melted</li><li>4 Large Free-Range Egg Yolks</li><li>1 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Buttermilk</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Frozen Blueberries (Wild, If Available)</li><li>Vegetable Oil, For Deep-Frying</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Blueberries</li><li>1 Tbsp Lemon Juice, Plus Extra If Needed</li><li>3 Large Basil Leaves</li><li>1 Tbsp Bourbon</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Vanilla Bean Paste</li><li>200-225G/7-8Oz Icing Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For scone scones</li><li>Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a bowl. Whisk well to combine. </li><li></li><li>Put sugar in a mixer bowl fitted with the paddle attachment. Mix on medium speed for 1 minute. </li><li></li><li>Add butter and mix on medium speed for 30 seconds. Add egg yolks and vanilla and mix on high speed for 1 minute. Scrape down sides of bowl and bowl. </li><li></li><li>Reduce speed to low and gradually add flour mixture. Beat until combined. With mixer running, add buttermilk and mix until just combined. Remove 1 cup for glaze. Mix in blueberries. </li><li></li><li>Pour oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 2 inches deep. Heat oil on high heat to 350 degrees. Add batter by large spoonfuls. Fry in batches until lightly golden, 3 4 minutes. Using a wire skimmer, transfer scones to a paper towel lined plate. Sprinkle with salt while hot. </li><li></li><li>In a bowl, mix together blueberries, lemon juice, basil, bourbon, salt, vanilla bean paste and icing sugar. Beat with an electric mixer until well combined. Spoon over scones and serve. </li></ul>
