<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>165G/6Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>265G/9Oz Panko Or Sourdough Breadcrumbs</li><li>2 Tsp Sea Salt</li><li>Glug Of Olive Oil</li><li>4 X 150G/5 1/2Oz-200G/7Oz Hake Fillet, Skin-On, Pin-Boned</li><li>1 Bunch Flatleaf Parsley</li><li> 1/2 Bunch Fresh Tarragon</li><li>4-6 Anchovy Fillets</li><li>1 Tbsp Small Capers</li><li>2 Cloves Garlic, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Tsp Dijon Mustard</li><li>1 Unwaxed Lemon, Finely Grated Zest Only</li><li>2 Tbsp Red Wine Vinegar</li><li>4-6 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Crushed</li><li>1 Orange, Finely Grated Zest And Juice</li><li>2 Tbsp White Wine Vinegar</li><li>1 Tsp Dijon Mustard</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>2 Fennel Bulbs, Thinly Sliced On A Mandolin</li><li>2 Green Apples, Cored, Sliced And Cut Into Matchsticks</li><li>1 Tbsp Finely Chopped Fresh Dill</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat 2 tbsp butter in a saute pan, add breadcrumbs and cook until they are toasted and dark brown. Remove from heat and cool. </li><li>Combine herbs, anchovy, capers, cloves, mustard, lemon juice, vinegar and oil. </li><li>Cut fish in 1 inch cubes and dredge into herb mixture. Set aside. </li><li>Combine oil, cloves, orange zest, lemon juice, vinegar and mustard. Whisk in oil and add other ingredients. </li><li>Steam fennel and apples. Cut into bite sized pieces. </li><li>Place fish in a terrine dish and cover with fennel, apples and panko. Sprinkle with dill and add an additional drizzle of oil. </li></ul>
