<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>50G/2Oz Arborio Risotto Rice</li><li> 1/2 Red Pepper, Stalk And Seeds Removed, Chopped</li><li>1 Spring Onion, Trimmed, Sliced</li><li>1 Heaped Tbsp Frozen Peas</li><li>2 Small Mushrooms, Peeled, Chopped Or Torn</li><li>50G/2Oz Cooked Leftover Chicken, Chopped</li><li>125-150Ml Boiling Water</li><li>Large Pinch Stock Or Bouillon Powder</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat the oil in a large saucepan and cook the risotto rice for 1 to 2 minutes stirring constantly. </li><li>Add the remaining ingredients and bring the mixture to the boil. </li><li>Reduce the heat, cover and simmer until all the liquid is absorbed and the rice is cooked and soft and the grains are tender about 20 to 25 minutes. Serve immediately. </li></ul>
