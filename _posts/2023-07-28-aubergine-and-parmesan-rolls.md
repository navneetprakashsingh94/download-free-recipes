<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>To prepare the aubergine, arrange the aubergines on a platter or other flat surface. Sprinkle the cut sides with the cloves, and scatter with the onion slices. Prick the tomatoes with a fork. Place the tomatoes, rosemary, and thyme on top of the aubergines and sprinkle with 2 tablespoons of the virgin oil and the sea salt. Season the tomato mixture with the pepper and let sit for 1 hour, or until all of the liquid has been absorbed. </li><li>When the tomatoes have absorbed the liquid, arrange the aubergine slices over the tomatoes and sprinkle with the chopped walnuts or almonds. Pour on the pan juices and sprinkle the breadcrumbs on top. Sprinkle the cheese over the top and bake in the middle of a preheated 350 f oven for 15 to 20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the aubergine is soft and warm. </li><li>For the tomato sauce, heat the oil in a saucepan. Add the onion and cook until it is softened but not colored. Add the clove, cover and simmer for 10 minutes. Strain the tomato juices into the pan, breaking up the tomatoes with a fork. Simmer the sauce for 20 minutes.
