<h2>Description</h2>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#good-quality dark chocolate cent cocoa solids" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Good-Quality Dark Chocolate Cent Cocoa Solids</a> <a href="/tags#egg whites use egg carton concerns eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg Whites Use Egg Carton Concerns Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#tsp lemon juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Lemon Juice</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>170G/6Oz Good-Quality Dark Chocolate, Minimum 60 Per Cent Cocoa Solids, Roughly Chopped (Dairy Free, If Necessary)</li><li>7 Free-Range Egg Whites (Use Pasteurised Egg White From A Carton If You Have Concerns About Raw Eggs)</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Lemon Juice</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 170c and put a baking tray into the oven while you prepare the souffle if you don t have a baking tray you can line with baking paper. </li><li>Crack the eggs into a large heatproof bowl and put over an ice bath and stir until they are cool and frothy, about 3 minutes. </li><li>Add the lemon juice and the sugar and stir until the sugar has dissolved. </li><li>Beat the egg whites till stiff and begin to colour. </li><li>Stir one quarter of the beaten egg whites into the melted chocolate. </li><li>Fold the rest of the whites into the chocolate chocolate mixture. </li><li>Spoon the mixture into eight serving dishes and bake for about 6 minutes. </li><li>Sprinkle with shaved dark chocolate. </li><li>Serve immediately. </li></ul>
