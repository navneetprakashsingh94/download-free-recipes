<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>Vegetable Oil, For Deep Frying</li><li>110G/4Oz Plain Flour, For Dusting</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>5-6 Shallots, Peeled And Sliced Into Thin Rings</li><li>110Ml/4Fl Oz Milk</li><li>2 X 300G/10Oz Beef Flank Steaks</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>150G/5Oz Mixed Wild Mushrooms, Cleaned</li><li>1 Tbsp Good Quality Cider Vinegar</li><li>3 Tbsp Rapeseed Oil</li><li>1 Tsp English Mustard</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>110G-150G/4Oz-5Oz Watercress, Washed, Thick Stalks Removed</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Prepare the steaks. Heat a large frying pan, add the oil and saute the shallots for 10 minutes. Remove and reserve. Dredge the flank steaks in the flour and brown them for 2 minutes each side, seasoning with salt and pepper. Remove from the pan and cook the flank steaks under a hot grill for 5 minutes each side. Set aside and keep warm. Pour the milk into a saucepan, bring to a simmer, add the shallots and cook, stirring, for 8 minutes. Remove from the heat. Strain into a bowl, leaving the shallots in the pan and set aside to cool. </li><li>In a bowl, combine the vinegar, oil and mustard. Season with salt and pepper. Add a little of the shallot mixture to the dressing and mix. </li><li>Serve the steaks hot with the watercress, mushrooms and shallots drizzled with the dressing. </li></ul>
