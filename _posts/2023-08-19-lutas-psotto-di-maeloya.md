<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>500G/1Lb Sirloin Steak, Trimmed And Cut In Strips</li><li>Olive Oil</li><li>1 Medium Onion, Peeled And Sliced Thinly</li><li> 1/2 Red Pepper, De-Seeded And Sliced</li><li> 1/2 Green Pepper, De-Seeded And Sliced</li><li>25G/1Oz Butter</li><li>Sea Salt And Black Pepper</li><li>2 Tbsp Tomato Puree</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Beef Stock</li><li>3 Tbsp Paprika</li><li>Small Bunch Flatleaf Parsley</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Soured Cream</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat oil in a deep pan and brown steak strips on both sides. Remove the meat and set aside. Add onion and peppers to the same pan and cook for about 3 minutes. Remove and set aside. </li><li></li><li>Add butter and melt. Add tomato puree, broth, and paprika. Season and let boil for a few minutes. Return meat to pan and cover with the onions and peppers. Stir. Bring back to the boil, reduce heat and let simmer for 15 minutes. </li><li></li><li>Add parsley and cream, serve. </li></ul>
