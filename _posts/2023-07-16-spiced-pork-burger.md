<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>5 Tbsp Soy Sauce</li><li>4 Tbsp Chilli Powder</li><li>3 Tbsp Chinese Five-Spice Powder</li><li>2 Tbsp Light Muscovado Sugar</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li>4Cm/1 1/2In Fresh Root Ginger, Peeled And Finely Chopped</li><li>3Kg/6Lb 8Oz Boneless Pork Shoulder, Rind Removed</li><li>3 Tbsp Sesame Oil</li><li>2 Tbsp Soy Sauce</li><li> 1/2 Red Cabbage, Very Thinly Sliced</li><li>1 Red Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Large Carrot, Cut Into Thin Matchsticks</li><li>2 Red Chillies, Finely Chopped (Seeds Removed If You Prefer Less Heat)</li><li>Handful Coriander Leaves (Optional)</li><li>Black Pepper</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Mayonnaise</li><li>10-12 Sesame Seed Buns, Split Open And Toasted</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Mix together soy sauce, chilli powder, chile powder, sugar, cloves and ginger in a bowl. Add pork and turn to coat. Cover and place in refrigerator for 2 hours. </li><li>Heat sesame oil in a large non stick frying pan or wok over high heat. Remove pork from marinade and pat dry with paper towels. Cook in batches for 5 6 minutes each side, until cooked to 145 f. </li><li>Mix together soy sauce, cabbage, onion, carrot and chillies. Mix in coriander and season with pepper. </li><li>Mix mayonnaise into pork mixture until combined. Divide mixture into 6 portions. </li><li>Using hands, shape pork mixture into burgers and grill for 2 3 minutes each side, until cooked through. </li><li>Spread insides of buns with mayonnaise, then top with pork burgers and a spoonful of vegetable mixture. Serve immediately. </li></ul>
