<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Large Potato, Cut Into Wedges</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Unpeeled</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Sprig Fresh Thyme, Leaves Only</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li> 1/4 Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Minced Beef</li><li>Pinch Dried Chilli Flakes</li><li>Dash Olive Oil</li><li>2 Slices Ciabatta Or 1 Ciabatta Roll, Halved</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Line the bottom of a tray with foil and place potato wedges on top. </li><li>Next put cloves on top and drizzle over olive oil</li><li>Bake at 200c for 20 minutes</li><li>Heat up a frying pan, add olive oil and the onion</li><li>Cook until soft and golden</li><li>Add the beef and fry until browned</li><li>Add the potato wedges, chilli flakes and thyme and stir well for 2 minutes</li><li>Take off heat and season with salt and pepper to taste</li><li>Cover the rolls and put a scoop of the beef mixture onto the bottom half of the roll. </li><li>Top with another slice of bread and enjoy</li><li>Tip it s better to let the slices sit in the microwave for 5 8 minutes before serving</li></ul>
