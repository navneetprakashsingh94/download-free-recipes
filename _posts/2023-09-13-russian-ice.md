<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#ice cubes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ice Cubes</a> <a href="/tags#vodka shot" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Vodka Shot</a> <a href="/tags#lemon juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lemon Juice</a> <a href="/tags#dashes Worcestershire sauce" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dashes Worcestershire Sauce</a> <a href="/tags#dashes Tabasco sauce" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dashes Tabasco Sauce</a> <a href="/tags#tomato juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tomato Juice</a> <a href="/tags#pinch salt ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pinch Salt Ground Pepper</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Ice Cubes</li><li>Vodka, Double Shot</li><li> 1/2 Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>6 Dashes Worcestershire Sauce</li><li>3 Dashes Tabasco Sauce</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Tomato Juice</li><li>Pinch Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Add ice cubes to shaker. </li><li>Add vodka shot, lemon juice, and heat to high. </li><li>Shake for 30 seconds. </li><li>Pour into martini glass. </li><li>Add salt, pepper, and cheese as desired. </li></ul>
