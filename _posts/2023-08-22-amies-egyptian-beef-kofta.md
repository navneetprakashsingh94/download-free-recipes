<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>4 Pitta Breads, Split Open And Cut Into Nacho-Sized Triangles</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Greek-Style Yoghurt</li><li>5 Tbsp Tahini, At Room Temperature</li><li>2 Lemons, Juice Only</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Crushed</li><li>1-2 Tsp Sea Salt Flakes, To Taste</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Pomegranate Seeds</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Toasted Pine Nuts</li><li>1 Tbsp Finely Shredded Mint Leaves</li><li>3 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Small Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Aubergine, Cut Into Small Cubes</li><li>2 Tsp Ground Cumin</li><li>2 Tsp Ground Coriander</li><li>1 Tsp Aleppo Pepper Or Paprika, Plus Extra For Sprinkling</li><li>1-2 Tsp Sea Salt Flakes, To Taste</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Beef Mince</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 350f and cook the pitta breads for 10 minutes. </li><li>Set aside until cool to the touch. </li><li>Mix together the remaining ingredients for the kofta mixture and set aside. </li><li>Place the pine nuts and mint leaves in a shallow baking tray and toast in the oven for 10 minutes. </li><li>Remove from the oven and let cool. </li><li>To make the ground beef, heat the oil in a large pan and cook the onion over a medium heat until soft. </li><li>Add the aubergine cubes, cumin, coriander, paprika and salt flakes. </li><li>Cook gently for 15 minutes. </li><li>Add the mince, reduce the heat, cover and cook for 10 minutes until the meat is cooked and the aubergine is tender. </li><li>To assemble the kofta, divide the ground beef mixture between 4 pitta breads. </li><li>Spread some of the mint nut mixture on each pitta. </li><li>Place a rounded tablespoon of the aubergine mixture on top and sprinkle some pomegranate seeds. </li><li>Cut into wedges and serve. </li></ul>
