<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Beef Stewing Steak</li><li>Salt And Pepper</li><li>2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil</li><li>1 Tbsp Cornflour</li><li>1 Onions, Chopped</li><li>2 Carrots, Chopped</li><li>2 Sprigs Thyme, Leaves Picked</li><li>160G/5 3/4Oz Whole Button Mushrooms</li><li>1 Beef Stock Cube, Dissolved In 400Ml/14Fl Oz Water</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg Yolk, Beaten</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Puff Pastry, Rolled To 5Mm Thick</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Season the steak with salt and pepper. </li><li>Heat the oil and cook the beef till cooked, drain and set aside. </li><li>Add in the melted cheese and vegetables to the pan and fry till the vegetables are browned. </li><li>Add in the beef stock cube water, bring to the boil and then simmer for 20 30 min, skimming the froth from the surface. </li><li>Remove the thyme and cut into 5 cm cubes. </li><li>Add in the beef and mix, return the pan to the heat and simmer for 5 min. </li><li>Preheat the oven to gas mark 5 160c. </li><li>Line the bottom and sides of an 8 inch pie pan with one sheet of pastry and season well. </li><li>Arrange the meat mix on top and smooth with the back of a fork. </li><li>Roll out the second sheet of pastry and use to line the top of the meat mix. </li><li>Fold over and crimp edges to crimp. </li><li>Brush the top with the egg yolk and prick the top with a fork. </li><li>Bake for 40 50 min or possibly till well risen and golden. </li></ul>
