<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>6 Very Ripe Bananas</li><li>125Ml/4 1/2Fl Oz Vegetable Oil</li><li>115G/4Oz Butter, Softened, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>335G/11 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>4 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>450G/1Lb Plain Flour</li><li>2 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>1 Tbsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cloves</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Salt</li><li>225G/8Oz Raisins</li><li>225G/8Oz Pecans</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. </li><li>Preheat the oven to gas mark 4</li><li>2. </li><li>Peel the bananas and mash, i like my bananas a little chunky</li><li>3. </li><li>Cream the butter and the sugar together</li><li>4. </li><li>Beat in the eggs one at a time</li><li>5. </li><li>Fold in the bananas and nuts</li><li>6. </li><li>Sift together the flour, baking powder, spices and salt</li><li>7. </li><li>Fold in the nuts and raisins</li><li>8. </li><li>Spoon into a buttered and floured bundt tin</li><li>10. </li><li>Bake for 30 35 minutes</li></ul>
