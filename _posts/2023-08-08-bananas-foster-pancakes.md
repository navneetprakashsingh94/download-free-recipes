<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>55G/2Oz Butter, Cubed</li><li>275G/9 3/4Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>2 Tbsp Soft Light Brown Sugar</li><li>300Ml/ 1/2 Pint Whole Milk</li><li>1 Large Banana, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1-2 Tbsp Sunflower Oil</li><li>8 Rashers Rindless Streaky Bacon</li><li>Maple Or Golden Syrup, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 200 f. Grease a 9 inch pie plate. Combine the flour and baking powder in a large bowl and season. Beat the eggs and sugar in a separate bowl. Gradually beat in the milk and mashed banana. Fold in the flour mixture until combined. </li><li>Heat the oil in a large skillet on medium heat. Working in batches, cook 2/3 cup of the batter for 2 3 mins, or until golden brown and crisp on the underside, then flip and cook for 1 2 mins on the other side. Repeat with the remaining batter. </li><li>Serve the pancakes topped with bacon, maple syrup, maple syrup and additional butter if desired. </li></ul>
