<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>3 Flat Mushrooms</li><li>3 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li> 1/2 Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Rashers Unsmoked Back Bacon, Chopped</li><li>2 Tbsp White Wine</li><li>3 Tbsp Double Cream</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Barkham Blue Cheese, Or Other Blue Cheese, Crumbled</li><li>1 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Sage Leaves</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Wipe mushrooms with damp cloth and cut in half lengthways. </li><li>Grease bottom of a shallow ovenproof baking dish. </li><li>Finely chop onion and peel cloves and cut bacon into small pieces. </li><li>Heat oil and fry onion with garlic till soft, add bacon and mushrooms. </li><li>Pour over wine and cream and cook till heated through. </li><li>Meanwhile, cut cheese slices into thin pieces and melt slightly. </li><li>Stuff mushrooms with bacon mixture and re coat well with the cheese, reserving a few for garnish. </li><li>Sprinkle with sage and place under hot grill to brown cheese. </li></ul>
