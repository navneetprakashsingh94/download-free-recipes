<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>30 Large Conchiglioni Pasta Shells</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil, Plus Extra For Drizzling</li><li>2 Shallots, Diced</li><li> 1/2 Fennel Bulb, Diced</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Parmesan, Grated</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Ricotta</li><li>1 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Oregano</li><li>25Ml/1Fl Oz Olive Oil</li><li>1 Fennel Bulb, Grated (Fronds Reserved)</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Chopped</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Cherry Tomatoes, Halved</li><li>1 Tsp Dried Oregano</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Good-Quality Black Olives, Pitted</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Passata</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Parmesan, Grated</li><li>2 Tsp Fresh Oregano (Leaves Only)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>In a large pot of boiling water, cook the shells until just underdone. Drain and set aside. </li><li>In the meantime, make the filling. In a large saute pan heat the oil and add the shallots and fennel bulb. Cook over a low heat until softened. Add the parmesan, ricotta, oregano and a pinch of salt. </li><li>Heat the oil and add the fennel bulb fronds and cloves. Cook for a couple of minutes, then add the tomatoes, oregano, olives and passata. Mix. Simmer for another 5 minutes. </li><li>Meanwhile preheat oven to 350 degrees. </li><li>Meanwhile fill the shells and place them in a baking pan. Add the cheese and season with salt and pepper. </li><li>Pour the tomato/fennel mixture over the shells and place in the oven for 30 minutes. Check for doneness by tasting one for doneness. </li></ul>
