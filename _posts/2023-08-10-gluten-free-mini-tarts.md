<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#fast-action yeast" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Fast-Action Yeast</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Flour</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#fruit" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Fruit</a> <a href="/tags#muscovado sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Muscovado Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#beaten egg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Beaten Egg</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 X 7G Sachet Fast-Action Yeast</li><li>1 Tsp Caster Sugar</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Strong White Flour</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, Melted</li><li>250G/9Oz Dried Mixed Fruit</li><li>50G/2Oz Light Muscovado Sugar</li><li>Beaten Egg, For Glazing</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>In a large bowl, combine the yeast, sugar and a little warm water stir until dissolved. </li><li>Stir in the flour and butter and combine well. </li><li>Add the fruit, mix to combine then spoon into 6 lined custard cups and place them in a warm place to rise for about 45 minutes. </li><li>Dust with a little muscovado sugar before removing from the cup or use a piping bag. </li><li>Brush the tops of the breads with egg and place them under the preheated grill for about 3 5 minutes until the sugar has melted. </li><li>Cool on wire racks. </li></ul>
