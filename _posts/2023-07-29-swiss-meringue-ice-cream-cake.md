<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Litre/1 3/4 Pints Vanilla, Coffee Or Chocolate Ice Cream</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Dark Chocolate, In Drops Or Broken Into Pieces</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Butter, Softened, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs, Beaten</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Self-Raising Flour, Sifted</li><li>3 Free-Range Egg Whites</li><li>200G/7Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>Pinch Cream Of Tartar</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>200G/7Oz Dark Chocolate, In Drops Or Chopped Into Pieces</li><li>1-2 Tbsp Rum Or Brandy (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place ice cream in a large bowl and break up with a fork. </li><li>Stir in chocolate drops i shaved the chocolate from top to bottom with a vegetable peeler and stir to combine. </li><li>Freeze for 20 minutes. </li><li>Preheat oven to 150c. </li><li>Grease and line the base and sides of a 20cm loose bottomed loaf tin with baking paper. </li><li>Line the base and sides of the tin with baking paper. </li><li>Melt butter and pour into a mixing bowl. </li><li>Add sugar, eggs and flour and mix well. </li><li>Spoon into tin and smooth the top. </li><li>Bake for 1 hour or until firm to the touch and springy to the touch. </li><li>Let the cake cool in the tin. </li><li>To make the meringues, combine egg whites, caster sugar and cream tartar in a metal bowl. </li><li>Place over a saucepan of simmering water, whisking constantly. </li><li>Cook for 8 minutes or until whites are stiff but not dry. </li><li>Whisk in cream. </li><li>Pour mixture into a large heatproof bowl and put bowl over a saucepan of simmering water. </li><li>Whisk until melted and fluffy. </li><li>Remove bowl from over the hot water. </li><li>Let stand until cool enough to handle. </li><li>Add chocolate drops and whisk to combine. </li><li>Pour rum brandy on top of the cooled cake and let stand for 3 hours or overnight. </li><li>Unmold cake and spread top and sides with warm ganache. </li><li>Place back in the refrigerator for 30 minutes. </li><li>Use a spatula to cut the cake into slices and serve. </li></ul>
