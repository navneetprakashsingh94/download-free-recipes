<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>70G/2 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, Softened</li><li>70G/2 1/2Oz Icing Sugar</li><li>2 Free-Range Egg Whites</li><li>80G/3Oz Plain Flour, Sifted</li><li>1 Tsp Liquorice Essence</li><li> 1/2-1 Tsp Black Food Colouring Paste</li><li>3 Large Free-Range Eggs, At Room Temperature</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Caster Sugar, Plus Extra For Sprinkling</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Butter, Softened</li><li>225G/8Oz Icing Sugar</li><li>1 Tbsp Milk</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Liquorice Essence</li><li>200G/7Oz Blackcurrant Jam</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cream butter and sugar</li><li>Add egg whites</li><li>Add sifted flour, flavouring and food paste if using</li><li>Whisk egg and sugar until pale and light</li><li>Gradually fold in combined butter and sugar mix, stir in flour</li><li>Bake for 1 1/4 hours in a 350 f oven</li><li>Mix icing sugar and butter in a bowl until it is crumbly. Add milk and liquorice essence. Mix until smooth. Spread jam over cake</li><li>Cut and serve</li></ul>
