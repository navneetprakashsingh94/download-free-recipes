<a href="/tags#shortcrust pastry" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Shortcrust Pastry</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#lemon zest juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lemon Zest Juice</a> <a href="/tags#Bramley apples" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bramley Apples</a> <a href="/tags#butter moulds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter Moulds</a> <a href="/tags#tsp ground cinnamon" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Ground Cinnamon</a> <a href="/tags#blueberries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Blueberries</a> <a href="/tags#medium free-range egg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Medium Free-Range Egg</a> <a href="/tags#cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cream</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>450G/1Lb Ready-Made Shortcrust Pastry</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar, Plus Extra For Sprinkling</li><li> 1/2 Lemon, Zest And Juice</li><li>550G/1Lb 3 1/2Oz Bramley Apples</li><li>25G/1Oz Butter, Plus Extra For Greasing The Moulds</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon (Optional)</li><li>150G/5 1/4Oz Blueberries</li><li>1 Medium Free-Range Egg, Lightly Beaten</li><li>Clotted Cream, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the crust, combine sugar and lemon zest together and add the apples and butter moulds or muffin tins, spreading sugar mixture evenly. Sprinkle with cinnamon and bake at 300f for 40 45 mins or until apples are tender and mixture is golden. </li><li></li><li>To assemble, mix the blueberries, egg and cream. Spoon into one pie crust. Cut the remaining pie crust into rectangles and cover the pie. Arrange the berries around the edge of the pie, with overlapping as necessary. </li><li></li><li>Bake at 300f for 20 30 mins until berries are heated through and pastry is golden. </li></ul>
