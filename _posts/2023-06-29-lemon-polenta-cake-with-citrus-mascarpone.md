<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>225G/8Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>225G/8Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>3 Free-Range Eggs, Beaten</li><li>225G/8Oz Ground Almonds</li><li>115G/4Oz Instant Polenta</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder (Gluten-Free If Necessary)</li><li>3 Lemons, Zest And Juice Only</li><li>Icing Sugar, For Dusting</li><li>2 Lemons, Juice Only</li><li>3 Tbsp Caster Sugar</li><li>250G/9Oz Mascarpone</li><li>2 Oranges, Zest Only, Plus Splash Of Juice</li><li>50-75G/1 3/4-2 1/2Oz Icing Sugar, Sieved</li><li>75-100G/3-4Oz Caster Sugar, For Dusting</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Orange Juice</li><li>4 Satsumas, Peeled And Cut In Half Horizontally</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 350 f. Grease a 9 inch round cake tin and line with baking paper. </li><li>Beat butter and 1/2 cup sugar together in a mixer until pale and creamy. Stir in egg yolks, almonds, polenta, powder, lemon juice and sugar then pour into prepared pan. Bake for about 35 mins, until a skewer inserted in the center comes out clean. </li><li>Meanwhile, for the citrus mascarpone, mix lemon juice and 2 tbsp sugar together in a small saucepan. Bring to a boil, stirring, then reduce heat and simmer, stirring, for 10 mins, until syrupy. Set aside to cool. </li><li>Meanwhile, whisk egg whites to soft peaks then whisk in remaining sugar. Fold orange juice into lemon mascarpone mixture then gently fold in whipped egg whites. Spread over cake then sprinkle with orange satsumas. </li><li>For the orange satsumas, combine icing sugar, caster sugar and orange juice in a small saucepan over a medium heat until sugar dissolves. Bring to a boil and cook, stirring, for 2 mins, until slightly thickened. Remove from heat. Spoon over fruit. Chill for 30 mins, until just set. </li><li>Slice cake and serve with orange satsumas. </li></ul>
