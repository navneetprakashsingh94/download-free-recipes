<a href="/tags#fettuccine" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Fettuccine</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Oil</a> <a href="/tags#bacon pancetta batons" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bacon Pancetta Batons</a> <a href="/tags#ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ground Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#spears" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Spears</a> <a href="/tags#free-range eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Free-Range Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#parmesan" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Parmesan</a> <a href="/tags#parsley" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Parsley</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Fettuccine</li><li>2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil</li><li>300G/11Oz Smoked Bacon Or Pancetta, Cut Into Batons</li><li>1 Tsp Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>12 Spears Asparagus, Blanched</li><li>3 Large Free-Range Eggs</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>110G/4Oz Parmesan, Freshly Grated, Plus Extra, To Serve</li><li>Handful Fresh Parsley, Chopped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cut the asparagus in 1/2 and cook until tender, add to the hot cooked pasta. </li><li>In a separate pan, heat the oil, cook the bacon, remove and keep warm. </li><li>Whisk the eggs in a bowl, add the tomatoe oil and season with the pepper. </li><li>Add the eggs to the pasta with the parmesan and mix gently. </li><li>Plate, top with the cooked bacon and spagetti, sprinkle with parsley and serve immediately. </li></ul>
