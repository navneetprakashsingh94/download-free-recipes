<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Milk</a> <a href="/tags#oz cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Cream</a> <a href="/tags#peanut butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Peanut Butter</a> <a href="/tags#milk powder" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Milk Powder</a> <a href="/tags#stem ginger" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Stem Ginger</a> <a href="/tags#almonds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Almonds</a> <a href="/tags#chocolate chips" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chocolate Chips</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>20G/ 3/4Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>400G/14Oz Condensed Milk</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>4 Tbsp Smooth Peanut Butter</li><li>200G/7Oz Milk Powder</li><li>80G/3Oz Stem Ginger, Roughly Chopped</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Flaked Almonds</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Dark Chocolate Chips</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Mix dry cake mix, sweetened condensed milk, cream cheese, peanut butter, malted milk powder, and stem ginger, and spread the mixture into a well greased 8 x 8 pan. </li><li>Sprinkle almonds on top. </li><li>Bake at 350 for 20 minutes. </li><li>Place semisweet chocolate chips in a small pot. </li><li>Heat over low heat, stirring, for 10 minutes. </li><li>Drizzle chocolate over hot bars. </li><li>Chill for 15 minutes or until chocolate is set and cut into bars. </li></ul>
