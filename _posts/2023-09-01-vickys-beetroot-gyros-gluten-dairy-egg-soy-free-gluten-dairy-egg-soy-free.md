<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#rapeseed oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Rapeseed Oil</a> <a href="/tags#beetroot pieces" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Beetroot Pieces</a> <a href="/tags#nigella seeds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Nigella Seeds</a> <a href="/tags#fennel seeds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Fennel Seeds</a> <a href="/tags#tsp chilli flakes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Chilli Flakes</a> <a href="/tags#salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt</a> <a href="/tags#lime juice tsp malt vinegar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lime Juice Tsp Malt Vinegar</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Rapeseed Oil</li><li>375G/13Oz Peeled And Trimmed Beetroot, Cut Into Bite-Sized Pieces</li><li>1 Tsp Nigella Seeds</li><li>1 Tsp Fennel Seeds</li><li>1-2 Tsp Chilli Flakes</li><li>1 Tsp Fine Salt</li><li> 1/2 Large Lime, Juice Only (Alternatively Use 4 Tsp Malt Vinegar)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat the oil in a wok or large pan over medium heat. </li><li>Once oil is hot, add beetroot pieces and fry for 5 7 minutes until beetroot is slightly softened. </li><li>Remove beetroot from heat, and set aside until cool enough to handle. </li><li>When cooled, remove skin and cut beetroot into fine cubes and put in a bowl. </li><li>Add all the remaining ingredients and toss to mix. </li><li>Serve at room temperature. </li></ul>
