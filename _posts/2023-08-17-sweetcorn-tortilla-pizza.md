<div>Delicious Sweetcorn Tortilla Pizza using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#tin beans chilli sauce" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tin Beans Chilli Sauce</a> <a href="/tags#tin kidney beans water" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tin Kidney Beans Water</a> <a href="/tags#sweetcorn" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sweetcorn</a> <a href="/tags#flour tortillas" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Flour Tortillas</a> <a href="/tags#cheddar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cheddar</a> <a href="/tags#cherry tomatoes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cherry Tomatoes</a> <a href="/tags#lime wedges" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lime Wedges</a> <a href="/tags#ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ground Pepper</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>400G Tin Mixed Beans In Chilli Sauce</li><li>400G Tin Red Kidney Beans In Water, Drained</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Frozen Sweetcorn</li><li>8 Regular Flour Tortillas</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Cheddar, Finely Grated</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Cherry Tomatoes, Halved Or Quartered (Optional)</li><li>1 Lime, Cut Into Wedges (Optional)</li><li>Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat a large frying pan over a medium heat. </li><li>Drain the beans and kidney beans in a sieve or colander, reserving the soaking liquid and then add them into the pan. </li><li>Add the reserved soaking liquid and cover the pan. </li><li>Cook for 10 minutes until the mixture thickens, stirring occasionally. </li><li>Remove the cover, add the sweetcorn and stir well to combine. </li><li>Heat a grill pan over a medium high heat. </li><li>Lightly coat the frying pan with olive oil and season the tortillas with pepper and fry them in batches for 10 seconds on each side. </li><li>Place the tortillas on a baking tray and sprinkle them with the cheddar and tomatoes, then pour over the chilli sauce. </li><li>Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes or until the topping is bubbly and the cheese is melted. </li><li>Serve with lime wedges. </li></ul>
