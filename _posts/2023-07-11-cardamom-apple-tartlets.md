<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#puff pastry" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Puff Pastry</a> <a href="/tags#Braeburn apples fridge" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Braeburn Apples Fridge</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#cardamom pods" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cardamom Pods</a> <a href="/tags#cinnamon stick" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cinnamon Stick</a> <a href="/tags#creme fraiche" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Creme Fraiche</a> <a href="/tags#figs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Figs</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>320G Packet Ready-Rolled All-Butter Puff Pastry</li><li>6-8 Braeburn Apples, Peeled, Quartered And Left To Dry Out In The Fridge Overnight</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Cold Butter, Softened</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Golden Caster Sugar</li><li>2 Cardamom Pods</li><li>1 Cinnamon Stick</li><li>200G/7Oz Creme Fraiche</li><li>4 Fresh Figs, Chopped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 180c. </li><li>Roll out half the pastry and cut out 4 circles of parchment paper. </li><li>Brush each circle of pastry with water. </li><li>Place 1 apple piece on each pastry circle, cover with another pastry circle, pinching edges together to seal. </li><li>Chill for 15 mins. </li><li>In a pan, add the butter, sugar, cardamom and cinnamon and stir over a low heat until the sugar is dissolved. </li><li>Remove from the heat and allow to cool. </li><li>Line a 12 hole mini muffin tray with pastry cases. </li><li>Spoon in the cooled spiced butter and bake for 12 15 mins, until the pastry is puffed and golden. </li><li>Serve the tartlets warm or cold with creme fraiche and fig halves. </li></ul>
