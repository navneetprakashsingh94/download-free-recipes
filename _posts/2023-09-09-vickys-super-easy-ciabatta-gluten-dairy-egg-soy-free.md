<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Large Onion, Chopped</li><li>4 Garlic Cloves, Crushed</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cumin</li><li>2 X 400G Tins Black Beans, Drained And Liquid Reserved</li><li> 1/2 Vegetable Stock Cube</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Cherry Tomatoes, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Jalapeno Chilli, Finely Chopped</li><li> 1/2 Lime, Juice Only</li><li> 1/2 Small Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>20G/ 3/4Oz Fresh Coriander, Roughly Chopped</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>2 Crusty Bread Rolls, Such As Mini Baguettes Or Ciabatta Rolls, Halved</li><li>1 Tbsp Salted Butter, Softened</li><li>4 Heaped Tbsp Refried Beans (See Above)</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Mozzarella Ball, Drained And Roughly Chopped (Or 125G/4 1/2Oz Chihuahua Cheese, Grated)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat the oil in a pan, add the onion and fry until translucent. </li><li>Add the cloves and cumin, cook and stir for 1 minute. </li><li>Add the beans and stock cube and simmer gently, covered, for 40 minutes or until the beans are tender. </li><li>You may need to add a little hot water if the sauce is too thick. </li><li>Stir in the tomatoes, chilli, lime juice, onion, coriander and seasonings. </li><li>Preheat the broiler/grill and melt the butter in a frying pan. </li><li>Smear the ciabatta slices with the butter and cook until golden on both sides. </li><li>Arrange the bean sauce on top, add the sliced cheese and then the ciabatta and serve. </li></ul>
