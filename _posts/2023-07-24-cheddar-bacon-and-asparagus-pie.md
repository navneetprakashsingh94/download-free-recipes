<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#pastry" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pastry</a> <a href="/tags#" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank"></a> <a href="/tags#English mustard" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">English Mustard</a> <a href="/tags#mature cheddar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Mature Cheddar</a> <a href="/tags#ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ground Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#slices bacon" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Slices Bacon</a> <a href="/tags#egg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 X 375G/13Oz Puff Pastry</li><li>Plain Flour, For Dusting</li><li>1 Tbsp English Mustard</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Mature Cheddar, Grated</li><li>Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>14 Slices Good-Quality Streaky Bacon</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Lightly Beaten</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 200 c approximately 400 f and line 2 baking trays with baking paper</li><li></li><li>Mix together asparagus and mustard until combined</li><li></li><li>Roll out the pastry, cut into 4 4cm squares, place a tablespoon of asparagus mix on each square, spread over cheese and season to taste with pepper</li><li></li><li>Arrange bacon and asparagus and place in a circle in the middle of each pastry square</li><li></li><li>Brush edges with beaten egg, fold pastry over bacon and asparagus, pinch to seal</li><li></li><li>Bake for 20 mins and serve hot</li><li></li><li>Tip to make a quick pastry for a main course, make an egg wash using 2 tbsp water, stir in 1 tbsp milk and brush over the pastry ridges then place in the oven to bake for 15 mins. </li></ul>
