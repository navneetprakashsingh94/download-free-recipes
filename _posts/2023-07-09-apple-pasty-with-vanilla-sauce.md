<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>175G/6Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>75G/3Oz Butter</li><li>3 Tbsp Water</li><li>50G/2Oz Dried Cranberries</li><li>50G/2Oz Sultanas</li><li>50G/2Oz Raisins</li><li>1Kg/2Lb 2Oz Dessert Apples, Such As Cox'S Orange Pippin, Peeled, Cored And Quartered</li><li>200G/7Oz Filo Pastry, Cut Into 5Cm/2In-Wide Strips</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>1 Clementine, Zest Only</li><li>1 Lemon, Zest Only</li><li>75G/3Oz Clarified Butter</li><li>4 Free-Range Egg Yolks</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Milk</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>2-3 Tbsp Armagnac</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Combine all the sauce ingredients in a heavy saucepan. </li><li>Stir over heat until sugar dissolves. </li><li>Boil gently, without stirring, until thickened. </li><li>Cool to room temperature. </li><li>Melt butter, spread over pastry. </li><li>Roll pastry to a 1/8 inch thickness. </li><li>Cut into 12 squares. </li><li>Place a teaspoon of apple filling in center. </li><li>Moisten edges of pastry with water. </li><li>Fold over, forming half moon shapes. </li><li>Press the edges with a fork to seal. </li><li>Prick bases with a fork. </li><li>Brush with oil. </li><li>Place in a 13x9 inch baking pan. </li><li>Prick all over with a fork. </li><li>Cover with foil. </li><li>Bake at 180c for 10 minutes. </li><li>Remove foil and bake for another 10 minutes or until golden. </li><li>Cool. </li><li>Remove pastry to a cutting board. </li><li>Brush pastry with butter. </li><li>Meanwhile, to make the ganache, whisk egg yolks and sugar together until thick and pale. </li><li>Stir in milk, cream and 2 tbsp of the orange and lemon juices. </li><li>Add remaining orange and lemon juices and remaining orange juices. </li><li>Cook over a pan of simmering water until mixture thickens. </li><li>Stir in vanilla. </li><li>Serve with apple tart. </li></ul>
