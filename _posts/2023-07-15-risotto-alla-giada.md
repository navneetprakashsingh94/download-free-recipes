<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Lemon, Juice Only Or 1 Tbsp White Wine Vinegar</li><li>3 Globe Artichokes</li><li>1.2 Litres/2 Pints Vegetable Stock</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>3 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Onion, Chopped</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Arborio Or Carnaroli Rice</li><li>125Ml/4Fl Oz Dry White Wine</li><li>200G/7Oz Fresh Broad Beans, Double Podded If Not Young (600-700G/1Lb 5Oz-1Lb 9Oz Weight In Pods)</li><li>200G/7Oz Fresh Peas, Podded (About 600G/1Lb 5Oz Weight In Pods)</li><li> 1/2 Small Unwaxed Lemon, Juice And Grated Zest Only</li><li> 1/2-1 Tsp Salt</li><li>8 Turns Black Peppermill</li><li>70G/2 1/2Oz Freshly Grated Pecorino (Or Other Vegetarian Hard Cheese)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For risotto</li><li>1. Heat butter and oil in a saucepan. Add onion and saute for 1 minute. Stir in rice to coat in oil and butter. </li><li>2. Add the wine and simmer until wine is absorbed. Continue adding stock, 1 cup at a time, and simmering and stirring until it is almost completely absorbed before adding the next cup. Simmer for 20 25 minutes or until the rice is cooked through and the liquid is completely absorbed. </li><li>3. Stir in fava beans and peas and cook until the vegetables are tender and the rice is cooked through. </li><li>4. Remove from heat and stir in lemon juice, salt and pepper and pecorino. </li></ul>
