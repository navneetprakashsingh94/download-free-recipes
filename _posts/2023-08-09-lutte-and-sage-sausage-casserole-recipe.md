<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>4 Baking Potatoes</li><li>Knob Of Butter, Plus Extra To Serve</li><li>2 Cumberland Sausage Rings</li><li>Sea Salt And Black Pepper</li><li>Olive Oil</li><li>25G/1Oz Butter</li><li>2 Red Onions, Finely Sliced</li><li>2 Tsp Dark Brown Soft Sugar</li><li>2 Tsp Plain Flour</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Red Wine</li><li>500Ml/18Fl Oz Chicken Stock</li><li> 1/2 Tsp English Mustard</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 180 degrees c/375 degrees f/ gas mark 5. </li><li>Scrub the potatoes, then slice into 1/4 to 1/2 inch slices. </li><li>Heat the butter and oil in a large frypan, place the potatoes and cook for 15 min, turning once. </li><li>When the potatoes are almost cooked, add in the sausage rings and fry for 2 min, stir to cover and keep hot. </li><li>Fry the onions in the remaining butter and oil till golden brown then stir in the sage and brown sugar. </li><li>Stir in the flour and cook for 1 minute. </li><li>Add in the wine and stir to deglaze the pan. </li><li>Add in the stock and bring to the boil. </li><li>Stir through the mustard and season with salt and pepper. </li><li>Pour the sauce over the sausage and potatoes and put back in the oven for 10 15 min. </li></ul>
