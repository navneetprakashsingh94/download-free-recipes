<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Gluten Free Fruitcake using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 50 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#cherries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cherries</a> <a href="/tags#sultanas" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sultanas</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#eggs free-range" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Eggs Free-Range</a> <a href="/tags#ripe bananas" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ripe Bananas</a> <a href="/tags#rice flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Rice Flour</a> <a href="/tags#cornflour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cornflour</a> <a href="/tags#baking powder" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Baking Powder</a> <a href="/tags#tsp salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Salt</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Cherries</li><li>75G/3Oz Sultanas</li><li>110G/4Oz Butter, Softened</li><li>110G/4Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>2 Large Eggs, Preferably Free-Range</li><li>3 Large Ripe Bananas</li><li>175G/6Oz Fine Rice Flour</li><li>50G/2Oz Cornflour</li><li>2 Tsp Gluten-Free Baking Powder</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Salt</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 180 degrees c and grease a 23cm round, ovenproof tin. </li><li>Put the cherries, sultanas and a pinch of salt in a food processor and puree until smooth. </li><li>Cream butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. </li><li>Add eggs, one at a time. </li><li>Gently mix in the banana. </li><li>Sift in the flours, baking powder and salt and mix well. </li><li>Fold in the fruit. </li><li>Pour into the tin and bake for approx 1 hour. </li><li>Cool and serve with custard or cream. </li></ul>
