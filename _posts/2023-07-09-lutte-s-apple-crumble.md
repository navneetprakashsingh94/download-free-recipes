<div>Delicious Lutte S Apple Crumble using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 30 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#cooking apples" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cooking Apples</a> <a href="/tags#lemon juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lemon Juice</a> <a href="/tags#muscovado sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Muscovado Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#porridge oats" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Porridge Oats</a> <a href="/tags#muscovado sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Muscovado Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#walnuts" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Walnuts</a> <a href="/tags#sunflower seeds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sunflower Seeds</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1.5Kg/3Lb 5Oz Cooking Apples</li><li> 1/2 Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Light Muscovado Sugar</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Plain Flour</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Porridge Oats</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Light Muscovado Sugar</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Walnuts, Roughly Chopped</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Sunflower Seeds</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Softened Butter</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 190c or 350f and grease a 7. 5 x 8 cup baking dish. </li><li>Line baking pan with baking parchment and grease paper. </li><li>Place apples in large bowl with lemon juice, muscovado sugar and flour. Toss to coat and divide mixture between prepared baking pan. </li><li>Combine porridge oats, sugar, walnuts and sunflower seeds in another large bowl and use a pastry blender or your fingers to rub in the butter until mixture resembles coarse breadcrumbs. Sprinkle over the apple mixture and pat down with a spatula to settle. </li><li>Bake for 30 to 40 minutes until topping is golden. </li></ul>
