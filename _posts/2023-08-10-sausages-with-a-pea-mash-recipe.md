<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>8 Sausages</li><li>2 Onions, Sliced</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Dried Mixed Herbs</li><li> 1/2 Tsp English Mustard</li><li>600Ml/20Fl Oz Stock (Ideally Beef, Although Chicken Or Vegetable Is Fine)</li><li>Salt And Black Pepper</li><li>2 Tsp Softened Butter</li><li>2 Tsp Plain Flour</li><li>900G/2Lb Potatoes, Peeled And Chopped Into Equally Sized Cubes</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Butter</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Milk</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Frozen Peas</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cook and drain sausages. </li><li>Grease large frying pan and saute/fry onions and herbs in butter till onions are soft and golden. </li><li>Add in sausages to onions and cook till cooked through. </li><li>Add in mustard and 2 c. Stock and bring to a boil, then simmer for 10 min, stirring occasionally. </li><li>Add in remaining stock and cook gently for 2 to 3 min, or possibly till reduced. </li><li>Season with salt pepper. </li><li>Mix flour with butter and stir into stock. </li><li>Continue to cook and stir for 5 min or possibly till thickened. </li><li>Place potatoes in a large saucepan of boiling water and cook for 15 to 20 min or possibly till tender. </li><li>Drain. </li><li>Remove from heat, while they are hot, mash with butter and lowfat milk till smooth. </li><li>Place one sausage in each serving dish. </li><li>Pour cream sauce over. </li><li>Decorate with peas. </li><li>Makes 6 servings. </li></ul>
