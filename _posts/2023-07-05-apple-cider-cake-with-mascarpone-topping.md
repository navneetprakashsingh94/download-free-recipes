<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>330Ml/11 1/2Fl Oz French Cider</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>2 Tsp Bicarbonate Of Soda</li><li>1 Tsp Mixed Spice Powder</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>Pinch Freshly Grated Nutmeg</li><li>4 Apples, Peeled, Cores Removed And Grated</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Soft Dark Brown Sugar</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Melted Butter</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>200G/7Oz Golden Sultanas</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Pecans, Chopped</li><li>250G/9Oz Mascarpone</li><li>Splash Double Cream</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 300 f. Combine the flour and spice powder in a bowl with the nutmeg, bicarbonate of soda, and a pinch of salt. In a separate bowl, combine the apples, sugar, butter, eggs, sultanas, and nuts. Mix together the dry ingredients and mix well. </li><li>Pour the batter into a buttered, round cake tin and bake for 1 1/4 hours or until a toothpick comes out clean. </li><li>Meanwhile, make the mascarpone topping and set aside. Mix the mascarpone with the cream and a sprinkling of cinnamon. Serve the cooled cake with the mascarpone topping. </li></ul>
