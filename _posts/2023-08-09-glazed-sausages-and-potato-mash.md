<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>900G/2Lb Floury Potatoes, Peeled And Cubed</li><li>1 Tbsp Sunflower Oil</li><li>1 Large Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Tsp Dijon Mustard</li><li> 1/2Pint Chicken Stock (From A Stock Cube)</li><li>1 Tsp Gravy Browning (Optional)</li><li>8 Pork Sausages</li><li>50G/1 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Double Cream Or Milk</li><li>1 Tbsp Each, Fresh Parsley And Chives</li><li>3 Spring Onions, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cook potatoes in boiling salted water for 15 20 mins, until tender. Drain. Cool slightly. Cut in half. </li><li>Meanwhile, heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Saute onions for 5 mins, until soft and translucent. Add mustard, stock cube and gravy browning. Cook, stirring, for 5 10 mins, until thickened. Set aside. </li><li>Cook sausages in a separate frying pan over high heat for 2 3 mins per side, until browned. Set aside. </li><li>Mash potatoes with butter, milk, parsley, spring onions and sausage. Season with salt and pepper. Fold in onion. Divide between serving bowls. </li></ul>
