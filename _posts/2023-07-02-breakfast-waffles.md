<a href="/tags#sausagemeat" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sausagemeat</a> <a href="/tags#flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Flour</a> <a href="/tags#tsp baking powder" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Baking Powder</a> <a href="/tags#pinch salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pinch Salt</a> <a href="/tags#buttermilk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Buttermilk</a> <a href="/tags#free-range eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Free-Range Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#maple syrup" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Maple Syrup</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Sausagemeat</li><li>450G/1Lb Self-Raising Flour</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>Pinch Of Salt</li><li>600Ml/20Fl Oz Buttermilk</li><li>3 Large Free-Range Eggs, Separated</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Butter, Melted, Plus Extra For Frying</li><li>Maple Syrup, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>In a large saucepan, place sausage meat over medium heat and cook until it s browned all over and cooked all the way through. </li><li>Pour the sausage meat into a mesh strainer and set aside to drain. </li><li>If you have a large, heat proof bowl, add the dry ingredients and whisk together. </li><li>In a separate bowl, whisk the buttermilk and eggs together until smooth. </li><li>Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. </li><li>Add in the drained sausage and reheat in the microwave for about 30 seconds to a minute, stirring in between. </li><li>Preheat an 8 inch griddle or a skillet to medium heat. </li><li>Lightly butter griddle. </li><li>Scoop about 1/4 cup of the batter onto the griddle, using a spoon to spread it out so that it is about 1/2 inch thick. </li><li>Cook for 2 minutes on each side. </li><li>Repeat with the remaining batter. </li><li>Serve the waffles topped with maple syrup, if using. </li><li>For more information about making waffles, visit</li><li>Https //www. Epicurious. Com/recipe/waffles/ kate david lebovitz</li></ul>
