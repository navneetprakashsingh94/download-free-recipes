<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Raisins (Or Sultanas)</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Rum (Or Brandy Or Whisky)</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Salted Butter</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Light Muscovado Sugar (Or Soft Brown Sugar)</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Sea Salt</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Double Cream</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>250G/9Oz Unrefined Caster Sugar (Or Any Sugar You Have)</li><li>75G/2 3/4Oz Golden Syrup (Or Honey Or Maple Syrup)</li><li>275G/9 3/4Oz Dark Chocolate, Minimum 70% Cocoa Solids, (Or Any Chocolate You Have) Broken Into Small Pieces</li><li>4 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>2 Or 3 Very Ripe Bananas, Mashed</li><li>70G/2 1/2Oz Plain Flour</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>To make the rum butter sauce soak the raisins in the rum for 1 hour. </li><li>While the raisins are soaking, mix the muscovado sugar, salt and butter in a saucepan until the sugar is dissolved. Bring to the boil over a low heat and simmer for 5 minutes. Remove from the heat and allow to cool. </li><li>To make the granola butter cream, heat the milk until just about to boil. Remove from the heat and add the sugar and honey syrup. Let stand for 5 minutes. </li><li>Beat the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then fold in the sifted flour. </li><li>Cut the bananas into rounds and cut into sixths. Then gently fold into the chocolate butter mixture. Do not overmix. </li><li>Put 1/4 of the batter into a greased muffin tin. Use the remaining batter to line the muffin tins. Bake at 200 c for 20 25 minutes. </li><li>Remove from the oven and leave to cool in the tins for 15 minutes. Remove from the tins and serve. </li></ul>
