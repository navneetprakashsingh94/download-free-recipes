<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>Plain Flour, For Dusting</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Puff Pastry</li><li>200G/7Oz Low-Fat Cream Cheese</li><li> 1/4 Lemon, Finely Grated Zest Only</li><li>Flaked Sea Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>30 Medium Asparagus Spears</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten, To Glaze (Alternatively Use A Little Milk)</li><li>6 Medium Free-Range Eggs</li><li>25G/1Oz Parmesan, Made Into Shavings With A Potato Peeler</li><li>Good Handful Small Basil Leaves</li><li>Drizzle Extra Virgin Olive Oil (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Prepare the cream cheese. </li><li>In a bowl, mix the cream cheese, lemon zest, lemon juice and salt pepper. </li><li>Add the asparagus, mix and set aside. </li><li>Roll out the puff pastry and line a 15cm round springform pan. </li><li>Put the tart crust in the fridge while preparing the egg mix. </li><li>In a bowl, add the eggs, parmesan, and salt pepper. </li><li>Mix well and set aside. </li><li>With a fork, gently mix the eggs with the cream cheese, being careful not to break the yolks. </li><li>Pour the cheese mix into the tart. </li><li>Make a decorative pattern of the mixture using a knife. </li><li>Bake in an oven preheated to 180c for 45 minutes or until golden and the filling is set. </li><li>Garnish with the basil leaves and drizzle with some olive oil. </li></ul>
