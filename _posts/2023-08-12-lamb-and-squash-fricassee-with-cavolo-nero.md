<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>700G/1Lb 9Oz Of Lamb Neck Fillet Or Lamb Leg Steaks, Cut Into 4 Even Portions</li><li>Olive Oil, For Cooking</li><li>Sea Salt And Pepper</li><li>2 Butternut Squash, Or About 1.2Kg/2Lb 12Oz Mixed Onion/Spaghetti Squash, Unpeeled And Cut Into Thin Wedges</li><li>1 Red Chilli, Seeds Removed, Roughly Chopped</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Peeled And Roughly Chopped</li><li>110G/4Oz Unsalted Butter, Softened</li><li>1 Tbsp Blossom Honey</li><li>1 Large Head Cavolo Nero, Leaves Only</li><li>Extra Virgin Olive Oil, For Drizzling</li><li>30G/1 1/4Oz Pickled Capers</li><li> 1/2 Lemon, Zest And Juice Only</li><li>4 Tinned Anchovies, Finely Chopped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Prepare lamb by brushing the lamb fillet with oil, sea salt and pepper, and put in a roasting tray and marinate for an hour. </li><li>Next prepare a squash slice and slice into bite size pieces. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 400f. </li><li>Place the squash, chives, the onion and the cloves in a baking tray. </li><li>Bake until browned and soft, approx 15 mins. </li><li>Remove and allow to cool. </li><li>Crush the chilli seeds and blend in a spice grinder with the butter and honey. </li><li>Roast the lamb leg over a high heat, until cooked to your liking approx 25 mins. </li><li>Meanwhile prepare the cavolo nero. </li><li>Toss the nero with the olive oil, lemon zest, capers, salt and pepper and allow to sit. </li><li>Drain the cavolo nero and place it on a plate. </li><li>Top with the lamb and serve with spaghetti squash wedges. </li><li>Garnish with sprigs of mint. </li></ul>
