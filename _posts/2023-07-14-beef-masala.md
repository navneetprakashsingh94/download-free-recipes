<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>125Ml/4Fl Oz Ghee Or Vegetable Oil</li><li>400G/14Oz Beef Braising Steak, Diced Into 3Cm Chunks</li><li>1 Medium Onion, Diced</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cumin</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Coriander</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Turmeric</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Black Pepper</li><li>1 Tsp Chilli Powder</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Ginger</li><li>3 Cloves</li><li>3 Cardamom Pods (Optional)</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li> 1/2 X 400G/14Oz Can Chopped Tomatoes</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Natural Yoghurt</li><li>1 Tbsp Vinegar</li><li>Salt</li><li>Pinch Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat the oil in a heavy bottom pan and brown the beef chunks in batches. Remove the meat from the pan and cover with cold water. </li><li></li><li>Add the onion and cloves, and cook, stirring, for 5 mins. Return the beef to the pan and add the cumin, coriander, turmeric, pepper, chilli, ginger, cloves, cardamom and cinnamon. Simmer for 1 hour or until the meat is tender. </li><li></li><li>Add the tomatoes, yoghurt and vinegar. Simmer, stirring, for 5 mins. Season with the salt and sugar. </li><li></li><li>Serve hot with rotis, rice or couscous. </li></ul>
