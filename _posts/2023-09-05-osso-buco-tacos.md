<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Piece Beef Osso Buco (Beef Shin On The Marrowbone)</li><li>2 Tbsp Sunflower Oil</li><li>700G/1Lb 9Oz Braising Steak, Cut Into 10Cm/4In Chunks</li><li>5 Guajillo Chillies, Stem And Seeds Removed</li><li>2 Ancho Chillies, Stem And Seeds Removed</li><li>3 Plum Tomatoes, Cut Into Chunks</li><li>1 Onion, Cut Into Chunks</li><li>5 Garlic Cloves, Peeled</li><li>1 Tsp Dried Oregano</li><li>6 Black Peppercorns</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Ginger</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cumin</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>2 Cloves</li><li>1 Tsp White Wine Vinegar</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Sea Salt Flakes</li><li>18 Corn Tortillas, Warmed</li><li>Small Bunch Fresh Coriander, Chopped</li><li> 1/2 Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Limes, Cut Into Wedges</li><li>Spicy Salsa Of Your Choice</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. Heat oil in a heavy based pan over medium heat and sear steak pieces on all sides, 5 6 minutes. Transfer to a plate, add the whole chillies, chillies stems, tomatoes, onion, whole cloves, oregano, peppercorns, ginger, cumin, cinnamon and whole cloves to pan. Cook for 5 minutes, then add vinegar and simmer for 25 30 minutes until reduced by 50. Add a pinch of sea salt. </li><li>2. Heat tortillas in a frying pan, brush them with a tbsp of olive oil and cook for 1 2 minutes until light golden. Remove to a plate and add coriander, onion and meat to tortillas, garnish with lime wedges and salsa. </li></ul>
