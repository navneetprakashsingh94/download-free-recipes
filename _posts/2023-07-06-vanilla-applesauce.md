<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Vanilla Applesauce using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 30 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#Granny Smith apples" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Granny Smith Apples</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#vanilla pod split" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Vanilla Pod Split</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>750G/1Lb 10Oz Granny Smith Apples, Peeled, Cored And Chopped</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Vanilla Pod Split</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Melt butter in saucepan, add apples and sugar. </li><li>Stir over low heat for 3 minutes. </li><li>Add vanilla seeds and bring to the boil. </li><li>Cook, stirring for 2 3 minutes until syrup has thickened slightly. </li><li>Leave to cool slightly before serving. </li></ul>
