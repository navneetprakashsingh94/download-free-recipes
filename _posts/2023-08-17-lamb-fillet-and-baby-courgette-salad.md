<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>400G/14Oz Lamb Leg, Diced Into 2Cm/ 3/4In Dice</li><li>4 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Dried Thyme</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Dried Oregano</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Dried Mint</li><li>1 Lemon, Zest And Juice</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Blue Cheese</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Soured Cream</li><li>1 Tsp Finely Chopped Fresh Mint</li><li>1 Tsp Finely Chopped Fresh Parsley</li><li>6 Baby Courgettes</li><li>2 Little Gem Lettuces</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Wash lamb and cut in strips, place in bowl</li><li>Sprinkle with oil, thyme, oregano, mint, lemon zest and seasoning mix</li><li>Cover and leave for 2 hours. </li><li>Meanwhile make a pesto by combining together cheese, cream, mint and parsley. </li><li>Set aside</li><li>In a separate bowl, cook zucchini in boiling salted water for 2 minutes. </li><li>Drain and refresh under cold water</li><li>Divide baby courgettes, baby lettuces and lamb fillet between 2 plates and drizzle with a little pesto. </li></ul>
