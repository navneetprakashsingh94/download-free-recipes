<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>. Crush the cucumber,sugar and vinegar, put into an ovenproof dish. </li><li>Add the butter,nutmeg,salt and pepper,mayonnaise,mustard,lemon thyme,salt,cayenne and ground almonds. </li><li>Season the turbot,beanfly,slarve at least 4 inches thick. </li><li>Remove the leek stalks,then place in a pan with water until almost covered,and simmer for 20 minutes, drain, save liquid. </li><li>Cook girolles and leeks in melted butter and lemon juice,cool and tame to a pulp. </li><li>Cook the leeks in the almond butter until soft, strain and cool. </li><li>Combine cucumbers,lemon juice, chicken stock,capers,pepper,tomato,thyme,and parsley in a food processor. </li><li>Pour this mixture over the fish,carrots,leeks and girolles. </li><li>Place in oven tray and keep warm till serving time. </li></ul>
