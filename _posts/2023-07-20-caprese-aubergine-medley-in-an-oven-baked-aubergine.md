<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to gas mark 4 / 180 c / 400f</li><li>Wash and dry the aubergines slices, place them onto a baking tray and sprinkle with olive oil. </li><li>Roast for 30 minutes until golden and cooked. </li><li>Then place the tomatoes in the oven with the aubergines, season with salt pepper. </li><li>Roast for 15 minutes until soft. </li><li>In a frying pan, put 1 tbsp oil, 1 clove sprig rosemary, 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar and 1 tsp balsamic vinegar. </li><li>Cook on a medium heat for 2 minutes until the tomatoes start to caramelise. </li><li>Season with salt pepper, stir and leave to cool. </li><li>In a bowl, stir the chopped herbs into the cooled tomato puree, mix well and set aside. </li><li>Slice 3 aubergines in half horizontally, brush both sides with the remaining oil and season with salt and pepper. </li><li>Sprinkle some salt and pepper on the aubergine slices and set aside. </li><li>Mix the tomato puree with the mozzarella balls, parmesan cheese and the 1 tbsp of pecorino. </li><li>Mix the breadcrumbs, ground pepper and 2 tbsp of salt, and toss with the aubergine slices. </li><li>Bake for 12 15 minutes until warmed through and the cheese is beginning to brown. </li><li>For</li></ul>
