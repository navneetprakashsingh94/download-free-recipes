<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>225G/8Oz Beef Mince</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Onion, Peeled And Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Carrot, Peeled And Finely Diced</li><li>1 Green Pepper, Seeded And Finely Diced</li><li>1 Clove Garlic, Peeled And Crushed</li><li>125G/4Oz Smoked Bacon Lardons</li><li>1 Tbsp Dried Mixed Herbs</li><li>400G Tinned Tomatoes</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Red Wine</li><li>Salt And Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat oil in a heavy based pan. </li><li>Add meat and onion, frying until no longer pink, then add carrot, pepper and garlic. </li><li>Add bacon lardons and seasonings and cook over low heat, stirring from time to time, for about 10 minutes. </li><li>Add tomato and wine, cover and simmer for 30 minutes until thickened. </li></ul>
