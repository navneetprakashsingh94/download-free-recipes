<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#beefsteak tomatoes ripe firm tops quarter way tomato seeds pulp spoon hollowed-out tomatoes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Beefsteak Tomatoes Ripe Firm Tops Quarter Way Tomato Seeds Pulp Spoon Hollowed-Out Tomatoes</a> <a href="/tags#salt ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Ground Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#virgin oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Virgin Oil</a> <a href="/tags#onion" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Onion</a> <a href="/tags#basil leaves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Basil Leaves</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#aubergines rounds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Aubergines Rounds</a> <a href="/tags#free-range eggs pinch salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Free-Range Eggs Pinch Salt</a> <a href="/tags#mozzarella" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Mozzarella</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>6 Large, Equally-Sized Beefsteak Tomatoes, Ripe But Firm, Tops Sliced Off A Quarter Of The Way Down The Tomato And Reserved, Seeds And Pulp Scooped Out Using A Spoon And Reserved, Hollowed-Out Tomatoes Reserved</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>9 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Plus Extra For Drizzling</li><li>1 Small Onion, Peeled, Finally Chopped</li><li>2 Handfuls Fresh Basil Leaves</li><li>2-3 Tbsp Plain Flour, For Dredging</li><li>400G/14Oz Small Aubergines, Trimmed, Thinly Sliced Into Rounds</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs, Beaten With A Large Pinch Of Salt</li><li>200G/7Oz Smoked Mozzarella</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 200c</li><li>Cut the aubergines into 1/2 mm slices. Dip the slices into the seasoned flour, then cut the slices lengthways into small cubes</li><li>Grease an ovenproof dish with olive oil and place the cubes in a single layer. </li><li>Season with salt and pepper and place in the preheated oven for about 25 minutes. </li><li>In a pan reserving 1 tablespoon of oil, cook the aubergine slices in the reserved oil for about 5 minutes each side. Set aside on a plate. </li><li>Heat the reserved oil in the pan and add the onion, stirring and making sure that it does not burn. Add the basil and cook for another couple of minutes. </li><li>Remove the tomato seeds from the tomato and add to the onion and basil. Place the tomato mixture into the baking dish in a single layer. </li><li>Pour the tomato mixture over the aubergine slices. </li><li>Put a little of the mozzarella on top of each layer. </li><li>Mix the beaten egg with a pinch of salt and pour over the top. </li><li>Bake in the oven for 30 40 minutes or until the top is crisp and the eggs are set. </li><li>Season with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper, sprinkle with extra fresh basil and serve. </li></ul>
