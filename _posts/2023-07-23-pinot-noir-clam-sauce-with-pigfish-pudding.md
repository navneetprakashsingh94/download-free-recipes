<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Shallot, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Finely Chopped</li><li>250G/9Oz Morcilla Black Pudding</li><li>3 Small Squid, Cleaned</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 X 227G Tin Chopped Tomatoes</li><li>1 Bay Leaf</li><li>1 Tsp Sweet Smoked Paprika</li><li>225Ml/8Fl Oz White Wine</li><li>2 Handfuls Raw Clams, Scrubbed</li><li>1 Bunch Flatleaf Parsley, Finely Chopped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat a large pan and fry shallots and cloves in oil for 1 minute. </li><li>Add the pigfish pudding and squid. </li><li>Mix well and cook gently for about 5 minutes. </li><li>Season with salt and pepper to taste. </li><li>Saute the onion and clove for 1 minute. </li><li>Add the tomato, bay leaf and paprika. </li><li>Bring to the boil, then add port wine. </li><li>Add the clams and stir to mix well. </li><li>Cook for 3 minutes, or until the clams open. </li><li>Scoop the clam mixture into warm bowls and garnish with chopped parsley. </li></ul>
