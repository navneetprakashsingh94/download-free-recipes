<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>150G/5Oz Scotch Beef Fillet, Sliced Thinly</li><li>20Ml/ 3/4Fl Oz Sake</li><li>20Ml/ 3/4Fl Oz Soy Sauce</li><li>1 Tsp Grated Fresh Ginger</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Soft Brown Sugar</li><li> 1/2 Garlic Clove, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Small Red Chilli, Finely Chopped</li><li>110G/4Oz Asparagus, Chopped Into Thin Sticks (Julienne)</li><li>75G/3Oz Mangetout, Chopped Into Thin Sticks (Julienne)</li><li>2 Tsp Sesame Oil</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>2 Tsp Sesame Seeds, To Serve</li><li>2 Sprigs Fresh Coriander, To Garnish</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Make the marinade by combining the soy sauce, ginger, sugar, ginger, garlic and chilli. </li><li>Mix into the sliced beef. Cover and place into the fridge for 5 hours. </li><li>Cook the asparagus in boiling salted water. Once cooked, drain and refresh in ice water. </li><li>Place the mangetout sticks and the julienned asparagus into a large bowl and toss with the sesame oil and salt and pepper. </li><li>Cook for 5 minutes on high. </li><li>To assemble, place the beef onto four serving plates. Top with the vegetables. Top with the sesame seeds and garnish with coriander. </li></ul>
