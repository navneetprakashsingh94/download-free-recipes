<div>Delicious Gluten Free Breakfast Omelette using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#free-range eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Free-Range Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#handful cheddar cheese" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Handful Cheddar Cheese</a> <a href="/tags#Salt pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#slice ham" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Slice Ham</a> <a href="/tags#spinach dry" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Spinach Dry</a> <a href="/tags#spring onions" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Spring Onions</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>Handful Grated Cheddar Or Other Cheese (About 25G/1Oz)</li><li>Salt And Pepper</li><li>1 Slice Cooked Ham, Chopped</li><li>1 Nugget Frozen Spinach, Thawed And Squeezed Dry</li><li>2 Spring Onions, Chopped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Beat the eggs and season with salt and pepper, then pour into a pan coated with cooking spray. Cook over a low heat, constantly rubbing the egg and sides of the pan, until the eggs are thick and browned around the edges. </li><li>Add the ham and spinach, fold over and then cook until set. </li><li>Serve with spring onion. </li></ul>
