<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>350G/12Oz Readymade Shortcrust Pastry</li><li>225G/8Oz Blueberries</li><li>225G/8Oz Blackberries</li><li> 1/2 Lemon, Juice And Zest</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>25G/1Oz Demerara Sugar</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Lightly Beaten</li><li>4 Tbsp Apricot Jam</li><li>4 Tbsp Water</li><li>150G/5Oz Clotted Cream, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 400 f. Line a 9 inch pie plate with pastry. Bake for 8 mins. Remove from the oven and reduce oven heat to 325 f. </li><li>Mix the blueberries, blackberries, lemon juice, cinnamon and sugar. Fill the tart base with the berry mixture. </li><li>Beat the egg in a small bowl. Brush the edges of the pastry with the egg. Bake for 35 40 mins, until the pastry is golden. </li><li>Meanwhile, combine the jam and water in a small saucepan. Stir over a low heat until the jam is melted and the mixture thickens. </li><li>Serve tart warm with the apricot cream and whipped cream. </li></ul>
