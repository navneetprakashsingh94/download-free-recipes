<div>Delicious Lemon Gelato using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 40 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#pint cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pint Cream</a> <a href="/tags#jar lemon curd" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Jar Lemon Curd</a> <a href="/tags#lemon juice zest" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lemon Juice Zest</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>290Ml/ 1/2 Pint Double Cream</li><li>1 Jar (340G/12Oz) Lemon Curd</li><li> 1/2 Lemon, Juice And Zest</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>With a mixer, blend all of the ingredients together until you have a smooth consistency. </li><li>Use immediately or refrigerate for up to 24 hours before using. </li><li>Serve at once with crackers. </li></ul>
