<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>600G/1Lb 5Oz Full-Fat Cream Cheese, At Room Temperature</li><li>175G/6Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>3 Large Free-Range Eggs, At Room Temperature</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Soured Cream, At Room Temperature</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Fine Sea Salt</li><li>25G/1Oz Cornflour</li><li>Blackberries Or Other Berries, To Serve</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Hard Pure Liquorice Pellets</li><li>90G/3 1/4Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>Pinch Fine Sea Salt (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 160c. Place two small square trays in the oven to heat and grease them. </li><li>In a large bowl beat the cream cheese and sugar together until light and fluffy. </li><li>In a separate bowl beat the eggs, room temperature cream and salt until soft and fluffy. </li><li>Gradually beat the cream cheese mixture into the egg mixture and add the cornflour. </li><li>Pour the mixture into a prepared trays in a roasting pan and place on the centre of the oven. Bake for about 1 1/2 hours or until the skewer inserted comes out clean. </li><li>Turn off the oven and allow the cheesecakes to cool in the oven with the door slightly opened for about 15 minutes before removing them from the oven. </li><li>To make the compote combine the blackberries, liquorice, sugar, salt and 1 cup boiling water in a saucepan. </li><li>Heat gently for about 5 minutes until the berries are soft. </li><li>Pour the berries onto the cooled cheesecake and serve hot with a scoop of ice cream. </li></ul>
