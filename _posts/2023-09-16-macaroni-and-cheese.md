<div>Delicious Macaroni And Cheese using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Milk</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#cheese Stilton" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cheese Stilton</a> <a href="/tags#pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pepper</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>400Ml/14Fl Oz Full-Fat Milk</li><li>25G/1Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>25G/1Oz Plain Flour</li><li>75G/2 3/4Oz Blue Cheese (Such As Stilton)</li><li>White Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Bring milk to a boil in a pot. </li><li>Melt butter in a saucepan and blend in flour and cook until bubbly. </li><li>Gradually add the beaten egg and continue to cook until thickened. </li><li>Add the cheese, stirring constantly until melted. </li><li>Add the pepper and keep warm. </li></ul>
