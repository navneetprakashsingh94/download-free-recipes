<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Macaroons using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 60 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#medium egg whites" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Medium Egg Whites</a> <a href="/tags#almond essence" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Almond Essence</a> <a href="/tags#icing sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Icing Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#pinch salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pinch Salt</a> <a href="/tags#ground almonds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ground Almonds</a> <a href="/tags#almonds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Almonds</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>4 Medium Egg Whites</li><li>1 Tsp Almond Essence</li><li>550G/1Lb 4Oz Icing Sugar, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>550G/1Lb 4Oz Ground Almonds</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Flaked Almonds</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 170c. Place egg whites and almond essence in a bowl and whisk till frothy. Slowly beat in icing sugar and salt till smooth and creamy. Stir in ground almonds till combined. </li><li>Roll batter into small balls and roll them in toasted almonds. Place them 2 inches apart onto parchment paper lined cookie sheets. I usually turn them over at least once. </li><li>Bake for 30 35 minutes, till cookies are just lightly browned at the edges. Remove from oven and let stand a minute then remove to a cooling rack. I usually do this in the oven, so they don t get too brown, so i usually leave them in for about 10 minutes. </li></ul>
