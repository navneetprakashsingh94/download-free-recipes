<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>250G/9Oz Plain Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>1 1/2 Tsp Fast-Action Dried Yeast</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Salt</li><li>1 Tbsp Honey</li><li>60Ml/4 Tbsp Tepid Milk</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>85G/3Oz Butter, Softened</li><li>75Ml/5 Tbsp Honey</li><li>2 Pieces Stem Ginger, Very Finely Sliced</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>1-2 Tsp Whisky, Bourbon Or Rum (To Taste)</li><li>Candied Lime Zest</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Butter, Softened</li><li>250G/9Oz Icing Sugar, Well Sifted</li><li>2 Tbsp Honey</li><li>2 Tbsp Ginger Syrup (From The Jar Of Stem Ginger)</li><li>2 Pieces Stem Ginger, Finely Chopped (Optional)</li><li>1-2 Tsp Whisky, Bourbon Or Rum (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>To make the dough in a bowl, mix together the flour, yeast, and salt. </li><li>In a small pot, warm the milk over low heat. </li><li>Place the eggs into a separate small bowl and stir in the warm milk. </li><li>Make a well in the middle of the dry ingredients and pour in the liquids. </li><li>Mix until smooth. </li><li>Cover the bowl tightly with a clean kitchen towel and allow to sit in a warm place for about an hour or until doubled in size. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 350f. </li><li>Butter a 13 x 9 inch baking sheet. </li><li>Combine the yeast and honey. </li><li>Whisk in 1/2 cup of the flour and 1/2 cup of the milk. </li><li>Add the rest of the flour and mix. </li><li>Add half of the melted butter and stir together until a thick dough is formed. </li><li>Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead lightly until it is smooth. </li><li>Shape the dough into a ball and place it into the prepared baking sheet. </li><li>Cover it with a clean cloth and set aside in a warm place to rise until doubled in size about 45 minutes. </li><li>To make the glaze brush the honey over the dough and bake the buns until golden brown around the edges about 20 minutes. </li><li>While the buns are baking prepare the glaze. </li><li>Melt the remaining butter in a small bowl over low heat and allow it to simmer. </li><li>When the butter is melted, pour in the sugar and whisk together. </li><li>Stir in the honey and bourbon</li></ul>
