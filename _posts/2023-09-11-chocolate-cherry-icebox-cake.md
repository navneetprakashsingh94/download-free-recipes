<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Dark Chocolate, Broken Into Pieces</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Full-Fat Milk</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>1 Vanilla Pod, Split</li><li>6 Free-Range Egg Yolks</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>2 Tbsp Cocoa Powder</li><li>1 Tbsp Cornflour</li><li>400G/14Oz Chocolate Cake Or Brownies</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Black Cherry Or Morello Cherry Jam</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Kirsch Or Cherry Brandy</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Pitted Black Cherries</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Amaretti Biscuits</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>Chocolate Curls, To Decorate</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Melt the chocolate pieces over double boiler. </li><li>Combine milk, cream, vanilla pod and seeds into a medium saucepan over a medium heat. </li><li>When hot, remove pod and add the milk chocolate mixture. </li><li>Return to the heat and cook, stirring, for 5 minutes. </li><li>Cover the pan and set aside. </li><li>Whisk together egg yolks, sugar and cocoa. </li><li>Add to the milk chocolate mixture and cook until thick enough to coat the back of a metal spoon and coats the back of a spoon. </li><li>Remove pan from heat and let cool. </li><li>Stir in the cocoa powder and cocoa powder. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 170 degrees celcius. </li><li>Butter a 20cm square cake tin and line with baking parchment. </li><li>Spread 1 cup of the brownie crumbles over the bottom of the tin. </li><li>Spread the cherry morello jam over the brownie crumbles. </li><li>Drizzle with cherry brandy. </li><li>Sprinkle with cherries. </li><li>Cover with a layer of the mousse. </li><li>Top with another layer of brownie crumbles. </li><li>In a small bowl, blend the cream, remaining brownie crumbles and chocolate. </li><li>Spread this mixture over the top of the cake. </li><li>Finish with the last layer of mousse and cherries. </li><li>Chill for 4 hours in the refrigerator. </li><li>Remove from the tin and arrange the chocolate curls over the top and serve. </li></ul>
