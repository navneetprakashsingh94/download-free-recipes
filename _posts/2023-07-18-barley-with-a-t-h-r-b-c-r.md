<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Chicken Stock</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Single Cream</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>30G/1Oz Butter</li><li>1 Shallot, Finely Chopped</li><li>225G/8Oz Pearl Barley</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz White Wine</li><li>800Ml/1 Pint 8Fl Oz Chicken Stock</li><li>2 Bunches English Asparagus, Woody Ends Snapped Off And Reserved</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Bacon Lardons</li><li>85G/3Oz Cooked And Shelled Broad Beans</li><li>3 Tomatoes, Skinned And Seeds Removed, Cut Into 1Cm/ 1/2In Cubes</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 180 degrees celsius. </li><li>Put your broth in a saucepan on medium heat, add barley and simmer, covered, stirring occasionally, until barley is quite tender, about 25 minutes. Add burgundy and bring to a boil, then add chicken stock slowly, stirring often, until it has mostly been absorbed by the barley, about 20 25 minutes. Remove the barley from heat, stir in the bacon, asparagus, tomatoes, and cream and continue to cook, stirring often, until everything is heated through. </li><li>Serve immediately. </li></ul>
