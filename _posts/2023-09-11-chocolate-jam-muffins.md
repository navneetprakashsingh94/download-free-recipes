<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Chocolate Jam Muffins using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 13 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#jam" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Jam</a> <a href="/tags#chocolate spread" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chocolate Spread</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Milk</a> <a href="/tags#egg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg</a> <a href="/tags#honey syrup" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Honey Syrup</a> <a href="/tags#flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Flour</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Jam (Any Will Do), Plus Extra For Serving</li><li>2 Tbsp Chocolate Spread, Plus Extra For Serving</li><li>3 Tbsp Oil</li><li>3 Tbsp Milk</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg</li><li>2 Tbsp Honey Or Golden Syrup</li><li>4 Tbsp Self-Raising Flour</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 180c. </li><li>Line a muffin pan with muffin papers. </li><li>Put the jam in a heatproof bowl, let it stand for a few minutes, then mix in the chocolate spread. </li><li>Beat the oil, milk, egg and honey syrup together and stir in the flour. </li><li>Then pour into the muffin tins and bake for 20 minutes. </li></ul>
