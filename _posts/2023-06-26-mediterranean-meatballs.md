<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>80G/2 3/4Oz Breadcrumbs</li><li>2 Tbsp Milk</li><li>1 Medium Free-Range Egg</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Beef Mince</li><li>2 Tbsp Finely Chopped Fresh Parsley</li><li>2 Tbsp Finely Chopped Fresh Basil</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Feta</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Peeled And Cut In Half</li><li>400G/14Oz Cherry Tomatoes, Cut In Half Lengthways</li><li>1 Medium Shallot, Finely Chopped (Approx. 50G/1 3/4Oz)</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Tbsp Balsamic Vinegar</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>400G/14Oz Dried Spaghetti</li><li>2 Tbsp Butter</li><li>1 Tbsp Roughly Chopped Fresh Parsley</li><li>1 Tbsp Roughly Chopped Fresh Basil</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Feta, Crumbled</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Combine breadcrumbs, milk and egg in bowl, mix well. Add beef and mix until just combined. Add herbs, cheese and 1/4 cup hot water and gently combine. Shape into balls or small meatballs. </li><li></li><li>Combine tomatoes, shallot, oil and vinegar in bowl. Add salt and pepper to taste. Refrigerate for at least 15 minutes. </li><li></li><li>Cook pasta in boiling salted water for 10 12 minutes, drain. Melt butter in frying pan. Add tomato mix and simmer for 3 4 minutes. Remove from heat and add parsley and basil. Add spaghetti and toss well. Sprinkle with feta. </li></ul>
