<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>2 Onions, Chopped</li><li>2 Celery Sticks, Finely Chopped</li><li>675G/1Lb 8Oz Beef Mince</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Crushed</li><li>2 Tbsp Tomato Puree</li><li>2 X 400G Tins Chopped Tomatoes</li><li>350-500Ml/12-18Fl Oz Beef Stock</li><li>2 Tsp Worcestershire Sauce</li><li>2 Tsp Redcurrant Jelly</li><li>1 Tbsp Butter</li><li>250G/9Oz Chestnut Mushrooms, Sliced</li><li>1 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Thyme Leaves</li><li>225G/8Oz Penne Pasta</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Cheddar, Grated</li><li>30G/1Oz Parmesan, Grated</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>Green Or Tomato Salad, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan and cook the onions and celery over a medium heat for 3 4 minutes or until soft. </li><li>Add the beef and cook for 2 3 minutes. </li><li>Add the cloves and tomato puree and cook for 2 3 minutes. </li><li>Add the tomatoes, stock, worcestershire sauce and redcurrant jelly. </li><li>Bring to the boil, reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes or until the sauce thickens slightly. </li><li>Melt the butter in a pan and saute the mushrooms and thyme until the mushrooms begin to soften and then remove from the heat and set aside. </li><li>Cook the pasta in boiling salted water according to package directions. </li><li>Drain and put back into the pan, add the cheeses and sauce and toss well. </li><li>Season to taste. </li><li>Serve with salad on the side. </li></ul>
