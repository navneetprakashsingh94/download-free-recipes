<a href="/tags#milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Milk</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#honey" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Honey</a> <a href="/tags#yeast" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Yeast</a> <a href="/tags#rye flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Rye Flour</a> <a href="/tags#plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#asparagus" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Asparagus</a> <a href="/tags#cod fillets cubes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cod Fillets Cubes</a> <a href="/tags#truffle" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Truffle</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#lamb lettuce" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lamb Lettuce</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>750Ml/26Fl Oz Full-Fat Milk</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>70G/2 1/2Oz Honey</li><li>25G/1Oz Fresh Yeast</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Rye Flour</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>6 Green Asparagus, Trimmed, Peeled And Halved</li><li>2 X 150G/5 1/2Oz Cod Fillets, Skin-On, Pin-Boned And Cut Into 2Cm/ 3/4In Cubes</li><li>1 Tbsp Fresh Truffle</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>25G/1Oz Lamb'S Lettuce, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat milk, butter and honey in a saucepan on medium heat until warm. Add yeast and combine. Stir in flour. Stir until combined. Knead on a floured surface until smooth and elastic. Transfer to an oiled bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Let stand in a warm place for 45 mins. </li><li>Meanwhile, boil, steam or microwave asparagus until tender. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup cooking liquid. Season. </li><li>To make a truffle butter, heat a small skillet on medium heat. Fry cod until browned all over. Drain. </li><li>Cut the stems off asparagus and place, cut side down, on a baking tray lined with parchment paper. Top with remaining asparagus and fry in oil until golden. Add asparagus to pan with fish. Season to taste. Arrange asparagus on a serving platter with truffle butter and lamb lettuce. Serve hot. </li></ul>
