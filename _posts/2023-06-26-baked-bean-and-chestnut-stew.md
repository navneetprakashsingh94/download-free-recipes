<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Small Brussels Sprouts, Peeled And Halved</li><li>180G/6 1/4Oz Ready-To-Eat Chestnuts, Halved</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Pancetta Cubes</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Saute rutabagas, sprouts, chestnuts in oil for 2 3 minutes. </li><li>Add the pancetta and simmer for 3 4 minutes, or until the pancetta is crisp. </li><li>Salt to taste and serve hot. </li></ul>
