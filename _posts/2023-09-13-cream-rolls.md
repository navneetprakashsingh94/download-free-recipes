<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Flour</a> <a href="/tags#milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Milk</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp yeast" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Yeast</a> <a href="/tags#eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#oz cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Cream</a> <a href="/tags#tsp salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Salt</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>280G/10Oz Strong White Flour</li><li>180Ml/6Fl Oz Milk</li><li>1 Tbsp Dried Yeast</li><li>2 Eggs, Separated</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>1 Tsp Salt</li><li>30G/1Oz Butter For Frying</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the dough</li><li>Sift flour and salt together</li><li>In small saucepan heat the milk and add butter and briskly stir until butter has melted</li><li>Dissolve yeast in the milk, add eggs and cream to yeast mixture, add flour and mix into a soft dough</li><li>Turn out the dough onto a floured board and knead for 15 minutes</li><li>Roll to about 1 cm thick and cut into triangles</li><li>Melt butter in a pot and cook each croissant in the hot butter till golden brown</li></ul>
