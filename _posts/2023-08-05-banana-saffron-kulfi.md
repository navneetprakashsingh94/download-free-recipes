<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#pint butter butter sieve" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pint Butter Butter Sieve</a> <a href="/tags#semolina" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Semolina</a> <a href="/tags#sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#medium banana ripe" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Medium Banana Ripe</a> <a href="/tags#pint milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pint Milk</a> <a href="/tags#saffron strands tbsp milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Saffron Strands Tbsp Milk</a> <a href="/tags#pinch nutmeg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pinch Nutmeg</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp sultanas" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Sultanas</a> <a href="/tags#cardamom seeds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cardamom Seeds</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp almonds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Almonds</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>150 Ml/ 1/4 Pint Clarified Butter, (Unsalted Butter, Melted And Strained Through A Sieve)</li><li>125G/5 Oz Semolina</li><li>100G/4Oz Sugar</li><li>1 Medium Banana, Ripe</li><li>550Ml/1 Pint Milk, Warmed</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Saffron Strands, Soaked In 1 Tbsp Milk</li><li>Pinch Of Grated Nutmeg</li><li>1 Tbsp Sultanas</li><li>1 Tsp Cardamom Seeds, Crushed</li><li>1 Tbsp Flaked Almonds, Lightly Toasted</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Soak the bananas in the milk. </li><li>Add the semolina, sugar, cardamom and nutmeg to a bowl and mix. </li><li>When the milk is hot, remove it from the heat, add the bananas and the saffron strands. </li><li>Heat and stir gently until the bananas are soft. </li><li>Cool before making into small balls. </li><li>Spread out the butter on a flat pan and heat under a high flame till the butter is golden brown. </li><li>Dip each ball in the butter and then dredge with the semolina mixture, coating evenly. </li><li>Place all the balls on to a baking tray. </li><li>When done, remove the pan and sprinkle a spoon of sultanas and almonds over the kulfi. </li><li>Place the kulfi on serving plates and serve. </li></ul>
