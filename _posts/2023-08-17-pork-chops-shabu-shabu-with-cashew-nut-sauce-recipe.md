<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place cashews in a small, dry frying pan, over low heat, and stir for 5 7 minutes until they start to brown. </li><li>Remove from the heat, drain off the excess water and place in a food processor with ginger, cloves, lemon juice, sweetner, and fish sauce. </li><li>Process until the mixture is the consistency of mayonnaise, then transfer to a jar and refrigerate until ready to use. </li><li>In a mortar and pestle, grind chillies, basil leaves, and banana shallots with a pinch of salt and pepper until fine. </li><li>Add to jar of cashew nut sauce. </li><li>Mix well and refrigerate for 2 days. </li><li>For the spicy pork in a large resealable container, add gochujang, soy sauce, honey, ginger and cloves and mix well. </li><li>Add pork chops to marinade and shake well to coat. </li><li>Marinate in the fridge for 4 to 6 hours. </li><li>Preheat grill to high. </li><li>Remove pork chops from marinade and place on hot grill. </li><li>Cook for 10 minutes or until cooked to your liking, turning once. </li><li>Remove from grill and serve on bed of lettuce, cucumber, mint and coriander leaves. </li><li>Pour off any excess marina</li></ul>
