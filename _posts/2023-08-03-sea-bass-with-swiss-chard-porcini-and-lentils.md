<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>70G/2 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>2 Sea Bass Fillets, About 175G/6Oz Each</li><li>1 Fennel Bulb, Core Removed, Sliced And Blanched</li><li>1 Fresh Porcini Mushroom, Trimmed And Sliced</li><li>2 Fresh Thyme Sprigs</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Dry White Vermouth</li><li>400G/14Oz Rainbow Chard, Trimmed And Washed</li><li>1 Tsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>Sea Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Cooked Umbrian Lentils (Lentils De Castellucio), To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Put sea bass fillets on heavy plate, cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 10 minutes. </li><li>Heat saute pan on medium high heat. </li><li>Pat sea bass dry with paper towel, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and season with olive oil. </li><li>Sear fillets on both sides, do not move them. </li><li>Remove from heat, place fillets on serving plate, drizzle with oil and discard thyme. </li><li>In saute pan, saute fennel in 2 tblsp butter until just tender and lightly browned. </li><li>Return sea bass fillets to pan, add porcini, and thyme sprigs. </li><li>Deglaze pan with vermouth, reduce and reduce. </li><li>Pour over fish. </li><li>Meanwhile, in bowl, combine swiss chard and remaining butter. </li><li>Add to saute pan. </li><li>Stir and toss until wilted. </li><li>Season with salt and pepper. </li><li>Serve fish over lentils. </li></ul>
