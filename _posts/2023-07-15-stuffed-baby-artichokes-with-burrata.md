<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>8 Banana Shallots</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Olive Oil, Plus Extra For Drizzling</li><li>10G/ 1/4Oz Thyme</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>1 Bottle Red Wine, Preferably Merlot</li><li>500Ml/18Fl Oz Veal Stock</li><li>10 Baby Artichokes</li><li>1 Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>6 Slices Prosciutto Cotto</li><li>2 Burrata</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For thyme lemon butter saute onions in oil, then add the thyme. </li><li>Add the wine. </li><li>Let cook for 1 2 minutes on high heat until evaporated. </li><li>Add the veal stock and let cook for an additional 30 minutes or so. </li><li>For artichokes cut the artichokes in half and gently remove the choke. </li><li>Cook for a few minutes in boiling salted water. </li><li>Add a drizzle of fresh lemon juice to the pan and allow it to cook down. </li><li>As the artichokes are cooked, place on a small baking pan and place in a 350 degree oven. </li><li>For cheese filling mix the cheese with the shallot and thyme and season with salt and pepper. </li><li>If you don t have a food processor a hand blender works fine. </li><li>If you have a mixer you can use your food processor. </li><li>If you don t have a food processor, it s fine to simply puree the cheese and shallots in a food processor. </li><li>Set aside. </li><li>Heat a large saute pan over medium high heat and add 1 tablespoon butter. </li><li>Add the artichokes and season with salt and pepper. </li><li>Saute until slightly softened and slightly browned. </li><li>Next, add the balsamic vinegar and cook until reduced by half. </li><li>Turn off the heat and add the strained veal stock. </li><li>Combine well. </li><li>Add the remaining butter and stir well until melted. </li><li>To serve, place the baby artichokes on the bottom of 4 shallow bowls. </li><li>Put a slice of cotto cheese on each artichoke. </li><li>Pour the sauce over the artichokes and top with a spoonful of the burrata. </li></ul>
