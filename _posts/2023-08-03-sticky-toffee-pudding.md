<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Unsalted Butter, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Soft Dark Brown Demerara Sugar</li><li>175G/6Oz Pitted Dried Dates</li><li>1 Tbsp Golden Syrup</li><li>2 Tbsp Black Treacle</li><li>1 Tbsp Vanilla Bean Paste</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>200G/7Oz Self-Raising Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>1 Tbsp Bicarbonate Of Soda</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>80G/2 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Soft Dark Brown Sugar</li><li>1 Tbsp Black Treacle</li><li>1 Tbsp Golden Syrup</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 160c/360f grease and line a 6 cup capacity ovenproof dish with baking paper. </li><li>Place butter, sugar, dates, dark karo syrup and treacle in a large saucepan and heat gently until sugar and dates are dissolved. </li><li>Remove from heat and stir in vanilla, eggs, flour and bicarbonate of soda. </li><li>Beat until smooth, then pour into prepared dish, smoothing top with a metal spoon. </li><li>Place in oven and bake for 1 1/2 hours or until pudding is cooked through. </li><li>For sauce, place all ingredients in saucepan and bring to the boil, stirring constantly. </li><li>Simmer for 10 minutes or until thickened. </li><li>Remove pudding from oven and spread sauce over top. </li><li>Serve with ice cream, whipped cream, whipped cream or creme fraiche. </li></ul>
