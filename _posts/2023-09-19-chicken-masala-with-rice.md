<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Make the curry</li><li>Mix the oil and chopped onion in a bowl with the ground ginger, garlic, tomato paste, the tsp of spices and 2 tbsp of the water. </li><li>Add the chicken and stir to coat in the spices, adding some additional water if necessary. </li><li>Put the lid on the bowl, and marinate in the fridge for a day or more, turning the chicken over at least once or twice. </li><li>Put a large heavy pan, preferably one of the large ones, with enough water to come halfway up the sides of the pan. </li><li>Season the water with salt, add the spices and bring to the boil. </li><li>Lower the heat and simmer gently for 15 mins, then add the coriander and chillies. </li><li>Cook for 2 minutes, then remove the pan from the heat and add the butter. </li><li>Remove the chicken from the refrigerator and slice the meat into strips. </li><li>Cook the rice according to the pack instructions. </li><li>Heat the pan again over a medium heat, and add the butter. </li><li>Add the chicken and stir until just browned, then add the tomatoes and juice. </li><li>Stir for 30 seconds, then add salt and pepper to taste. </li><li>Turn down the heat and continue to cook for 10 mins until the chicken is cooked and tender. </li><li>Meanwhile, make the yoghurt. </li><li>Heat a frying pan, add a little olive oil and brown the cumin seeds for 2 3 mins. </li><li>Then add the cucumbers and</li></ul>
