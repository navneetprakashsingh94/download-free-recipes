<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>9 Cherry Tomatoes, Halved</li><li>1 Small Garlic Clove, Thinly Sliced</li><li>2 Tsp Good-Quality Olive Oil</li><li>1 Tsp Balsamic Vinegar</li><li>5 Basil Leaves, Torn, Plus A Few Extra To Serve If You Like</li><li>5 Pitted Olives, Sliced</li><li>1 Large Free-Range Egg</li><li>1 Tsp Small Capers, Rinsed</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>Crusty Bread Or Warm Ciabatta, To Serve (Optional)</li><li>1 Thick Slice Of Bread From A Small Loaf (Such As Ciabatta) - About 40G/1 1/2Oz</li><li>2 Tsp Good-Quality Olive Oil</li><li>2 Slices Cured Ham, Such As Like Prosciutto Or Parma Ham</li><li>2 Tbsp Stock, Made With A Pinch Of Vegetable Or Chicken Stock Cube</li><li>1 Large Free-Range Egg</li><li>20G/ 3/4Oz Gruyere Cheese, Coarsely Grated</li><li>Chopped Parsley Or Chives, To Serve (Optional)</li><li>Small Handful Chopped Kale, Stalks Discarded</li><li> 1/2 Lemon</li><li>75G/3Oz Creme Fraiche (Half Or Whole Fat)</li><li>1 Tsp Grainy Mustard</li><li>50G/2Oz Hot-Smoked Mackerel, Peppered Works Well</li><li>1 Large Free-Range Egg</li><li>Wholemeal Toast Or Rye Bread</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Pre heat oven to 200c. </li><li>Mix together all the ingredients in a small bowl. </li><li>Arrange the tomatoes and tomatoes on a roasting tray and bake for 10 15 minutes. </li><li>While the tomatoes are cooling, toss the bread with a splash of oil and cook in the oven for a couple of minutes until slightly crisp. </li><li>Cut the bread into cubes and arrange on a large serving platter. </li><li>Toss the bread with the extra oil and toast in the oven for about 5 minutes until lightly golden. </li><li>Place in a bowl with the ham prosciutto and keep warm. </li><li>Cook the stock cube until the stock comes to the boil, then immediately pour over the bread and let rest for 3 minutes. </li><li>Remove the cube and place in a saucepan with the egg, and simmer for 1 minute. </li><li>Remove from the heat and whisk in the cheese, parsley, chives, kale and lemon. </li><li>To serve, spoon the bread salad into individual plates and place the mackerel on top and spoon over the sauce. </li><li>Drizzle with creme fraiche and sprinkle with grainy mustard, and serve immediately with toast. </li></ul>
