<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place the tangelos and ancho chillies in a hot pan with a drizzle of oil. Cover with a lid, let the chillies sweat for a few minutes until the skins split. Remove from the pan and allow the chilies to cool. Place the chilies and oregano in a blender and blend until smooth. Set aside. </li><li>Mix the vinegar and lime juice together and pour over the onions and garlic and leave to marinate for a few hours. </li><li>Remove the chilies from the fridge and add the onions, garlic and ancho chilies to a blender with 1 tbsp oil, vinegar and a splash of lime juice. Blend until smooth. </li><li>Season the steak with salt and pepper and add the steak to the veg and mix well. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large frying pan and add the steak mixture. Cook on medium high heat for 5 minutes, turning frequently. Remove the meat and keep warm. </li><li>Add the remaining tbsp oil, asparagus and spring onions to the pan and stir fry for 3 4 minutes. </li><li>Add the corn and tomatoes and continue to stir fry for another minute. Add the sauce and cook gently for 2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper. </li><li>To serve, place a tortilla on a plate with 2 strips of steak, a few tomato and corn mixture, the avacado and juice of 1 lime</li></ul>
