<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Pecan Nuts</li><li>170G/5 3/4Oz Plain Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Chilled Unsalted Butter, Cut Into Cubes, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>3 Large Free-Range Eggs</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Pumpkin Puree (Available Online And In The Baking Section Of Some Large Supermarkets)</li><li>85G/3Oz Soft Dark Brown Sugar</li><li>1 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>1 1/2 Blood Oranges, Zest Of Both And Juice Of One</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Evaporated Milk</li><li>2 Pasteurised Egg Whites</li><li>115G/4Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>25G/1Oz Pecan Nuts</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Blood Orange Juice (Available In Large Supermarkets)</li><li>100G/3 1/2Fl Oz Granulated Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 180c. Butter a 23cm round ring tin and line with baking paper. </li><li>Lightly toast the macadamias until a light golden brown and the skins are cracked. Let cool. Grind in a food processor to a fine powder. </li><li>In a food processor, combine the ground macadamia nuts, flour, salt, butter and eggs. Pulse until the mixture resembles fine breadcrumbs. Add the pumpkin puree and process to combine. Transfer the mixture to a large bowl and add the sugar and cinnamon. Stir in the zest juice. Add the milk and stir to combine. Transfer the dough to the prepared tin and smooth out the top. </li><li>Bake for 40 to 45 minutes until the edges are browned and set. Turn the oven off but leave the tart in the oven for another hour to cool in the tin. </li><li>Meanwhile, beat the egg whites in a bowl until the whites form stiff peaks. Gradually add the sugar, beating until the peaks form. </li><li>Remove the tart from the oven and set aside to cool for 5 minutes. Set aside and place the macadamia nuts on the base of the meringue. </li><li>Place the cooled tart on a plate and pour the juice on top. Dust with sugar, decorate with the macadamia nuts and serve warm or at room temperature with whipped cream. </li></ul>
