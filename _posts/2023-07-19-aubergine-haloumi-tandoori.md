<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Marinade the aubergines in the spiced tamarind paste for two hours. </li><li>Heat the oil in a heavy based pan and gently saute the onions, cloves and cinnamon until golden. </li><li>Add the tomatoes and tamarind paste and cook for 5 minutes. </li><li>Stir in the honey and season. </li><li>Meanwhile, cook the basmati rice in plenty of salted water according to packet instructions. </li><li>To cook the halloumi, cut in slices 1/4 inch thick and grill on both sides until browned. </li><li>Mix the harissa, honey and lime juice and divide the dressing between 4 bowls. </li><li>Layer the aubergines in a dish, season with salt and pepper and mash slightly with the back of a fork. </li><li>Set aside. </li><li>Heat the oil in a non stick pan over a high heat. </li><li>Add the aubergines and cook for 2 3 minutes until just cooked through. </li><li>Season the halloumi with salt, pepper and harissa. </li><li>Drain excess oil from the pan and add the onions. </li><li>Saute for 1 minute. </li><li>Add the remaining ingredients, season and mix well. </li><li>Place the rice in a frying pan, sprinkle with salt and roast until golden brown. </li><li>Set aside. </li><li>To finish, arrange a portion of rice on the bottom of each plate. </li><li>Top with the aubergines and halloumi. </li><li>Spoon the</li></ul>
