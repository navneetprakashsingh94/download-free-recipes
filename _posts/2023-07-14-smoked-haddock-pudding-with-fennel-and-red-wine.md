<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Whole Milk</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Smoked Haddock, Preferably Arbroath Smokie Haddock</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Smoked Salmon</li><li>1 Bay Leaf</li><li>10 Black Peppercorns</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>4 Free-Range Eggs, Beaten</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Mature Cheddar Cheese, Grated</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li> 1/2 Butterhead Lettuce, Leaves Removed And Washed</li><li> 1/2 Head Fennel, Thinly Sliced Lengthways On A Mandolin</li><li>5 Radishes, Sliced</li><li>20Ml/ 3/4Fl Oz French Dressing</li><li>30G/1Oz Honey</li><li>2 Tbsp White Balsamic Vinegar</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Vegetable Oil</li><li>1 Shallot, Finely Chopped</li><li> 1/2 Garlic Clove, Thinly Sliced</li><li>1 Tsp Chopped Fresh Basil</li><li>1 Tsp Chopped Fresh Thyme Leaves</li><li>1 Tsp Chopped Chives</li><li>1 Tsp Chopped Fresh Parsley</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Pour the milk into a pan. Add the haddock, the smoked salmon, the bay leaf, peppercorns and bring to the boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 4 minutes. Remove the haddock from the milk, flake the fish and set aside. Strain the milk and return to the pan and simmer for a further 3 minutes. </li><li>Meanwhile, cream the butter and eggs together. Gradually add the flour, mix well and stir in the cheese, salt and pepper. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 325 degrees f and butter a 2 cup pudding mold. Pour the flour mixture into the mold and place in a roasting pan. Add enough boiling water to come halfway up the sides of the mold. Bake for 45 minutes, or until set. </li><li>To make the dressing, whisk the honey, vinegar and oil together and season. Set aside. </li><li>To serve, place the fennel in a serving bowl. Scatter with the radishes and the smoked haddock. Sprinkle with dressing. </li></ul>
