<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#almonds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Almonds</a> <a href="/tags#sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#pint cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pint Cream</a> <a href="/tags#free-range eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Free-Range Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#icing sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Icing Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp vodka" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Vodka</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp cherry syrup" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Cherry Syrup</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Blanched Almonds</li><li>75G/3Oz Granulated Sugar</li><li>Olive Oil, For Oiling</li><li>600Ml/1 Pint Double Cream</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs, Separated</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Icing Sugar, Sifted</li><li>2 Tbsp Vodka</li><li>1 Tbsp Cherry Syrup (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 200 degrees celsius. </li><li>Grease and line a bundt cake tin. </li><li>Toast slivered almonds, then add sugar and oil. </li><li>Mix well. </li><li>Separate egg whites. </li><li>Beat egg yolks and 2 teaspoons sugar together until thick and lemon coloured. </li><li>Add egg yolks and cooled almond mixture to egg whites. </li><li>Beat egg whites until they form soft peaks. </li><li>Add remaining sugar and beat until stiff peaks form. </li><li>Fold in egg whites into almond mixture, until well combined. </li><li>Pour batter into prepared pan, smooth out surface and bake 30 35 minutes, until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. </li><li>Remove from oven, set on rack and let cool for a few minutes. </li><li>After cool in pan, remove from pan, then frost cake with a little butter. </li><li>Place icing sugar into a large ziploc bag and squeeze out a small amount. </li><li>You will need 1/2 cup of icing sugar. </li><li>Put 2 teaspoons of icing sugar and 3 teaspoons of water into a small bowl. </li><li>Stir in vodka. </li><li>This syrup will stain the spoon. </li><li>Dip a thin knife in the icing to loosen the sugar and cut through the icing to make small squares. </li><li>Dip the knife in the water to smooth out the icing. </li><li>Once you re done, gently turn your knife back over and wipe the edge of the knife clean. </li><li>Spoon over the filling and let stand to dry. </li><li>Add the cherry syrup to one spoon and brush the cherries down the sides. </li><li>Decorate the icing with remaining cherry syrup. </li><li>Chill for a couple of hours before serving. </li><li>Store the top in a clear container. </li></ul>
