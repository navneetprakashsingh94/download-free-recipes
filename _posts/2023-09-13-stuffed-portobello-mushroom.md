<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Anchovies</li><li>4 Garlic Cloves</li><li>Milk, To Cover</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Olive Oil</li><li>2 Blood Oranges, 1 Juiced, 1 Peeled And Segmented</li><li>1 Fennel Bulb, Thinly Sliced Lengthways</li><li>1 Small Red Chicory, Thinly Sliced Lengthways</li><li>1 Braeburn Apple, Peeld, Cored And Thinly Sliced</li><li>1 Celery Heart, Thinly Sliced Lengthways</li><li>2 Large Bulb Spring Onions, Thinly Sliced Lengthways</li><li>2 Baby Peppers, Thinly Sliced Lengthways</li><li>2 Coloured Baby Heritage Carrots, Peeled And Thinly Sliced Lengthways</li><li>3 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Tbsp Vinegar</li><li>1 Whole Burrata</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place anchovies and cloves in a heatproof container. </li><li>Scald milk and add to the anchovies. </li><li>Cover and let stand for 10 mins, drain. </li><li>Whisk in cream, season with salt and pepper, bring to boil, remove from heat and whisk in butter. </li><li>Heat oil in frying pan, add halved mushrooms and cook for 8 10 mins until golden. </li><li>Remove and set aside. </li><li>Add the oranges, fennel, apples, artichoke hearts, spring onions, carrots and peppers to the pan, season with salt and pepper and cook for 5 mins. </li><li>Remove from heat. </li><li>Cut each mushroom in half horizontally and scoop out flesh and seeds. </li><li>Add filling to mushrooms and fill the cavity. </li><li>Mix the oil and vinegar in a small bowl and season with salt and pepper. </li><li>Brush oil and vinegar over mushroom to coat, arrange mushrooms on a plate and drizzle with dressing. </li><li>Serve with burrata. </li></ul>
