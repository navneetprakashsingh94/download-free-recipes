<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>900G/2Lb Mixed Fruit, Raisins, Sultanas, Chopped Nuts And Walnuts</li><li>350G/12Oz Sugar</li><li>350G/12Oz Margarine</li><li>1 Tsp Bicarbonate Of Soda</li><li>250Ml/9Fl Oz Cold Water</li><li>250Ml/9Fl Oz Brandy</li><li>240G/8 1/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>240G/8 1/2Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>4 Free-Range Eggs, Beaten</li><li>2 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>1 Tsp Mixed Spice</li><li>1 X 200G/7Oz Tub Glace Cherries, Chopped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Bring margarine, water, soda and brandy to the boil and cool. </li><li>Sift flours, baking powder and spice together into a large bowl and make a well in the centre. </li><li>Add eggs and cooled ingredients. </li><li>Blend into flours and raisin mixture. </li><li>Bake in an 18 x 18cm tube tin for 2 1/4 hours at 300 f or until a skewer comes out clean. </li><li>Leave in the tin for 30 minutes, then turn onto a wire rack to cool completely before slicing. </li><li>If you want to decorate with glace cherries, decorate top of cake with a couple of cherries. </li><li>The cake is best the next day, preferably after a week or two of infusions. </li></ul>
