<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#beetroot" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Beetroot</a> <a href="/tags#tin chickpeas beans" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tin Chickpeas Beans</a> <a href="/tags#cloves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cloves</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp lemon juice bottle" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Lemon Juice Bottle</a> <a href="/tags#pinch salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pinch Salt</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp seeds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Seeds</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Cooked Beetroot</li><li>400G Tin Chickpeas, Drained And Rinsed (Alternatively Use Butter Beans)</li><li>2 Large Garlic Cloves, Chopped</li><li>1 Tbsp Lemon Juice (Fresh Or From A Bottle)</li><li>Pinch Of Salt</li><li>2 Tbsp Oil</li><li>1 Tbsp Mixed Seeds (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Wash the beetroot. </li><li>Wrap in foil and put into a preheated 200c oven for about 1 hour. </li><li>Peel the beetroot and cut into 4cm cubes. </li><li>Add in to a pot along with tin of chickpeas, cloves, lemon juice, salt and 2 tbsp boiling water. </li><li>Cover with a lid and cook for about 1 hour. </li><li>Then remove the lid, add in oil and tbsp seeds and cook for 10 minutes. </li><li>It should be crunchy and tasty. </li><li>Pour the mixture into a serving dish and serve immediately. </li></ul>
