<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>375G/13Oz Plain Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>200G/7Oz Salted Butter, Chilled And Diced</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Icing Sugar</li><li>1 Large Free-Range Egg, Separated, Plus 1 Large Free-Range Egg Yolk</li><li>2 Bramley Apples, Peeled, Cored And Diced</li><li>1 Lemon, Zest And Juice</li><li>75G/2 3/4Oz Granulated Sugar, Plus Extra For Sweetening And Dusting</li><li>4 Eating Apples (A Sharp, Crisp Variety Such As Braeburn), Peeled, Cored And Cut Into Large Chunks Or Slices</li><li>250-300G/9-10 1/2Oz Blackberries (Frozen Is Fine)</li><li>Vanilla Ice Cream, Clotted Or Single Cream, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. </li><li>Sift flour into a large bowl. </li><li>Add butter and icing sugar and rub them into the flour using your fingertips until the mixture resembles fine breadcrumbs</li><li>Beat in the egg yolk, add enough of the iced water to make a soft dough</li><li>Turn onto a lightly floured surface and knead briefly until smooth. </li><li>Wrap in plastic and chill for 30 minutes. </li><li>2. </li><li>Heat oven to moderate 180 c, gas mark 3. </li><li>Roll out pastry on a lightly floured surface until it is about 3/4cm thick. </li><li>Lay pastry over a 20cm fluted flan tin and press it to within one centimeter of the edge. </li><li>Trim the excess pastry along the edges then use a fork or knife to crimp around the edge. </li><li>3. </li><li>Place on a baking tray lined with baking paper and bake for 15 20 minutes or until golden. </li><li>Meanwhile, to make the apples, mix the apples, lemon zest juice and caster sugar. </li><li>Toss together and allow to rest in the fridge for 1 2 hours. </li><li>4. </li><li>Meanwhile, preheat oven to 220 c, gas mark 6. </li><li>Add the apple chunks and blackberries to the tin. </li><li>Then add the apple mixture and combine. </li><li>Place in the preheated oven for 30 35 minutes or until cooked. </li><li>5. </li><li>Set aside to cool. </li><li>6. </li><li>To serve, place apple mixture in a piping bag with a small star nozzle and pipe into the tart case. </li><li>Top with ice cream and serve. </li></ul>
