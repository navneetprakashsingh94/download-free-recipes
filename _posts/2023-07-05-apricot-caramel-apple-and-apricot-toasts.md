<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#cooking apples" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cooking Apples</a> <a href="/tags#apricot jam" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Apricot Jam</a> <a href="/tags#slices bread" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Slices Bread</a> <a href="/tags#custard" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Custard</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>175G/6Oz Butter</li><li>120G/4 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1Kg/2 1/4Lb Cooking Apples, Peeled, Cored And Chopped</li><li>4 Tbsp Smooth Apricot Jam</li><li>12 Slices White Bread, Crusts Removed And Discarded</li><li>Custard, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Melt the butter and sugar in a non stick frying pan, stirring constantly, until the sugar has dissolved. </li><li>Add the apples and cook, stirring often, over a low heat for 5 minutes or until a deep golden brown. </li><li>Spread with the apricot jam and arrange the apple mixture over one slice of bread. </li><li>Repeat with the remaining bread and the remaining apple slices. </li><li>Place on a baking tray and cook in a moderate oven 180c for 10 to 12 minutes or until golden. </li><li>Serve with custard. </li></ul>
