<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Banana Fritters using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#flour tbsp" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Flour Tbsp</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#tsp ground cinnamon" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Ground Cinnamon</a> <a href="/tags#tsp ground nutmeg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Ground Nutmeg</a> <a href="/tags#pinch ground cloves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pinch Ground Cloves</a> <a href="/tags#oil deep-frying" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil Deep-Frying</a> <a href="/tags#medium bananas chunks" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Medium Bananas Chunks</a> <a href="/tags#pinch sea salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pinch Sea Salt</a> <a href="/tags#runny honey" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Runny Honey</a> <a href="/tags#sesame seeds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sesame Seeds</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>180G/6 1/4Oz Self-Raising Flour, Plus 1 Tbsp For Coating</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Ground Nutmeg</li><li>Generous Pinch Ground Cloves</li><li>Oil, For Deep-Frying</li><li>4 Ripe Medium Bananas, Peeled And Cut Into 3Cm/1 1/4In Chunks</li><li>Generous Pinch Fine Sea Salt</li><li>2 Tbsp Runny Honey</li><li>4 Tsp Sesame Seeds, Lightly Toasted</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Combine flour, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg and cloves in a bowl. </li><li>Heat oil in a non stick skillet or frying pan. </li><li>Place bananas in a plate with a teaspoon of salt and honey and coat bananas. </li><li>Then dredge bananas in the flour and sugar mixture, pressing gently to adhere. </li><li>Fry a few pieces at a time in the hot oil until golden brown, about 2 3 minutes. </li><li>Drain on paper towel. </li><li>Sprinkle with toasted sesame seeds and serve warm. </li></ul>
