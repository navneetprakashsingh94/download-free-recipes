<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Mediterranean Sweet Potato Salad using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 50 min. Enjoy!</div>

<a href="/tags#medium sweet potato" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Medium Sweet Potato</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#fennel bulb" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Fennel Bulb</a> <a href="/tags#pepper cubes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pepper Cubes</a> <a href="/tags#onion wedges" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Onion Wedges</a> <a href="/tags#handful cherry tomatoes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Handful Cherry Tomatoes</a> <a href="/tags#bulgur wheat" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bulgur Wheat</a> <a href="/tags#sprigs dill" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sprigs Dill</a> <a href="/tags#salt ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Ground Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#yoghurt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Yoghurt</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Medium Sweet Potato (About 350G/12Oz), Washed And Dried</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li> 1/2 Fennel Bulb, Thinly Sliced</li><li> 1/2 Red Pepper, Cut Into 2Cm/ 3/4In Cubes</li><li> 1/2 Red Onion, Cut Into Wedges</li><li>Small Handful Cherry Tomatoes</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Bulgur Wheat</li><li>2 Sprigs Dill, Roughly Chopped</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>2 Tbsp Natural Yoghurt, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 200c. </li><li>Slice the sweet potato in half and cut into cubes. </li><li>Heat some oil in a skillet on medium heat. Saute the sweet potatoes until golden brown and tender, about 12 minutes. </li><li>Remove and set aside. </li><li>In the same skillet, toss together the fennel, peppers, onion, and tomatoes. Cook until just starting to soften, about 5 minutes. </li><li>Mix the potatoes into the pan, sprinkle the bulgur and dill on top and season with pepper. </li><li>Sprinkle the yoghurt over the top. </li></ul>
