<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>175G/6Oz Softened Butter, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>175G/6Oz Golden Caster Sugar</li><li>3 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>175G/6Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>Red Food Colouring Paste</li><li>6 Tbsp Apricot Jam</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Ready-Made Marzipan</li><li>2-3 Tbsp Icing Sugar, For Rolling.</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Line 20 25 x 12 cm tin with nonstick baking paper, extending paper 2cm 1inch 2cm above tin sides. </li><li>Cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs, 1 at a time, mixing well after each addition. Fold in the flour and vanilla extract. Transfer to prepared tin. Tap out the excess air. </li><li>Bake in preheated oven 180 c for 30 35 mins, or until cooked when tested with a skewer. </li><li>For the filling, mix the paste, jam and marzipan until smooth. Spread over the hot loaf. Bake for another 15 mins, until the filling is just set. </li><li>Dust with icing sugar. Cut into 8 slices. </li></ul>
