<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>75G/3Oz Plain Flour</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>750G/1 1/2Lb Braising Steak, Cut Into 5Cm/2In Cubes</li><li>2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil</li><li>2 Shallots, Chopped</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Baby Onions (Or Small Onions), Peeled</li><li>400G/14Oz Carrots, Cut Into Large Pieces</li><li>4 Sprigs Fresh Thyme</li><li>3 Bay Leaves</li><li>4 Tbsp Tomato Puree</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Ale</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Hot Beef Stock</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Ready-Made Puff Pastry, Rolled To 0.5Cm/ 1/2In Thickness</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten</li><li>Steamed Vegetables Of Your Choice, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 375 f. For the steak mixture, sift flour, salt and pepper into a large mixing bowl. Add steak and toss to coat. Heat half of the oil in a large dutch oven over medium high heat. Add meat and cook, stirring to crumble, for 4 5 mins, or until browned all over. Transfer to a bowl. </li><li>Add shallots, baby onions, carrots, thyme and bay leaves to hot oil in dutch oven and cook, stirring, for 5 6 mins, or until browned. Stir in tomato puree, ale and stock. Return meat to dutch oven and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and braise, covered, for 2 hours, or until meat is tender. </li><li>Meanwhile, on a lightly floured surface, roll out pastry to a 10 inch round. Cut 5 discs and place on baking sheets lined with parchment paper. Brush with egg white and top with pastry disks. </li><li>Bake for 25 30 mins, or until pastry is crisp. Serve with the vegetables. </li></ul>
