<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2Kg/2Lb 4Oz Baby Back Pork Ribs, In Racks</li><li>2 Celery Stalks, Trimmed, Halved</li><li>1 Onion, Thickly Sliced</li><li>2 Red Chillies, Cut In Half Lengthways</li><li>1 Garlic Bulb, Cut In Half Horizontally</li><li>250Ml/9Fl Oz Apple Juice</li><li>125Ml/4 1/2Fl Oz Tomato Ketchup</li><li>150G/5Oz Dark Muscovado Sugar</li><li>2 Tbsp Worcestershire Sauce</li><li>1 Tsp Hot Smoked Paprika</li><li>1 Tsp Sweet Smoked Paprika</li><li>75Ml/2 1/2Fl Oz White Wine Vinegar</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Bourbon Whiskey</li><li>3 Free-Range Egg Yolks</li><li>1 Tbsp Dijon Mustard</li><li>2 Tbsp Cider Vinegar</li><li>300Ml/10 1/2Fl Oz Rapeseed Oil</li><li>1 Celeriac, Julienned And Placed In Acidulated Water</li><li>2 Tbsp Roughly Chopped Fresh Flatleaf Parsley</li><li>Sea Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place ribs and celery, onion, chillies, fennel, apple juice, ketchup, sugar, vinegar, and bourbon in a large pan and bring to a simmer. </li><li>Cook on a medium heat for about 1 hour. </li><li>Remove ribs from pan. </li><li>Transfer sauce to a jug and stir in paprika, wine vinegar and bourbon. </li><li>Cool and pass sauce through a fine sieve to remove fennel and chilli. </li><li>Transfer ribs to a large roasting tin and roast on a moderate oven about 375f for 30 minutes. </li><li>Remove from oven and pour barbecue sauce over ribs. </li><li>Return ribs to the oven for about 1 hour. </li><li>For the celeriac mayonnaise</li><li>Whisk egg yolks, mustard and vinegar together until pale. </li><li>Slowly pour in oil, beating in between, until thick and shiny. </li><li>Add celeriac water and parsley and season with salt and pepper to taste. </li><li>Serve ribs with celeriac mayonnaise. </li></ul>
