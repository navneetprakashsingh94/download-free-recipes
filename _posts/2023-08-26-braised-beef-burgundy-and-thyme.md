<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1Kg/2Lb 2Oz Braising Steak, Trimmed, Cut Into 5Cm/2In Chunks</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>1 Tbsp Vegetable Oil</li><li>75G/3Oz Butter</li><li>150G/5Oz Baby Onions, Or Pickling Onions, Peeled</li><li>4 Carrots, Peeled, Cut Into Chunks</li><li>1 Tbsp Plain Flour</li><li>200Ml/7Floz Red Wine</li><li>400Ml/14Fl Oz Fresh Beef Stock</li><li>2 Fresh Thyme Sprigs</li><li>1Kg/2Lb 2Oz Potatoes, Cut Into 5Mm/ 1/4In Slices</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Season the beef chunks with salt pepper</li><li>Heat a large non stick pan over medium heat and add the oil. Brown the beef on all sides</li><li>Add the butter to the pan to melt</li><li>Add the onions carrots and cook for 5 minutes</li><li>Add the flour and cook for 2 minutes</li><li>Add the wine beef stock and bring to the boil</li><li>Reduce the heat to low cover simmer for 30 40 minutes</li><li>Add the thyme and season to taste. </li><li>Serve with potatoes</li></ul>
