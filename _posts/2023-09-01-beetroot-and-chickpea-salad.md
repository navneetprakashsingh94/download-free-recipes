<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Tsp Cumin Seeds</li><li>250G/9Oz Cooked Beetroot, Drained And Quartered</li><li>400G Tin Chickpeas, Drained And Rinsed</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Peeled</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Coriander</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Flaked Sea Salt, And To Taste</li><li>2 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>2 Tbsp Fresh Lemon Juice</li><li>Ground Black Pepper</li><li>1 Slice Sourdough Bread</li><li>Generous Dollop Beetroot Hummus (From Above)</li><li>Handful Watercress</li><li>1 Tsp Mixed Chopped Nuts And/Or Seeds</li><li>Drizzle Tahini</li><li>1 Slice Wholegrain Bread</li><li>Generous Dollop Of Beetroot Hummus (From Above)</li><li>2 Tbsp Kimchi</li><li>1 Slice Rye Bread</li><li>Generous Dollop Beetroot Hummus (From Above)</li><li>Small Handful Sprouted Lentils And/Or Sprouted Beans</li><li>Few Parsley Leaves, Torn</li><li>Few Mint Leaves, Torn</li><li>Drizzle Olive Oil</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Dry roast the cumin seeds in a frying pan until fragrant. </li><li>Process the beetroot, chickpeas, clove, coriander, salt and 2 tbsp oil until smooth. </li><li>Drizzle the lemon juice and pepper. </li><li>Mix the beetroot, chickpeas, hummus and watercress in a bowl. </li><li>Drizzle with tahini and toss to coat. </li><li>Toast the bread slices under a moderate heat. </li><li>Drizzle some more tahini and sprinkle on the bread. </li><li>Arrange the salad on top and garnish with extra watercress. </li><li>For the hummus, combine kimchi with a handful of bean garbanzo beans. </li><li>Slice the bread and drizzle some tahini and bake in the oven at 400 f for about 8 minutes. </li><li>Spread a teaspoon of the hummus on each slice of bread and wring it with the pan frying oil. </li><li>Mound the hummus on the bread slices and tuck the bean bean halves into the hummus on top. </li><li>Garnish with fresh mint and parsley. </li></ul>
