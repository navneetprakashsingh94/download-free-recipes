<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>4 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Medium Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>4 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Celery Sticks, Finely Chopped</li><li>1Kg/2Lb 4Oz Lean Beef Mince</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Red Wine</li><li>400G Tin Chopped Tomatoes</li><li>2 Tbsp Tomato Puree</li><li>10Cm/4In Cinnamon Stick</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cloves</li><li>1 Tbsp Dried Oregano</li><li>2 Tbsp Fresh Chopped Oregano</li><li>3 Fresh Bay Leaves</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Tubular Pasta, Such As Rigatoni, Penne Or Tortiglioni</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs, Lightly Beaten</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Greek Kefalotiri Cheese Or Parmesan, Finely Grated</li><li>2 Tbsp Melted Butter</li><li>10G/ 1/3 Oz Fresh White Breadcrumbs</li><li>115G/4Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>115G/4Oz Plain Flour</li><li>1.2 Litres/2 Pints Full-Fat Milk, Plus Extra To Let Down The Sauce</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Freshly Grated Nutmeg</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat oil and fry onion, cloves, and celery till lightly browned. Add mince and stir fry for 1 minute or until all the pink color has disappeared. Add wine and stir well, bring to a boil. Stir in all other ingredients including bay leaves, salt and pepper. Reduce heat and simmer for 2 hours. Cool and freeze for a later use. </li><li></li><li>To make the sauce, melt butter in a small saucepan over medium low heat. Add flour, whisking until mixture bubbles. Add milk sauce and continue to whisk until sauce is smooth and thickened. Season with salt and pepper. </li><li></li><li>Preheat oven to 180 c. </li><li></li><li>Prepare pasta according to package directions, drain and place in a single layer in a large baking dish. Pour th half of the sauce over and sprinkle with 2 tbsp cheese. Place eggs into the sauce and sprinkle with the remaining cheese and breadcrumbs. Bake for 15 20 minutes. </li><li></li><li>To make the cream sauce, melt butter in a saucepan. Add flour and cook for 1 minute, whisking until smooth. Whisk in milk and simmer for 2 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, nutmeg and remaining tbsp cheese. </li><li></li><li>To serve, place pasta on 4 plates and top with cheese sauce and the beaten egg. </li></ul>
