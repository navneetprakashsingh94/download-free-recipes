<div>Delicious Pappardelle With Porcini Mushrooms using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 30 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Milk</a> <a href="/tags#porcini mushrooms" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Porcini Mushrooms</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#salt ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Ground Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#pappardelle" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pappardelle</a> <a href="/tags#pancetta pieces" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pancetta Pieces</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>500Ml/18Fl Oz Milk</li><li>20G/ 3/4Oz Dried Porcini Mushrooms</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>25G/1Oz Plain Flour</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>100G/4Oz Pappardelle</li><li>50G/2Oz Pancetta, Cut Into 2Cm/1In Pieces</li><li>4-5 Tbsp Freshly Grated Parmesan</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 425 f. Bring milk, dried mushrooms, and 1/2 cup water to boil in medium saucepan. Remove from heat cover and set aside. </li><li></li><li>Heat butter in heavy large saucepan over medium heat. Add flour and whisk 1 minute. Whisk in milk mixture. Bring to boil, whisking constantly, and cook 3 minutes, or until thickened. Season to taste with salt and pepper. </li><li></li><li>Bring 4 quarts water to boil in large pot. Add salt. Add pasta, cover pot and cook until almost tender, stirring occasionally, about 8 minutes. Drain pasta. Transfer to large bowl. Add sauce toss to coat. Add pancetta and cheese. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer to 13x9 inch glass baking dish. </li><li></li><li>Bake until mixture is hot and bubbling, about 25 minutes. Serve. </li></ul>
