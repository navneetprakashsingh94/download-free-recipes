<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#mature Cheddar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Mature Cheddar</a> <a href="/tags#parmesan Parmigiano Reggiano" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Parmesan Parmigiano Reggiano</a> <a href="/tags#pint/570ml milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pint/570Ml Milk</a> <a href="/tags#plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#pinch cayenne pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pinch Cayenne Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#nutmeg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Nutmeg</a> <a href="/tags#salt pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Pepper</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2Oz/50G Mature Cheddar, Grated</li><li>1Oz/25G Parmesan (Parmigiano Reggiano), Finely Grated</li><li>1 Pint/570Ml Milk</li><li>1 1/2Oz/40G Plain Flour</li><li>1 1/2Oz/40G Butter</li><li>Pinch Of Cayenne Pepper</li><li>A Little Freshly Grated Nutmeg</li><li>Salt And Freshly Milled Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Melt butter over medium heat in a large saucepan. Whisk in flour and slowly add milk, whisking until smooth and slightly thickened. Add cheese and allow to melt, 1 2 minutes. Season with cayenne, nutmeg, and season to taste. </li><li></li><li>Remove sauce from heat and transfer to serving bowl. Serve with crusty french bread. </li></ul>
