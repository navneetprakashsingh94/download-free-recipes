<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>250G/9Oz Plain Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Cold Butter, Cut Into Cubes, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Cold Lard, Cut Into Cubes</li><li>2 X 410G Tins Apricot Halves In Juice, Drained</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Flaked Almonds, Roughly Chopped, Plus A Few Extra To Decorate (Optional)</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, Melted</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Soft Brown Sugar (Or Sugar Of Your Choice)</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten, To Glaze (Alternatively, Use A Little Milk)</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Wholemeal Or Brown Bread</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Full-Fat Milk</li><li>3 Free-Range Egg Yolks</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>400Ml/14Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Sultanas (Or Raisins)</li><li>2 Tsp Fresh Breadcrumbs (Ideally From Brown Bread)</li><li>1 Tsp Sugar</li><li>Few Pinches Ground Cinnamon</li><li>4 Over-Ripe Bananas, Chopped Into Pieces And Frozen</li><li>100-200G/3 1/2-7Oz Any Type Of Yoghurt</li><li>Pinch Ground Cinnamon</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Vanilla Extract (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 180 c. </li><li>Combine the flour, butter, lard, lemon juice and almonds and knead with the palm of your hand until you have a soft dough. You may need to add extra flour, as this is a very sticky dough. </li><li>Roll the dough out on a floured board. </li><li>Place in a greased cake tin and cut out 2 cm wide slits in the centre of the pastry. </li><li>Melt the butter and add the brown sugar. Stir in the egg milk and spread over the pastry. </li><li>Layer the bread cubes on top of the pastry. </li><li>Pour the milk over this, leave to soak. </li><li>In a bowl, beat the eggs and the sugar, add the cream, pour into the bread mixture and mix well. </li><li>Arrange the bananas over the pastry. Sprinkle with the sugar and cinnamon and pour the cream mixture over this. </li><li>Bake for 30 40 minutes, until the pastry is golden brown. </li><li>Allow the clafouti to cool. </li><li>Serve the bananas and yoghurt with a squeeze of lemon. </li></ul>
