<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Onion, Chopped</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Chopped</li><li> 1/2 Yellow Pepper, Chopped</li><li>1-2 Smoked Chipotle Chillies, Or To Taste (Available At Some Supermarkets And From Online Suppliers)</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Canned Black-Eye Beans, Rinsed And Drained</li><li>2 Tbsp Tomato Ketchup</li><li>1 Red Onion, Cut Into 8 Wedges</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Tbsp Balsamic Vinegar</li><li>1 Tbsp Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Lime, Juice Only</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Soured Cream</li><li> 1/2 Avocado, Peeled, Stone Removed, Chopped</li><li>Small Handful Fresh Coriander, Chopped</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>4 Slices Ciabatta, Toasted, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>To prepare bbq icing, heat the oil and fry the onion until translucent. </li><li>Add the spices and cook another 2 3 minutes until aromatic. </li><li>Add the beans and ketchup, season well with salt and pepper, and simmer for 10 minutes, until everything is heated through and creamy. </li><li>To prepare the onion, peel and slice the onion and soak in water to cover for 10 minutes then drain. </li><li>Heat the oil in a pan and add the vinegar and sugar. </li><li>Simmer for 3 5 minutes to dissolve the sugar. </li><li>Add the lime juice, cream, avocado, coriander leaves and seasoning. </li><li>Mix gently. </li><li>Cut the ciabatta in half. </li><li>Spread one side with the bbq ketchup, then add a quarter of the bean mash. </li><li>Top with a second slice of ciabatta. </li><li>Top with the second slice of ciabatta and serve immediately. </li></ul>
