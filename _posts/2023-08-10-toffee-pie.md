<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Toffee Pie using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 50 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#biscuits" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Biscuits</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#muscovado sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Muscovado Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#milk tip" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Milk Tip</a> <a href="/tags#vanilla extract" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Vanilla Extract</a> <a href="/tags#oz cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Cream</a> <a href="/tags#bananas" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bananas</a> <a href="/tags#chocolate cocoa powder" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chocolate Cocoa Powder</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>150G/5 1/2 Oz Digestive Biscuits (About 10)</li><li>75G/2 3/4Oz Butter</li><li>75G/2 3/4Oz Butter</li><li>75G/2 3/4Oz Light Muscovado Sugar</li><li>1 X 397G/14Oz Full-Fat Condensed Milk (See Tip)</li><li>1 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>3 Small Just-Ripe Bananas, Sliced</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Dark Chocolate, Melted Or Cocoa Powder</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 180c/350f/gas mark 4. </li><li>Place the biscuits into the tin and then with a fork mash them until they are slightly broken up. </li><li>Add the melted butter and mix well. </li><li>Press the biscuits into the base of a greased 20cm pie dish. </li><li>Bake the biscuit base for about 20 minutes until golden and crispy. </li><li>Mix together the muscovado sugar, milk and vanilla extract then stir through the toffee and cream. </li><li>Spoon the mixture over the biscuit base and scatter over the bananas and then sprinkle with the chocolate cocoa powder. </li><li>Return to the oven and bake for about 30 minutes until the mixture is melted and bubbling. </li><li>Chill before serving. </li></ul>
