<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Soft Light Brown Sugar</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Softened Butter, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>250G/9Oz Golden Syrup</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Black Treacle</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Oat Flakes</li><li>175G/6Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>3 Tbsp Ground Ginger</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Nutmeg</li><li>1 Tsp Mixed Spice</li><li>Pinch Of Salt</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs, Beaten</li><li>25Ml/1Fl Oz Milk</li><li>Vanilla Ice Cream, To Serve</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Butter</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Orange, Zest Only</li><li>4 Sticks Rhubarb, Cut Into 5Cm/2In Lengths</li><li>200G/7Oz Golden Syrup</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Dry Cider</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Mixed Spice</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Ginger</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Ground Nutmeg</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Grease a 5cm base size cake tin or shallow 3cm deep baking tin. </li><li>Mix together brown sugar and butter and cut into cubes until fine crumbs are formed. </li><li>Stir in the dark karo syrup and treacle and bring to boil. </li><li>Add the oat flakes and cook, stirring, for about 3 minutes. </li><li>Sift together the flour and spices. </li><li>In a separate bowl, beat together the eggs and milk. </li><li>Using a wooden spoon, gradually add the flour and fruit mixture to the sugar mixture and stir until well combined. </li><li>Pour the mixture into the prepared tin and bake in a moderately hot oven of 200c for approximately 1 hour and 15 minutes or until a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean. </li><li>Leave to cool in the tin for 5 minutes before turning out onto a wire rack to cool completely. </li><li>Make the syrup in a saucepan, melt the butter and sugar and bring to boil. </li><li>Simmer for a further 3 minutes, then add the orange juice and lemongrass. </li><li>Meanwhile, boil the cider for 5 minutes, then cool. </li><li>Peel the lemongrass leaves, remove the stalk and slice very finely. </li><li>Puree the ginger and molasses into the cider, add the spices and simmer for about 10 minutes. </li><li>Serve with the pudding and ice cream. </li></ul>
