<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1Kg/2.2Lb Bramley Apples, Peeled, Cored And Diced</li><li>1Kg/2.2Lb Conference Pears, Peeled, Cored And Diced</li><li>2 Large Onions, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Cloves Garlic, Crushed</li><li>2 Limes, Grated Zest And Juice</li><li>1 Tbsp Mustard Seeds</li><li>1 Litre/1 3/4 Pint White Wine Vinegar</li><li>225G/8Oz Each Raisins And Chopped Dried Apricots</li><li>7.5Cm/3In Piece Root Ginger, Peeled And Grated</li><li>2 Tsp Ground Ginger</li><li>1 Tbsp Sea Salt</li><li>1Kg/2Lb2Oz Dark Muscovado Sugar</li><li>Warm Sterilised Jam Jars And Jam Pot Covers - Prepare Jars By Washing In Hot Soapy Water And Leaving To Dry And Warm In A Cool Oven - 130C/250F/Gas  1/2 For 10-15 Minutes.</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Put all the ingredients for the jam in a large heavy based saucepan. </li><li>Cover with a lid and cook for 1 hour, stirring occasionally, until the mixture has reached the consistency of wet apple butter. </li><li>Press a piece of kitchen parchment paper into the surface of the relish to stop it from cooking further. </li><li>Discard the parchment paper and the cloves. </li><li>Leave to cool. </li><li>Check the seasonings before using. </li><li>The relish can be stored in an airtight container in a cool, dry place for up to 5 months. </li><li>Check and correct for seasoning. </li><li>Use your favourite jam jars for this jam or make it in the microwave. </li><li>Makes a good jam jar to fill your baking trays or 1 litre jars. </li></ul>
