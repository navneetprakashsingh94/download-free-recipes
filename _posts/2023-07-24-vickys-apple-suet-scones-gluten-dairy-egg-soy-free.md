<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>170G/6Oz Self-Raising Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Wholemeal Self-Raising Flour</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>120G/4 1/2Oz Suet</li><li>1 Onion, Sliced</li><li>10 Rashers Streaky Bacon, Chopped</li><li>1 Braeburn Apple, Peeled And Sliced</li><li>1 Tsp Thyme Leaves, Chopped</li><li>6 Sage Leaves, Chopped</li><li>Ground White Pepper To Taste</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>2 Shallots, Diced</li><li>10G/ 1/3 Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz White Wine</li><li>Splash White Wine Vinegar</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Chicken Stock</li><li>50-100Ml/2-3 1/2Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>3 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Curly Parsley</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to gas mark 6/180c/325f</li><li>Whisk the flours, salt and suet in a large bowl</li><li>Rub in the butter until a sandy texture. </li><li>Tip it out onto a lightly floured surface and knead into a smooth dough</li><li>Divide dough into 6 portions and roll out to a 5mm thickness. </li><li>Prick the dough with a fork and place onto a baking tray. </li><li>Bake for 20 minutes</li><li>Meanwhile prepare the shallots, thyme, sage and butter. </li><li>To make the shallot and bacon bits saute the shallots in butter until softened. </li><li>Add the wine and vinegar and simmer for 5 minutes. </li><li>Add the stock and cream. </li><li>Simmer until the liquid has reduced to a sauce consistency. </li><li>Season to taste. </li><li>Remove the baked scones from the oven and top with the shallots and bacon. </li><li>Dust with chopped parsley. </li><li>Serve warm. </li></ul>
