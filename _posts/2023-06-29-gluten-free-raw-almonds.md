<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Put the almonds on the tray, place the salt and leave to soak overnight</li><li>Bake in the oven at 170c for about 45 minutes until golden brown</li><li>Let them cool down a bit then put in a storage bag</li></ul>
