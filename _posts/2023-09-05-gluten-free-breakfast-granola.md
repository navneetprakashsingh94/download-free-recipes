<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Apple</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Rolled Oats</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Raisins</li><li>1 Tbsp Sunflower Seeds</li><li>1 Tbsp Chia Seeds</li><li>125Ml/4Fl Oz Almond Milk (Or Other Plant-Based Milk)</li><li>1 Tbsp Maple Syrup, Date Syrup Or Honey (Optional)</li><li>Handful Of Blueberries, Raspberries Or Strawberries (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 350 degrees f. </li><li>On a large baking sheet, combine the apple, oats, raisins, flaxseeds and chia seeds. </li><li>Add a splash of almond milk milk just to moisten. </li><li>Bake in a preheated oven for 15 20 minutes, stirring every 5 minutes or so, until granola is golden. </li><li>Granola should be crispy and crunchy and golden brown in spots. </li><li>Drizzle with maple syrup. </li><li>Enjoy</li><li>Serves 8. </li></ul>
