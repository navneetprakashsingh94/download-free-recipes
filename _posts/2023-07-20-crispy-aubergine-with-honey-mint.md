<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#medium aubergine discs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Medium Aubergine Discs</a> <a href="/tags#cloves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cloves</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank"></a> <a href="/tags#sprigs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sprigs</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Medium Aubergine, Cut Into 1Cm/ 1/2In Discs</li><li>3 Garlic Cloves, Chopped</li><li>6 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>400G/14Oz Can Chickpeas, Drained</li><li>3 Sprigs Rosemary</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Roast eggplant in 400 oven until skin is charred but still soft. </li><li>Use knife to split off aubergine discs and scoop out inside. </li><li>Using spoon, scoop insides into a bowl, leaving a thin shell in the centre. </li><li>Add oil and honey, mixing well. </li><li>Scoop tops out of aubergine. </li><li>Discard skin, if any. </li><li>Dip aubergine shells in glaze and sprinkle with 1 1/2 tbsp. </li><li>Honey. </li><li>Refrigerate 15 to 30 minutes to set. </li><li>Garnish with mint sprigs. </li></ul>
