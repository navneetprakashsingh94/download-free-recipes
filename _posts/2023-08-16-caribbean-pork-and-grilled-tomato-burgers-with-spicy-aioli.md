<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1-1.5Kg/2Lb 4Oz-3Lb 5Oz Pork Belly</li><li>1 Bulb Garlic</li><li>1 Spanish Onion, Peeled And Halved</li><li> 1/2 Head Celery, Roughly Chopped</li><li>20 Peppercorns</li><li>2 Bay Leaves</li><li>Small Bunch Thyme</li><li>250Ml/9Fl Oz Vegetable Oil</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Streaky Bacon, Coarsely Chopped</li><li>1 Large Egg Yolk</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Rice Wine Vinegar</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Dijon Mustard</li><li>1 Tbsp Lemon Juice</li><li>2 Tbsp Light Soy Sauce</li><li>20Ml/ 3/4Fl Oz Olive Oil</li><li>1 Small Red Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Small Garlic Cloves, Crushed</li><li>1 Tsp Smoked Paprika</li><li>1 Lemongrass Stalk, Trimmed And Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Tsp Caster Sugar, Or To Taste</li><li>1 Tsp Red Wine Vinegar</li><li>2 Tsp Fish Sauce</li><li>1 Tsp Lime Juice</li><li>200G/7Oz Vine-Ripened Plum Tomatoes</li><li>Olive Oil</li><li>4 Brioche Burger Buns, Halved</li><li>4 Crisp Little Gem Lettuce Leaves</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Bring pork, garlic, onion, celery, peppercorns, bay leaves, and thyme to the boil, reduce heat to a simmer, cook gently uncovered for 30 mins. Add cold water to cover the pork and simmer for 1 hour until tender. </li><li>Strain and reserve the pork stock. In a blender, whizz the aioli ingredients together. Slowly whisk in the oil. </li><li></li><li>Preheat the grill. Brush the bacon slices with oil. Grill over high heat for 4 5 mins each side until crispy. Set aside. </li><li></li><li>In a saucepan, heat oil over medium heat. Cook the onion until softened, stirring. Add the cloves, paprika and lemongrass. Cook for 1 min then add the sugar, vinegar, fish sauce and lime juice. Reduce heat to medium low and simmer for 10 mins. Let cool. </li><li></li><li>Toss the tomatoes in the cooled sauce, then season. Refrigerate until ready to use. </li><li></li><li>In a bowl, drizzle the tomatoes with oil, salt and pepper. Grill for 5 mins per side. Grill the buns for about 2 mins each side until golden. </li><li></li><li>Place</li></ul>
