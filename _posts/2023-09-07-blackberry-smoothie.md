<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#blackberries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Blackberries</a> <a href="/tags#honey syrup" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Honey Syrup</a> <a href="/tags#handful almonds walnuts" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Handful Almonds Walnuts</a> <a href="/tags#porridge oats oats oats" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Porridge Oats Oats Oats</a> <a href="/tags#milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Milk</a> <a href="/tags#drops extract" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Drops Extract</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Frozen Blackberries</li><li>1 Tsp Runny Honey Or Agave Syrup</li><li>Small Handful Chopped Almonds Or Walnuts</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Porridge Oats Or Toasted Rolled Oats (Use Gluten-Free Oats If Required)</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Milk Or Vegan Alternative</li><li>3 Drops Almond Extract (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Peel the blackberries and roughly chop in a blender. </li><li>Mix with honey and a few drops of extract extract. </li><li>Heat milk and whisk until very hot. </li><li>Pour milk and extract mixture into blender. </li><li>Keep blending until completely smooth. </li><li>Serve immediately and top with a swirl of granola, almonds and blackberry sauce. </li></ul>
