<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1.5Kg/3Lb 5Oz Belly Pork, With Rind Still On</li><li>570Ml/1 Pint Pork Or Chicken Stock (If Available, Otherwise Water)</li><li>12 Spring Onions</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Light Soy Sauce</li><li>75Ml/2 1/2Fl Oz Chinese Rice Wine</li><li>25Ml/1Fl Oz Rice Wine Vinegar</li><li>2 Tbsp Demerera (Or Light Brown) Sugar</li><li>3 Star Anise</li><li>10Cm/4In Piece Of Fresh Ginger Root, Peeled And Sliced In Rounds</li><li>A Good Pinch Of Dried Chilli Flakes</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Combine all ingredients for sauce and bring to boil. Add rind. Cover and cook on med low heat, stirring occasionally, until water is almost evaporated. </li><li>Cool, then squeeze to remove excess water. </li><li>Cover and refrigerate. </li><li>Reserving the cooking liquid, strain and reserve. </li><li>Add marinade to meat and bring to a boil, then simmer for 10 min, stirring often. </li><li>Remove from heat and leave to marinate for an hour, turning meat occasionally. </li><li>Add spring onions to mixture for last 15 min, stirring occasionally, being careful not to let any pieces burn. </li></ul>
