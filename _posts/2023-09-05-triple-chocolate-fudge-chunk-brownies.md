<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Butter, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>175G/6Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>175G/6Oz Soft Light Brown Sugar</li><li>4 Large Free-Range Eggs</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Cocoa Powder</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Plain Dark Chocolate</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz White Chocolate</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Shelled Pecan Nuts (Or Walnuts)</li><li>70G/2 1/2Oz Packet Dried Sour Cherries</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 350 f. Grease an 8 inch square baking pan and line bottom with baking paper. </li><li>Beat butter and sugars together until light and creamy. Beat in eggs. Sift flour and cocoa into a large bowl. Fold in chocolate and remaining ingredients. Transfer to prepared pan and bake for 50 mins, until a skewer inserted in the centre comes out clean. Cool in pan for 5 mins before inverting onto a wire rack to cool completely. </li><li>Run a thin knife around edges of pan to loosen. Turn out onto a cutting board and cut into squares. </li></ul>
