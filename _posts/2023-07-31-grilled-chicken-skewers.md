<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Skinless Chicken Breast Fillet</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Olive Oil</li><li>Flaked Sea Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>Large Pinch Dried Chilli Flakes, Plus Pinch Dried Oregano (Optional)</li><li>Pinch Dried Mixed Herbs Plus Pinch Garlic Granules (Optional)</li><li>2 Pinches Cajun Seasoning (Optional)</li><li>2 Pinches Ground Paprika, Plus Small Pinch Dried Thyme (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Coat the chicken with the ingredients. </li><li>Soak in a bowl of water, and the mixture will be a little bit extra salty. </li><li>Use your hands to massage the marinade into the chicken. </li><li>Mix gently. </li><li>Cook the chicken in a frying pan with olive oil on medium heat. </li><li>Place onto paper towels to dry. </li><li>Arrange on a plate with shaved carrots, sliced olives and a glass of wine. </li><li>The colour of the chicken is pretty and the recipe is really similar. </li><li>I tried sakura pepper. </li><li>My chicken was cooked at 60 secs. </li><li>I used the gyoza and halved the fillet. </li><li>You can use a cut up beef pattie or any stuffed chicken fillet for extra flavour. </li><li>Cook the chicken with the marinade for 2 3 mins with the marinade. </li><li>You can freeze it so it s better if you let it come to room temperature before grilling. </li><li>Please check the video of this recipe. </li><li>This is how we served this chicken with some grilled green onion slaw. </li></ul>
