<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Asparagus Soup using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 30 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#asparagus" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Asparagus</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#shallot" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Shallot</a> <a href="/tags#pints stock" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pints Stock</a> <a href="/tags#creme fraiche" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Creme Fraiche</a> <a href="/tags#egg yolks" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg Yolks</a> <a href="/tags#fino sherry" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Fino Sherry</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp parsley" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Parsley</a> <a href="/tags#sea salt flakes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sea Salt Flakes</a> <a href="/tags#pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pepper</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz White Asparagus</li><li>50G/2Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>1 Shallot, Finely Chopped</li><li>700Ml/1 1/4 Pints Chicken Stock</li><li>70Ml/4 1/2 Tbsp Creme Fraiche</li><li>2 Egg Yolks</li><li>1 Tbsp Dry Fino Sherry</li><li>2 Tbsp Chopped Parsley, To Garnish</li><li>Sea Salt Flakes</li><li>White Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cut stalks from asparagus, boil until just tender, drain, set aside</li><li>Melt butter in pan, add shallots, cook till translucent</li><li>Add asparagus, cover and cook till asparagus is tender</li><li>Add stock, mix well. </li><li>Cook till almost all liquid is absorbed. </li><li>Whisk together egg yolks and creme fraiche to incorporate. </li><li>Add yolks to soup mix well. </li><li>Add sherry and parsley, bring to boil and serve immediately. </li><li>Garnish with fresh chives</li></ul>
