<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>180Ml/6Fl Oz Extra-Virgin Olive Oil, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>220G/8Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>2 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>180G/6Oz Chunky Apple Sauce</li><li>200G/7Oz Ground Almonds, Sifted</li><li>200G/7Oz Plain Flour Or Gluten-Free Flour, Sifted</li><li>1 Tsp Mixed Spice</li><li>250Ml/9Fl Oz Apple Juice</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Tsp Mixed Spice</li><li>80Ml/2 2/3 Fl Oz Extra-Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>80G/2 3/4Oz Icing Sugar, Sifted</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place the oil, sugar, baking powder and chunky apple sauce in a bowl and mix to combine. </li><li>Sift in the flour and spice and mix to form a smooth, soft dough. </li><li>Take small spoonfuls of the mix and use the palms of your hands to form a round shape. </li><li>Put the dough on the cookie sheet lined with baking paper and bake in a preheated oven at 180c 350f for 10 12 minutes. </li><li>To make the apricot filling, place the apple juice, sugar and spice into a small saucepan over medium heat and cook, stirring often, until the sugar dissolves and the mixture starts to simmer. </li><li>Remove from the heat and allow to cool completely. </li><li>When the cake is just finished baking, combine the apple filling and oil in a large bowl and beat until smooth. </li><li>Remove the baked cake from the oven and place it on a wire rack to cool. </li><li>Spread the apricot filling evenly over the cake, leaving a small border all around. </li><li>Carefully roll the cake up and serve dusted with icing sugar. </li></ul>
