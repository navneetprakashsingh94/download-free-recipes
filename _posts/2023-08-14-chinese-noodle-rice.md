<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Chinese Noodle Rice using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#jasmine rice water" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Jasmine Rice Water</a> <a href="/tags#pint boiling water" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pint Boiling Water</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>350G/12Oz Jasmine Rice, Rinsed Until The Water Runs Clear</li><li>600Ml/1 Pint Boiling Water</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Drain the rice well. </li><li>Add the boiling water, cover tightly and let sit overnight in the refrigerator, allowing the rice to swell slightly. </li><li>After the two hours, remove the lid and fluff the rice with a fork. </li></ul>
