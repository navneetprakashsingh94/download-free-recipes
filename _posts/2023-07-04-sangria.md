<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Bottle Red Wine (Quality Doesn'T Matter)</li><li>1-2 Sticks Cinnamon</li><li>5G Dried Root Ginger</li><li>5G Dried Seville Orange Peel (Or Other Orange If You Can'T Get Seville)</li><li>7 Green Cardamom Pods</li><li>15-16 Whole Cloves</li><li>80G/3Oz Sugar</li><li>Flaked Almonds</li><li>Raisins</li><li>Splash Of Either Vodka, Aquavit, Rum Or Cognac (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>In a large pitcher, combine all ingredients except the last two and stir until combined. </li><li>Cover and let stand for 3 to 4 days, until the aroma intensifies. </li><li>Before serving, strain the liquid into a separate bowl and discard the cinnamon stick, ginger, orange peel and cardamom pods. </li><li>Stir in the raisins and vodka to taste. </li><li>If you like, serve with ice. </li></ul>
