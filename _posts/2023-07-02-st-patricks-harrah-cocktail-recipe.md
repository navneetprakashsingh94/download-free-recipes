<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#oz Campari" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Campari</a> <a href="/tags#vermouth" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Vermouth</a> <a href="/tags#handful ice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Handful Ice</a> <a href="/tags#soda water" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Soda Water</a> <a href="/tags#orange slices" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Orange Slices</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>30Ml/1Fl Oz Campari</li><li>30Ml/1Fl Oz Sweet Red Vermouth</li><li>Handful Ice</li><li>Soda Water</li><li>Orange Slices, To Garnish (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place ice and campari in a cocktail shaker. </li><li>Add in vermouth and shake vigorously. </li><li>If you want the drink to be a bit sweeter, add in a bit of hot water. </li><li>Add a splash of soda water, then serve garnished with the orange slices. </li></ul>
