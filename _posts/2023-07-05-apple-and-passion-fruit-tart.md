<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>250G/9Oz Plain Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>250G/9Oz Very Cold Butter, Cut Into Small Cubes</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Salt</li><li>125Ml/4 1/2Fl Oz Ice Cold Water</li><li>3 Free-Range Egg Yolks</li><li>120G/5Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>20G/ 3/4Oz Plain Flour</li><li>250Ml/9Fl Oz Milk</li><li> 1/2 Vanilla Pod, Split Lengthways</li><li>1 Tbsp Icing Sugar</li><li>3 Medium Cox Apples, Peeled And Thinly Sliced</li><li>3 Passion Fruit, Pulp And Seeds Scraped Out, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. Mix the flour, butter and salt in a large bowl. Add the water and mix with your fingertips until the mixture resembles fine breadcrumbs. </li><li>2. Roll out the pastry on a lightly floured surface into a 10 inch circle and fit the pastry into a 10 inch fluted tart tin. Chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes. </li><li>3. Preheat the oven to 400 f. Place the pastry case on a baking sheet and line with baking parchment. Fill with baking beans and fill with baking beans. Bake for 12 15 minutes or until golden. Remove the paper and beans and bake for a further 3 5 minutes or until golden. </li><li>4. Combine the egg yolks and sugar. Stir in the plain flour, mixing well. Bring the milk, vanilla pod and scraped seeds to the boil. Whisk in a small amount of the hot milk mixture until smooth. Add the hot milk mixture to the egg mixture in the pan and bring to the boil, stirring constantly. Boil for 1 minute. Remove from the heat and cool for 30 minutes. Cool. </li><li>5. Place the pastry case into a roasting tin. Pour the apple and passion fruit mixture into the pastry case. Dust the apple wedges with icing sugar. Bake for 50 55 minutes until browned. </li></ul>
