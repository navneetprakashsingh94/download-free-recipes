<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Medium Celeriac, Skin Scrubbed But Left On, Cut In Half Lengthways</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>10G/ 1/4Oz Thyme Sprigs</li><li>80G/2 3/4Oz Creme Fraiche</li><li>20G/ 3/4Oz Fresh Horseradish, Peeled And Finely Grated (Use Horseradish Cream As An Alternative)</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Smoked Trout Or Salmon, Sliced Into 3Cm/1 1/4In Strips</li><li>2 Tsp Lemon Juice</li><li>Flaky Sea Salt And Black Pepper</li><li>20G/ 3/4Oz Small Capers (Or Regular Capers, Chopped)</li><li>1 Tbsp Finely Chopped Parsley</li><li>1 Tsp Finely Chopped Tarragon</li><li>1 1/2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 400 degrees. </li><li>Remove roots and tops of celeriac. Cut out stems, and with a paring knife, slice crosswise into rounds that will fit into a pie pan or a small pie dish. Lay sliced celeriac on pie pan and bake for 15 minutes. Check the underside to see that it is done. You may need to check it halfway through. </li><li></li><li>In a small mixing bowl, mix cream fraiche and horseradish, until combined. </li><li></li><li>In another bowl, toss salmon with 1 tbsp lemon juice, salt and pepper. Set aside. </li><li></li><li>Once celeriac is tender, place a small scoop of celeriac in the centre of a flat serving plate. Fold salmon and creme fraiche mixture over celeriac. Sprinkle with capers, tarragon and parsley. Drizzle with oil. </li></ul>
