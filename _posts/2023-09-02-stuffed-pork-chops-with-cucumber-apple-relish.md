<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>650G/1Lb 6Oz Pork Belly</li><li>150G/5Oz Salt</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Soy Sauce</li><li>50G/2Oz Honey</li><li>25Ml/1Fl Oz Mirin</li><li>25Ml/1Fl Oz Sesame Oil</li><li>4 Slices Shonka Ham (Available From Specialist Butchers)</li><li> 1/2 Cauliflower Head, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Large Onion, Roughly Chopped</li><li>50G/2Oz Salt</li><li>600Ml/1Pint 1Fl Oz White Wine Vinegar</li><li>300Ml/11Fl Oz Malt Vinegar</li><li>Pinch Dried Chilli</li><li>2 Tsp Cornflour</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Turmeric</li><li>25G/1Oz English Mustard Powder</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Sugar</li><li> 1/2 Cucumber, Peeled, Seeds Removed</li><li>2 Granny Smith Apples</li><li>2 Tsp Lemon Juice</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>1 Tbsp Apple Balsamic Vinegar</li><li>4 Large Cauliflower Florets, Thinly Sliced On A Mandolin</li><li>1 Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>Sea Salt</li><li>3 Tbsp Coriander Cress</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cumin</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the pork, season with salt and pepper, and place in a shallow dish. </li><li>Mix together soy sauce, honey, mirin, and sesame oil and pour over pork. </li><li>Marinate for at least 1 hour. </li><li>Place ham in a non stick fry pan with about 1/2 inch of water and heat gently, turning occasionally to cook. </li><li>For the apple cucumber relish, place the cabbage, onion, vinegar and spices into a small pan and bring to the boil, cook for 30 to 45 minutes. </li><li>Remove from heat and allow to cool. </li><li>Mash all the ingredients in a bowl, season to taste and set aside until cold. </li><li>For the veg, combine all ingredients and place in a small bowl. </li><li>Preheat oven to 180c/350f. </li><li>Remove the meat from the marinade. </li><li>Cut a pocket in the top of each piece of meat, and stuff with the apple apple relish. </li><li>Place stuffed pork chops on a rack in a roasting dish and spoon the apple apple relish over each piece of pork. </li><li>Tuck ham over the top. </li><li>Roast in the oven for 40 minutes and serve. </li></ul>
