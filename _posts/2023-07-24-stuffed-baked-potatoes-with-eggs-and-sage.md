<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>Handful Of Leftover Roast Potatoes (Or 1 Large Potato, Chopped And Roasted)</li><li>25G/1Oz Butter</li><li>1 Shallot, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Parsnip, Grated</li><li>1 Apple, Peeled, Cored And Cut Into 1Cm/ 1/2In Cubes</li><li>Olive Oil, For Frying</li><li>2 Bacon Chops</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>5-6 Fresh Chopped Sage Leaves</li><li>Sea Salt Flakes And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Pre heat the oven at 180 c</li><li>Bake the potatoes in an ovenproof dish for 40 minutes, until tender and golden. </li><li>In the meantime, in a pan, heat a little of the butter. Add the shallot and cook until golden. </li><li>Add the parsnip and apple and fry for 2 minutes. </li><li>Remove from the heat. </li><li>Meanwhile, heat the remaining oil and fry the bacon until crisp. </li><li>Remove from the pan and set aside. </li><li>Drain the excess oil from the apples and parsnip and reserve. </li><li>Season both sides of the potato with salt and pepper. </li><li>Fry the potatoes for 2 minutes on each side. </li><li>Cut the potatoes in half and fill the inside with the eggs, parsnip and apple mixture. Season the insides with salt and pepper and crumble the bacon over the eggs. </li><li>Place the potato halves back in the oven for 3 4 minutes until the eggs are cooked and browned. </li></ul>
