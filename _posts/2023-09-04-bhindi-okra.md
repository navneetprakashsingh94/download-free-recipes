<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Sunflower Oil</li><li>1 Tsp Cumin Seeds</li><li>8 Large Garlic Cloves, Thinly Sliced</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Fresh Root Ginger, Peeled And Thinly Sliced</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Bhindi (Okra), Trimmed And Cut Into 4Cm/1 1/2In Slices</li><li>2-3 Green Chillies (Bird'S Eye Or Similar), Sliced In Half Lengthways</li><li>2 Tsp Tomato Puree</li><li>1 Tsp Turmeric</li><li>1 Tsp Salt</li><li>1 Tsp Amchoor (Mango Powder)</li><li>2 Tsp Ground Coriander</li><li>175Ml/6Fl Oz Coconut Milk</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat oil, add cumin seeds, cloves, ginger, okra, chillies and tomato puree</li><li></li><li>Fry for 2 minutes</li><li></li><li>Stir in salt, amchoor powder and coriander. </li><li></li><li>Add coconut milk. Stir. </li><li></li><li>Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes</li><li></li><li>Serve garnished with lemon slices. </li></ul>
