<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tsp Softened Butter, For Greasing</li><li>4 Medium Free-Range Eggs</li><li>4 Tbsp Double Cream</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>4 Cherry Tomatoes, Cut Into Quarters</li><li>2 Rashers Cooked Smoked Streaky Bacon, Cut Into Small Pieces</li><li>4 Tsp Grated Cheddar</li><li>12 Leaves Baby Spinach, Finely Shredded</li><li>8 Black Olives, De-Stoned And Roughly Chopped</li><li>4 Tbsp Crumbled Feta</li><li>4 Tbsp Greek-Style Or Natural Yoghurt</li><li>4 Tbsp Finely Chopped Pepper (Any Colour)</li><li>4 Pinches Dried Red Chilli Flakes</li><li>4 Pinches Ground Cumin</li><li>1 Tsp Finely Shredded Fresh Parsley, To Be Sprinkled After Cooking</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. Preheat oven to 200 c or 180 c fan forced. Melt the butter in a frying pan. </li><li>2. Saute the asparagus tips for 2 3 minutes. </li><li>3. Whisk the eggs and cream together in a large bowl. Add the asparagus tips, tomatoes, bacon and cheese. Season. Divide mixture between four oven proof dishes. Bake in the oven for 15 20 minutes. </li><li>4. Combine the ingredients for the feta dressing. Serve eggs warm with salad. </li></ul>
