<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>450G/1Lb Self-Raising Flour</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon (Optional)</li><li>600Ml/20Fl Oz Buttermilk</li><li>3 Large Free-Range Eggs, Separated</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Butter, Melted, Plus A Small Knob For Frying</li><li>1 Tsp Vanilla Extract, Or Seeds Scraped From 1 Vanilla Pod</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Blueberries, Plus Extra To Serve</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Butter, Softened</li><li>Pinch Of Ground Cinnamon</li><li>Small Pinch Salt</li><li>4 Tbsp Maple Syrup</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Whisk together the flour, baking powder, cinnamon and salt. Whisk together the buttermilk, eggs, butter and vanilla seeds. </li><li>Add the dry ingredients to the liquid ingredients and stir quickly. </li><li>Fold in the blueberries and allow to rest for 5 minutes. </li><li>Heat a large non stick frying pan on a medium heat. When hot, spoon out a quarter of the batter and cook for about 5 minutes or until bubbles form. Flip the pancakes to cook the other side. </li><li>Continue cooking the remaining batter for about 4 minutes. </li><li>To make the pecan butter melt the butter in a small pan and add the cinnamon and salt, stirring until the cinnamon is dissolved. When the butter is bubbling, add the maple syrup and let cool. To serve, spoon a spoonful of the butter in the center of a serving plate and top with a pancake. </li></ul>
