<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>180G/6Oz Pasta</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Leftover Roast Chicken, Shred Into Bite-Sized Pieces</li><li>1 Tbsp Plain Flour</li><li>Large Knob Of Butter</li><li>8 Large Chestnut Mushrooms, Peeled And Sliced</li><li> 1/2 Bunch Spring Onions, Roughly Chopped</li><li>Generous Splash Dry White Wine (Optional)</li><li>1 1/2 Tbsp Single Cream</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place a large pan of water on the stove on medium heat, add some salt and cook the pasta for 8 minutes or until cooked. </li><li>Remove the chicken from the pan, leaving the juices in the pan. </li><li>Cut the chicken into small cubes, toss with the flour and shake off any excess. </li><li>Remove the chicken pieces from the pan and set aside. </li><li>When the water is boiling add the pasta, return the chicken pieces to the pan, reduce the heat and cover. </li><li>Cook for 5 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through. </li><li>Remove the lid and add the butter and stir fry until the butter is melted. </li><li>Stir in the mushrooms and spring onions and cook for 2 minutes, stirring. </li><li>Add the wine and cream and stir to combine, season with salt and pepper. </li><li>Return the chicken pieces to the pan, toss together, and serve with crusty bread. </li></ul>
