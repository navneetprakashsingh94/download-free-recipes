<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#camembert box" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Camembert Box</a> <a href="/tags#clove" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Clove</a> <a href="/tags#sprigs thyme" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sprigs Thyme</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#ciabatta loaf half" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ciabatta Loaf Half</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#clove" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Clove</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Whole Camembert, In Its Box</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Sliced</li><li>3-4 Sprigs Fresh Thyme</li><li>1 Tsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Small Ciabatta Loaf, Cut In Half</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Peeled</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>With a sharp knife, carefully score the inside of the camembert along the length of the bottom of the round. </li><li>Press the clove into the side of each rind of the camembert, place the thyme on top of the camembert. </li><li>Brush each rind with oil and bake for 15 minutes at 375. </li><li>Cool in a covered container and set aside. </li><li>Slice the ciabatta in half lengthwise and brush with oil and the clove. </li><li>Cut into 3 pieces and serve. </li></ul>
