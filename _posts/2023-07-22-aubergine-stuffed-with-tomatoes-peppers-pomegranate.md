<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Aubergine Stuffed With Tomatoes Peppers Pomegranate using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 30 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#aubergines" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Aubergines</a> <a href="/tags#vine tomatoes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Vine Tomatoes</a> <a href="/tags#pepper jar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pepper Jar</a> <a href="/tags#bunch flatleaf parsley leaves garnish" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bunch Flatleaf Parsley Leaves Garnish</a> <a href="/tags#clove" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Clove</a> <a href="/tags#pomegranate molasses" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pomegranate Molasses</a> <a href="/tags#virgin oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Virgin Oil</a> <a href="/tags#pomegranate" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pomegranate</a> <a href="/tags#salt ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Ground Pepper</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Large Aubergines</li><li>2 Large Vine Tomatoes, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Roasted Red Pepper, Roughly Chopped (From A Jar Is Fine)</li><li>1 Small Bunch Fresh Flatleaf Parsley, Chopped, Reserving A Few Whole Leaves For Garnish</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Crushed</li><li>1 Tbsp Pomegranate Molasses</li><li>2 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>1 Pomegranate, Seeds Only</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Wash the aubergines and let them stand in a large bowl in the fridge for a couple of hours. </li><li>Slice the aubergine into thin rounds. The aubergines should be almost all soft. </li><li>Place in a casserole dish with a lid. </li><li>Prick the tomatoes with a fork. Spread out the tomatoes, cut side up, and place on the aubergine slices. </li><li>Re cover the aubergines and preheat the oven to 200 c. </li><li>Add the garlic to the tomatoes and drizzle with the pomegranate molasses. </li><li>Place in the oven until cooked, stirring them frequently. The aubergine should be golden brown. </li><li>Drain the aubergines, remove the pieces with a slotted spoon and place on a baking tray. </li><li>Sprinkle with the salt and pepper and sprinkle with the parsley leaves. </li><li>Serve warm with the pomegranate seeds on the side. </li></ul>
