<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Lemongrass Stalks, Dry Outer Leaves Removed, Roughly Chopped</li><li>3 Medium-Sized Red Onions, Quartered</li><li>6 Garlic Cloves, Peeled</li><li>25G/1Oz Fresh Root Ginger, Peeled And Roughly Chopped</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Chunk Galangal, Peeled And Roughly Chopped</li><li>3 Plump Red Chillies, Roughly Chopped Without Deseeding</li><li>3 Tbsp Sunflower Oil</li><li>2 Tsp Ground Cumin</li><li>1 Tbsp Ground Coriander</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Turmeric</li><li>1.5Kg/3Lb 5Oz Beef Chuck Steak (Or Any Good Braising Beef), Trimmed And Cut Into 3Cm/1 1/4In Cubes</li><li>400Ml/14Fl Oz Can Coconut Milk</li><li>4 Fresh Makrut Lime Leaves</li><li>1 Cinnamon Stick</li><li>1 Tbsp Soft Light Brown Sugar Or Palm Sugar</li><li>2 Tsp Tamarind Paste Or Freshly Squeezed Juice Of 1 Lime</li><li>2 Tbsp Dark Soy Sauce</li><li>2 Tsp Flaked Sea Salt, Plus Extra To Season</li><li>Ground Black Pepper</li><li>Toasted Coconut Flakes, To Serve (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Peel the onions, cut into 4 5cm wedges. </li><li>Finely dice the root ginger, galangal and chilli. </li><li>Heat the oil in a large saucepan and add the first 6 ingredients. </li><li>Saute on a medium heat until the onions are translucent, about 3 4 minutes. </li><li>Add the beef and fry for 5 minutes. </li><li>Add the milk, the spices, tamarind and sugar. </li><li>Simmer gently for 20 minutes, covered. </li><li>Add the lime juice and soy sauce. </li><li>Simmer for another 30 minutes. </li><li>Add the salt and pepper. </li><li>Leave to cool and thicken for about 5 10 minutes. </li><li>Serve over rice with the coconut flakes on top. </li></ul>
