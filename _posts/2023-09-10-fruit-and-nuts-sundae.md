<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Fruit And Nuts Sundae using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 30 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#cherries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cherries</a> <a href="/tags#Bramley apples" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bramley Apples</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#lemon juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lemon Juice</a> <a href="/tags#hazelnuts" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Hazelnuts</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>400G/14Oz Fresh Cherries</li><li>200G/7Oz (About 2) Bramley Apples</li><li>350G/12Oz Granulated Or Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Small Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>50G/2Oz Chopped, Roasted Hazelnuts</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cut the apples into eighths and remove the cores. </li><li>Combine the apples, cherries, sugar and lemon juice in a blender. Blend until smooth. </li><li>Divide the sundae between four bowls and sprinkle with the hazelnuts. </li></ul>
