<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#ripe banana smooth" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ripe Banana Smooth</a> <a href="/tags#chocolate drops" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chocolate Drops</a> <a href="/tags#sheets pastry" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sheets Pastry</a> <a href="/tags#tsp oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Oil</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li> 1/2 Small Ripe Banana, Peeled, Mashed Until Smooth</li><li>1 Tsp Chocolate Drops</li><li>2 Sheets Filo Pastry</li><li>3-4 Tsp Vegetable Oil</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Prepare the puff pastry with a rolling pin. Roll the pastry out onto a lightly floured surface. Preheat the oven to 220 celcius / 400 f. </li><li>Using a fork, poke holes in the middle of each pastry sheet. </li><li>Put the pastry on a cookie sheet and cook for 8 10 min, until they are golden and puffed. </li><li>Cut the bananas in half, insert a chocolate drop in the top of the banana and cook for a further 2 3 mins. </li></ul>
