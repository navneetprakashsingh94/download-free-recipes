<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#cooking apples windfalls need" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cooking Apples Windfalls Need</a> <a href="/tags#cider" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cider</a> <a href="/tags#sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#tsp ground cloves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Ground Cloves</a> <a href="/tags#tsp ground cinnamon" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Ground Cinnamon</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1Kg/2Lb 4Oz Cooking Apples, Plus A Few Extra If Using Windfalls, No Need To Peel Or Core</li><li>500Ml/18Fl Oz Cider</li><li>400-500G/14Oz-1Lb 2Oz Granulated Sugar</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cloves</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Core apples and cut into quarters, leaving skins on. </li><li>Mix together apple quarters and cider and cook over medium heat until apples are tender. </li><li>Remove apple quarters and place in small pan to steam, heat through. </li><li>Stir in sugar, cloves, and cinnamon. </li></ul>
