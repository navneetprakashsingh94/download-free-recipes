<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>4 Italian Sausages</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Red Onion, Cut Into Wedges</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Crushed</li><li>400G Tin Chopped Tomatoes</li><li>1 Red Pepper, Cut Into Strips</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Chilli Flakes</li><li>2 Tsp Dried Oregano</li><li>2 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Parsley, Optional</li><li>200G/7Oz Whole Wheat Pasta Of Your Choice</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. Cook sausages and set aside</li><li>2. Add oil to pan</li><li>3. Add onions, pepper strips, cloves and tomato and cook until onion is soft</li><li>4. Add sausages and seasonings</li><li>5. Simmer for around 5 minutes</li><li>6. Serve with cooked pasta</li></ul>
