<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Rib-Eye Steak, Cut Into 5Cm/2In Pieces</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>3 Shallots, Finely Sliced</li><li>1 Clove Garlic, Chopped</li><li>1 Tsp Tomato Puree</li><li>1 Tsp Chilli Powder</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Turmeric Powder</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Coconut Milk</li><li>250G/9Oz Jasmine Rice, Boiled Until Tender</li><li>20G/1Oz Chopped Fresh Coriander, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat the oil in a frying pan and fry the beef. Season well with salt and pepper and cook for 5 minutes on each side. </li><li>In a seperate pan, fry the shallots or green onions and garlic. Add the tomato puree, chilli powder and urad dal powder. Saute for 5 minutes. </li><li>Add the coconut milk and bring the mixture to the boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper and serve on a bed of rice with chopped coriander. </li></ul>
