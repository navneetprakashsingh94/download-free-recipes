<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>500Ml/18Fl Oz Pear Juice</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Cinnamon Stick</li><li>10 Cloves</li><li>1 Bay Leaf</li><li>10 Black Peppercorns</li><li>2 Williams Pears, Peeled And Cored</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Puff Pastry</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Membrillo</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Walnuts, Roughly Chopped</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Blue Cheese, Crumbled</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Creme Fraiche</li><li>1 Vanilla Pod, Seeds Scraped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 200c/fan 160c/gas 6. </li><li>Combine the cognac, sugar, cinnamon, cloves, bay leaf, peppercorns and 6 cups water in a large saucepan. </li><li>Bring to the boil over a medium heat, stirring occasionally. </li><li>Reduce the heat to low and simmer, uncovered, for about 25 minutes or until thickened and reduced to 5 cups. </li><li>Add the pears, return to the boil and simmer for 8 minutes or until just tender. </li><li>Drain and cool. </li><li>Place the puff pastry on a lightly floured surface. </li><li>Cut each pastry sheet in half. </li><li>Roll out each half into a 5 inch round. </li><li>Place one pastry round on the oiled baking sheet. </li><li>Spread 1/3 cup membrillo over the pastry and top with a pear, cut side up. </li><li>Repeat with the remaining pastry round and membrillo. </li><li>Brush the pastry edges with the egg and sprinkle with the walnuts. </li><li>Bake for 30 minutes or until golden. </li><li>Combine the roquefort, creme fraiche and vanilla seeds and brush with the remaining egg. </li><li>Serve with the pears. </li></ul>
