<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>6 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>250G/9Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Cocoa Powder</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Butter, Melted And Cooled, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Morello Cherry Jam</li><li>425G Tin Pitted Black Cherries, Drained</li><li>500Ml/18Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>2 Tbsp Icing Sugar</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Dark Chocolate, To Decorate</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 325 f. </li><li>Lightly grease the bottoms and sides of a 25cm deep round loose bottomed tart pan with removable base. Line with baking parchment and fill with baking beans. Bake for 10 12 mins, or until golden brown. </li><li>Meanwhile, to make the pastry, sift flour and cocoa into a large bowl and whisk together. Melt butter in a small saucepan over a low heat. Gradually whisk in cocoa mixture then gently whisk in eggs one at a time, stirring until blended. Transfer to a large clean bowl and fold in flour, just until combined. Spoon into prepared tart pan and smooth top. Bake for 15 20 mins, or until just set. Remove from oven and cool in pan for 5 mins then transfer to a wire rack. Cool completely. </li><li>Heat a small saucepan over a low heat. Brush a small amount of jam over the pastry base then bake for 6 8 mins, or until just firm. Turn over and bake for a further 3 mins, or until lightly toasted. Reduce heat to 350 f and carefully remove from tart pan. Allow to cool completely. </li><li>For the topping, line a baking sheet with parchment paper. In a small bowl, beat cream until soft peaks form. Gradually beat in icing sugar until stiff peaks form. Spread over base and sprinkle with grated chocolate. Refrigerate until set. </li><li>Using a serrated knife, cut tart into 8 wedges, leaving 3/4 in between them. Serve in slices with morello cherries and cream on the side. </li></ul>
