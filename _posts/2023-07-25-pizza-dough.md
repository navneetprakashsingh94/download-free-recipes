<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Flour</a> <a href="/tags#salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt</a> <a href="/tags#action yeast" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Action Yeast</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#water" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Water</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>250G/9Oz Strong White Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>5G/ 1/4Oz Salt</li><li>5G/ 1/4Oz Fast Action Dried Yeast</li><li>30Ml/1Fl Oz Olive Oil, Plus Extra For Oiling</li><li>180Ml/6Fl Oz Water</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Mix together the flour, salt and yeast. </li><li>Stir in water and oil. </li><li>Mix until a stiff dough is formed. </li><li>Let sit in a greased bowl for 30 minutes. </li></ul>
