<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>8 Large Flour Tortilla Wraps</li><li>320G/11Oz Crunchy Or Smooth Peanut Butter</li><li>8 Medium-Small Ripe Bananas</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Butter, Melted</li><li>75G/2 2/3 Oz Caster Sugar</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>240G/8 1/2Oz Dark Chocolate, Chopped</li><li>160Ml/5 1/2Fl Oz Boiling Water</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>First make your banana filling by blending the peanut butter with the a pinch of salt until smooth. </li><li>Slice the banana into 3 equal pieces. </li><li>Take a tortilla wrap, spread with the peanut butter and place banana pieces on top. </li><li>Mix the egg, butter, cinnamon, sugar, a pinch of salt and pour into the middle of the wrap. </li><li>Fold the side of the wrap over the filling, and roll. </li><li>Cut into one inch pieces. </li><li>Melt chocolate in boiling water and dip the top of the wrap into the chocolate. </li><li>Serve with sauce and ice cream. </li></ul>
