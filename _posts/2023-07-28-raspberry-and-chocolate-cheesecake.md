<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>23Cm/9In Ready-Made Sponge Base</li><li>3 Tbsp Raspberry Liqueur</li><li>200G/7Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Lemon, Zest And Juice Only</li><li>4 Tbsp Cornflour</li><li>850G/1Lb 14Oz Full-Fat Cream Cheese</li><li>3 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>1 Vanilla Pod, Seeds Scraped Out</li><li>375Ml/13Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>250G/9Oz Raspberries</li><li>150G/5Oz White Chocolate, Roughly Chopped</li><li>350G/11Oz Raspberries</li><li>3 Tbsp Raspberry Liqueur</li><li>1 Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>2 Tbsp Icing Sugar, Or To Taste</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Double Cream, Lightly Whipped, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 180c/350f. </li><li>Place the raspberry liqueur, sugar, lemon zest, and olive oil in a small saucepan. </li><li>Stir over a low heat until the sugar has dissolved. </li><li>Remove from the heat and set aside to cool. </li><li>With an electric mixer fitted with a paddle attachment, beat the cream cheese and eggs for 2 minutes, or until light and creamy. </li><li>Beat in the raspberry liquid. </li><li>Beat in the vanilla seeds. </li><li>Add the cream and beat until smooth. </li><li>Pour the mixture into the cake shell. </li><li>Arrange the raspberries on top. </li><li>Bake for 35 minutes, or until a skewer inserted in the centre of the cake comes out clean. </li><li>The cake will puff up during baking. </li><li>Remove from the oven and allow the cake to cool completely. </li><li>Once cooled, cover the cake with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours. </li><li>Decorate with melted chocolate and raspberries. </li><li>To make the topping puree the raspberries, liqueur, lemon juice, and icing sugar in a blender until smooth. </li><li>Strain through a fine sieve and cool slightly. </li><li>Beat in the cream. </li></ul>
