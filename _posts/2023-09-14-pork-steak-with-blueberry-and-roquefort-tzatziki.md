<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>800G/1Lb12Oz Lean Belly Of Pork</li><li>800Ml/1 Pint 8Fl Oz Rich Chicken Stock</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Cumin Seeds</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Fennel Seeds</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Crushed Cardamom</li><li>350G/12Oz Frozen Blueberries, Defrosted</li><li>125Ml/4Fl Oz Water</li><li>2 Tbsp Caster Sugar Or Honey</li><li>2 Tbsp Lemon Juice</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Mild Chilli Flakes</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>55G/2Oz Feta Cheese, Crumbled</li><li>3 Tbsp Plain Yoghurt</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cumin</li><li>2 Baby Gem Lettuces, Leaves Only</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Cumin Seeds</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Black Sesame Seeds</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Marinate the pork in the chicken stock, cumin seeds, fennel seeds and cardamom for an hour. </li><li>Cover and refrigerate. </li><li>Put the blueberries and water in a heavy based saucepan, bring to the boil and simmer for 10 minutes, breaking up the blueberries with the back of a fork. </li><li>Stir in the honey, lemon juice, chilli flakes, salt and pepper. </li><li>Bring to the boil again. </li><li>Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 10 minutes. </li><li>Add the roquefort, stir through and let cook a further 5 minutes until the roquefort has completely melted. </li><li>Meanwhile, mix together the yoghurt and ground cumin in a large bowl, season with black pepper and refrigerate. </li><li>Heat a grill pan or a non stick frying pan. </li><li>Cook the steak for 2 3 minutes on each side until cooked to a medium rare. </li><li>Remove from the pan and leave to rest. </li><li>Cut each steak into 3 pieces. </li><li>Place the baby gem lettuce leaves on 4 serving plates and top with the pork and the blueberry sauce. </li><li>Sprinkle with cumin seeds and sesame seeds and serve. </li></ul>
