<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2Kg/4Lb 8Oz Ham Hock</li><li>2 Carrots, Peeled And Chopped</li><li>1 Onion, Chopped</li><li>1 Head Of Celery, Chopped</li><li>1 Head Of Garlic, Chopped</li><li>1 Leek, Trimmed And Chopped</li><li>2 Bay Leaves</li><li>Bunch Of Fresh Thyme, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1.5 Litres/2 3/4 Pints Chicken Stock</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz White Wine</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz White Wine Vinegar</li><li>5 Banana Shallots, Peeled And Chopped</li><li>1 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Parsley</li><li>Handful Of Watercress, To Garnish</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Baby Potatoes, Peeled</li><li>3 Tbsp Snipped Fresh Chives</li><li>1 Red Onion, Thinly Sliced</li><li>2 Celery Sticks, Thinly Sliced</li><li>4 Free-Range Hard-Boiled Eggs (Leave Slightly Runny On The Inside), Quartered</li><li>6 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>2 Tbsp White Wine Vinegar</li><li>2 Tbsp Creme Fraiche</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place ham hock in a large saucepan. Add remaining ingredients. </li><li>Cover and allow to marinate for at least 2 hours, turning every half hour. </li><li>Remove from refrigerator and allow to come to room temperature, at least 1 hour. </li><li>Remove ham hock and strain the liquid. </li><li>Prepare the potatoes by boiling in lightly salted water for around 20 minutes until tender. </li><li>Meanwhile make the salad. Whisk together oil and vinegar. Season to taste. </li><li>Slice potatoes into bite size chunks. Toss potatoes with onion, celery, egg slices and tomato. </li><li>Drizzle with some dressing and toss to coat. </li><li>Prepare the escarole by steaming and grilling the escarole until the leaves are soft and just beginning to wilt. </li><li>Season to taste. </li><li>In a large bowl, mix together the beans and croutons. </li><li>Arrange on a large plate, top with ham hock and pour over remaining dressing. </li><li>Garnish with cilsanto, watercress and chives. </li><li>Add the roasted potatoes and then the hard boiled eggs and enjoy</li></ul>
