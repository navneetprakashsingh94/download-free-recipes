<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>125Ml/4Fl Oz Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>4-5 Cloves Of Garlic, Peeled And Crushed</li><li>12 Anchovies Preserved In Olive Oil, Drained And Chopped</li><li>120G/4Oz Unsalted Butter, Cut Into Cubes</li><li>Selection Of Raw Vegetables Such As Fennel, Cauliflower, Endive, Celery, Peppers And Courgettes, Cut Into Bite-Sized Pieces</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat the oil in a frying pan, add the garlic, anchovies, and butter, reduce the heat to low and fry, stirring all the time, for approximately 6 8 minutes</li><li>Add the vegetables and fry for a further 2 3 minutes until cooked through, add salt and freshly ground black pepper if necessary. </li><li>Divide the mixture between 4 plates and serve immediately</li></ul>
