<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>3 Bramley Apples, Peeled, Cored And Roughly Chopped</li><li>2 Ripe Pears, Peeled, Cored And Sliced</li><li>4 Bay Leaves, Lightly Crushed</li><li>1 Tbsp Brown Sugar</li><li>1 Orange, Grated Zest And Juice</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Fresh Raspberries</li><li>Creme Fraiche, Ice Cream Or Custard, To Serve</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Rolled Oats</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Plain Flour</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Ground Almonds</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Butter, Cubed</li><li>Pinch Sea Salt</li><li>6 Amaretti Biscuits, Finely Crushed</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Soft Brown Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 200c/160c fan/gas 4. </li><li>For the crumble topping, place the oats, flour and ground almonds in a bowl and stir. </li><li>Add the butter and salt. </li><li>Rub together with fingers until combined. </li><li>Press into the base and sides of a lightly greased 25cm base measurement baking dish. </li><li>For the berries, in a large saucepan, cover the apples, pears and bay leaves with a splash of water. </li><li>Add the sugar, orange zest and a pinch of salt and bring to the boil. </li><li>Reduce the heat to a simmer and cook, covered, for 20 25 minutes until the apples and pears are soft. </li><li>Add the raspberries and simmer a further 5 minutes. </li><li>Remove from the heat and set aside. </li><li>Drain the fruit, reserving 1/3 cup of the cooking liquid. </li><li>Pour the custard into the baking dish. </li><li>Arrange the apple and pear slices over the custard then top with the crumble. </li><li>Bake for 20 25 minutes, or until the crumble is golden brown and golden. </li><li>Leave to cool slightly, then spoon over the raspberries. </li><li>Serve with a dollop of creme fraiche. </li></ul>
