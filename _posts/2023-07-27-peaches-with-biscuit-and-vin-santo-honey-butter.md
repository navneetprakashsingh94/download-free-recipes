<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Peaches With Biscuit And Vin Santo Honey Butter using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#biscuits" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Biscuits</a> <a href="/tags#honey" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Honey</a> <a href="/tags#dash vin santo dessert wine" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dash Vin Santo Dessert Wine</a> <a href="/tags#peaches stones" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Peaches Stones</a> <a href="/tags#demerara sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Demerara Sugar</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>50G/2Oz Butter</li><li>12 Amaretti Biscuits, Crushed</li><li>2 Tbsp Honey, Plus Extra For Drizzling</li><li>Good Dash Vin Santo, Or Similar Sweet Dessert Wine, Plus Extra For Drizzling</li><li>4 Peaches, Halved And Stones Removed</li><li>Demerara Sugar, To Sprinkle</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 180c. </li><li>Melt the butter in a small saucepan over a low heat and stir in the honey and vin santo. </li><li>Spread half the biscuits in a 9 inch pie plate and top with half the peaches and half the liquids. </li><li>Repeat with the rest of the biscuits and peaches. </li><li>Sprinkle with demerara sugar and bake for 8 10 minutes until golden brown and piping hot. </li></ul>
