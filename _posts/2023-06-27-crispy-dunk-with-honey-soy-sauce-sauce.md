<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Crispy Dunk With Honey Soy Sauce Sauce using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 3 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#free-range eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Free-Range Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#oz soy sauce" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Soy Sauce</a> <a href="/tags#oz mirin" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Mirin</a> <a href="/tags#oz" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>6 Large Free-Range Eggs</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Light Soy Sauce</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Mirin</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Sake</li><li>6 Tbsp Caster Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>In a saucepan add the dashi, sugar, soy sauce, and mirin. </li><li>Heat gently, stir well with a wooden spoon. </li><li>Remove from the heat. </li><li>Let it cool before proceeding to the rest of the recipe. </li><li>Heat the wok on medium high, add the soy sauce and mirin mixture and gently heat. </li><li>Turn the heat down to medium low and simmer to make a thick sauce. </li><li>Turn the heat up to med high, add the dashi, reduce the sauce by 2/3 and pour in the eggs. </li><li>Scramble and cook until the eggs are cooked through. </li></ul>
