<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Large Onion, Thinly Sliced Into Crescent Moons</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Tsp Dried Oregano</li><li>1 Tsp Sweet Paprika</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Hot Paprika</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Allspice</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cumin</li><li>Pinch Ground Cloves</li><li>Pinch Ground Cinnamon</li><li>250G/9Oz Black Pudding, Peeled And Diced</li><li>2 Tomatoes, Seeds Removed, Diced</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>Thin Slices Baguette, Toasted</li><li>2 Tbsp Finely Chopped Parsley</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat oil in a skillet. Add onion moons, cloves and oregano. Cook onion for 5 minutes. Add spices, chorizo and diced tomatoes. Cook for 4 minutes. Season with salt and pepper and remove from heat. </li><li>Coat each slice of bread with oil. Dip in spiced tomato mix. Coat again. Place on a baking sheet and bake in preheated 425 degree oven until golden, about 10 minutes. </li><li>Serve with tortilla chips. </li></ul>
