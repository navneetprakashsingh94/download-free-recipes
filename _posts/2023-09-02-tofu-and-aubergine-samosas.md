<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#aubergines rounds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Aubergines Rounds</a> <a href="/tags#pinch ground turmeric" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pinch Ground Turmeric</a> <a href="/tags#salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt</a> <a href="/tags#tsp chilli powder" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Chilli Powder</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Oil</a> <a href="/tags#plain yoghurt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Yoghurt</a> <a href="/tags#tsp sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#tsp cumin seeds frying pan ground coffee grinder pestle mortar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Cumin Seeds Frying Pan Ground Coffee Grinder Pestle Mortar</a> <a href="/tags#coriander leaves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Coriander Leaves</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>400G/14Oz Small Japanese-Style Aubergines, Thinly Sliced Into Rounds</li><li>Good Pinch Ground Turmeric</li><li>Salt, To Taste</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Red Chilli Powder</li><li>2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil</li><li>250Ml/9Fl Oz Plain Yoghurt</li><li>1-1 1/2 Tsp Sugar</li><li>1 Rounded Tsp Cumin Seeds, Toasted In A Frying Pan And Ground In A Clean Coffee Grinder Or Pestle And Mortar</li><li>Handful Fresh Coriander Leaves, Chopped, To Garnish</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat the oil and add cumin seeds. </li><li>Once they are fragrant, put in the tofu. </li><li>Sprinkle turmeric, salt and chilli powder. </li><li>Cook for a few minutes and mix thoroughly. </li><li>Add yoghurt, sugar and pestle mortar. </li><li>Blend well. </li><li>Add mashed aubergine strips and blend all well. </li><li>Add coriander leaves and keep aside to allow flavours to develop. </li><li>If desired, add crushed paprika and stir. </li><li>Samosas are ready to serve with yogurt/tartart salad or hot bread. </li></ul>
