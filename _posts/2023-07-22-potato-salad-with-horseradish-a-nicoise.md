<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>250G/9Oz Salad Potatoes, Diced</li><li> 1/2 Small Bunch Spring Onions</li><li> 1/2 Apple, Grated Or Finely Chopped</li><li>Fresh Horseradish (To Taste), Peeled And Finely Chopped</li><li>25G/1Oz Anchovies Or Pickled Herring</li><li>Half A Head Of Lettuce, Shredded</li><li>2 Carrots, Grated</li><li>4 Medium Cooked Beetroot</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs, Hard Boiled</li><li>6 Cornichons, Finely Chopped</li><li>A Few Sprigs Of Dill, Finely Chopped</li><li>Half Large Cucumber, Seeds Removed And Finely Chopped</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place all the ingredients, except the half cucumber, into a bowl. </li><li>Add the reserved seasonings to the boiled eggs and mix well. </li><li>Stir through the salad, but keep it in a warm place for 15 to 20 minutes. </li><li>Before serving, sprinkle with cucumber seeds. </li></ul>
