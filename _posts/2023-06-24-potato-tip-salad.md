<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Baking Potato (See Recipe Tips And Weigh Before Cooking), Scrubbed And Dried</li><li>Light Rapeseed, Vegetable Or Sunflower Oil</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>Small Handful Grated Cheddar</li><li>1 Ripe Tomato, Diced</li><li>Few Green Jalapeno Pepper Slices From A Jar</li><li>1 Small, Ripe Avocado (Or A Few Frozen Avocado Slices)</li><li> 1/2 Lime Or Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>Handful Mixed Seeds, Dukkah, Za'Atar Or Chilli Flakes</li><li>227G Tin Baked Beans</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Curry Powder</li><li>Natural Yoghurt And Lime Pickle (Optional), To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat oven to 200 degrees. </li><li>Peel potato tips and slice very thin, either with a mandolin or a food processor. </li><li>Mix in the oil, salt and pepper and toss well. </li><li>Place in a baking tray lined with baking paper and bake in the oven for 30 minutes, stirring them from time to time, until golden and crispy. </li><li>Allow to cool. </li><li>For the dressing in a bowl, mix the cheese, tin beans, tomato, pickles, avocado, lime juice, seeds dukkah and chilli flakes until well combined. </li><li>To assemble, put the toasted potato tips in a bowl and drizzle over the dressing. </li></ul>
