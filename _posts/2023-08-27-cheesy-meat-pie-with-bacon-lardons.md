<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>300G/10 1/2Oz '00' Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>3 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Large Carrot, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Celery Stalk, Trimmed, Finely Chopped</li><li>200G/7Oz Smoked Bacon Lardons</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Beef Mince</li><li>2 Tbsp Tomato Puree</li><li>1 X 400G Tin Tomatoes</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Red Wine</li><li>Handful Fresh Basil Leaves, Roughly Torn</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>3-4 Tbsp Freshly Grated Parmesan, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Put the noodles into a bowl and make a well in the centre and make a hole in the middle. </li><li>Crack the eggs into the well and sprinkle over the top and stir with a fork to bring the eggs together. </li><li>Add water, a little at a time and knead to a thick dough. </li><li>Knead well and place in a large bowl. </li><li>Wrap in clingfilm and leave to rise for 1 hour. </li><li>Fry the bacon in a skillet until crispy. </li><li>Reserve on a plate with paper towels. </li><li>Drain most of the fat from the pan and add the onion, cloves, carrot and celery and fry until soft. </li><li>Drain off any excess fat. </li><li>Chop the bacon lardons into smaller pieces and add to the pan. </li><li>Fry until golden then add the mince and fry, breaking up the lumps with a wooden spoon until browned. </li><li>Mix in the tomato puree, chopped tomatoes, wine and basil. </li><li>Season with salt and pepper, add the veg, season with salt and pepper and bring to the boil. </li><li>Reduce the heat to low and cook for 30 mins, stirring occasionally. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 190c, 170c fan, gas mark 4. </li><li>Put a piece of baking paper on a baking tray, and spoon the mince mixture into it, smoothing out over the surface. </li><li>Sprinkle the parmesan over the top and bake for 30 mins or until golden brown. </li><li>Serve with a green salad and a bottle of good white wine. </li></ul>
