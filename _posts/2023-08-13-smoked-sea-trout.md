<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#sea trout" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sea Trout</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#miso paste" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Miso Paste</a> <a href="/tags#samphire woody" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Samphire Woody</a> <a href="/tags#chillis" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chillis</a> <a href="/tags#lemon" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lemon</a> <a href="/tags#salt ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Ground Pepper</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Sea Trout (400G/14Oz), Pin-Boned</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Butter, Cubed</li><li>25G/1Oz White Miso Paste</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Samphire, Woody Ends Removed</li><li>2 Red Chillis</li><li>1 Lemon, Halved</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place fish in the bottom of a steamer. </li><li>Cover with water, and heat to boiling point. </li><li>Cover and steam over simmering water for 10 to 12 minutes depending on size of fish. </li><li>Heat oil and butter in a pan and cook fish for 3 minutes on each side. </li><li>Reduce heat to medium, and reduce heat to low. </li><li>Add miso paste to pan, stirring well to combine. </li><li>Stir in fish samphire, chillis and lemon juice. </li><li>Season with salt and pepper to taste. </li><li>Cook for 1 minute, serve fish on a warm plate. </li><li>Drizzle with miso mixture. </li></ul>
