<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Large Celeriac</li><li>150G/5Oz Butter, Plus Extra For The Sauce</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>1 Large Onion</li><li>2 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>3 Cox'S Apples, Peeled</li><li>425Ml/ 3/4 Pint London Pride Ale (Or Similar)</li><li>4 Pork Chops</li><li>2 Tsp Vegetable Oil</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Dijon Mustard</li><li>1 Tbsp White Wine Vinegar</li><li>3 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>250G/9Oz Rocket Leaves</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Boil the celeriac in boiling salted water for 10 minutes. Drain, refresh with cold water, drain again, shave and set aside</li><li></li><li>Make the creme fraiche</li><li>Cut the onion into sliced wedges and caramelise lightly in a sautee pan, a drizzle of oil and the butter sauce. Keep warm. </li><li></li><li>Heat a drizzle of oil in a pan and add the onions. Cook, stirring often, for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally, then stir in the apple and beer. Cook over high heat, stirring frequently until the mixture is reduced to about 1 cup. Remove from the heat. </li><li></li><li>Season the pork chops with the salt and pepper. Dredge them in the flour and add the tsp oil to pan. Sear the chops for 2 minutes on each side. Reduce heat to low and add the reserved creme fraiche. Cover and cook over low heat for 5 minutes. </li><li></li><li>Remove from the heat and stir in the roasted celeriac. Season with salt and pepper and serve on salad plate. For the sauce</li><li></li><li>Make the dressing</li><li>Whisk the mustard with the vinegar. Gradually whisk in the oil in a steady stream. Season to taste with salt and pepper. </li><li></li><li>To serve, arrange the rocket leaves on individual plates. Arrange the pork chops around the plates. Spoon the creme fraiche sauce over the chops. Serve with the apple gratin. </li></ul>
