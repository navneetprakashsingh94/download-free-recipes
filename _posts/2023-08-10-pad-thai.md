<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Soak noodles in hot water for 15 minutes. </li><li>Place all other ingredients in a small bowl and stir well until combined. </li><li>Combine all ingredients for the noodle salad and toss together. </li><li>Drain the noodles well and divide between two bowls. </li><li>Add the noodle salad to the bowl, toss to combine. </li></ul>
