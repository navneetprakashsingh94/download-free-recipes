<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 X 376Ml Tin Condensed Milk</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>3 Tbsp Coconut Rum</li><li>Large Pinch Sea Salt</li><li>1 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>200G/7Oz Unsalted Butter, Softened</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Golden Caster Sugar, Plus 2 Tbsp</li><li>4 Medium Free-Range Eggs</li><li>2 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Mixed Fresh Or Frozen Berries</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Ginger</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For scone ice cream</li><li>In a small pan, simmer 2 tbsp of tepid milk until slightly reduced. </li><li>Add 2 tbsp of rum. </li><li>Stir and set aside. </li><li>In a large mixing bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light. </li><li>Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each. </li><li>Add vanilla, stir, then fold in flour and add berry, cinnamon and ginger. </li><li>Pour into a nonstick pan lined with baking parchment and bake for 30 minutes at 355f. </li><li>Remove from oven. </li><li>Cool on a rack, then store in an airtight container. </li><li>I prefer a pan that is big enough to hold my ice cream mix, for this. </li><li>You can also use a crockpot. </li></ul>
