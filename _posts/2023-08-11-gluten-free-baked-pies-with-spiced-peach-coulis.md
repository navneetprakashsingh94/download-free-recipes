<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Freshly Brewed Strong Black Tea</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Soft Dark Brown Sugar</li><li>100G/3 1/2 Oz Mixed Dried Fruit</li><li>4 Large Free-Range Eggs</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Caster Sugar, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>30G/1Oz Cornflour</li><li>250Ml/9Fl Oz Milk</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Mixed Spice</li><li>Icing Sugar, For Dusting</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the pie preheat the oven to 180 c/350 f/gas 4. Grease four 6 ounce ramekins. In a bowl using a hand mixer, beat the philly and brown sugar until smooth and creamy. Stir in the chopped fruit and fruit. Set aside. </li><li>In another bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and sugar until thick and creamy. Whisk in the cornflour, milk and spice. </li><li>In another bowl, beat egg whites until firm peaks form. Fold one third of the beaten egg whites into the philly mixture. Mix in the remaining egg whites. Divide the fruit mixture between the ramekins, filling to the top. Place the ramekins in a baking pan and pour enough boiling water into the pan to come halfway up the sides of the ramekins. Bake for 30 40 mins or until lightly golden. Turn off the oven but leave the pies in the oven for a further 20 mins or until cooked. </li><li>Sprinkle with raw sugar. Serve the pies with a dollop of the creme fraiche and a sprig of mint. </li></ul>
