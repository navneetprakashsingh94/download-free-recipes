<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Tbsp Chipotle Paste</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Lime, Cut In Half</li><li>250G/9Oz Gnocchi</li><li>1 Red Pepper, Seeds Removed And Cut Into Chunks</li><li>1 Orange Or Yellow Pepper, Seeds Removed And Cut Into Chunks</li><li>1 Small Red Onion, Cut Into Chunks</li><li>Salt</li><li>Avocado Slices</li><li>Soured Cream</li><li>Tomato Salsa</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>To make the sauce i used 2 tbs of oil. In a bowl, mix together the chipotle paste and 2 tbs oil and whisk to combine. Set aside. Grate the zest from the lime half and mix with the gnocchi in a mixing bowl. Set aside. </li><li>Heat a non stick frying pan or wok on a medium high. Put the chipotle lime mixture into the pan and stir to combine. Add the ground gnocchi, toss to combine, and turn off the heat. Let the gnocchi steam for about 2 minutes. Toss and turn off the heat. </li><li>Add a little olive oil to the frying pan or wok and stir to coat the pan. Add the pepper seeds and a pinch of salt and pepper. Let it sit for 1 minute. </li><li>Take out the gnocchi from the pan, and pour the oil into the wok or pan. Keep the wok/wok warm until you are ready to serve. </li><li>Once the wok/wok is heated, add a small amount of oil to the wok/wok and stir fry the gnocchi and the pepper seeds until fragrant. Add in the diced onion and fried peppers, stir fry until the onion is golden and the peppers are tender. </li><li>Then drain the gnocchi in a colander and add it to the pan with the hot oil and spice mixture. Add the sour cream to the wok/wok and stir fry the gnocchi until lightly browned. </li><li>Add the chopped avocado, stir fry until heated through, and spoon into warmed bowls. Garnish with a dollop of sour cream, and salsa. Serve immediately</li></ul>
