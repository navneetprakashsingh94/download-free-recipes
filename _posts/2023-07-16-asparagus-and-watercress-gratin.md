<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>50G/2Oz Butter</li><li>2 Shallots, Chopped</li><li>2 Sprigs Fresh Thyme</li><li>450G/1Lb Asparagus, Stalks Roughly Chopped, Tips Set Aside For The Stir Fry</li><li>125Ml/4 1/2Fl Oz White Wine</li><li>450Ml/16Fl Oz Chicken Stock (Vegetarians May Substitute With Vegetable Stock)</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>110G/4Oz Jersey Royal Potatoes, Cooked And Cut Into Cubes</li><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>60G/2 1/2Oz Watercress, Leaves Only</li><li>1 Small Bunch Fresh Chives, Chopped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Melt butter over medium high heat</li><li>Add shallots and thyme</li><li>Saute until softened</li><li>Add asparagus, a little bit of water, salt and pepper</li><li>When asparagus is cooked, remove from heat and set aside. </li><li>In another saucepan, cook asparagus reserving water, and add a bit more water and cook until tender. </li><li>Add wine and cook about 3 5 minutes</li><li>Add the asparagus water and cook until heated through. </li><li>Mix in cream and season with salt and pepper. </li><li>Saute potatoes in small amount of oil. </li><li>Arrange asparagus mixture in gratin dish. </li><li>Season with salt and pepper and cover. </li><li>Place potatoes on the asparagus and cover with the remaining asparagus water. </li><li>Garnish with watercress and chives and serve. </li></ul>
