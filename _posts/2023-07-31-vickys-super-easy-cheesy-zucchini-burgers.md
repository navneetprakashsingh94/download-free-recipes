<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Vickys Super Easy Cheesy Zucchini Burgers using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#dash oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dash Oil</a> <a href="/tags#medium courgette" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Medium Courgette</a> <a href="/tags#rasher bacon" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Rasher Bacon</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp plain yoghurt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Plain Yoghurt</a> <a href="/tags#feta" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Feta</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp parmesan" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Parmesan</a> <a href="/tags#pinch ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pinch Ground Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#egg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg</a> <a href="/tags#tomatoes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tomatoes</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>Dash Vegetable Oil, For Greasing</li><li>1 Medium Courgette, Trimmed, Grated</li><li>1 Rasher Cooked Bacon, Chopped</li><li>1 Heaped Tbsp Plain Yoghurt</li><li>25G/1Oz Feta</li><li>1 Heaped Tbsp Grated Parmesan</li><li>Pinch Ground Black Pepper</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten</li><li>2 Tomatoes, Sliced, To Serve (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat oven to gas 6 / 400f</li><li>Grease a griddle and place in the oven to heat for 20 minutes</li><li>Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a pan then cook the zucchini, bacon and yoghurt for 8 10 minutes</li><li>Remove from heat and leave in the pan to cool</li><li>Crumble the feta and parmesan together</li><li>Chred the courgette and mix with the bacon, yoghurt and zucchini mixture</li><li>Divide the mixture between 6 swiss rolls and roll up</li><li>Lightly coat each with oil and place on the griddle, seam side down</li><li>Cook for 10 15 minutes or until the bottoms are lightly browned and crispy</li><li>Serve the burgers with the egg and diced tomatoes</li></ul>
