<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Salted Butter</li><li>4 Shallots, Finely Chopped</li><li>200G/7Oz Parmesan, Grated</li><li>70G/2 1/2Oz Mrs Temple'S Alpine Cheese Or Gruyere, Grated</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>350Ml/12Fl Oz Chicken Stock</li><li>Salt And White Pepper</li><li>Pickled Asparagus, Cut Into Small Pieces</li><li>Wild Garlic Leaves (Optional)</li><li>Crispy Fried Shallots</li><li>Rocket Cress</li><li>Selection Edible Flowers (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>First prepare the cream sauce by adding the shallots to a saucepan over a low heat for 5 min. </li><li>Add in the cream and chicken stock, stirring continuously. </li><li>Add in cheeses, salt and pepper, bring to the boil, stirring constantly. </li><li>Reduce the heat and simmer for 5 min. </li><li>Remove from heat. </li><li>Set aside till cool to room temperature. </li><li>Then transfer the cheese sauce to a heavy duty food processor, add in the fresh asparagus, and process till finely minced. </li><li>Combine the butter, shallots and cheese sauce in a large saute/fry pan, and heat over low heat for 5 min. </li><li>Remove from the heat and chill. </li><li>In a shallow serving bowl, arrange the asparagus. </li><li>Eat with crackers, the crispy shallots, the rocket cress, and any choice of flowers. </li></ul>
