<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>250G/9Oz Risotto Rice</li><li> 1/4 Bottle White Wine</li><li>290Ml/10Fl Oz/ 1/2 Pint Chicken Stock (Or 2 Stock Cubes And  1/2 Pint Boiling Water)</li><li>1 Bunch Asparagus, Chopped</li><li>150G/5Oz Fresh Peas (Shelled Weight)</li><li>100G/4Oz Mature Cheddar, Grated</li><li>25G/1Oz Parmesan</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place oil in saucepan and soften onion. </li><li>Add in the risotto rice. </li><li>When the rice is translucent add in wine and bring to boil to allow the wine to evaporate then add in the stock cubes and water. </li><li>Once the stock has been absorbed remove from heat and allow to rest for a few min. </li><li>Cut the asparagus into pcs and simmer in a pan of boiling water for 3 min. </li><li>Remove asparagus and peas, then drain and leave to cold. </li><li>In a large ovenproof dish, combine the asparagus and peas, add in the risotto and season lightly with salt and pepper. </li><li>Scatter the cheddar and parmesan over the top and cook in the oven at 200c for a further 20 min, till the cheese is melted and bubbling. </li></ul>
