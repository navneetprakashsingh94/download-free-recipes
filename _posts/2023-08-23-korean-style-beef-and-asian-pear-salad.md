<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>300-400G/10 1/2-14Oz Rib-Eye Steak</li><li>2-3 Spring Onions, Each Chopped Into 2-3 Pieces</li><li>2 Little Gem Lettuces, Halved Lengthways</li><li>1 Tbsp Sesame Seeds, Toasted</li><li> 1/2 Ripe Asian Pear, Cored And Cut Into Chunks (See Recipe Tip)</li><li>4 Tbsp Light Soy Sauce</li><li>2 Tbsp Soft Brown Sugar</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves</li><li>25G/1Oz Fresh Root Ginger, Peeled And Chopped</li><li>2 Spring Onions, Chopped</li><li>1 Tbsp Sesame Seeds</li><li>1 Tbsp Sesame Oil</li><li>Pinch Salt And Pepper</li><li>2 Tbsp Gochujang (Korean Fermented Chilli Paste)</li><li>1 Tbsp Miso Paste</li><li> 1/2 Tbsp Light Soy Sauce</li><li>1 Tbsp Honey</li><li>1 Tsp Sesame Oil</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Combine the seasoning ingredients in a jar and shake well. </li><li>Marinate the ribeye steak in the bowl of marinade for at least 1 hour. </li><li>Mix together the remaining marinade ingredients and set aside. </li><li>Slice the steak very thinly across the grain. </li><li>Heat a small non stick pan with 1 tablespoon of oil and quickly saute the steak over moderately high heat for 1 minute on each side. </li><li>Transfer the steak to a warm plate, discarding the marinade. </li><li>Heat the pan with 1 tablespoon of the oil. </li><li>Saute the spring onions over moderately high heat for 2 to 3 minutes. </li><li>Add the lettuce and toss until wilted. </li><li>Place on plates, top with the steak and sesame seeds and spoon the pear juice on top. </li><li>Top with a mound of the greens. </li><li>Serve immediately. </li></ul>
