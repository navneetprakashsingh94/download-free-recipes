<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Pork Sausages</li><li>3 Spring Onions</li><li>2Cm/ 3/4In Piece Root Ginger, Grated</li><li>Handful Fresh Coriander, Leaves Picked, Stalks Chopped</li><li>4 Tsp Dark Soy Sauce</li><li>2 Small Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Pinches Dried Chilli Flakes</li><li>1 Tsp Sesame Oil (Optional)</li><li>2 Tsp Vegetable Oil</li><li>1 Carrot, Cut Into Thin Matchsticks</li><li>1 Nest Dried Egg Noodles</li><li>Handful Beansprouts</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat your grill on medium high. </li><li>Place the pork sausages in a large bowl and mix with spring onions, ginger, coriander and soy sauce. </li><li>Put the cloves, chilli, sesame oil and 2 tbsp of oil in a small jug and whisk well. </li><li>Rub the sausage mix onto both sides of each roll and wrap it up tightly and tie it with kitchen string, being careful not to break the string. </li><li>Prepare the carrot matchsticks according to packet instructions. </li><li>Place the sausages on the grill and cook for 10 minutes, turning once, until lightly browned. </li><li>Remove from the grill, wrap and keep warm. </li><li>Cook the egg noodles in a large pan of boiling salted water until just tender, drain, toss in a large bowl and mix in the beansprouts. </li><li>Serve the sausage rolls with the noodles. </li></ul>
