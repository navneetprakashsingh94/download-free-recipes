<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Sea Bass Or Sea Bream Fillets (Each Around 200G/7Oz), Scales Removed But Skin On</li><li>1 Tsp Flaked Sea Salt, Plus Extra To Season</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Cayenne Pepper</li><li>2 1/2 Tsp English Mustard Powder</li><li>Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>4 Tbsp Sunflower Oil</li><li>1 Heaped Tsp Yellow Mustard Seeds</li><li> 3/4 Tsp Black Mustard Seeds</li><li> 3/4 Tsp Cumin Seeds</li><li>1 Medium Onion, Finely Sliced</li><li>2 Long Green Chillies, Stalk Trimmed And Cut In Half Without Deseeding</li><li>1 Bay Leaf</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Turmeric</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Garam Masala</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Ripe Tomatoes, Roughly Chopped</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Rice, Cooked According To Packet Instructions</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place fish fillets in a bowl and add spices salt pepper. Cover in plastic wrap and refrigerate for 15mins. </li><li>Heat a frying pan over med. Heat add oil and mustard seeds and cumin seeds and allow to crackle. Add onion chillies and bay leaf and saute for 4mins. </li><li>Add turmeric, garam masala and tomatos to frying pan. Cover and cook for 5mins stirring occasionally. Remove the bay leaf and serve over rice. </li></ul>
