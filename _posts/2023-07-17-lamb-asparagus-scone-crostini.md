<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the scone dip place hazelnuts in a small pan. Roast for 5 to 10 minutes on low. You want them to become fragrant. The skins will loosen, and you will have a nut filled picada. Drain the hazelnuts from the skin and place in a food processor. Add garlic, lemon zest and a pinch of sugar. Blend and add a few tablespoons of olive oil at a time until you have a smooth paste. Set aside. </li><li>For the filling heat 2 tbsp oil in a pan. Add onions and saute until translucent. Add lime juice, salt and sugar. Cook over low heat, stirring often. Allow to cool. </li><li>For the pastry sift flours, buckwheat groats and salt into a food processor. Add butter cubes and pulse till mixture resembles coarse meal. Combine egg yolk and cream and add to flour mixture. Mix thoroughly. Add water, 1/2 tbsp at a time, while pulsing. Keep kneading until dough comes</li></ul>
