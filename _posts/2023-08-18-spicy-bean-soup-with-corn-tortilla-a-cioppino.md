<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>First make the bean soup. </li><li>The sauce. </li><li>Roast the cumin and coriander seeds and stir until toasted. </li><li>Add a drizzle of oil in a big frying pan and cook the onions, and cloves with the green chillies until soft. </li><li>Blitz the roasted seeds, chili, bay leaves, tomatoes, and spices until smooth. </li><li>Put a little oil in a large pot and add the tin of smashed tomatoes and cook on medium heat until softened and then add the tomato, chili paste, beans, and lemon pepper and season. </li><li>Add the stock and spices, cover with a lid, and simmer on medium heat for 30 minutes until beans are soft and have a creamy consistency. </li><li>For the grilled tortillas. </li><li>Grill the tortillas in a frying pan with a little oil for just a few seconds until just crisp. </li><li>Wrap each tortilla in a towel and cook on a baking tray until hot and pliable. </li><li>For the garnish. </li><li>Cut the corn in half and pull out the kernels and put in a bowl. </li><li>Smash the tomatoes and mix with the peppers, lime juice, cream and seasonings. </li><li>Spoon the soup into a bowl and top with a tortilla. </li><li>Garnish with sliced onion and coriander. </li></ul>
