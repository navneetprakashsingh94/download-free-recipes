<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Semolina, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>1 Tbsp Squid Ink</li><li>140Ml/4 1/2Fl Oz Lukewarm Water</li><li>Salt</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Small Red Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>200G/7Oz Courgettes, Grated</li><li>4 Plum Tomatoes, Peeled And Chopped</li><li>250G/9Oz Cooked Prawns, Cut Into 1-2 Cm/ 1/2- 3/4In Pieces</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Live Clams, Scrubbed</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Crushed</li><li>Pinch Dried Chilli Flakes</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz White Wine</li><li>4 Courgette Flowers, Shredded</li><li>2 Tbsp Finely Chopped Fresh Parsley</li><li>Extra Virgin Olive Oil, For Drizzling</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Put the semolina, squid ink and salt into a large bowl and mix in the water. </li><li>Knead until soft and pliable about 5 minutes. </li><li>Cool the dough and use to line a 10 inch tin or shallow oven proof dish and press it flat. </li><li>Cover with clingfilm and leave to rest for at least 30 minutes. </li><li>Heat half the oil in a large pan and saute the onion until it softens. </li><li>Add the courgettes and cook until slightly golden. </li><li>Add the tomatoes and their liquid and cook for 5 minutes. </li><li>Add the prawns and clams and cook for another 5 minutes. </li><li>Add the clove, chilli flakes and white wine. </li><li>Cook the sauce for 3 4 minutes and add the courgette flowers. </li><li>Season to taste with salt and ground black pepper. </li><li>Sprinkle in the parsley and serve with a dollop of butter and fresh mozzarella. </li></ul>
