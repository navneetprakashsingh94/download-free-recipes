<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#pasta penne" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pasta Penne</a> <a href="/tags#oil oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil Oil</a> <a href="/tags#cloves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cloves</a> <a href="/tags#tsp flakes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Flakes</a> <a href="/tags#tin tomatoes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tin Tomatoes</a> <a href="/tags#salt ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Ground Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#cheddar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cheddar</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Dried Pasta, Such As Penne</li><li>3 Tbsp Oil, Ideally Olive Oil</li><li>4 Garlic Cloves, Very Thinly Sliced</li><li> 1/4- 1/2 Tsp Dried Chilli Flakes, To Taste</li><li>400G Tin Chopped Tomatoes</li><li>Salt And Ground Black Pepper</li><li>25G/1Oz Cheddar, Finely Grated, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat oil in a pan on medium heat</li><li>Add cloves, flakes and tomatoes</li><li>Add pasta, salt, pepper and 2/3 of the cheese</li></ul>
