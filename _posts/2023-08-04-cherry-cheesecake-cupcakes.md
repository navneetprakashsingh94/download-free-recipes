<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Plain Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>90G/3 1/4Oz Icing Sugar</li><li>45G/1 3/4Oz Ground Almonds</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Butter, Cubed And Chilled, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten With 1 Free-Range Egg Yolk, Chilled</li><li>1-2 Tbsp Ice-Cold Water</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Butter, At Room Temperature</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Icing Sugar</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Ground Almonds</li><li>2 Large Free-Range Eggs</li><li>25G/1Oz Plain Flour</li><li>410G Tin Black Cherry Pie Filling (Not Cherries In Syrup)</li><li>340G/11 3/4Oz Full-Fat Cream Cheese</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Mascarpone</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>10 Glace Cherries, Halved</li><li>10 Whole Cherries, With Stalks</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Flaked Almonds, Toasted</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the pastry, place the flour, sugar and almonds in a food processor and pulse until combined. </li><li>Add the butter, egg yolk and water and pulse until well combined. </li><li>Roll into a small rectangle, wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate. </li><li>Preheat oven to 375 f. Line a 12 hole cupcake pan with paper liners. </li><li>For the batter, beat the butter and icing sugar in a bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy, then beat in the almonds and eggs, one at a time. </li><li>Add the flour and beat well. </li><li>Spoon 1/4 cup of batter into each lined cupcake liners. </li><li>Bake in preheated oven for 25 30 mins. </li><li>Remove the cupcakes from the oven and set aside to cool completely. </li><li>For the topping, stir together the cherries syrup, cream cheese, mascarpone and caster sugar in a bowl until smooth. </li><li>Stir in vanilla. </li><li>For the icing, whip the cream in a bowl until stiff. </li><li>Fold in the glace cherries and reserved cherry topping. </li><li>Pipe the icing on the warm cupcakes. </li><li>Top with the frozen cherries. </li><li>Decorate with chopped almonds. </li><li>Serve. </li></ul>
