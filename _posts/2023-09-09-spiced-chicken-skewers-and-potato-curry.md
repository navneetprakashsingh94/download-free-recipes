<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Combine chicken, potato, onion, chillies and cloves in a food processor and blitz into a smooth mixture. </li><li></li><li>Combine chicken, turmeric, cumin, coriander, salt, garam masala and season with pepper. </li><li></li><li>Thread chicken onto 12 12 skewers and brush with oil. </li><li></li><li>Heat a bbq pan and cook kebabs for 3 4 mins each side, until golden brown and cooked through. </li><li></li><li>Mix potato florets and turmeric in a large pan and cover with water. Bring to the boil, then simmer for 10 12 minutes until just tender. Drain. </li><li></li><li>Heat ghee or clarified butter in a large pan, add onion and cook for 2 mins until just starting to brown. Add ginger and cook for 2 mins, stirring. </li><li></li><li>Season chicken and marinade with salt and pepper. Toss in onion and cook for 1 min. Mix in lemon juice. Arrange chicken and potatoes on a serving platter with potato florets and coriander leaves. </li><li></li><li>Serve with naan or roti bread. </li></ul>
