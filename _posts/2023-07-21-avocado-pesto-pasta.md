<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#pasta penne fusilli" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pasta Penne Fusilli</a> <a href="/tags#avocado" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Avocado</a> <a href="/tags#cloves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cloves</a> <a href="/tags#coconut oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Coconut Oil</a> <a href="/tags#tsp salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Salt</a> <a href="/tags#lemon juice zest" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lemon Juice Zest</a> <a href="/tags#mint leaves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Mint Leaves</a> <a href="/tags#peas" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Peas</a> <a href="/tags#chilli" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chilli</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>375G/13Oz Pasta, Such As Penne Or Fusilli</li><li>1 Large Avocado (Or 2 Small)</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves</li><li>2 Tbsp Coconut Oil, Melted</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Salt</li><li>1 Lemon, Juice And Zest</li><li>6 Fresh Mint Leaves</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Fresh Peas (Or Frozen And Defrosted)</li><li>1 Large Red Chilli (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat the oven to 180c. </li><li>Cut the avocado in half and remove the stone. </li><li>Cut out the avocado flesh with a teaspoon and place into a food processor with the cloves, a splash of lemon juice and the salt and process until smooth. </li><li>While the machine is running add the coconut oil a little at a time until the pesto has a creamy consistency and the pesto is smooth. </li><li>Then if the pesto is too thick, add a little water. </li><li>Add the pesto to the pasta and gently stir in to coat. </li><li>Cook the pasta in a boiling salted water until al dente. </li><li>Drain the pasta and place on a serving platter. </li><li>Top with the mint leaves, peas, chilies and avocado pesto. </li></ul>
