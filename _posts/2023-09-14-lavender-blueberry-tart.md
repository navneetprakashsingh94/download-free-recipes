<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>350G/12Oz Self-Raising Flour, Sifted, Plus A Little Extra For Dusting</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>85G/3Oz Unsalted Butter, Softened</li><li>45G/1 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Tsp Dried Lavender, Crushed</li><li>1 Lemon, Zest Only</li><li>175Ml/6Fl Oz Full-Fat Milk, At Room Temperature</li><li>2 X 227G Tubs Clotted Cream</li><li>1 Tsp Vanilla Bean Paste</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Blueberry Jam</li><li>180G/6Oz Fresh Blueberries</li><li> 1/2 Lemon, Zest Only</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the pastry</li><li>Mix the flour, baking powder and salt in a bowl. Mix the sugar, lavender and lemon zest together and put in the bowl. Add the butter and cut in by hand. Use a pastry blender or two knives to cut the butter into the flour mix. If it s too dry, add a bit more milk. Divide the dough in two, wrap in cling film and chill in the fridge for an hour. </li><li></li><li>When the pastry has chilled, preheat your oven to 180 c, and grease a 30 x 15cm cake pan. On a lightly floured surface, roll out half of the dough into an 8cm circle. Place the pan into the pan and press the dough evenly around the bottom and up the sides. Leave the extra dough to cover the pie tin. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes. Cool on a wire rack. </li><li></li><li>For the topping</li><li>Whip the cream with the vanilla essence until thickened. Spread over the tart and fill in the gaps with jam, covering completely. Top with blueberries and lemon juice. Refrigerate for 30 minutes. </li></ul>
