<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>75G/3Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>2 Red Chillies, Roughly Chopped</li><li>3 Plum Tomatoes, Roughly Chopped</li><li>8 Lime Leaves</li><li>2 Lemongrass Stems, Tough Outer Leaves Removed, Finely Chopped</li><li>25G/1Oz Roughly Chopped Fresh Ginger</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Peeled, Left Whole</li><li>2 Small Shallots, Roughly Chopped</li><li>4 Tbsp Thai Fish Sauce (Nam Pla)</li><li>40Ml/1 1/2Fl Oz Sesame Oil</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Dark Soy Sauce</li><li>2 Tbsp Clear Honey</li><li>3 Limes, Juice And Zest</li><li>110G/4Oz Creme Fraiche</li><li>110G/4Oz Arbroath Smokie, Skinned, Boned And Flaked Into Small Pieces</li><li>110G/4Oz Mashed Potato</li><li>1 Red Chilli, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Tsp Medium Curry Powder</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>1 Lime, Juice Only</li><li>75G/3Oz Plain Flour</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs, Beaten</li><li>90G/3 1/2Oz Panko Breadcrumbs</li><li>Vegetable Oil, For Deep Frying</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Make the chilli chutney in a medium saucepan, combine sugar, chillies, tomatoes, lime leaves, lemongrass, ginger and cloves. Bring to the boil, stirring occasionally, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 10 15 minutes until thickened and reduced. Cool completely. </li><li>Remove stems and seeds from lemongrass and discard. Cut lemongrass into 1 inch wide pieces and set aside for later use. </li><li>In a blender or food processor, combine shallots, fish sauce, sesame oil, dark soy sauce, honey and lime juice zest. Puree until very smooth. Add to chilli mixture and stir well. Mix in creme fraiche. </li><li>Preheat oven to 180 c and line a baking tray with baking paper. </li><li>In a large bowl, mash smokie pieces with your fingers until it is smooth. Add minced chilli and mash again until completely mixed and it is evenly combined. </li><li>Spread mashed potato evenly on the baking tray and spread around evenly. Sprinkle with curry powder and season to taste with salt, pepper and lime juice. </li><li>Bake for 45 minutes, or until crispy. Remove from the oven and allow to cool. </li><li>In a shallow dish, combine flour and egg. In another dish, mix pan</li></ul>
