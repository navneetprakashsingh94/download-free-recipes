<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Strong White Bread Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>60G/2 1/4Oz Plain Flour</li><li>1 Tsp Salt</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, Melted</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, At Room Temperature</li><li>25G/1Oz Strong White Bread Flour</li><li>25G/1Oz Plain Flour</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Granulated Sugar</li><li>2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>Icing Sugar, For Dusting</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 400 f. </li><li></li><li>Combine flour and salt in processor. Add butter. Using on/off turns, blend just until mixture resembles coarse meal. Scatter butter cubes evenly on top. Using on/off turns, process just until combined. Turn dough out onto lightly floured surface. Knead until smooth and elastic, about 5 minutes. Form into ball. Flatten into disk. Wrap in plastic and chill 15 minutes. </li><li></li><li>Press dough into 5 1/2 to 6 inch diameter tart pan with removable bottom. Roll dough onto lightly floured surface to 12 inch round. Cover with plastic and chill 30 minutes. Roll out dough into 12 inch round. Transfer to buttered 8 1/2 to 9 inch diameter tart pan with removable bottom. Trim dough to 1 inch overhang. Fold dough under itself and press to seal. Transfer pan to baking sheet. </li><li></li><li>Whisk flours, sugar and cinnamon in medium bowl. Add cold water toss to combine. Sprinkle dough with 1/2 cup sugar. Sprinkle dough with remaining sugar. Cover with plastic and chill overnight. </li><li></li><li>Position rack in bottom third of oven and preheat to 375 f. Brush tart crust with 3 tablespoons lukewarm water. Bake until golden brown, about 35 minutes. Cool completely on rack. </li></ul>
