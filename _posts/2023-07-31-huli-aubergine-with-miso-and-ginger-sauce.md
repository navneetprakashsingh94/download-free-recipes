<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Dried Chinese Wood Ear Mushrooms</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Tsp Ginger</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cumin</li><li>1 Aubergine, Peeled And Diced</li><li>1 Tbsp Hoisin Sauce</li><li>50M/2Fl Oz Vegetable Stock</li><li>1 Tsp Cornflour</li><li>1 Tsp Soy Sauce, To Taste</li><li>Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>7G Fresh Yeast</li><li>280G/10Oz Strong White Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>Pinch Caster Sugar</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil</li><li>1 Tsp Custard Powder</li><li>1 Tbsp Black Sesame Seeds</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Chinese Black Vinegar</li><li>1 Tbsp Grated Fresh Root Ginger</li><li>1 Tbsp Chilli Oil</li><li>2 Tsp Caster Sugar</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Soy Sauce</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Put the mushrooms in a bowl and cover with boiling water, leaving to soak for 1 hour. </li><li>Drain and chop into pieces. </li><li>Heat the oil in a large pan, add the onion and saute gently for 1 min. </li><li>Add the garlic, ginger, cumin and aubergine, saute for 3 mins. </li><li>Add the hoisin, stock, miso paste, soy sauce, pepper and soaked mushrooms, and cover and simmer gently for 15 mins. </li><li>Mix the custard powder, sugar and salt in a small bowl. </li><li>Add the oil and mix well. </li><li>Stir in the yeast and the flour. </li><li>Knead the mixture, adding extra flour as required. </li><li>Cover and rest for 10 mins. </li><li>Roll out the dough and cut into squares, then cut each square into squares. </li><li>Put a tbsp of filling into the center of each square, and roll up. </li><li>Place on a baking sheet. </li><li>Cover and rise for 10 mins. </li><li>Fry in oil in a frypan until crisp. </li><li>Drain. </li><li>Heat the sauce ingredients in a pan, bring to a boil, then simmer for 10 mins. </li><li>Serve with the aubergine. </li></ul>
