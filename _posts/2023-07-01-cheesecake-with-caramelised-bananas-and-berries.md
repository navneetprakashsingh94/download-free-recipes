<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>25Cm/10In Sponge (Cut From A Large Bought Flan Case)</li><li>200G/7Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>Finely Grated Zest Of 3 Lemons</li><li>4 Tbsp Cornflour</li><li>3 Tbsp Sultanas, Soaked In A Bit Of Bourbon</li><li>845G/29 3/4Oz Full Fat Soft Cream Cheese</li><li>3 Medium Eggs</li><li>7.5Ml/1 1/2 Tsp Vanilla Essence</li><li>1 Vanilla Pod</li><li>Jack Daniels To Taste Or Bourbon (Optional)</li><li>375Ml/12 1/2Fl Oz Double Or Single Cream</li><li>10 Small Bananas</li><li>25G/1Oz Butter</li><li>2 Tbsp Sugar</li><li>Caramel Sauce Or Maple Syrup</li><li>Sprig Of Mint Or Basil</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 180c/gas 4. Line the base of the spring form pan with baking paper. </li><li>Fill a pan with water and bring to the boil. Pour 1/2 cup of water into the pan and add sugar and lemon zest. Mix together, bring to the boil and remove from the heat. Add lemon juice, lemon juice and bourbon. Pour over prepared flan case. Bake for 8 minutes. </li><li>Meanwhile, for the cream cheese filling, beat cream cheese with an electric mixer until smooth. Add eggs and vanilla essence, mix until combined. Mix in chocolate and double cream. Pour into the prepared pan. </li><li>Place a tray in the middle of the oven and add boiling water into the tray so that the water level is at least 1cm higher than the springform tin. Bake for 50 60 minutes until the cheesecake is set. Remove from the oven and run a knife around the edge of the pan to loosen the cheesecake. Leave to cool in the pan. </li><li>To make the caramelized bananas, melt the butter in a pan, add the sugar and sautee for a couple of minutes until the sugar caramelizes. Remove from the heat and allow to cool slightly. </li><li>Melt the caramel sauce in a saucepan. Remove from the heat, add the bananas and the caramel sauce and mix together, taking care to break up the bananas. Spread the bananas over the cheesecake, pour the caramel over the bananas, sprinkle with mint leaves and serve. </li></ul>
