<a href="/tags#onion" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Onion</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#skinless boneless turkey breast thigh" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Skinless Boneless Turkey Breast Thigh</a> <a href="/tags#Mexican seasoning taco" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Mexican Seasoning Taco</a> <a href="/tags#tomatoes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tomatoes</a> <a href="/tags#tin sweetcorn" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tin Sweetcorn</a> <a href="/tags#cheese Cheddar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cheese Cheddar</a> <a href="/tags#tortilla wraps" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tortilla Wraps</a> <a href="/tags#yoghurt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Yoghurt</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Small Onion</li><li>Oil, For Cooking</li><li>600G/1Lb 5Oz Skinless, Boneless Turkey Breast Or Thigh</li><li>3 Tsp Mexican Seasoning, Such As Mild Taco Seasoning</li><li>2 Tomatoes, Halved</li><li>200G Tin Of Sweetcorn, Drained</li><li>200G/7Oz Grated Cheese, Such As Cheddar</li><li>4 Large Soft Tortilla Wraps</li><li>4 Tbsp Natural Yoghurt, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat the oil in a large frying pan over medium heat and saute the onion for 5 minutes. </li><li>Add the turkey and cook for 4 5 minutes or until the turkey is just cooked. </li><li>Stir in the taco seasoning mix and cook for 3 minutes. </li><li>Stir in the tomato and corn and simmer for 5 minutes or until the corn is tender. </li><li>Preheat a frying pan over medium high heat. </li><li>Sprinkle each tortilla wrap with the cheese, leaving a 1/2 inch border. </li><li>Sprinkle with a quarter of the meat mixture and fold in the sides, then roll up the wraps. </li><li>Place on the pan and press firmly to flatten the wraps. </li><li>Bake for 6 8 minutes or until the cheese is melted and the wraps are crisp and golden. </li><li>Serve immediately with yoghurt. </li><li>For more information about the taco seasoning, check out the blog link for a printable version. </li></ul>
