<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#slices brioche" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Slices Brioche</a> <a href="/tags#egg room temperature" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg Room Temperature</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp plain yoghurt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Plain Yoghurt</a> <a href="/tags#honey maple syrup" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Honey Maple Syrup</a> <a href="/tags#raspberries fruit" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Raspberries Fruit</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Slices Brioche</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, At Room Temperature</li><li>3 Tbsp Plain Yoghurt</li><li>3 Tsp Runny Honey Or Maple Syrup</li><li>75G/2 3/4Oz Fresh Or Frozen Raspberries Or Other Soft Fruit</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Toast the bread. </li><li>Beat the egg, yoghurt and honey maple syrup until frothy. </li><li>Heat a frying pan, grease it with butter and cook the egg mixture for 2 3 minutes on each side. </li><li>Transfer the egg to the toast, top with berries and serve immediately. </li></ul>
