<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Cornflour</li><li>200G/7 1/4Oz Plain Flour</li><li>1 Tsp Fine Sea Salt</li><li>330Ml/11 1/2Fl Oz Real Ale</li><li>2 Tbsp White Wine Vinegar</li><li>Sunflower Oil, For Deep Frying</li><li>4 Tbsp Plain Flour</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Fine Sea Salt</li><li>4 X 200G/7 1/4Oz Thick White Fish Fillets, Such As Haddock Or Whiting</li><li>Chips, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Put the corn flour in a large mixing bowl and add the salt, beer and vinegar. Whisk well and then add enough water until a thick sauce forms. Cover with plastic wrap and leave to rest overnight, up to 3 days. </li><li></li><li>Heat oil to 360 f. </li><li></li><li>Whisk together flour and salt. Coat fish in flour, shake off excess, then deep fry until golden. </li><li></li><li>Remove from oil, drain and continue to dredge in beer batter. Place on paper towels to drain. </li><li></li><li>Serve immediately with chips. </li></ul>
