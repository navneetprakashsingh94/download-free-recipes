<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>750G/1Lb 10Oz Braising Steak (Ideally Chuck Steak)</li><li>4 Tbsp Sunflower Oil</li><li>2 Onions, Halved And Sliced</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Tsp Ground Cumin</li><li>2 Tsp Ground Coriander</li><li>1 Tsp Hot Chilli Powder</li><li>400G/14Oz Can Chopped Tomatoes</li><li>400G/14Oz Can Chickpeas, Drained And Rinsed</li><li>3 Tbsp Clear Honey</li><li>1 Beef Stock Cube</li><li>1 Cinnamon Stick</li><li>1 Medium Sweet Potato (Around 400G/14Oz)</li><li>2 Large Eating Apples</li><li>25G/1Oz Bunch Fresh Coriander</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz No-Soak Dried Prunes, Halved</li><li>Flaked Sea Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>1 Large Red-Skinned Eating Apple</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>1 Tbsp Clear Honey</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>To make the stew, brown the steak in a little oil in a frying pan. Add the onions, cloves, spices and tinned tomatoes. Cook for 5 minutes. Add the cream, honey, stock cube and cinnamon. Add the sweet potatoes, apple and coriander. Cover and cook on low for 3 4 hours, or until the beef is tender. </li><li>Remove the cinnamon and cinnamon stick. Add the prunes to the stew and cook for 2 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper. </li><li>To make the salad, mix the apples with the butter, honey and a pinch of salt. Put in the fridge for 1 hour. </li><li>Meanwhile, cook the beef in a pan of boiling salted water for 5 7 minutes until cooked through. Drain and combine with the warm stew. Taste for seasoning and serve with the salad. </li></ul>
