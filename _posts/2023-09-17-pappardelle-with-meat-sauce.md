<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>2 Onions, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Large Carrot, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Sticks Celery, Finely Chopped</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Lean Minced Pork</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Beef Mince</li><li>3 Garlic Cloves, Crushed</li><li>3 Tbsp Sun-Dried Tomato Paste</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz White Wine</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Passata</li><li>400G Tin Chopped Tomatoes</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Beef Stock</li><li>3 Bay Leaves</li><li>2 Tbsp Chopped Thyme Leaves</li><li>4 Tbsp Double Cream</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>450G/1Lb Pappardelle Pasta, To Serve</li><li>Parmesan, To Serve</li><li>Basil Leaves, To Garnish</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat oil in a large pan and add onion, carrot and celery. Cook, stirring, for 5 minutes until softened. Add meats and season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring, for 5 minutes. </li><li>Add cloves, tomato paste and wine and cook, stirring, for 1 minute. Add passata, tomatoes, beef stock and bay leaves. Bring to the boil. Reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 1 hour. </li><li></li><li>Remove bay leaves and thyme leaves. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Remove from heat and stir in cream. </li><li></li><li>Cook pappardelle in a pan of boiling salted water until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water drain pasta and toss with meat sauce. Serve with parmesan and basil. </li></ul>
