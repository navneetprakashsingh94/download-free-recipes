<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#self flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Self Flour</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#blackberries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Blackberries</a> <a href="/tags#oz buttermilk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Buttermilk</a> <a href="/tags#plain yoghurt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Yoghurt</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>25G/1Oz Butter, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Self Raising Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>25G/1Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>16 Fresh Blackberries</li><li>80-100Ml/3-3 1/2Fl Oz Buttermilk</li><li>Plain Yoghurt, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to gas 4, 220c fan, 180c / 350f</li><li>Melt the butter over a low heat in a large saucepan</li><li>Combine the self raising flour and sugar in a large bowl</li><li>Add the buttermilk and yoghurt</li><li>Stir to combine and then add the melted butter and blackberries</li><li>Spoon the mixture into a 2l round 25cm in cake tin</li><li>Crumble over the additional sugar and a pinch of salt</li><li>Bake for 25 minutes</li><li>Serve warm with custard or cream</li></ul>
