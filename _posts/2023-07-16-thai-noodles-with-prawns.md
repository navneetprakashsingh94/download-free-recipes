<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>5 Spring Onions, Roughly Chopped</li><li>4 Garlic Cloves, Crushed</li><li>2 Thai Shallots, Roughly Chopped</li><li>5 Tbsp Dark Soy Sauce</li><li>5 Tbsp Fish Sauce</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Pork Belly, Cut Into 2Cm/ 3/4In Strips</li><li>5 Tbsp Fish Sauce</li><li>5 Tbsp Rice Vinegar</li><li>1 Tbsp Soft Brown Sugar</li><li>1 Red Chilli, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Lime, Zest And Juice</li><li>1 X 200G Packet Thin Rice Noodles, Cooked According To Packet Instructions, Drained And Rinsed In Cold Water</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Beansprouts</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Chinese Lettuce, Shredded</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Chinese Broccoli Leaves</li><li>Handful Fresh Thai Mint Leaves</li><li>Handful Fresh Thai Basil Leaves</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Marinate the prawns in the sauce ingredients for 45 mins, turning in between. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, whisk together the sauce ingredients and set aside. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 180 c. Line a baking pan with foil. Place the prawns on the pan and roast for about 15 20 mins or until cooked through. Drain off all the excess oil. </li><li>In a large saucepan of boiling, salted water, cook the noodles until tender, about 8 mins. Drain well. </li><li>Mix the noodles, beansprouts, lettuce, broccoli, mint and basil in a large bowl. Add the sauce, prawns and dressing to the bowl and toss well to combine. Serve immediately. </li></ul>
