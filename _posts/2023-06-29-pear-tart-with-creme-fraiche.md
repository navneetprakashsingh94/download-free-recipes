<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>110G/3 1/2Oz Butter, Softened, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>280G/9 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>3 Free-Range Eggs, Beaten</li><li>2-3 Drops Almond Extract</li><li>110G/3 1/2Oz Ground Almonds</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>2 Oranges, Rind Only, Cut Into Thin Strips, To Decorate</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Flaked Almonds, To Decorate</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Mixed Nuts, To Decorate</li><li>Creme Fraiche, To Serve</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Red Wine</li><li>130G/4 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>Pared Rind Of 1 Unwaxed Lemon</li><li>1 Cinnamon Stick</li><li>4 Dessert Pears</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>First prepare pears. </li><li>Peel the pear slices and cut them into thin slices. </li><li>Chop the almonds and pine nuts, set aside. </li><li>For the pastry, cream together the butter and sugar, and beat until smooth. </li><li>Beat in the eggs and the extract, beat until the mixture is light and creamy. </li><li>Fold in the almonds and flour, combining well. </li><li>Preheat oven to 180c, grease and line a 22cm round tin with baking paper and place on a baking tray. </li><li>Place half the pastry round over the base and brush with the orange zest. </li><li>Sprinkle half the almonds and pine nuts over the base and top with the pear slices. </li><li>Pour the egg mixture over top, cover with the remaining pastry and press down gently to seal. </li><li>Bake for about 40 minutes, or until a skewer inserted into the middle of the tart comes out clean. </li><li>Leave to cool in the tin for about 5 minutes before turning out onto a plate and brushing off the paper. </li><li>To make the sauce, bring to the boil the wine, sugar, lemon rind and cinnamon, then add the pears. </li><li>Simmer the pears gently, covered for 5 minutes until they are soft. </li><li>Remove the pears to a serving bowl. </li><li>Spoon the sauce over the top and scatter with pine nuts. </li></ul>
