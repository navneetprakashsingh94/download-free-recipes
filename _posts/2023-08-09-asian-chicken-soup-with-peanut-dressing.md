<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the soup, place the chicken stock, ginger, cassia, star anise and soy sauce in a medium sauce pan. Bring the liquid to a boil, then turn down the heat and simmer for 10 minutes. Remove the pan from the heat and let cool. Strain the liquid through a fine sieve and chill thoroughly. </li><li></li><li>For the dressing, mix the peanut butter, oil, soy sauce, honey and vinegar in a small mixing bowl. Stir in the cayenne, ginger, chili, peanuts and sesame seeds. </li><li></li><li>To serve, add the vegetables, chicken, lime juice and thai basil leaves to the chilled stock. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Spoon the dressing onto plates, top with the salad and serve. </li></ul>
