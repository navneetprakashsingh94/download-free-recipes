<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#pineapple" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pineapple</a> <a href="/tags#pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#onion" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Onion</a> <a href="/tags#chilli pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chilli Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#mint" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Mint</a> <a href="/tags#limes juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Limes Juice</a> <a href="/tags#pinch salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pinch Salt</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Ripe Pineapple, Peeled And Trimmed</li><li>1 Red Pepper</li><li> 1/2 Red Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Jalapeno Chilli Pepper, Finely Chopped</li><li>Large Handful Mint, Chopped</li><li>2 Limes, Juice And Zest Only</li><li>Pinch Salt</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>In a bowl combine pineapple, jalapeno pepper, onion, chili pepper and mint. </li><li>Add the lime juice and salt. Mix well. </li><li>Pour the salsa into a serving dish. </li></ul>
