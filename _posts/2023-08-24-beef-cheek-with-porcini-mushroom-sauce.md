<div>Delicious Beef Cheek With Porcini Mushroom Sauce using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 50 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#pints water" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pints Water</a> <a href="/tags#porcini mushrooms" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Porcini Mushrooms</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#beef cheek" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Beef Cheek</a> <a href="/tags#onion" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Onion</a> <a href="/tags#butternut squash chunks" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butternut Squash Chunks</a> <a href="/tags#orzo pasta" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Orzo Pasta</a> <a href="/tags#round sourdough loaf" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Round Sourdough Loaf</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp parsley leaves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Parsley Leaves</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Litres/4 1/4 Pints Boiling Water</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Dried Porcini Mushrooms</li><li>4 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>800G/1Lb 12Oz Beef Cheek</li><li>1 Large Onion, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Butternut Squash, Peeled, De-Seeded, Cut Into 5Cm/2In Chunks</li><li>250G/9Oz Orzo Pasta</li><li>1 Large Round Sourdough Loaf (The Largest You Can Find)</li><li>3 Tbsp Chopped Fresh Parsley Leaves, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Bring water to a boil. Add mushrooms cook, uncovered, for 10 mins or until tender. Drain. Reserve mushrooms and water. Remove mushrooms from liquid coarsely chop. </li><li>Heat oil in a large skillet on high heat. Brown beef cheeks in batches, removing as they brown. Transfer to a bowl and keep warm. Add onion and cook, stirring, until soft. Return beef cheeks to pan and add 1 tbsp mushroom water cook, covered, over medium low heat for 1 hour. </li><li>Add squash to pan cook, covered, 10 mins or until tender. </li><li>Meanwhile, cook orzo in a saucepan of boiling salted water for 8 mins or until tender drain. </li><li>Spoon beef cheeks and accumulated juices over orzo. Serve with bread and remaining sauce sprinkled with parsley. </li></ul>
