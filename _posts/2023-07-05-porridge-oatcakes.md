<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#porridge oats" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Porridge Oats</a> <a href="/tags#coconut" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Coconut</a> <a href="/tags#brown sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Brown Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#pinch salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pinch Salt</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#syrup" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Syrup</a> <a href="/tags#bicarbonate soda" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bicarbonate Soda</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp boiling water" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Boiling Water</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>175G/6Oz Porridge Oats</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Desiccated Coconut</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Soft Light Brown Sugar</li><li>Pinch Of Salt</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, Diced</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Golden Syrup</li><li>1 Tsp Bicarbonate Of Soda</li><li>2 Tbsp Boiling Water</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 180 c. Grease two baking trays. </li><li></li><li>Put the flour, oats, coconut, sugar and salt in a bowl and mix well. </li><li></li><li>Put the butter and corn syrup in a saucepan and cook over medium heat until butter is melted and mixture is a little thickened. Set aside. </li><li></li><li>Dissolve the soda in the boiling water and stir into the syrup. </li><li></li><li>Pour over the dry ingredients and mix well. </li><li></li><li>Drop teaspoons of mixture onto each tin, leaving about 3cm between each. </li><li></li><li>Bake for 15 minutes, or until golden and cooked through. </li><li></li><li>Cool completely on a wire rack and store in an airtight container. </li></ul>
