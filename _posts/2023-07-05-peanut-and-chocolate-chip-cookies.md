<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>250G/9Oz Unsalted Butter, Softened</li><li>150G/5Oz Golden Granulated Sugar</li><li>2-3 Drops Vanilla Extract</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten</li><li>220G/ 1/2Lb Plain Flour</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>1-2 Handfuls Chocolate Chips (Optional)</li><li>1-2 Handfuls Nuts (Such As Peanuts, Macadamia Nuts, Or Brazil Nuts) (Optional)</li><li>1-2 Handfuls Crystallised Ginger</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 150 c. </li><li>Cream butter and sugar, then add vanilla extract and egg. </li><li>Mix flour, baking powder and salt, add it a little at a time into the butter mixture, incorporating them to incorporate. </li><li>Add all the remaining nuts and chocolate and mix all ingredients to combine. </li><li>Shape tablespoonfuls of batter into 1 inch balls and flatten on baking tray. </li><li>Bake for 15 minutes. </li></ul>
