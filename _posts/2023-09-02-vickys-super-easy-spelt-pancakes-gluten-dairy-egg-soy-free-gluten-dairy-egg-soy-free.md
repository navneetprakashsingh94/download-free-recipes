<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>1 Tbsp Caster Sugar</li><li>150Ml/ 1/4 Pint Whole Milk</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs, Separated</li><li>40G/1 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, Melted</li><li>1 Tsp Vanilla Extract Or Vanilla Bean Paste</li><li>Salt</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Sift the flour and baking powder together into a large bowl</li><li>Add in the sugar</li><li>Now add the yogurt, egg, melted butter and vanilla paste and mix together</li><li>Add in the salt and mix until just combined</li><li>Heat a non stick skillet over medium heat. </li><li>Scoop a ladleful of batter for each pancake and cook on each side until the surface is golden brown</li><li>I recommend cooking each side for 4 5 minutes</li><li>If you want a really firm and fluffy pancake it makes the perfect pancake if you have a smaller one, you can leave it as is</li><li>Serve the pancakes and top with lots of jam, icing sugar, fresh blueberries or any fruit youre feeling</li><li>You can also add more or less flour</li></ul>
