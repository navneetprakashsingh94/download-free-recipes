<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Asparagus And Gnocchi Stir Fry using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#packs gnocchi" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Packs Gnocchi</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#oz mayonnaise" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Mayonnaise</a> <a href="/tags#tsp pesto" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Pesto</a> <a href="/tags#salt ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Ground Pepper</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 X 400G Packs Fresh Gnocchi</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>160G/5 2/3 Oz Mayonnaise</li><li>3 Tsp Pesto</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Steam the asparagus for 5 10 minutes until tender, drain well</li><li>I used frozen asparagus so i did not thaw before steaming so i baked them as it was in the oven</li><li>Cut the asparagus into bite size pieces, remove and chop the gnocchi</li><li>Heat the oil in a pan and pan fry the gnocchi for 2 3 minutes until lightly golden. </li><li>Remove the gnocchi and asparagus from the pan and keep aside. </li><li>Heat the oil again and add the mayonnaise and pesto and mix well. </li><li>Add the gnocchi and asparagus back to the pan and mix well. </li><li>Season with pepper and serve. </li><li>This goes well with a salad. </li></ul>
