<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Pour the oil into a large frying pan over a high heat. Season the cod fillets in with salt and pepper and cook for 2 3 mins on each side, turning occasionally. Drain on kitchen paper. </li><li>In a bowl, mix together the soy sauces, mirin, miso paste and sugar and season to taste. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 300 f. Clean, trim and cut all the bok choi, pak choi and broccoli. Cut all the pak choi and broccoli a total of about 3/4 inch pieces. </li><li>In a saucepan over medium heat, simmer the dark soy sauce, cloves, ginger and oil for 15 mins, stirring occasionally. Remove from the heat. Stir in the vegetables. </li><li>Place a large frying pan over medium high heat and add the sliced bok choi and broccoli. Season lightly with salt, then toss well with the sauce. Reduce the heat to medium and toss constantly, turning the vegetables occasionally. When the broccoli is tender, toss the vegetables with the sauce. Arrange the vegetables on 2 serving plates and top with the cod fillets. Garnish with sesame seeds. Serve with steamed rice and lime wedges. </li><li>For the kimchi chili paste</li><li>Add the kimchi and chili paste to the pan and heat gently for a couple of minutes over a medium heat, until the kimchi is softened and the paste is reduced. Remove from heat and allow to cool for 10 15 minutes. Add the enoki</li></ul>
