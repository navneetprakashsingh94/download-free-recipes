<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#aubergine" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Aubergine</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#onion" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Onion</a> <a href="/tags#cloves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cloves</a> <a href="/tags#bunch basil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bunch Basil</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#loaf ciabatta" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Loaf Ciabatta</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Aubergine, Peeled And Chopped</li><li>6 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li> 1/2 Red Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Crushed</li><li>1 Large Bunch Fresh Basil</li><li>Splash Olive Oil</li><li>1 Loaf Ciabatta</li><li>Extra Olive Oil, To Brush</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Clean aubergine and slice as thin as possible. </li><li>Heat the 1 teaspoon oil in a frypan and fry onion, cut cloves in half, pour over chopped aubergine, cover and steam for 20 minutes. </li><li>Combine basil and oil and pour over the aubergine, serve with ciabatta sliced, and a sprinkling of oil. </li></ul>
