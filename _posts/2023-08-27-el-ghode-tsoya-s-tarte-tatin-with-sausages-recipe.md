<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Leftover Cooked Vegetables, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Tbsp Chopped Flatleaf Parsley</li><li>Sea Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Leftover Beef Gravy, Plus Extra To Serve</li><li>2 X 375G/13Oz Ready-Made All-Butter Puff Pastry</li><li>Plain Flour, For Dusting</li><li>2 Tsp Wholegrain Mustard</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Leftover Cooked Beef, Cut Into Thin Slices</li><li>2 Free-Range Egg Yolks, Beaten</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Pre heat the oven to 180c/400f/gas 6. </li><li>Heat a large frying pan over a medium heat and add a tablespoon of olive oil. Add the sausages, season with salt, pepper and ground black pepper. Cook for 5 minutes or until all sides are browned. </li><li>Add 2 tbsp of parsley to the frying pan, and cook for 3 4 minutes or until almost all of the liquid is absorbed. Set aside. </li><li>Heat the gravy in the microwave for 2 minutes, stir in 1 tsp each of the mustard and the remaining parsley and season. Set aside. </li><li>Roll out the puff pastry to a rectangle about 1/16 inch thick. Brush the puff pastry with 1 tbsp olive oil. Top with the sausages and vegetables. Fold the pastry around the meat. Brush the top with the egg yolks. </li><li>Put the pastry on a baking tray and pour the gravy over the top. Bake for 25 30 minutes or until the pastry has risen and browned. Remove from the oven and allow to cool for 10 15 minutes before serving. </li></ul>
