<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>3 Free-Range Eggs</li><li>1 Tbsp Milk</li><li>1 Tsp Butter</li><li>1 Tomato, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Tbsp Grated Cheddar</li><li>1 Tbsp Finely Chopped Fresh Parsley Or 1 Tsp Dried Parsley</li><li>2 Thin Slices Good-Quality Ham</li><li>Salt And Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat a non stick skillet on medium heat add butter</li><li>When butter is melted add parsley and parsley, stir well</li><li>Crack eggs into skillet</li><li>Add ham, tomato and parsley</li><li>Cook for about 2 minutes, stirring frequently</li><li>Sprinkle on cheese</li><li>Cook an additional 2 minutes or until eggs are cooked through, stir occasionally</li><li>Season with salt pepper</li></ul>
