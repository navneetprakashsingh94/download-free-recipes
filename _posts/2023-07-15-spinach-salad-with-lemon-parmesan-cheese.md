<div>Delicious Spinach Salad With Lemon Parmesan Cheese using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 10 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#Parmesan cheese chunks" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Parmesan Cheese Chunks</a> <a href="/tags#clove" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Clove</a> <a href="/tags#artichoke hearts weight" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Artichoke Hearts Weight</a> <a href="/tags#pulp pips" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pulp Pips</a> <a href="/tags#handful parsley leaves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Handful Parsley Leaves</a> <a href="/tags#oz virgin oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Virgin Oil</a> <a href="/tags#squeeze lemon juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Squeeze Lemon Juice</a> <a href="/tags#salt ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Ground Pepper</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>30G/1Oz Parmesan (Or A Similar Vegetarian Hard Cheese), Broken Into Chunks</li><li>1 Large Garlic Clove, Roughly Chopped</li><li>175G/6Oz Grilled Artichoke Hearts, Drained Weight, Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 Small Preserved Lemon, Pulp And Pips Removed, Chopped</li><li>Small Handful Of Parsley Leaves, Roughly Chopped, Plus Extra For Garnish</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>Squeeze Lemon Juice</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Make the oil with lemon juice and vinegar and add in chopped parsley and parmesan cheese. </li><li>Add in 1 tbsp oil and salt and pepper. </li><li>Remove and discard the tough stem and the leafy leaves from the kale. </li><li>Cut the stem into 1/4 inch slices, then slice each slice lengthwise. </li><li>In a bowl combine all other ingredients. </li><li>Pour over the mixture and mix well. </li><li>Season to taste and serve. </li></ul>
