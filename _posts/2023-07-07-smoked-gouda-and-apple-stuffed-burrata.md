<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#bulgur wheat" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bulgur Wheat</a> <a href="/tags#water" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Water</a> <a href="/tags#gouda cubes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Gouda Cubes</a> <a href="/tags#apples" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Apples</a> <a href="/tags#bunch flatleaf parsley" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bunch Flatleaf Parsley</a> <a href="/tags#dressing" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Dressing</a> <a href="/tags#salt ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Ground Pepper</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Bulgur Wheat</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Just-Boiled Water</li><li>120G/4 1/4Oz Gouda, Cut Into Cubes</li><li>3 Green Apples, Cored And Diced</li><li>Large Bunch Fresh Flatleaf Parsley, About 60-80G/2 1/4-2 3/4Oz, Roughly Chopped</li><li>2 Tbsp French Dressing</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Bring bulgur and water to a boil over medium heat in a large pan. Reduce heat, cover and simmer 15 minutes until most of the water is absorbed. </li><li>Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Mix everything together and stuff the bulgur in the pan. </li><li>Bake 20 25 minutes until the bulgur is browned and the juice is bubbling. </li></ul>
