<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#aubergine" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Aubergine</a> <a href="/tags#pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#leaf parsley" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Leaf Parsley</a> <a href="/tags#cloves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cloves</a> <a href="/tags#virgin oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Virgin Oil</a> <a href="/tags#sea salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sea Salt</a> <a href="/tags#bread baton pieces" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bread Baton Pieces</a> <a href="/tags#lemon juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lemon Juice</a> <a href="/tags#ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ground Pepper</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Small-Medium Aubergine (Around 250G/9Oz)</li><li>1 Large Red Pepper</li><li>25G/1Oz Bunch Fresh Flat Leaf Parsley, Leaves Roughly Chopped, Plus Extra To Garnish</li><li>3 Garlic Cloves, Roughly Chopped</li><li>8 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Plus Extra For Drizzling</li><li>Flaked Sea Salt</li><li>1 Fresh French Bread Baton, Diagonally Sliced Into 6 Pieces</li><li>2 Tbsp Fresh Lemon Juice</li><li>Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 400 f. </li><li>Slice aubergine and pepper into small bite sized pieces and mix together with chopped parsley, salt, pepper, lemon juice, a squeeze of lemon juice and olive oil to taste. </li><li>Toast the bread batons and add to the aubergine mixture. </li><li>Serve hot or cold with green salad and grated acorn squash. </li></ul>
