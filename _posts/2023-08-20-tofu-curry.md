<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Vegetable Oil</li><li> 1/2 Red Onion</li><li>1 Tomato, Cut Into Wedges</li><li> 1/2 Broccoli Stem, Sliced</li><li>1 Garlic Clove</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Turmeric</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Fresh Ginger, Grated</li><li>150G/5 1/2 Oz Beef, Sliced</li><li>2 Tbsp Soy Sauce</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. Heat oil in pan, saute onion and cook for 3 minutes or until soft. </li><li>2. Add tomato, spices and stock. </li><li>3. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and cook 20 minutes or until sauce has thickened. Add water if too thick. Add tamari, simmer 3 minutes. </li><li>4. Mix rice into sauce and heat through. </li></ul>
