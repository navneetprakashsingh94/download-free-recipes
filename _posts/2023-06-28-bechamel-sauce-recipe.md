<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#pint milk type" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pint Milk Type</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#salt pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Pepper</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>300Ml/ 1/2 Pint Milk (Any Type)</li><li>25G/1Oz Butter</li><li>20G/ 3/4Oz Plain Flour</li><li>Salt And Pepper, To Taste</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cook butter, flour, nutmeg, salt pepper over low heat. </li><li>Then slowly stir in lowfat milk. </li><li>Season to taste. </li><li>Cook until thickened. </li><li>Serve over meat of your choice. </li><li>Enjoy</li><li>To make a thicker sauce, double the amount of flour and use two tablespoons butter. </li></ul>
