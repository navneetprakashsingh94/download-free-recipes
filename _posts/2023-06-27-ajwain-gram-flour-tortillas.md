<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Ajwain Gram Flour Tortillas using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Flour</a> <a href="/tags#tsp salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Salt</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#tsp ajwain seeds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Ajwain Seeds</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>250G/9Oz Wholemeal Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Salt</li><li>7 Tsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 1/2 Tsp Ajwain Seeds</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Put flour in a bowl and make a well in the center. Add salt and oil and mix well. </li><li>Heat a nonstick frying pan and add ajwain seeds. Once the seeds start to pop, pour in the flour mixture and stir well. </li><li>Cook over a medium heat and keep stirring until the mixture is slightly firm. </li><li>Allow to cool for several minutes, then add water to make a paste. </li><li>In the meantime, heat a small pan, add 1 tbsp ajwain seeds and cook on low heat until the seeds crackle. </li><li>Remove from heat. </li><li>Roll out the tortilla with a rolling pin in a circle. It will be thin because the tortilla will spread out. </li><li>Put a scant tablespoon of the ajwain paste and fold it over in half to make a triangle. </li><li>Gently place on a plate. Repeat with the other half. </li><li>Let the tortillas cool for about 10 minutes. </li><li>Heat a griddle, then add a bit of oil and gently toast it. </li><li>Place each tortilla on the griddle. </li><li>Cook it for 3 to 4 minutes until golden brown. Flip and cook for another 3 to 4 minutes until golden brown and crispy. </li><li>Serve with salsa. </li></ul>
