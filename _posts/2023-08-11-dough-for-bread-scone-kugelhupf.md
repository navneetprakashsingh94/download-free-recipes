<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#tsp salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Salt</a> <a href="/tags#tsp action yeast" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Action Yeast</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#tsp baking powder" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Baking Powder</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp vegetable oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Vegetable Oil</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>265G/9 1/2Oz Plain Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Salt</li><li>1 1/2 Tsp Fast Action Dried Yeast</li><li>2 Tbsp Caster Sugar</li><li>2 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>2 Tbsp Vegetable Or Sunflower Oil, Plus Extra For Brushing</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Stir the flour with the salt, yeast, sugar and baking powder, then add oil and mix thouroughly. </li><li></li><li>Knead the dough and place into a warm spot and let rise till the dough has doubled in volume about an hour. </li><li></li><li>Divide the dough in to 4 equal parts. Roll each piece of dough out into a long rope about 11 inches long. Divide each rope into 4 pieces and shape each piece into a scone. </li><li></li><li>Place the dough pieces onto a greased baking sheet and cook on the middle shelf of the preheated oven, 180c, for 30 40 minutes or until the scone is golden brown and crusty on top. Turn the loaf over and cook for 15 20 minutes longer or until the loaf sounds hollow when tapped. </li></ul>
