<a href="/tags#plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#buckwheat flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Buckwheat Flour</a> <a href="/tags#level tsp ground cinnamon" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Level Tsp Ground Cinnamon</a> <a href="/tags#eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#creme fraiche" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Creme Fraiche</a> <a href="/tags#Granny Smith apple" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Granny Smith Apple</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp calvados" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Calvados</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp calvados" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Calvados</a> <a href="/tags#cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cream</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>50G/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li>25G/1Oz Buckwheat Flour</li><li>1 Level Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>2 Eggs</li><li>1X200Ml Tub Creme Fraiche</li><li>1 Large Granny Smith Apple</li><li>2 Tbsp Calvados</li><li>50G/2Oz Melted Butter</li><li>Caster Sugar</li><li>2 Tbsp Calvados</li><li>Double Cream, Well Chilled</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 220c, fan oven gas mark 5. </li><li>Grease a cake pan with butter. </li><li>Line base and sides with baking paper, and grease paper. </li><li>Mix flours and cinnamon in a bowl. </li><li>Whisk egg yolks with creme fraiche. </li><li>Mix into flour mixture. </li><li>Stir apple and calvados into batter. </li><li>Beat egg whites in a clean bowl, until stiff but not dry. </li><li>Fold egg whites gently into batter, but do not overmix. </li><li>Pour into prepared pan and bake for about 40 mins, or until golden. </li><li>Set aside for 10 mins, and transfer to a wire rack to cool. </li><li>To make caramel sauce, heat butter in a small saucepan on medium. </li><li>Stir in sugar and cook for 4 minutes or until golden. </li><li>Add calvados and simmer for 3 minutes. </li><li>Serve cake with caramel sauce, cream and calvados caramel. </li></ul>
