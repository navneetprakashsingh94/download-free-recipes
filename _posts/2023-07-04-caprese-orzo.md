<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Orzo Pasta</li><li>120G/4 1/4Oz Sun-Dried Tomatoes</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Artichokes</li><li>80G/2 3/4Oz Jarred Roasted Peppers</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Olives, Pitted, Or 80G/2 3/4Oz Pitted Olives</li><li>2 Tbsp Capers, Drained</li><li>400G Tin Chickpeas, Drained And Rinsed</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Parmesan (Or Similar Vegetarian Hard Cheese), Coarsely Grated</li><li>Extra Virgin Olive Oil (Optional)</li><li>Salt</li><li>1 Bunch Parsley, Finely Chopped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 350 degrees f. </li><li>Cook pasta as directed on package in large pot of boiling salted water until al dente drain. </li><li>Meanwhile, combine all ingredients in large bowl. </li><li>Season with salt and pepper. </li><li>Cover and chill until ready to serve. </li><li>If desired, garnish with extra parsley. </li></ul>
