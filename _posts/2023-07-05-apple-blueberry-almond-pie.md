<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#Bramley apples" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bramley Apples</a> <a href="/tags#blueberries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Blueberries</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#ground almonds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ground Almonds</a> <a href="/tags#eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#almonds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Almonds</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>750G/1Lb 10Oz Bramley Apples</li><li>250G/9Oz Blueberries</li><li>200G/7Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>200G/7Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>200G/7Oz Ground Almonds</li><li>3 Eggs</li><li>55G/2Oz Almonds, Sliced Finely</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Melt the butter with the sugar and stir until the sugar is dissolved. </li><li>Mix in the ground almonds, the eggs, and blueberries. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 180c. </li><li>Using a spoon, spread the batter over the apples and place in a 20cm x 30cm pie tin. </li><li>Press the sliced almonds on top of the pie tin. </li><li>Bake in the oven for about 45 minutes, until golden and a skewer inserted in the centre comes out clean. </li><li>Serve with a yoghurt, vanilla ice cream, a glass of your favourite strong tea or tea. </li></ul>
