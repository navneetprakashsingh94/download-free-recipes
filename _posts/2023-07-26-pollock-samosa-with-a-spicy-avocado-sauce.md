<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>150G/5 1/2Oz White Fish Fillet (Such As Pollock), Cut Into Strips</li><li>Vegetable Oil, For Deep-Frying</li><li>Fresh Coriander, To Serve</li><li>75G/2 2/3 Oz Plain Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>1 Tbsp Vegetable Oil</li><li>30Ml/2 Tbsp Very Hot Water</li><li>Salt</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Plain Flour</li><li>25G/1Oz Cornflour</li><li> 1/4 Tsp Ground Cumin</li><li>80Ml/2 3/4Fl Oz Lager</li><li>1 Plum Tomato, Finely Diced</li><li> 1/4 Red Onion, Finely Diced</li><li> 1/2 Red Chilli, Finely Chopped</li><li>Small Handful Fresh Coriander, Chopped</li><li> 1/2 Lime, Juice Only</li><li>1 Very Ripe Avocado</li><li>2 Tbsp Mayonnaise</li><li>2 Tbsp Soured Cream</li><li>1 Lime, Juice Only</li><li>Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the marinade in a large bowl mix the marinade ingredients then roll the pollock in the batter. </li><li>For the dip add the mayonnaise, cream, lime juice and pepper to the avocado and stir until blended. Cover the bowl and chill for at least 30 mins. </li><li>Preheat the oven to 170 c. </li><li>To make the samosas combine the flour, cornstarch and cumin and then gradually add lager, to form a thick batter. </li><li>Heat the oil in a large frying pan and fry the pollock for 3 4 mins, turning. When golden, drain on kitchen paper. </li><li>To serve arrange the pollock on plates, spoon the dressing over and sprinkle with coriander. </li></ul>
