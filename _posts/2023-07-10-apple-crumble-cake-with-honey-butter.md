<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Sultanas</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Chopped Dried Apple</li><li>90Ml/6 Tbsp Calvados</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Blanched Hazelnuts</li><li>175G/6Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>175G/6Oz Margarine</li><li>175G/6Oz Muscavado Sugar</li><li>3 Medium Free-Range Eggs</li><li> 1/2 Tbsp Vanilla Bean Paste (Or 1 Tsp Vanilla Extract)</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>1 Tsp Cinnamon</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>30G/1Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>5 Granny Smith Apples, Peeled, Cored And Sliced Into 1Cm/ 1/2In Wedges</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Blanched Hazelnuts</li><li>200G/7Oz Caster Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Pre heat the oven to 150 degrees c 350 degrees f. </li><li>Mix the apples, sultanas and dates together and transfer it into a rectangular baking tin. </li><li>Make a little well in the centre and add the apple mixture. </li><li>Make another well in the centre, add the eggs, margarine and vanilla extract, and mix until smooth. </li><li>Spoon the mixture over the fruit. </li><li>Mix the flour and baking powder, add to the cake batter, and mix well. </li><li>Spoon the cake into the lined tin and sprinkle with the cinnamon and caster sugar. </li><li>Bake for 1 hour. </li><li>While the cake is baking, make the maple butter by removing the apple skins. </li><li>Melt the butter over a low heat, and fry the apple skin side down until it starts to darken. </li><li>Sprinkle a thin layer of the sugar over the rim of the tin and bake for 8 10 minutes. </li><li>Remove the cake from the oven, spread a layer of honey butter over it and cover with sliced apples and hazelnuts, and return to the oven for another 25 minutes. </li></ul>
