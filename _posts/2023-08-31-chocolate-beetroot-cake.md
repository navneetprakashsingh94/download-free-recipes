<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>250G/9Oz Self-Raising Flour</li><li>4 Tbsp Cocoa Powder</li><li>2 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>250G/9Oz Cooked Beetroot In Natural Juices</li><li>3 Tbsp White Wine Vinegar</li><li>160Ml/5 1/2Fl Oz Buttermilk</li><li>160G/5 1/2Oz Butter, Softened</li><li>230G/8Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>3 Large Free-Range Eggs</li><li>280G/10Oz Cream Cheese</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Unsalted Butter, Softened</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Icing Sugar, Sifted</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Vanilla Extract</li><li>2 X Raw Beetroots</li><li>200G/7Oz Caster Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat the oven to 350f. </li><li>Grease a 9 inch tube tin or 2 mini loaf pans. </li><li>Sift the flour, cocoa and baking powder into a large bowl. </li><li>Add the beetroot juice, vinegar and buttermilk to the dry ingredients. </li><li>Beat the butter with an electric whisk or wooden spoon until smooth and creamy. </li><li>Gradually add the sugar, beating until light and fluffy. </li><li>Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. </li><li>Add a third of the sifted flour mixture to the mixture in 2 additions, beating until just combined. </li><li>Fold in the remaining flour mixture and the buttermilk mixture until smooth. </li><li>Divide the mixture between the prepared pans and bake for 30 35 minutes or until a skewer inserted into the centre of the cakes comes out clean. </li><li>Cool in the pans for 10 minutes before turning onto a wire rack to cool completely. </li><li>Meanwhile, beat the cream cheese and butter until smooth. </li><li>Gradually add the sugar and vanilla extract and beat until thick. </li><li>Spread the frosting over the cake. </li><li>Combine the chopped beetroot and sugar in a saucepan and cook over a low heat until the beetroot is tender. </li><li>Allow to cool slightly. </li><li>Spoon a dollop of cooled beetroot around the top of each cake and sprinkle over some chocolate chips or golden brown sugar. </li></ul>
