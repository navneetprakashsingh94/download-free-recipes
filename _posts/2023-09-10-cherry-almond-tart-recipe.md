<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#cherries stones" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cherries Stones</a> <a href="/tags#almond liqueur" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Almond Liqueur</a> <a href="/tags#oats" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oats</a> <a href="/tags#almonds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Almonds</a> <a href="/tags#good-quality dark chocolate % cocoa solids" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Good-Quality Dark Chocolate % Cocoa Solids</a> <a href="/tags#honey" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Honey</a> <a href="/tags#oz cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Cream</a> <a href="/tags#icing sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Icing Sugar</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>250G/9Oz Fresh Cherries, Stones Removed, Halved</li><li>4 Tbsp Almond Liqueur</li><li>125G/4 1/2Oz Rolled Oats</li><li>30G/1Oz Flaked Almonds</li><li>200G/7Oz Good-Quality Dark Chocolate (Minimum 70% Cocoa Solids), Preferably Fairtrade</li><li>1 Tbsp Clear Honey</li><li>400Ml/14Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Icing Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat the oven to 160 c, 375 f, gas mark 4. </li><li>To make the cherries, place the cherries and almond liqueur in a bowl and leave to macerate for one hour, or possibly till they re plump and syrupy. </li><li>Place the oats in a food processor and process till finely minced, remove and set aside. </li><li>Put the almonds into a shallow baking dish and sprinkle with the cocoa, cover with cling film and put in the oven for 8 min or possibly till fragrant. </li><li>Immediately remove the cling film and stir in the cherry mix to combine. </li><li>Transfer the mix to a bowl and pour over the cream. </li><li>Blend into a smooth paste with your hands, then scrape into a shallow dish. </li><li>Chill for a further hour and then cut into 5cm squares and arrange on serving plates. </li><li>Sprinkle with the icing sugar and serve. </li></ul>
