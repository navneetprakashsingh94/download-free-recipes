<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#fruit" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Fruit</a> <a href="/tags#sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#pint tea" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pint Tea</a> <a href="/tags#tsp spice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Spice</a> <a href="/tags#flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Flour</a> <a href="/tags#egg" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>450G/1Lb Dried Mixed Fruit</li><li>250G/9Oz Brown Sugar</li><li>300Ml/ 1/2 Pint Warm Black Tea</li><li>2 Tsp Mixed Spice</li><li>450G/1Lb Self-Raising Flour</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Beaten</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Make a syrup of tea, sugar and spices. </li><li>Add fruit. </li><li>Cook for 20 minutes until soft. </li><li>Sift flour and add to mixture. </li><li>Fold in beaten egg. </li></ul>
