<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>For the ice cream, place the milk, ginger, vanilla pod and seeds in a saucepan and bring to the boil. Remove from heat and stir in the agar powder. </li><li>In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, sugar and custard powder. Pour the hot milk into the yolk mixture and stir thoroughly. Return the mixture to the saucepan and stir over a medium heat for 5 7 minutes, until the mixture thickens. </li><li>Remove from the heat and stir in the cream. Transfer to an airtight container and chill in the refrigerator until cold. </li><li>For the rhubarb cream, preheat oven to 150c. Spray a 2 litre deep, 4 cup swiss roll tin with cooking spray. Layer half the rhubarb in the tin, reserving the rest for decoration. Sprinkle with half the sugar and the orange zest and add the other half of the rhubarb, the juice and zest of the orange. Bake for 30 40 minutes until the rhubarb is tender and golden brown. Leave to cool then crumble. </li><li>For the brie, cream the butter and sugar until pale and fluffy. Add the flour and ground almonds and beat until combined. Stir in the egg whites and mix until smooth. Place a sheet of wax paper over the pastry and using a sharp knife, cut the pastry into 24 squares. Spoon into 4 ovenproof ramekins or custard cups. Sprinkle over the rhubarb, add the shaved</li></ul>
