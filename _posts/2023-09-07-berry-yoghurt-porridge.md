<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Berry Yoghurt Porridge using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 10 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#berries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Berries</a> <a href="/tags#apple" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Apple</a> <a href="/tags#milk" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Milk</a> <a href="/tags#yoghurt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Yoghurt</a> <a href="/tags#porridge oats fibre" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Porridge Oats Fibre</a> <a href="/tags#almonds" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Almonds</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Frozen Mixed Berries</li><li>1 Apple, Cored And Sliced</li><li>200Ml/7Floz Semi-Skimmed Milk</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Fat-Free Natural Yoghurt</li><li>25G/1Oz Porridge Oats, With Extra Fibre</li><li>10G/ 1/4Oz Flaked Almonds</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Pre heat oven to 350 degrees f. </li><li>Place fruit in a large bowl. </li><li>Add milk and yoghurt to mixture and mix together. </li><li>Divide mixture among 4 teacups. </li><li>Sprinkle with oats and nuts. </li><li>Bake uncovered for 25 30 minutes, or until berries are tender. </li></ul>
