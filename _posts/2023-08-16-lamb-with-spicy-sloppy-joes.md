<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>250Ml/9Floz Red Wine Vinegar</li><li>1 Bay Leaf</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Coriander</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>75G/3Oz Light Soft Brown Sugar</li><li>4 X 400G/14Oz Tinned Tomatoes</li><li>1 Tsp Salt</li><li>1 Tbsp English Mustard Powder</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Crushed</li><li>Tabasco Sauce, To Taste</li><li>2 Tbsp Tomato Puree</li><li>1-2 Tbsp Cornflour</li><li>400G/14Oz Floury Potatoes, Peeled And Cut Into Thick Batons 6Cm/2 1/2In Long And Dried On Kitchen Paper</li><li>15G/2/3Oz Fresh Yeast</li><li>Pinch Sugar</li><li>200Ml/7Floz Beer</li><li>1 Tsp Cider Vinegar</li><li>200G/7Oz Plain Flour</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>4 X 175G/6Oz Cod Fillet, Skinned And Pin Bones Removed</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Boil the vinegar with bay leaf, coriander, cinnamon and brown sugar. </li><li>Pour over the tomatoes, cover and simmer gently for 45 minutes or until soft, stirring occasionally. </li><li>Discard the bay leaf. </li><li>Transfer the tomatoes to a blender, add the salt, mustard powder, clove, tabasco, tomato puree and cornflour and puree. </li><li>Serve the beef, potatoes and sauce over boiled mash potatoes. </li><li>For the sauce</li><li>Dissolve the yeast and sugar in the beer and vinegar and leave for 5 minutes. </li><li>Pour over the flour and knead until smooth. </li><li>Cover with a tea towel and leave in a warm place until the dough has doubled in size. </li><li>Divide the dough into 4 pieces and roll out into a thin rectangle. </li><li>Cut each length of dough into 4 pieces and cut each piece of dough into 4 equal squares. </li><li>Press the squares out to make a rectangular patty. </li><li>Put the fish in a large frying pan and place over a medium heat. </li><li>Cook until the fish is cooked through, about 10 minutes. </li><li>Serve the fish with the potatoes and sauce. </li></ul>
