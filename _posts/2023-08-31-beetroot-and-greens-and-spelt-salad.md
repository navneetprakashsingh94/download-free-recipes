<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Pearled Spelt</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Vacuum-Packed Beetroot, Steamed, Ready-To-Eat And Chopped Into Small Chunks</li><li>3 Tbsp Live Plain Yoghurt</li><li>20G/ 3/4Oz Fresh Dill, Finely Chopped, Plus A Few Sprigs To Garnish</li><li>1 Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>1 Tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil</li><li>Handful Walnuts, Roughly Chopped And Toasted</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Blue Cheese, Such As Gorgonzola Or Creamy Irish Blue Cheese, Crumbled</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cook spelt to the directions on the packet, and set aside. </li><li>Wash, clean and cut beetroot chunks into small bite size pieces. Cook in boiling water until it s tender when pierced with a fork. </li><li>To prepare the dressing mix all the ingredients. Mix well. </li><li>Arrange beetroot on salad plates and sprinkle with gorgonzola cheese. </li></ul>
