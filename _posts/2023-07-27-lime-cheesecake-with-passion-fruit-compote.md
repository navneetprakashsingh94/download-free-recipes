<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>10 Large Digestive Biscuits</li><li>6 Ginger Biscuits</li><li>85G/3Oz Butter</li><li>675G/1 1/2Lb Cream Cheese</li><li>150Ml/ 1/4 Pint Double Cream</li><li>140G/5Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>3 Large Free-Range Eggs</li><li>3 Limes, Zest Only</li><li> 1/2 Pineapple</li><li>4 Passion Fruit</li><li>300Ml/ 1/2 Pint Double Cream</li><li>Icing Sugar, To Taste, Plus Extra For Dusting</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat oven to 200 degrees celsius. </li><li>Crush biscuits in a bowl. Mix in butter until well combined. Press onto bottom of a 20cm springform pan. </li><li>Beat cream cheese, cream and sugar on medium speed until light and fluffy. Add eggs and lime zest and mix until just combined. Pour into pan and place in the oven. Bake for about 55 minutes or until just set. Remove from oven and turn off the oven. Leave for 20 minutes or until cold. Cover with plastic wrap and chill in the fridge. </li><li>Meanwhile, to make the passionfruit compote, process pineapple, passion fruit, pint cream and sugar in a food processor until smooth. Cover and chill until ready to use. </li><li>Serve cheesecake with passionfruit compote and pineapple compote. </li></ul>
