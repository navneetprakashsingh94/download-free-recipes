<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Fried Fish With Sage And Polenta Crust using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#sage leaves size" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sage Leaves Size</a> <a href="/tags#free-range eggs" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Free-Range Eggs</a> <a href="/tags#plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#breadcrumbs polenta" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Breadcrumbs Polenta</a> <a href="/tags#fillets" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Fillets</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>40 Large Sage Leaves, All Similar Size</li><li>2 Free-Range Eggs, Beaten</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Plain Flour</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Very Fine White Breadcrumbs Or Polenta</li><li>10 Anchovy Fillets, Drained</li><li>Vegetable Oil, For Frying</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place the sage in a shallow bowl. </li><li>Whisk together the eggs and 1 cup water. </li><li>Spread the flour on a plate. </li><li>Combine the breadcrumbs with the polenta. </li><li>Dip each fish fillet first in the beaten egg mixture, then in the seasoned breadcrumbs, coating both sides. </li><li>Heat the oil in a frying pan and fry the fish in batches for 3 5 mins on each side, until cooked through. </li><li>Drain on paper towels. </li><li>Serve with sage dressing. </li></ul>
