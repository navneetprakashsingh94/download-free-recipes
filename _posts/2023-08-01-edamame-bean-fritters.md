<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>Vegetable Oil For Greasing</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg</li><li>2 Dessertspoons Milk</li><li>2 Spring Onions</li><li>Small Handful Fresh Coriander</li><li>2 Dessertspoons Sweetcorn</li><li>8 Green Beans</li><li>50G/2Oz Plain Flour</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li>Pinch Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Whisk together egg, milk, spring onion, coriander and sugar. </li><li>Finely chop the edamame beans and add them to the mixing bowl. </li><li>Add the flour, baking powder, pepper and mix well. </li><li>Heat oil in a pan and drop spoonfuls of batter into the oil. </li><li>Cook the fritters, one at a time, until golden brown on both sides. </li><li>Drain on kitchen paper. </li></ul>
