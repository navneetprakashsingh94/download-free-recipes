<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Tbsp Corn Oil</li><li>350G/12 1/4Oz Smoked Streaky Bacon, Diced</li><li>2 Spring Onions, Finely Chopped</li><li>115G/4 1/4Oz Sweetcorn Kernels</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Dried Chilli Flakes</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Polenta</li><li>350G/12 1/4Oz Plain Flour</li><li>1 Tbsp Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Baking Soda</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Salt</li><li>375Ml/13 1/2Fl Oz Buttermilk</li><li>2 Eggs, Lightly Beaten</li><li>1 Tbsp Maple Syrup</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Black Pepper</li><li>2 Tbsp Unsalted Butter, Melted</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat 2 tbsp oil in a large frying pan and cook bacon and onion for a few minutes, until bacon is crisp and onions just begin to soften. Remove to a bowl. </li><li>Add remaining oil, corn and chilli flakes to pan, cover and cook over a low heat for 12 15 minutes, until corn is soft and lightly browned. Set aside to cool. </li><li>In a large bowl, combine polenta, flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt. </li><li>In another bowl, whisk buttermilk, eggs, maple syrup, pepper and butter. Add to flour mixture, stir until just combined. </li><li>Heat a non stick frying pan. Pour a ladle of batter, then a scant 1 1/4 tbsp of the corn mixture on to the pan, flatten and cook until bubbles form on top and the sides are golden. Flip and cook until golden, about 2 minutes more. </li><li>Transfer to a large serving plate. </li><li>Repeat with remaining batter, adding more butter to the pan as needed. </li></ul>
