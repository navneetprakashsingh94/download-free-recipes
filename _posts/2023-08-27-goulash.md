<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. Put flour, salt and thyme in a mortar. </li><li>2. Rub meat with pepper and let stand 30 minutes. </li><li>3. Brown meat well in a dutch oven over medium heat. </li><li>4. Add onion, celery, brown sugar, vinegar, beer, bay leaves, thyme, garlic, pepper, parsley. </li><li>5. Cover, reduce heat and cook for 1 hour until tender. </li><li>6. Add stout and briskly simmer 1 more hour. </li><li>7. Remove meat to warm platter. </li><li>8. Put back into gravy and thicken with butter and flour. </li><li>9. Garnish with chopped parsley and a little butter on the top of the wine, if desired. </li></ul>
