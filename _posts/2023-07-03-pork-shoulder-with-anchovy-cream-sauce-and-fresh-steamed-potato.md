<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Lamb Shoulder</li><li>6 Anchovy Fillets</li><li>2 Sprigs Rosemary, Broken Into Small Pieces</li><li>1 Head Garlic, Peeled And Chopped</li><li>400Ml/14Fl Oz Gravy, To Serve</li><li>1Kg/2Lb 4Oz Potatoes, Cut Into Matchsticks</li><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Swedish Anchovies, Chopped</li><li>1 Onion, Sliced</li><li>500Ml/18Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Fresh White Breadcrumbs</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>2 Handfuls Sprout Leaves Or Baby Sprouts, Broken Into Leaves</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Pre heat oven to 200 c. Chop the garlic and rosemary, add the onions, garlic and rosemary with 50g of butter. Season with salt, pepper, anchovies and mix well. Set aside. </li><li>Soak the potatoes for 15 min in water, drain and set aside. Season the lamb with salt and pepper and put in a deep pan, add the pan juices and cook until the lamb is cooked about 35 minutes. </li><li>For the bbq gravy</li><li>1. Saute the anchovies and garlic. </li><li>2. Place the chicken over a hot grill and cook for a few min on each side. </li><li>3. Place the lamb back into the pan, cover with a sheet of aluminium foil and roast for another 30 45 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 145 f. Remove the meat from the pan and allow it to rest on a platter for 5 minutes. </li><li>4. Slice the lamb and place the slices on a large plate. </li><li>5. Pour the cream into the pan, turn down the heat and boil the cream for a couple of minutes. </li><li>6. Cut the onion in half and finely chop. </li><li>7. Slice the roasted garlic into slivers. </li><li>8. In a deep frying pan, heat 3 tablespoons of butter and fry the garlic for 1 min. </li><li>9. Pour the cream into the frying pan with the anchovies and onions. Stir and add the sliced meat slices. </li><li>10. Mash the potatoes with a fork. </li><li>11. Grease a large baking dish with 3 tablespoons of butter. </li><li>12. Line the dish with the potatoes, then add the meat slices, then add the cream. </li><li>13. Top with the breadcrumbs and then butter. </li><li>14. Bake for 30 minutes, or until the top is crispy and brown and the gravy has thickened. </li><li>15. Garnish with the baby sprouts leaves. </li><li>16. Serve immediately. </li></ul>
