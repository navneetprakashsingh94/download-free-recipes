<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#tips" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tips</a> <a href="/tags#lemons" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Lemons</a> <a href="/tags#chillies" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chillies</a> <a href="/tags#packet mint" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Packet Mint</a> <a href="/tags#ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ground Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Asparagus Tips, Trimmed</li><li>4 Large Preserved Lemons, Deseeded And Finely Chopped</li><li>6 Pickled Chillies, Thinly Sliced</li><li> 1/2 Packet (About 15G) Fresh Mint, Thinly Sliced</li><li>Freshly Ground Pepper</li><li>Olive Oil, For Drizzling</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cut off the ends of the asparagus tips then throw them into a blender and grind together until you have a smooth consistency. </li><li>Take the lemon juice and pour over the green chillies, mint leaves, a little black pepper, and salt to taste. </li><li>Let it sit for 10 minutes then blend again in the blender. </li><li>Serve the soup on a salad plate and drizzle over a little more oil. </li><li>I like it plain, and it tastes great with a side of steamed rice. </li></ul>
