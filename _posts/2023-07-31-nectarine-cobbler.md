<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#tbsp plain flour" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Plain Flour</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#tsp demerara sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Demerara Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp oats" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Oats</a> <a href="/tags#nectarine stone" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Nectarine Stone</a> <a href="/tags#orange juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Orange Juice</a> <a href="/tags#plain yoghurt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Yoghurt</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>2 Tbsp Plain Flour</li><li>20G/ 3/4Oz Butter</li><li>1 Tsp Demerara Sugar</li><li>1 Heaped Tbsp Rolled Oats</li><li>1 Ripe Nectarine, Halved, Stone Removed</li><li>2 Tbsp Orange Juice</li><li>Plain Yoghurt, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 180c. </li><li>Place flour, butter, sugar, oats and nectarine slices in a bowl and toss to combine. </li><li>Place mixture in a buttered shallow 1 litre ovenproof dish and bake for 20 minutes or until topping is golden and fruit is bubbling. </li><li>Meanwhile, combine orange juice and yoghurt and drizzle over fruit. </li><li>Return to oven for another 10 15 minutes or until bubbling and juices are bubbling. </li><li>Serve warm or cold. </li></ul>
