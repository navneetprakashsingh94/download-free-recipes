<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>225G/8Oz Streaky Bacon, Diced</li><li>175-200Ml/6-7Fl Oz Good Olive Oil</li><li>2 Onions, Finely Diced</li><li>2 Sticks Celery, Peeled And Finely Diced</li><li>2 Carrots, Peeled And Finely Sliced</li><li>5 Cloves Garlic, Peeled And Crushed With A Little Salt</li><li>2 Tsp Fresh Soft Thyme Leaves</li><li>2 Bay Leaves</li><li>2 Tsp Dried Oregano</li><li>2 X 400G/14Oz Cans Chopped Tomatoes</li><li>2 Tbsp Tomato Puree</li><li>1 Tbsp Anchovy Essence</li><li>2 Tbsp Worcestershire Sauce</li><li>1.8Kg/4Lb Minced Beef</li><li>250G/9Oz Fresh Chicken Livers, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Bottles Dry Red Wine</li><li>1.7 Litres/3 Pints Chicken, Beef Or Lamb Stock</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Fry the bacon in the oil till crisp and remove. </li><li>Add in the onions, celery and carrots and fry gently for 4 min. </li><li>Mix in the spices. </li><li>Add in the tomatoes, tomato puree, anchovies and stock to the vegetables and herbs. </li><li>Stir well and bring to a boil, cover and simmer for 1 to 1 1/2 hrs. </li><li>Remove any fat that is solids at the bottom of the pan and put in anchovy essence, wine and stock. </li><li>Simmer for a further 30 min. </li><li>Bring to a gentle boil and add in the sausage. </li><li>Simmer for 1 1/2 hrs. </li><li>Remove from the heat and allow to stand for 30 min. </li><li>Strain into a clean saucepan, bring back to the boil and thicken with a little flour. </li><li>Serve with pasta. </li></ul>
