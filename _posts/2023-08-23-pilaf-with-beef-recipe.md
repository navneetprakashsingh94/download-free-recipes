<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Onion, Chopped</li><li>3 Garlic Cloves, Chopped</li><li> 1/2 Tbsp Cumin Seeds</li><li>1 Red Chilli, Seeds Removed, Finely Chopped</li><li>250G/9Oz Beef Mince</li><li>25G/1Oz Butter</li><li>200G/7Oz White Rice</li><li>1 Litre/1 3/4 Pint Chicken Or Beef Stock, Made From A Cube</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Frozen Peas</li><li>Salt And Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Brown the onion in the oil and the spices for about 5 min. </li><li>Add in the mince and mix well. </li><li>Place the butter and seperated rice in the cooker and sprinkle the stock cube over the top. </li><li>Cook on low for around 8 min till the rice is cooked and the stock cube is dissolved. </li><li>Add in the meat mix and peas if you like and cook for a further 1 hour till the peas are tender. </li></ul>
