<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Berry Delight Fudge using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 43 min. Enjoy!</div>

<a href="/tags#chocolate chips" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Chocolate Chips</a> <a href="/tags#syrup" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Syrup</a> <a href="/tags#butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Butter</a> <a href="/tags#pinch salt" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pinch Salt</a> <a href="/tags#tsp ground cinnamon" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Ground Cinnamon</a> <a href="/tags#biscuits digestives" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Biscuits Digestives</a> <a href="/tags#mini marshmallows marshmallows use scissors pieces" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Mini Marshmallows Marshmallows Use Scissors Pieces</a> <a href="/tags#apples pieces" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Apples Pieces</a> <a href="/tags#raisins" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Raisins</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp icing sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Icing Sugar</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz White Chocolate, Roughly Chopped (Or Use Chocolate Chips)</li><li>2 Tbsp Golden Syrup</li><li>130G/4 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>Pinch Of Salt</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>200G/7Oz Cinnamon Biscuits (Or Digestives)</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz White Mini Marshmallows (If You Can Only Find Big Marshmallows, Use Scissors To Chop Them Into Small Pieces)</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Dried Apples, Chopped Into Small Pieces</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Raisins</li><li>1 Tbsp Icing Sugar, For Dusting</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Line a pan with greaseproof paper. </li><li>Melt chocolate chips in a saucepan, stir in the berries syrup, butter and salt, mix well. </li><li>Cook on low heat, stirring frequently, for 3 minutes and then add the cinnamon, mix well. </li><li>Set aside to cool. </li><li>Place the crushed biscuits and mini marshmallows in a large bowl. </li><li>Pour in the chocolate/rum mixture, fold gently, pour into the baking pan, cover with lid and let it cool in the fridge. </li><li>Combine the apples, raisins and icing sugar in a bowl. </li><li>Stir well. </li><li>Spoon over the fudge, spread out evenly and let it cool completely. </li></ul>
