<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Lutte S Apple Blackberry Jam using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 50 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#Bramley apples chunks" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Bramley Apples Chunks</a> <a href="/tags#onions" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Onions</a> <a href="/tags#sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#vinegar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Vinegar</a> <a href="/tags#tsp ground cinnamon" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Ground Cinnamon</a> <a href="/tags#ground pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ground Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#blackberries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Blackberries</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1Kg/2.2Lb Bramley Apples, Peeled, Cored, Cut Into Large Chunks</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Onions, Peeled, Thinly Sliced</li><li>275G/10Oz Granulated Sugar</li><li>150Ml/5Fl Oz Balsamic Vinegar</li><li>2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li>Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Blackberries</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Put in pan apples, onions, sugar, vinegar, cinnamon and pepper. </li><li>Bring to a boil, stirring, for 5 minutes. </li><li>Reduce heat and simmer, stirring, for 25 minutes. </li><li>Add berries and stir for 15 minutes. </li><li>Pour into sterile glass jars. </li><li>Seal, let cool, seal and store. </li><li>Makes 3 1/2 pints of jam. </li></ul>
