<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Pancakes With Lemon Almond Butter Apricots using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#oz cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Cream</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp almond butter" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Almond Butter</a> <a href="/tags#tbsp blueberry conserve" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tbsp Blueberry Conserve</a> <a href="/tags#pancakes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Pancakes</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>4-6 Tbsp Almond Butter</li><li>4 Tbsp Blueberry Conserve</li><li>4 Pancakes, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Prepare pudding as directed on the box. </li><li>Whip cream until peaks form. Add the almond butter and blueberry conserve and fold into pudding. </li><li>Heat a frying pan and lightly coat with oil. </li><li>Take the batter into the pan and cook for 2 minutes on each side. </li><li>Place each pancake on the plate and spoon the topping on top. </li><li>Serve. </li></ul>
