<h2>Description</h2>
<div>Delicious Angel Delight Ice Cream using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 20 min. Enjoy!</div>

<a href="/tags#caster sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Caster Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#egg whites" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Egg Whites</a> <a href="/tags#sponge flan case half discs recipe" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sponge Flan Case Half Discs Recipe</a> <a href="/tags#Armagnac" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Armagnac</a> <a href="/tags#firm vanilla ice cream soft-scoop" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Firm Vanilla Ice Cream Soft-Scoop</a> <a href="/tags#plain chocolate" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Plain Chocolate</a> <a href="/tags#oz cream" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oz Cream</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>6 Free-Range Egg Whites</li><li>1 Small Ready-Made Sponge Flan Case, Cut In Half Horizontally To Create Two Thinner Discs (Only One Is Needed In This Recipe)</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Armagnac</li><li>1 X 500G/1Lb 2Oz Tub Firm Vanilla Ice Cream (Not Soft-Scoop)</li><li>200G/7Oz Plain Chocolate, Roughly Chopped</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Double Cream</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Place caster sugar in a saucepan and set over medium heat to melt. Make sure it does not come to a boil. Stir for approximately 7 minutes until the mixture turns dark amber. Remove from heat, stir in egg whites and pour into a mixing bowl. Place into the fridge for 2 hours to cool completely. </li><li>After 2 hours, beat with an electric mixer for 5 minutes. Cover with plastic wrap and return to the fridge for 1 hour. </li><li>Remove foil and roll the sponge sponge out as thinly as possible. Place on a flat baking tray covered with a silicone mat. Freeze for approximately 2 hours until firm. </li><li>Meanwhile, heat a large pan of water on the stove and bring the water to a boil. Using a metal spoon, stir the ice cream around until softened. Remove from heat, add the liqueur and mix well. Return to the stove and keep on a low simmer until thickened to consistency of cake batter. Add the melted chocolate liqueur. Remove the cake from the freezer, cut the cake into 4 even wedges. Place the cake wedges on dessert plates and top with the warm chocolate sauce. Sprinkle with icing sugar to serve. </li></ul>
