<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#blackcurrants" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Blackcurrants</a> <a href="/tags#approx sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Approx Sugar</a> <a href="/tags#tsp brandy" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tsp Brandy</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1Kg/2Lb 4Oz Blackcurrants</li><li>Approx. 500G/1Lb 2Oz Granulated Sugar</li><li>4-6 Tsp Brandy (Optional)</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>In a food processor, put the blackcurrants and raisins and blend to a fine puree. </li><li>In a saucepan, combine the blackcurrant puree with the sugar. </li><li>Heat gently over low heat, stirring, till the sugar has dissolved. </li><li>Add in the brandy. </li><li>Let simmer for 5 min, stirring occasionally. </li><li>Allow to cold slightly. </li><li>Refrigeratefor at least 1 day before serving. </li><li>Serve chilled. </li><li>This recipe yields 4 servings. </li></ul>
