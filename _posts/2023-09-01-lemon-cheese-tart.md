<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>200G/7Oz Plain Flour</li><li>2 Tbsp Icing Sugar</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, Chilled, Cut Into Roughly 1Cm/ 1/2In Cubes</li><li>1 Free-Range Egg, Lightly Beaten</li><li>1 Tsp Lemon Juice</li><li>350G/12Oz Cooked And Peeled Beetroot (Vacuum-Packed Is Fine, But Not Pickled Beetroot)</li><li>125Ml/4Fl Oz Double Cream</li><li>2 Large Free-Range Eggs</li><li>175G/6Oz Dark Muscovado Sugar</li><li>1 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Ground Ginger</li><li>1 Lemon, Finely Grated Zest Only</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Crust combine the flour, icing sugar and butter in a food processor and pound until it resembles breadcrumbs. </li><li>Add the egg white and juice and process until it forms a ball. </li><li>Wrap in cling film and put into the fridge to rest for 1 hour. </li><li>Roll out the pastry to a thickness of 1/4 in and line an 11cm loose bottomed flan dish with it. </li><li>Chill in the refrigerator for 30 minutes. </li><li>Filling meanwhile, mix together the beetroot and cream in a food processor. </li><li>Separate the eggs and place the yolks in a clean bowl and beat lightly with a fork to combine. </li><li>Gradually beat in the sugar, cinnamon, ginger and lemon juice. </li><li>Blend into the cream mixture until smooth. </li><li>Gently fold in the shredded beetroot. </li><li>Set a small bowl and a large plate on top of the pastry and carefully pour the filling into the pastry case, smoothing it out. </li><li>Bake in a preheated oven at 170c for 30 to 35 minutes, or until the top is golden brown. </li><li>Transfer to a rack to cool. </li></ul>
