<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>600G/1Lb 5Oz Raw Beetroot</li><li>2 Unwaxed Lemons, Finely Grated Zest Only</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Lemon Juice (About 2-3 Lemons)</li><li>150Ml/ 1/4 Pint Cider Vinegar</li><li>1 Red Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Large Cooking Apples, Peeled, Cored And Roughly Chopped (Approx 250G/9Oz Prepared Weight)</li><li>2 Tsp Fennel Seeds</li><li>200G/7Oz Granulated Sugar</li><li>2 Tbsp Capers Or Caper Berries, Roughly Chopped</li><li>Large Pinch Of Sea Salt</li><li>Few Grinds Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Steam or boil beetroot if using unpeeled, or until a knife slides through the skin easily. </li><li>Chop and place in a large glass or plastic bowl. </li><li>Squeeze lemon zest into the bowl, reserving juice. </li><li>In a medium saucepan, simmer juice, vinegar, onions, apples, fennel seeds and sugar, over a medium heat until the sugar dissolves. </li><li>Add to beetroot with capers, salt and pepper. </li><li>Stir until well combined. </li><li>Cover and let sit at least one hour and up to 2 days in the fridge. </li><li>When ready to use, stir. </li><li>If you would like a thicker relish, add in 1 tablespoon water at a time. </li></ul>
