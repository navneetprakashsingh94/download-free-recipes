<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#oil" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Oil</a> <a href="/tags#onion" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Onion</a> <a href="/tags#cloves" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cloves</a> <a href="/tags#tins tomatoes" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Tins Tomatoes</a> <a href="/tags#salt pepper" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Salt Pepper</a> <a href="/tags#medium aubergines" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Medium Aubergines</a> <a href="/tags#ball mozzarella pieces" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Ball Mozzarella Pieces</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>3 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>3 Garlic Cloves, Crushed</li><li>2 X 400G/14Oz Tins Chopped Tomatoes</li><li>Salt And Pepper</li><li>2 Medium To Large Aubergines</li><li>1 Ball Mozzarella, Drained And Torn Into Pieces</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Fry the onion in the oil for about 5 minutes till the onion is transparent. </li><li>Crush the cloves and add to the onions. </li><li>Add the tomatoes and cook over a very low heat for another 15 minutes. </li><li>Season to taste. </li><li>Cut the aubergines in thick slices, brush with oil and season with salt pepper. </li><li>Place on a baking tray and bake at 180c for 30 minutes or until softened. </li><li>Slice the mozzarella and stir the slices into the tomato sauce. </li><li>Fill the aubergine slices with tomato sauce. </li><li>Serve immediately with a crunchy salad. </li></ul>
