<div>Delicious Blueberry Sorbet using fresh ingredients, made with ❤️ for you. Give it a try today, easy to cook. Total time to cook is approx 50 min. Enjoy!</div>

<h2>Raw Ingredients</h2>
<a href="/tags#cooking apples" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Cooking Apples</a> <a href="/tags#blueberries" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Blueberries</a> <a href="/tags#apple juice" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Apple Juice</a> <a href="/tags#medium orange" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Medium Orange</a> <a href="/tags#sugar" class="badge badge-info p-2" target="_blank">Sugar</a> 

<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Cooking Apples (About 3)</li><li>250G/9Oz Blueberries</li><li>300Ml/10Fl Oz Clear Apple Juice</li><li>1 Medium Orange, Zest Only</li><li>350G/12Oz Sugar</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Core and cut apples into 1 inch chunks. </li><li>In a food processor, puree apples and blueberries until smooth. </li><li>Place in a medium saucepan with the apple juice, orange slices and enough sugar to taste. </li><li>Simmer, stirring often, until sauce is thick and syrupy, about 30 minutes. </li><li>Serve hot or cold. </li></ul>
