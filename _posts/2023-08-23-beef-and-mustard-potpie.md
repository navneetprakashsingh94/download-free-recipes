<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1Kg/2Lb 4Oz Beef Skirt, Cut Into Large Chunks</li><li>2 Tbsp Plain Flour</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>2 Tbsp Rapeseed Oil</li><li>200Ml/7Fl Oz Red Wine</li><li>400Ml/14Fl Oz Beef Stock</li><li>1 Onion, Finely Sliced</li><li>2 Large Carrots, Cut Into 2.5Cm/1In Pieces</li><li>3 Sprigs Thyme</li><li>2 Tbsp Wholegrain Mustard</li><li>2 Free-Range Egg Yolks, Beaten</li><li>400G/14Oz All-Butter Puff Pastry, Rolled To 5Mm/ 1/4In Thick</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Fine Green Beans</li><li>25G/1Oz Butter</li><li>Sea Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Season the beef with salt and pepper and dust in flour. </li><li>Heat the oil in a pot and cook the beef for 5 minutes on each side, until cooked to your liking. </li><li>Add the wine to the meat in the pot and reduce by half. </li><li>Add the stock and bring to a boil. </li><li>Reduce the heat and simmer for 45 minutes. </li><li>Remove the beef from the liquid, shred into pieces, and return to the liquid. </li><li>Add the onion, carrot and thyme. </li><li>Simmer for 5 minutes. </li><li>Remove from the heat. </li><li>Add the mustard and egg yolks and whisk to combine. </li><li>For the filling, melt butter in a saucepan and fry the beans until soft. </li><li>Season with salt and pepper. </li><li>Add the filling and mix well. </li><li>Divide the pastry between 4 oven proof bowls. </li><li>Distribute the filling between the bowls and top with the pastry. </li><li>Bake in a preheated oven at 400f for 20 minutes until golden brown. </li></ul>
