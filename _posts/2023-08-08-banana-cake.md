<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>150G/5 1/2Oz Unsalted Butter, At Room Temperature</li><li>175G/6Oz Golden Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Medium Egg, Lightly Beaten</li><li>175G/6Oz Plain Flour</li><li> 1/2 Teaspoon Bicarbonate Of Soda</li><li> 1/2 Teaspoon Ground Cinnamon</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Pecans (Or Walnuts), Roughly Chopped</li><li>75G/2 1/2Oz Dark Chocolate Chips</li><li>2 Ripe Bananas (200G/7Oz Peeled Weight), Mashed</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Cream butter and sugar together. </li><li>Add egg, mix until fluffy. </li><li>Sift flour, soda, cinnamon, and salt. </li><li>Mix the flour mixture into the butter mix, mix well. </li><li>Place bananas in a separate bowl. </li><li>Pour cake mix into cake pan, spread evenly. </li><li>Sprinkle walnuts and chocolate chips on top of cake mix, do not mix. </li><li>Place in oven at 200 degrees celsius, bake for 35 40 minutes or until inserted toothpick comes out clean. </li></ul>
