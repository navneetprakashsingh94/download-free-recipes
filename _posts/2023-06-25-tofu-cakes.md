<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>280G/10Oz Extra Firm Tofu, Drained</li><li>1 Tbsp Soy Sauce</li><li>1 Tbsp Vegetable Oil</li><li>1 Garlic Clove, Crushed Or Grated</li><li>1 Tsp Grated Fresh Root Ginger</li><li>1 Tsp Paprika</li><li>Pinch Salt</li><li>2 Tbsp Cornflour</li><li>Sweet Chilli Sauce, To Serve</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1 drain the tofu and slice into 2cm slices</li><li></li><li>2 add the oil to a frying pan and heat</li><li></li><li>3 add the tofu slices and fry lightly on both sides until golden</li><li></li><li>4 add the garlic, ginger, paprika, and stir fry briefly</li><li></li><li>5 add the soy sauce, ginger garlic paste, and stir fry for 3 5 minutes</li><li></li><li>6 put the tofu into a serving dish, and pour over the chilli sauce</li><li></li><li>7 add any other vegetables or vegetables you like and serve warm</li></ul>
