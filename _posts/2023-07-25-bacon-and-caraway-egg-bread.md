<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>60Ml /4 Tbsp Milk</li><li>1 Tsp Dried Yeast</li><li>250G/9Oz Streaky Bacon, Chopped</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Plain Flour, Plus Extra For Dusting</li><li>1 Tsp Baking Powder</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Salt</li><li>2 Tbsp Parmesan, Grated</li><li>1 Tbsp Caraway Seeds</li><li>120G/4 1/2 Oz Unsalted Butter</li><li>120Ml/4Fl Oz Soured Cream</li><li>2 Eggs, Beaten</li><li>Beaten Egg And A Drop Of Water, To Glaze</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>1. In a medium bowl stir together milk, yeast, and bacon. Set aside and allow to stand, stirring occasionally. In a medium bowl sift together flour, baking powder, salt, parmesan, and caraway. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse meal. </li><li>2. Mix in yeast mixture and cream. Add eggs and mix until dry ingredients are moistened and a sticky dough forms. Cover and let rise in a warm place until doubled in bulk, about 1 hour. </li><li>3. Preheat oven to 375 f. Brush a 9x5x3 inch loaf pan with vegetable oil. Roll dough out into a rectangle on a lightly floured surface. Cut into 9 strips. Gently twist each strip and place in loaf pan. </li><li>4. Brush tops of bread with egg drop water. Bake about 20 min or until well browned. Remove from pan and cool on rack before slicing. </li></ul>
