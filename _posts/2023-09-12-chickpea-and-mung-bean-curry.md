<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>400G Tin Chickpeas (Preferably Black Chickpeas), Drained</li><li>400G Tin Black Beans, Drained</li><li>250G Pouch Cooked Beluga Lentils, Drained</li><li>Small Handful Coriander, Roughly Chopped</li><li> 1/2 Lime, Juice Only</li><li>50Ml/2Fl Oz Sunflower Oil</li><li>1 Green Chilli, Finely Chopped</li><li>1 Small Red Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>3 Garlic Cloves, Minced</li><li>30G/1Oz Fresh Root Ginger, Peeled And Finely Grated</li><li>1 Tsp Black Mustard Seeds</li><li>2 Tsp Cumin Seeds</li><li>1 Tsp Nigella Seeds</li><li>10 Dried Curry Leaves</li><li>1 Tsp Fine Sea Salt</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Put the spices and salt into a tin of water for 10 minutes. </li><li>Heat the oil in a frying pan and add the onion and chilli and fry until the onion is soft but not coloured. </li><li>Now add the ginger, curry leaves, chickpeas, mung beans and lentils. </li><li>Stir well and cover with a lid and cook for 2 to 3 minutes. </li><li>Add the water and cook for 5 minutes on a medium heat. </li><li>Add the lime juice and stir well. </li><li>Serve over rice with some extra coriander leaves, chopped chilli and steamed rice. </li></ul>
