<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 1/2 Litres/2 1/2 Pints Whole Milk</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Short Grain Or Pudding Rice</li><li>15G/ 1/2Oz Butter</li><li>3 Tbsp Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Vanilla Pod, Split</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Blanched Almonds, Finely Chopped</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Cherries, Pitted</li><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Lemon, Juice Only</li><li>2 Tsp Arrowroot</li><li>Kirsch, To Taste (Optional)</li><li>100Ml/3 1/2Fl Oz Double Cream, Whipped</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 375 f</li><li>In a pan, mix 1/2 liter milk, rice and half the caster sugar until well combined. Bring to boil, cover, then lower heat and simmer 20 25 minutes until rice is tender. </li><li>Meanwhile, cook remaining milk, butter and remaining sugar until sugar dissolves and mixture thickens. Add vanilla pod and remove from heat. </li><li>Meanwhile, to make cherry cream combine cherries and sugar in a small pan and simmer over a medium heat until sugar dissolves, about 5 minutes. Add lemon juice, cook for 5 minutes more, until cherries are tender. </li><li>Mix remaining milk with arrowroot until smooth. Stir into cherry mixture, bring to boil, and simmer for 1 minute. </li><li>Drain risotto. Stir in kirsch, if desired. </li><li>Sprinkle almonds on top and serve with cherry cream. </li></ul>
