<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>1 Large Red Onion, Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Oil</li><li>1 Carrot, Quartered Lengthways And Chopped</li><li>2 Garlic Cloves, Crushed</li><li>1 Tsp Chilli Powder</li><li> 3/4 Tsp Smoked Sweet Paprika</li><li> 1/2 Tsp Dried Oregano</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Best-Quality Beef Mince</li><li>2 Tbsp Tomato Puree</li><li>1 X 400G/14Oz Tin Kidney Beans, Drained And Rinsed</li><li>1 Red Or Green Pepper, Seeds Removed And Roughly Chopped</li><li>1 X 400G/14Oz Tin Best-Quality Chopped Tomatoes</li><li>500-750Ml/18-22Fl Oz Beef Stock</li><li>Sea Salt And Black Pepper</li><li>2 Squares 70% Dark Chocolate, Grated</li><li>Small Bunch Coriander, Chopped</li><li>Cooked Rice</li><li>Fresh Coriander, Roughly Chopped</li><li>Soured Cream</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>In a large pan saute onion, carrot, spices and beef mince for 8 mins, or until meat is browned</li><li>Drain excess fat</li><li>Add the tomatoes, tomato puree, stock, salt pepper. Stir. Bring to the boil and simmer for 30 mins, or until the juices have thickened. </li><li>Add the chocolate and coriander and stir through. </li><li>Serve over rice garnished with coriander leaves and cream</li></ul>
