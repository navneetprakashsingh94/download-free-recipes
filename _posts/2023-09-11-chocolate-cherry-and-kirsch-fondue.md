<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Chocolate fondue. </li><li>Preheat oven to 160 degrees. </li><li>Butter a deep fondue pot or a souffle dish. </li><li>Combine butter, chocolate pieces and sugar and melt over a low heat. </li><li>Allow to cool. </li><li>Whisk eggs and vanilla into the chocolate mix. </li><li>Add chocolate mix. </li><li>Whisk in the flour, baking soda and chocolate pieces until fully incorporated. </li><li>Stir in cherries. </li><li>Pour into the fondue pot and cook for 30 40 minutes until golden and set. </li><li>When ready to serve, remove from the hot pot with a palette knife and place a serving plate upside down on the base of the pot to catch the fondue and hold in place. </li><li>Pour cream over the base, place back over the stove over a low heat until a little bit of the sauce is left, then pour over the dessert. </li><li>Kirsch to the fondue. </li><li>Whisk together all ingredients except the cherry syrup and conserve until smooth. </li><li>Pour into a bowl and set in a pan of simmering water and continue stirring occasionally until thickened. </li><li>Mascarpone. </li><li>Combine the mascarpone and cream and whip until soft peaks form. </li><li>Pour the cherry syrup over the mascarpone mixture. </li><li>Cover the fondue pot with the cream and chocolate pieces. </li><li>Serve immediately with the fruit. </li></ul>
