<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>3-4 Medium (Apple-Sized) Beetroot (About 500-600G/1Lb 2Oz-1Lb5Oz, Grated Coarsely, Or Choped Into Small Dice</li><li>500G/1Lb 2Oz Ripe Tomatoes, Halved</li><li>1 Clove Garlic, Chopped Roughly</li><li>1 Medium Onion, Peeled And Finely Chopped</li><li>2 Tbsp Olive Or Sunflower Oil</li><li>500Ml/16 1/2Fl Oz Good Strong Stock (Beef Is Best, But Chicken Or Vegetable Will Do)</li><li>Salt And Freshly Ground Black Pepper</li><li>125G/4Oz Real (I.E. Greek Not Danish) Feta Cheese</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Heat oil in a pan, add the onion and garlic and cook until the onion becomes translucent, then add the diced tomatoes and beetroot and cook until the vegetables soften, then add the stock, bring to the boil and then lower heat. </li><li>Season with salt and pepper to taste. </li><li>Add lulu noodle and cook just until the noodles are tender. </li><li>When the mixture is done remove from the heat, stir the lulu noodle well and serve with a sprinkling of freshly ground pepper. </li></ul>
