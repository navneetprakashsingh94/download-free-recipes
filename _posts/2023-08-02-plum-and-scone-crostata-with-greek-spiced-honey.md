<h2>Ingredients With Quantity </h2>
<ul><li>100G/3 1/2Oz Butter, Cut Into Pieces, Plus Extra For Greasing</li><li>300G/10 1/2Oz Self-Raising Flour, Sifted</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Caster Sugar</li><li>1 Orange, Zest And 2 Tsp Juice</li><li>150Ml/ 1/4 Pint Of Milk</li><li>700G/1Lb 9Oz Plums, Stoned And Halved</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Butter</li><li>50G/1 3/4Oz Demerara Sugar</li><li>2 Tsp Ground Cinnamon</li></ul>

<h2>Directions</h2>
<ul><li>Preheat oven to 200c/400f/gas mark 2. Put the soft butter in a large mixing bowl and beat with a wooden spoon until the mixture is crumbly. Set aside until needed. </li><li>Combine the flour, sugar and orange zest in a large bowl. Rub the butter into the flour using your fingers or a pastry blender until the mixture is crumbly. </li><li>Gradually stir in the milk until the mixture forms a smooth dough. </li><li>On a lightly floured surface, knead for 30 seconds. Place dough in an oiled bowl, cover with plastic wrap and let rest for 15 minutes. </li><li>Roll the dough out to a 7 x 8 rectangle, then use the dough to line a 13 x 9 inch pan. Brush with melted butter and sprinkle with demerara sugar and cinnamon. Arrange the plums in the baking dish. </li><li>Roll the dough out again to a 10 x 11 rectangle and cover with plastic wrap. Place in the fridge until ready to bake. </li><li>Cut the pastry into 4 rows by 3 strips and lay a second strip on top. Roll the dough up. Using a sharp knife, cut 3 diagonal cuts across the top in an overlapping pattern. Place the scones on a baking sheet and bake for 10 to 12 minutes until browned. </li><li>Place the scones in an ovenproof dish and return to the oven for 5 minutes. </li><li>Cut into 16 equal pieces and serve with butter and honey. </li></ul>
